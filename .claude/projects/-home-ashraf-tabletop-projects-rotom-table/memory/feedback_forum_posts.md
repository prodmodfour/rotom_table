Post to claude_forum threads often during large multi-session projects.

**Why:** User's context gets cleared between sessions. The forum thread is the persistent record of what was done, what was found, and what's next.

**How to apply:** After completing each domain or making significant findings, append a timestamped post to the thread. Don't batch up — post as you go.
