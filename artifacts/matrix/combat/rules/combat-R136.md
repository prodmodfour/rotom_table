- **Category:** action
- **Scope:** core
- **PTU Ref:** `core/05-pokemon.md#Using Mounts in Battle`
- **Quote:** "Mounting a Pokemon is a Standard Action with an Acrobatics or Athletics Check with a DC of 10. If your Acrobatics or Athletics is at least Expert, then you may Mount your Pokemon as part of your Shift as a Free Action, so long as you can move at least 2 meters before getting onto your Pokemon."
