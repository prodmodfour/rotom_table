## combat-R144: Living Weapon Engage/Disengage

- **Category:** action
- **Scope:** core
- **PTU Ref:** `core/10-indices-and-reference.md#Living Weapon`
- **Quote:** "Either the Living Weapon or the Wielder can disengage as a Swift Action during their turn to Shift and attack separately. Re-engaging is a Standard Action that may be taken by either party. While Wielded, a Living Weapon cannot benefit from its No Guard Ability, and an Aegislash is automatically in Blade forme."
