## Summary

`Math.trunc` is used instead of `Math.floor` in movementModifiers.ts for negative Speed Combat Stage calculations. For odd negative CS values (-3, -5), `Math.trunc` rounds toward zero while `Math.floor` rounds down, causing a 1m difference in movement.

## Suggested Fix

Replace `Math.trunc` with `Math.floor` for the negative CS movement calculation.

## Impact

Minimal — 1m movement difference at odd negative CS values only.
