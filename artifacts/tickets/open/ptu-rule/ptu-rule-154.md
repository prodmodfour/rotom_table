## Summary

Hazard terrain type exists visually on the VTT but has no mechanical effect. Entering hazard terrain should apply damage per PTU rules, but currently the movement cost is 1 with no damage trigger.

## Impact

Environmental hazards are cosmetic only; GM must manually apply hazard damage.
