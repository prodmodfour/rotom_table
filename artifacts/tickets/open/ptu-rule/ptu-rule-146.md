## Summary

PTU defaults to rolled damage. The app defaults to set damage with rolled damage available as secondary option. Some GMs may expect rolled damage as the primary workflow.

## Impact

Workflow mismatch with PTU RAW default. Functional but not aligned with core rules expectation.
