# ux-006: PTU injury markers may leak precise HP info in player mode

## Summary

PTU injury markers fire at exact HP thresholds: 50%, 0%, -50%, -100%. If injury markers are ever displayed on enemy tokens in player mode, players could deduce that an enemy crossed a specific threshold even though the HP bar shows a rounded 25%-increment tier.

Example: An enemy at 49% HP would show a 50% HP bar (via `roundToDisplayTier`), but a 50% injury marker would reveal that HP actually dropped below 50%.

Currently, `VTTToken` does not display injury markers on tokens, so this is a **future concern only**. This ticket exists to ensure HP masking consistency when/if injury markers are added to tokens.

## Affected Files

- `app/components/vtt/VTTToken.vue` (no injury markers currently)
- `app/composables/usePlayerGridView.ts` (getDisplayHp / roundToDisplayTier)

## Suggested Fix

When injury markers are added to VTTToken:
1. In player mode, suppress injury marker display on enemy tokens, OR
2. Round injury marker thresholds to match the display tier (e.g., only show injury markers at tier boundaries: 75%, 50%, 25%, 10%, 0%)

## Impact

- **Information asymmetry:** Without this fix, injury markers would contradict the HP bar rounding and leak precise HP state to players.
- **Current risk:** None — VTTToken has no injury marker display. This is preventive.
