## Summary

`resetSceneUsage()` exists to clear scene-frequency move counters, but it is never called from any scene lifecycle endpoint. Scene-frequency moves (usable once per scene) persist their usage counters across scene boundaries.

## Suggested Fix

Call `resetSceneUsage()` when transitioning between scenes (scene end or new scene start).

## Impact

Scene-frequency moves become permanently consumed after first use until manual reset.
