# ux-002: Trainer HP stat display shows raw stat without formula context

## Summary

The Player View character sheet displays the raw HP stat value (e.g., "13") in the stats grid alongside the derived HP total (e.g., "57/57 HP") in the header bar. Players unfamiliar with the PTU HP formula (`level*2 + HP*3 + 10`) may be confused why the stat says "13" when their HP is "57."

## Affected Files

- `app/components/player/PlayerCharacterSheet.vue` (lines 234-245)

## Suggested Fix

Label the stat cell as "HP Base" instead of "HP", or add a tooltip explaining the HP formula: `Level x2 + HP x3 + 10 = Max HP`.

## Impact

UX confusion only — the displayed value IS the correct PTU stat. No mechanical incorrectness. Low priority since experienced PTU players will understand, but new players may be confused.
