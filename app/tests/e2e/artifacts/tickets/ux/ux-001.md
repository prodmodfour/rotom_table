## Summary

The EquipmentCatalogBrowser modal closes immediately after a successful equip. If a GM wants to equip multiple items from the catalog in one session (e.g., body + offHand + accessory), they must reopen the browser each time.

## Affected Files

- `app/components/character/tabs/HumanEquipmentTab.vue` — `onCatalogEquipped()` sets `showCatalog.value = false` immediately
- `app/components/character/EquipmentCatalogBrowser.vue` — `equipToCharacter()` emits `equipped` on success

## Suggested Fix

Keep the modal open after equip. Show a brief success indicator (toast or checkmark on the item) and let the user close manually when done. Alternatively, add a small "equipped" visual indicator and keep the Equip button active.

## Impact

Low — functional but creates UX friction for multi-item equipping sessions.
