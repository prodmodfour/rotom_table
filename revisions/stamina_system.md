# Stamina Stat
	- Base Stamina operates on a similar scale to other base stats

# Energy 
	- A resource derived from Stamina, similar to how health is derived from HP.
	- Goes up slowly compared to health.
	- 1 Stamina -> 3 Energy
	- 100 Stamina -> 20 Energy
	- Need to derive a formula
	- Energy regain rate: Base 1 at the start of each turn. Goes up with traits.

# Frequency -> Energy Cost
	- We're getting rid of frequency entirely.
	- We're replacing them with Energy Costs
	- Each move will have an Energy Cost to balance how powerful it is
	- Some low distribution moves will have favourable costs despite their power as a way of giving a specific pokemon or set of poemon something signature and noteworthy
	- Some moves with downsides may have low energy costs to offset their downside

	
# Movement
	- Moving using MP costs no Energy.
	- You can use Energy to move (2 Energy for 1 movement point, modifiable by traits, accesible if you other don't have enough mp to do the move you want)

# Fatigue Levels
	- For each level of Fatigue, -2 to attack rolls, -2 to Evasions, -2 Movement Points (Floor is 0 for MP)
	- At 5 levels, you are unconscious
	- Taking a breather recovers 1 level of Fatigue
	- Whenever you go down to 0 Energy, you gain 1 level of fatigue
	- You can spend more Energy than you have, gaining 3 levels of fatigue. Floor is still 0.
