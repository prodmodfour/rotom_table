- Movement action (previously called Shift action) can be split in between other actions. The atomic unit is a Movement point. 
	- With 3 movement points, you can potentially split up your movement three ways. 
- Each Move has a start and an end. Any path can be taken in between those two points as long as it fits the budget.
- Movement Rates are determined and improved by Traits
	- Without a trait, rate is 0 Movement per point with no movement modes available
	- If your rate in a mode is 0, you can't move in that mode
- Movement Points have a base of 1
	- This is increased or decreased with Traits
# Movement Modes
	- Land
		- Usual rate is 4
	- Sky
	- Water
		- Usual rate is 2
	- Phase
	- Burrow
	- Teleporter
	- Most have Land and Water

# Sprint
	- Sprint action removed, we now have energy -> Movement point system
