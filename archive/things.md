
# Things
	- "Things" can have both quantities and qualities that determine how that Thing behaves and how it interacts with other Things.
	- Things are composed of Things
	- Things are composed of Things
	
## What is a quality?
	- A quality is a description that explains or modifies some thing
	- A quality is a Thing
	- A quality can have a varied composition
		- X Qualities, where X is a positive integer 
		- Y Qualities, where Y is a positive integer
		- Z Numbers, where Z is a positive integer
		- A Texts, where A is a positive integer
		- X + Y + Z + A >= 2
	- Qualities cannot have Facets.
	- Qualities cannot have Entities.
	
## What is a quantity?
	- A quantitity is a number that is used by something
	- A quantity is a Thing
	- A quantity is a Thing that is composed of 1 Number and 1 Quality
		
## What is a Number? 
	- A number is either an integer or a real
	- This is an atomic Thing, it is not composed of other things

## What is Text?
	- Text is a readable, meaningful collecion of words
	- This is an atomic Thing, it is not composed of other things
	
## What is an Atomic Thing
	- Atomic Things are always components
		- They are meaningless on their own
		- Cannot exist unless as a component in a Composed Thing. 

## What is a Composed Thing?
	- A Thing that is made up of multiple Things.

## What is a Facet?
	- A Composed Thing
	- Always a part of a larger Thing
	- An element of interaction 

# Entities
	- A Composed Thing
	- Can exist both as part of a larger Thing or on its own.
