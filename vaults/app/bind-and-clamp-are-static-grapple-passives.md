Bind (Normal/Status) and Clamp (Water/Status) are stored in the [[movedata-reference-table]] with frequency `Static`, range `--`, AC `null`, and damage base `null`. The [[move-frequency-utility]] treats Static moves as always blocked — they cannot be actively used as combat actions.

Both moves share identical effect text: they grant +1 to accuracy rolls for initiating Grapple Maneuvers, +2 to skill checks for Grapple Maneuvers or gaining Dominance, and deal a tick of HP damage to the grapple target whenever the user gains Dominance. They function as passive grappling bonuses rather than traditional attacks.

In the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]], Static moves appear greyed out and cannot be selected.
