Detect appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Detect Fighting Scene". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self, Trigger, Interrupt, Shield
- Effect text: "If the user is hit by a Move, the user may use Detect. The user is instead not hit by the Move. You do not take any damage nor are you affected by anty of the Move's effects"

Detect is a reactive defensive move with four range keywords: Self, Trigger, Interrupt, and Shield. The Trigger and Interrupt keywords indicate it activates in response to an incoming attack rather than proactively. The Shield keyword likely categorizes it among protective moves. Scene frequency limits it to a single use per encounter, making it a one-time emergency dodge. The effect text contains a typo ("anty" instead of "any") in the source data.
