Corrosive Gas appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Corrosive Gas Poison EOT AC 2". No DB value is displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2
- Range: Burst 1
- Effect text: "Targets hit have their current Held Item(s) unable to be used for the remainder of the Scene. In addition, Steel-Type targets hit lose their immunity to Poison until they Faint or Take A Breather; Poison instead counts as neutral against Steel."

Corrosive Gas serves a dual purpose: it shuts down Held Items for the Scene and strips Steel-types of their Poison immunity. The Burst 1 range centers on the user and hits all adjacent targets, making positioning important. EOT frequency allows repeated use, which matters since new items could theoretically be gained mid-encounter. The Steel-type interaction is notable — rather than making Steel weak to Poison, it neutralizes the immunity so Poison counts as neutral damage. This persists until the target Faints or uses Take A Breather.
