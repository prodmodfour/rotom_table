Dig appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Ground-type) button showing "Dig Ground DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: Burst 1, Set-Up, Full Action, Groundsource
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Set-Up Effect: The user shifts 25 meters underground and their turn ends. Resolution Effect: The user may shift horizontally using their burrow or overland speed, and then shifts 25 meters straight up. Upon reaching the surface, the user attacks with Dig, creating a Burst 1. *Grants: Burrow +3"

Dig is a two-turn Set-Up move. On the first turn (Full Action), the user burrows underground, becoming untargetable. On the second turn, the user surfaces and attacks in a Burst 1 area. The 25-meter underground shift followed by horizontal repositioning gives substantial movement, making Dig function as both an attack and a repositioning tool. The Groundsource keyword indicates interaction with ground-based mechanics. The Burrow +3 grant adds a permanent capability bonus. The Set-Up nature means the move takes two turns to complete, but the underground phase provides invulnerability.
