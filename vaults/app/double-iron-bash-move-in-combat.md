Double Iron Bash appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Steel-type) button showing "Double Iron Bash Steel DB 6 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Double Iron Bash Flinches the targets on 15+."

Double Iron Bash combines the Double Strike property with a 15+ Flinch effect, creating a move that hits twice with each strike capable of causing Flinch. The two hits each independently check for Flinch, giving a higher cumulative chance of disrupting the target. DB 6 per strike with AC 2 provides solid damage and accuracy. Compared to [[double-hit-move-in-combat]] which has no secondary effect, Double Iron Bash trades EOT frequency for Scene x2 but adds the Flinch threat. The Steel typing gives good neutral coverage and potential super-effective hits against Fairy, Ice, and Rock types.
