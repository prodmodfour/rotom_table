Crush Grip appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Crush Grip Normal DB 12 Scene AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 12 → 14, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge — the DB display shows the STAB-boosted value (12 → 14) inline
- Effect text: "For every 10% the target is below their full Hit Points, Crush Grip's Damage Base is reduced by 1."

Crush Grip has the highest observed base DB at 12 (14 with STAB), but it scales inversely with the target's missing HP. At full health the move hits at maximum power, but a target at 50% HP would reduce the DB by 5. This makes it an opener or finisher-denial move — strongest when used early before the target has taken damage. Scene frequency limits it to one use per encounter. The scaling mechanic is the opposite of moves that grow stronger as the target weakens.
