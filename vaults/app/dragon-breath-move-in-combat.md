Dragon Breath appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Breath Dragon DB 6 EOT AC 2". No STAB badge appears since the user is Normal-type.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 6, AC: 2
- Range: 6, 1 Target
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dragon Breath Paralyzes the target on 15+."

Dragon Breath is a mid-range Special attack with a Paralysis secondary effect on a roll of 15 or higher. DB 6 is modest, and the range of 6 provides decent reach without area coverage. AC 2 is highly accurate. EOT frequency allows regular use. The Paralysis chance adds utility beyond raw damage, potentially crippling faster opponents. Compared to Dragon Pulse (which has higher DB but no secondary effect), Dragon Breath trades damage for a debilitating status chance.
