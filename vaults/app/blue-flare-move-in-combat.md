Blue Flare appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red (Fire-type) button showing "Blue Flare Fire DB 13 Scene x2 AC 5".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 13, AC: 5, Range: 10, 1 Target, Smite
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Blue Flare Burns the target on 17+."

Blue Flare is a single-target ranged Special attack with exceptional range (10 meters) and high DB (13). The Smite keyword in the range imposes an accuracy penalty on targets who haven't acted yet. Scene x2 frequency. The Burn threshold of 17+ is lower than typical 19+ thresholds, making the status condition more likely to proc.
