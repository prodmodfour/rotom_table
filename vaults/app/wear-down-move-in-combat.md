Wear Down appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Wear Down NORMAL DB 5 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5 → 7 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Wear Down lowers the target's Defense by 1 Combat Stage on Even-Numbered Rolls."

Wear Down is a trainer Physical weapon move with a conditional Defense debuff — on even-numbered accuracy rolls, the target's Defense drops by 1 Combat Stage. The GM must check the roll parity and apply the debuff manually.
