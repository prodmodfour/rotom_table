Discharge appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a yellow (Electric-type) button showing "Discharge Electric DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: All Cardinally Adjacent Targets
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Discharge Paralyzes all legal targets on 15+."

Discharge uses a unique range descriptor — "All Cardinally Adjacent Targets" — meaning it hits every combatant directly above, below, left, or right of the user, rather than using a Burst or Cone area. Both combatants appeared as "Out of range" during observation since neither was cardinally adjacent to the Move Tester. The 15+ Paralysis effect applies to all targets hit, making it dangerous in crowded fields. DB 8 with AC 2 and EOT frequency provides strong sustained output. As a multi-target move with Paralysis potential, it rewards careful positioning to hit multiple enemies while avoiding allies.
