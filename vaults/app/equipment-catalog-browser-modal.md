The equipment catalog browser is a modal opened from the [[gm-character-detail-equipment-tab]] via a "Browse Full Catalog" button. It renders a full browsable list of all items defined in the [[equipment-constants-catalog]].

The modal has a header reading "Equipment Catalog" with a close button. Clicking the overlay background also closes the modal.
