On the [[gm-pokemon-detail-page]], the Stats tab displays a "Nature" heading followed by the nature name and its stat effects in abbreviated form (e.g., "Jolly +Spd -Sp.Atk").

The nature appears below the six base stat rows. If a Pokemon has no nature assigned (as with [[pokemon-creation-produces-manual-record|manually created Pokemon]]), the nature section is absent.

The displayed stat adjustments correspond to the modifiers defined in the [[nature-table-constant]].
