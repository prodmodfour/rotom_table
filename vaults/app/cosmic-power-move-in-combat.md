Cosmic Power appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Psychic-type) button showing "Cosmic Power Psychic EOT". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Defense 1 Combat Stage and raise the user's Special Defense 1 Combat Stage."

Cosmic Power raises both defensive stats by 1 Combat Stage each, making it a balanced defensive setup move. Unlike [[calm-mind-move-in-combat]] which boosts Special Attack and Special Defense, Cosmic Power trades offensive potential for physical durability. Compared to [[amnesia-move-in-combat]] which raises Special Defense by 2, Cosmic Power gives a smaller Special Defense boost but adds physical Defense as well. EOT frequency allows stacking over multiple turns for increasingly durable defenses.
