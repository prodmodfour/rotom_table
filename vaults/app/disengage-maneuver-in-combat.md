Disengage appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Disengage", a "Shift Action" action-type badge, and the description "Shift 1m without provoking Attack of Opportunity." It has no AC badge. Its icon is `arrow-u-down-left.svg`.

Clicking Disengage fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]]. There is no target selection — the maneuver is self-targeting. The GM applies the 1m Shift manually.

Disengage is the only Shift Action maneuver in the grid. It does not provoke Attacks of Opportunity by definition — that is its entire purpose.
