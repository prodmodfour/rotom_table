Brave Bird appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Flying-type) button showing "Brave Bird Flying DB 12 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 12, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Recoil 1/3
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "The target is pushed back 2 meters."

Brave Bird has three range keywords: Dash (the user Shifts before attacking, as in [[bounce-move-in-combat]]), Push (forces the target to move — the effect text specifies 2 meters), and Recoil 1/3 (the user takes 1/3 of the damage dealt as self-damage). This is the first observed move with both Push and Recoil keywords. DB 12 with AC 2 makes it highly accurate for its power level, but the Recoil 1/3 self-damage is a significant cost. Scene x2 frequency.
