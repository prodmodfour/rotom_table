# Encounter Toolbar

A row of controls in the [[encounter-header]] area. Contains:

- [[encounter-weather-selector]]
- **Serve to Group** button — pushes the encounter to the [[group-view-encounter-tab]] and connected player views; toggles to "Unserve" while served (see [[encounter-serve-toggles-between-serve-and-unserve]])
- **Undo** / **Redo** buttons — disabled when no undoable/redoable actions exist
- **Start Combat** button — enabled once at least one combatant is added; replaced by **Next Turn** once combat begins
- **End Encounter** button
- **Save Template** button — opens the [[encounter-save-template-modal]] to save the current encounter as a template in the [[encounter-library-page]]
- **Keyboard shortcuts (?)** button — opens the [[encounter-keyboard-shortcuts-dialog]]
