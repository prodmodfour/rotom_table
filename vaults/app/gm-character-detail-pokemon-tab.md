The Pokemon tab on the [[gm-character-detail-page]] lists the character's owned Pokemon. Each Pokemon appears as a clickable link card showing:

- The Pokemon's sprite image
- The Pokemon's nickname (e.g., "Chomps")
- The level (e.g., "Lv. 10")

Clicking a Pokemon card navigates to the [[gm-pokemon-detail-page]] at `/gm/pokemon/:id`.
