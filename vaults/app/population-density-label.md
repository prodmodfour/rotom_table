# Population Density Label

The [[habitat-detail-page]] metadata shows a "Population Density" field with a badge (e.g. "Moderate") and a description (e.g. "Small group -- a pack or family unit").

The [[habitat-settings-modal]] explicitly states this field is "informational -- does not control spawn count." It serves as a label and a spawn count suggestion in the [[generate-wild-encounter-modal]], where the Spawn Count field shows a suggestion based on the density (e.g. "Suggestion: 4 (Moderate -- Small group -- a pack or family unit)").
