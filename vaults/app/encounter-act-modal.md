# Encounter Act Modal

A modal opened by clicking the "Act" button on an [[encounter-combatant-card]]. The header shows the combatant's avatar, name, type badges, and the [[encounter-act-modal-action-economy-display]] (Standard and Shift counters, both default to 1). A close button (×) is in the top-right.

## Moves

The [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] appears at the top of the modal body, listing the combatant's known moves as color-coded buttons. Selecting a move opens the [[encounter-move-target-panel]] alongside the move list.

## Standard Actions

- **Shift** — Move 1 meter (uses the Shift action)
- **Struggle** — DB 4 Typeless attack (always available as a fallback)
- **Pass Turn** — End this combatant's turn

## Combat Maneuvers

A collapsible section listing [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] — standard tactical actions like Push, Trip, Grapple, Sprint, and more.
