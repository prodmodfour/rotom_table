Gouge appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Gouge NORMAL DB 5 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5 → 7 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target, Double Strike
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "If both hits of Gouge successfully hit the target, the target gains an Injury."

Gouge is a trainer Physical weapon move with the Double Strike keyword, meaning it rolls to hit twice. If both strikes connect, the target gains an Injury — a lasting penalty. The Injury application must be tracked manually by the GM. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Small Melee and Short Ranged Weapons Only" but this limitation is truncated from the displayed effect.
