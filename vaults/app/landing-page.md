# Landing Page

The `/` route displays the app title "Rotom Table" with the subtitle "Pokemon Tabletop United 1.05".

Two large link cards are offered:

- **GM View** — links to `/gm`, described as "Full control over encounters, characters, and combat"
- **Group View** — links to the [[group-view-page]], described as "Simplified view optimized for TV display"

Below is a "Quick Start" guide with four steps:

1. Open **GM View** on your laptop
2. Open **Group View** on your TV (via browser or cast)
3. Create or load characters in the Library
4. Start an encounter and serve it to the group!

There is no link to the [[player-view-character-selection]] (`/player`) from this page.
