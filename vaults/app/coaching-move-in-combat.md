Coaching appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Coaching Fighting EOT". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Burst 1
- Effect text: "The user and any allies in the Burst have their Attack and Defense raised by 1 CS."

Coaching is a team-support move that buffs both the user and nearby allies within a Burst 1 radius. It raises Attack and Defense by +1 CS each, providing both offensive and defensive benefits. The Burst 1 range means allies must be adjacent to benefit. EOT frequency allows repeated use each round for sustained team buffing. Unlike most self-buff moves, Coaching explicitly includes allies, making it valuable in multi-combatant encounters.
