# Encounter Table ID Route Highlights Card

Navigating to `/gm/encounter-tables/:id` does not open a separate detail view. Instead, the [[encounter-tables-list-page]] renders with the corresponding [[encounter-table-card]] in an active/highlighted state. The full card grid remains visible.

The [[habitats-list-page]] works differently — clicking a card at `/gm/habitats` navigates to the [[habitat-detail-page]] at `/gm/habitats/:id`.
