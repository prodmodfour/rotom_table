On the [[trainer-level-up-summary-step]], a "Warnings" section appears when any level-up allocations are incomplete.

Each warning is a single line describing what was not selected, such as "1 edge(s) not selected" or "1 bonus Skill Edge(s) not selected".

The warnings do not block the "Apply Level Up" button — the GM can choose to apply a partial level-up and handle the remaining allocations later.
