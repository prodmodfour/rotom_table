Bolt Strike appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a yellow (Electric-type) button showing "Bolt Strike Electric DB 13 Scene x2 AC 5".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 13, AC: 5, Range: 10, 1 Target, Smite
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Bolt Strike Paralyzes the target on 17+."

Bolt Strike is a high-power Physical single-target move with 10-meter range and Smite. DB 13 makes it one of the hardest-hitting moves observed, comparable to [[blast-burn-move-in-combat]] but single-target instead of AoE. The Paralysis threshold of 17+ is more generous than the typical 19+, giving roughly a 20% chance to inflict the condition. Scene x2 frequency.
