Belly Drum appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Belly Drum Normal Scene".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- No AC, DB, or attack stat fields
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "The user gains +6 Attack CS and loses HP equal to 1/2 of their Max HP."

Belly Drum is a high-risk, high-reward Self-targeting Status move that maximizes Attack combat stages at the cost of half the user's HP. The +6 Attack CS is the largest single-move stat boost observed. Both effects are described in the effect text and applied manually by the GM. Scene frequency.
