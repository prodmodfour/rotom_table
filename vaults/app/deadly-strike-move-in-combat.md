Deadly Strike appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Deadly Strike NORMAL DB 6 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "If Deadly Strike Hits, it is a Critical Hit."

Deadly Strike is a trainer Physical weapon move that is an automatic Critical Hit on any successful hit — the GM does not need to check for a natural 20. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Not usable by Large Melee Weapons" but this limitation is truncated from the displayed effect.
