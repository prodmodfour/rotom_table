Below the **Capture Conditions** label in the [[capture-section-on-wild-pokemon-cards]], three checkboxes allow the GM to set environmental context that affects certain ball types:

- **Target was baited (Lure Ball)** — activates Lure Ball's -20 conditional
- **Dark / low-light (Dusk Ball)** — activates Dusk Ball's -20 conditional
- **Underwater / underground (Dive Ball)** — activates Dive Ball's -20 conditional

Each wild Pokemon card maintains its own independent set of condition checkboxes. Checking a condition on one card does not affect the conditions on other cards.
