Attract appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Attract NORMAL Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2, Range: 3, 1 Target, Social
- Effect text: "Attract Infatuates the target if its gender is the opposite of the user's. Attract fails when used by or against Genderless targets."

Attract is a Status move that still has an AC value (2), requiring an accuracy roll. The "Social" keyword in the range indicates it's a social-category move. The gender-based restriction and Infatuated condition are described in text but not enforced by the app — the GM checks genders and applies the status manually. Scene x2 frequency.
