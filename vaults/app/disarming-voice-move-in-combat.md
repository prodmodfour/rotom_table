Disarming Voice appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Disarming Voice Fairy DB 4 At-Will". No AC value is displayed in the button.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4
- Range: Burst 1
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Disarming Voice cannot miss."
- A "Damage (DB 4): 1d8+6" line appears with a "Roll Damage" button

Disarming Voice trades power for reliability — it cannot miss, removing the accuracy check entirely. No AC value appears in either the button or the target panel, consistent with the auto-hit property. DB 4 makes it one of the weaker damaging moves, but At-Will frequency means unlimited uses. The Burst 1 range provides area coverage, hitting adjacent targets. The target panel includes an inline damage display showing the calculated roll formula (1d8+6), which combines the DB 4 base with the SP.ATK modifier. This is one of the few moves observed where the damage roll formula appears directly in the target panel alongside a Roll Damage button.
