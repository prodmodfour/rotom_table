Burning Jealousy appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red (Fire-type) button showing "Burning Jealousy Fire DB 7 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 7, AC: 2, Range: Burst 1, Spirit Surge, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "After Burning Jealousy has resolved, the user may choose to Burn foes within 5m that have had any CS raised since the beginning of that foe's last turn. This Effect may trigger only once per Scene."

Burning Jealousy punishes stat-boosting opponents. The Burn status application is conditional on the foe having raised combat stages, making it a counter to setup strategies like [[bulk-up-move-in-combat]] or [[calm-mind-move-in-combat]]. The Spirit Surge keyword appears in the range field. The Scene-limited Burn trigger is separate from the damage, applying after the move resolves. Burst 1 AoE means it can potentially Burn multiple buffed targets.
