Defense Curl appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Defense Curl Normal At-Will". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check. A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "The user becomes Curled Up. While Curled Up, the user becomes immune to Critical Hits and gains 10 Damage Reduction. However, while Curled Up, the user is Slowed and their Accuracy is lowered by -4. The user may stop being Curled Up as a Swift Action. If the user has Rollout or Ice Ball in their Move List, they do not become Slowed while Curled Up. Furthermore, when using the Moves Rollout or Ice Ball while Curled Up, the user gains a +10 bonus to the damage rolls of those Moves and does not suffer Accuracy Penalties from being Curled Up."
- A "Fixed Damage: 10 (ignores stats & type effectiveness)" line appears below the effect text

Defense Curl introduces a unique Curled Up state rather than simple Combat Stage adjustments. The trade-offs are significant: Critical Hit immunity and 10 DR in exchange for being Slowed and suffering -4 Accuracy. The Rollout/Ice Ball synergy removes these penalties and adds +10 damage, making Defense Curl part of a combo strategy. At-Will frequency means it can be reactivated freely. The "Fixed Damage: 10" display in the target panel represents the DR value, though the labeling as "damage" rather than "reduction" is notable. The STAB badge indicates the Move Tester is Normal-type.
