Charge appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a yellow (Electric-type) button showing "Charge Electric EOT". No DB or AC values are shown since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "If the user performs an Electric Attack on its next turn, add its Damage Dice Roll an extra time to the damage. Raise the user's Special Defense by +1 CS."

Charge has a dual effect: it doubles the damage dice of the user's next Electric attack (a powerful but turn-dependent buff), and it unconditionally raises Special Defense by +1 CS. This makes it a setup move that pairs naturally with Electric attacks like [[charge-beam-move-in-combat]]. The damage doubling only applies on the immediately following turn, requiring careful turn planning.
