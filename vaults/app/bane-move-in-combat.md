Bane appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Bane NORMAL DB 4 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4 → 6 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "The target loses a Tick of Hit Points at the start of their next three turns and suffers a -2 penalty to all Save Checks on those turns."

Bane is a trainer Special weapon move with Scene x2 frequency. It combines direct damage with a three-turn damage-over-time effect (Tick loss) and a debuff (-2 Save Checks). Both ongoing effects must be tracked manually by the GM.
