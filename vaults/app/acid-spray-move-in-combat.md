Acid Spray appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Acid Spray POISON DB 4 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: 4, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Acid Spray lowers the target's Special Defense by -2 CS."

Unlike [[acid-move-in-combat]] which has a conditional stat drop (on 18+), Acid Spray guarantees the -2 SP.DEF CS on hit. The stat drop is described in the effect text but not automatically applied — the GM must manually adjust combat stages via the [[encounter-combat-stages-modal]].

The EOT frequency means the move cannot be used on consecutive turns.
