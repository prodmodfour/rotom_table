The top section of the [[gm-pokemon-detail-page]] displays the Pokemon's identity information. On the left is the Pokemon's sprite image. To the right are fields arranged in rows:

- Species and Nickname (text fields, disabled in view mode)
- Level (number), EXP (number), Gender (text), and Shiny (checkbox)
- Location (text field with "e.g., Route 1" placeholder)
- Type badges showing the Pokemon's types (e.g., "Ice" and "--" for a single-typed Pokemon, or "Poison" and "Dark" for a dual-typed one)

A Pokemon with only one type shows the type name and "--" in the second type slot.

All fields are disabled in view mode and become editable in [[gm-pokemon-detail-edit-mode]].
