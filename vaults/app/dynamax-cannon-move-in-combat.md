Dynamax Cannon appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dynamax Cannon Dragon DB 10 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 10, AC: 2
- Range: 6, 1 Target
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The DB of Dynamax Cannon increases by +2 for each positive CS the target has, to a maximum of DB 20."

Dynamax Cannon's DB scales based on the target's positive combat stages — it punishes setup sweepers. A target with +5 total positive CS would push the DB to 20, the stated maximum. The base DB 10 is already strong, and the scaling makes it devastating against buffed opponents. AC 2 and range 6 provide reliable accuracy and decent reach. Scene x2 frequency limits usage. The DB scaling is described in the effect text and must be calculated manually by the GM — the target panel always shows the base DB 10.
