# Skill Rank Cap Warning

A validation message that appears in the [[full-create-background-and-skills-section]] when a skill's rank exceeds the maximum allowed for the character's level. For example, at level 1 the maximum rank is Novice. Selecting "Fitness Training" background at level 1 triggers the warning: "Skills exceed rank cap for level 1 (max Novice): Athletics (Adept)".

This indicates that the background's Adept assignment to Athletics violates the level-based rank cap.
