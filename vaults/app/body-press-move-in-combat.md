Body Press appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Body Press Fighting DB 8 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "The user's Defense Stat is added to the damage roll instead of the Attack Stat."

Despite being classified as Physical and showing the ATK stat in the panel, Body Press's effect overrides the damage calculation to use the Defense stat instead. The panel still displays ATK (80) as the offensive stat — the Defense substitution described in the effect text must be applied manually by the GM. This is the first observed move where the damage stat differs from what the class implies.
