Clanging Scales appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Clanging Scales Dragon DB 11 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 11, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target or Burst 1, Sonic, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The user adds half their Defense Stat to Clanging Scale's Damage Roll. After the Move is resolved, the user's Defense is lowered by 1 CS."

Clanging Scales is a powerful Dragon Special attack that uniquely adds half the user's Defense stat to the damage roll, making it scale with both Special Attack and Defense. The Sonic keyword means it bypasses Substitutes. It can target a single foe at range 6 or hit a Burst 1 area. The Defense -1 CS drawback after use makes the user more vulnerable to Physical attacks. Scene x2 frequency allows two uses per encounter.
