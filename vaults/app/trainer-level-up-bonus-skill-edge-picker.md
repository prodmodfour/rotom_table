Inside the [[trainer-level-up-edges-step]], the Bonus Skill Edge section displays all skills grouped under three headings: Body, Mind, and Spirit.

Each skill appears as a button showing the skill name and its current rank (e.g., "Acrobatics Untrained", "Survival Adept"). Clicking a skill button selects it for the rank-up.

Skills that are already at or above the maximum rank allowed by the Bonus Skill Edge are disabled. At level 2, skills at Novice or above cannot be raised further by this edge (the cap note reads "Cannot raise a skill to Adept with this edge"), and skills already at Adept or higher are disabled (e.g., Survival at Adept, Focus at Novice).
