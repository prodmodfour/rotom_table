The [[capture-section-on-wild-pokemon-cards]] shows a **Capture Rate** box with a colored circular icon (◉), a percentage value, and a difficulty label. The box has a colored border that appears to match the difficulty level. Observed examples:

- Thievul (Lv.15, full HP): **30%** "Difficult" with a red-toned border
- Nickit (Lv.1, full HP): **68%** "Easy"

The capture rate reflects the base rate before the ball modifier is applied. It accounts for the target's level and current HP. The ball modifier applies during the actual roll, not to the displayed rate.
