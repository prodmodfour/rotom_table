The Apricorn Balls category in the [[capture-ball-selector-dropdown]] contains seven balls. Most have conditional modifiers shown via [[capture-ball-conditional-modifier-preview]]:

- Level Ball: +0 (if target is under half the level of your active Pokemon → -20)
- Lure Ball: +0 (if target was baited → -20)
- Moon Ball: +0 (if target evolves with Evolution Stone → -20)
- Friend Ball: -5, with [[capture-ball-post-capture-effect-badge]] "+1 Loyalty"
- Love Ball: +0 (if same evo line + opposite gender → -30)
- Heavy Ball: +0 (if target WC above 1 → -5 per WC)
- Fast Ball: +0 (if target movement above 7 → -20)
