Arcane Fury appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Arcane Fury NORMAL DB 3 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 3 → 5 (with STAB), AC: 3, Range: Cone 2
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Arcane Fury's Targets become Vulnerable on 19+."

Arcane Fury is a trainer Special weapon move that targets a Cone 2 area. The Vulnerable condition on a high roll (19+) must be applied manually by the GM. Since it is a Cone area move, multiple targets can be hit — the "Select Target(s)" heading reflects this. The Smite keyword is absent, so it uses SP.DEF for damage reduction.
