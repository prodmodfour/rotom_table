Trip appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Trip", a "Standard" action-type badge, an "AC 6" badge, and the description "Trip target (opposed Combat/Acrobatics)". Its icon is `sneaker-move.svg`.

Clicking Trip fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]] — no target-selection step or damage panel. The GM resolves the accuracy check (AC 6), opposed skill check, and Tripped status application manually.

Trip carries the `maneuver_other` AoO trigger flag in the [[combat-maneuver-constants]], meaning it provokes an [[attack-of-opportunity-in-combat]] from adjacent foes when used against a non-adjacent target.
