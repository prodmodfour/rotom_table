# Player View Encounter Enemy List

A section within the [[player-view-encounter-tab]], below the player list, under the heading "Enemies." Lists enemy combatants in the encounter.

Each entry shows the Pokemon's sprite, name, type badge(s), and HP as a **percentage** (e.g. "100%") rather than exact values. This differs from the [[player-view-encounter-player-list]] which shows exact HP numbers.

The enemy whose turn it is displays a "Turn" label next to their name.
