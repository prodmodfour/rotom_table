# Player View Pokemon Capabilities

A section within an expanded [[player-view-pokemon-card]], labeled "Capabilities." Displays movement and physical capabilities in a grid of cells.

Each cell shows a capability label above and its numeric value below. Observed capabilities include:

- **Overland** — ground movement speed
- **Swim** — swimming speed
- **Burrow** — burrowing speed
- **Jump** — displayed as two values separated by a slash (e.g. "1/1" for long jump/high jump)
- **Power** — physical power rating

Not all capabilities appear for every Pokemon; only those relevant to the species are shown.
