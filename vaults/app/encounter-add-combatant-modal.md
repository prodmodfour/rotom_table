# Encounter Add Combatant Modal

A modal dialog opened by clicking "+ Add" in any [[encounter-participant-sections]] column. The title reflects the target section ("Add Player", "Add Ally", or "Add Enemy").

The modal has two tabs: **Pokemon** (default) and **Humans**. Below the tabs is a search textbox.

**Pokemon tab** lists all available Pokemon with their sprite image, name (e.g. "Bulbasaur 1"), level (e.g. "Lv.10"), and type badges (e.g. "Grass", "Poison").

**Humans tab** lists all human characters with an initial-letter avatar circle, name, level, and a "player" role label. It also includes an **Initiative Bonus** spinbutton (default 0) at the bottom of the modal.

Clicking a row selects it, enabling the **Add to Battle** button. The modal also has a **Cancel** button.
