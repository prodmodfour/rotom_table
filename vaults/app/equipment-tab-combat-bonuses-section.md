The "Combat Bonuses" section sits below the equipment slots and the "Browse Full Catalog" button on the [[gm-character-detail-equipment-tab]].

When no equipment is equipped, it reads "No equipment bonuses active." When items are equipped, it lists each active bonus as a line with a Phosphor icon and descriptive text — for example, equipping a Helmet shows "15 DR vs Critical Hits only." The section updates dynamically whenever equipment changes.

The bonuses displayed here reflect the mechanical effects defined on each item in the [[equipment-constants-catalog]], including damage reduction, evasion bonuses, speed combat stage modifiers, stat bonuses, and conditional effects.
