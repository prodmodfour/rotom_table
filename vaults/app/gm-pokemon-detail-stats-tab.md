The Stats tab on the [[gm-pokemon-detail-page]] is the default tab. It shows a six-stat grid: HP, Attack, Defense, Sp. Atk, Sp. Def, Speed.

Each stat displays two values side by side — the base stat on the left and the calculated stat on the right. For example, Attack might show "5" and "4", meaning 5 base stat points allocated and 4 as the calculated combat stat.

HP shows its base stat value and a "/" separator (the calculated max HP is likely displayed elsewhere in the identity section).

Below the stat grid is a Nature section showing the Pokemon's nature and its stat modifiers (e.g., "Hardy +ATK -ATK" — a neutral nature that boosts and penalizes the same stat).
