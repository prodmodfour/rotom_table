Dire Claw appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Dire Claw Poison DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dire Claw is a Critical Hit on a 19+. Dire Claw gives the target a Status ailment on a 15+. If this is triggered, roll 1d3; on 1 the target is Poisoned; on 2 the target is Paralyzed; on 3 the target is Flinched."

Dire Claw packs three effects into a single attack: an expanded Critical Hit range (19+ instead of 20), a 15+ status infliction trigger, and a random status from Poison/Paralysis/Flinch. The 1d3 randomness adds unpredictability but all three outcomes are impactful. DB 6 is modest, but the move compensates with its high probability of secondary effects — roughly 30% chance for a status ailment per hit. EOT frequency allows repeated attempts, increasing the cumulative chance of inflicting conditions over multiple rounds.
