Brick Break appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Brick Break Fighting DB 8 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Light Screen and Reflect may not be activated in response to Brick Break."

Brick Break is a straightforward Physical Fighting-type attack. Its At-Will frequency and solid DB 8 make it a reliable main attack. The effect prevents the target from using Light Screen or Reflect in response, making it useful against defensive setups. This is a standard melee single-target move with no range keywords.
