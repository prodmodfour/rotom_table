Attack Order appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a yellow-green (Bug-type) button showing "Attack Order BUG DB 9 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Attack Order is a Critical Hit on 18+."

Like [[air-cutter-move-in-combat]], has an expanded critical hit range (18+ instead of only 20).
