Double Swipe appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Double Swipe NORMAL DB 4 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4 → 6 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 2 Targets; or WR, 1 Target, Double Strike
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "--"

Double Swipe is a trainer Physical weapon move with a flexible targeting mode: either two separate targets at Weapon Range, or one target with the Double Strike keyword. The effect text shows "--" indicating no additional mechanical effect beyond the multi-target/multi-hit option. The GM chooses which mode to apply.
