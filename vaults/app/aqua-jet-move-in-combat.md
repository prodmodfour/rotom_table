Aqua Jet appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Aqua Jet WATER DB 4 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Priority
- ATK stat of the attacker
- No special effect text

Like [[accelerock-move-in-combat]], the range includes the Priority keyword. At-Will frequency.
