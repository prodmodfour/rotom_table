Absorb appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Absorb GRASS DB 2 At-Will AC 2".

Clicking it opens the [[encounter-move-target-panel]] with:
- Class: Special, DB: 2, AC: 2, Range: 4, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target."

When a target is selected, the panel shows Spec Evasion (since it is a Special-class move) and the accuracy threshold needed. After rolling accuracy (d20 vs threshold), the panel shows hit/miss per target with NAT 20/NAT 1 callouts.

Rolling damage shows a breakdown: Base Roll (dice from DB chart: 1d6+3 for DB 2), + SP.ATK stat, = Pre-Defense Total. A critical hit (NAT 20) adds the dice portion again. Per-target damage shows Pre-Defense − SP.DEF × effectiveness = final damage (minimum 1).

The drain effect (heal half damage dealt) is described in the effect text but is not automatically applied by the app — the GM must manually heal the attacker after confirming.
