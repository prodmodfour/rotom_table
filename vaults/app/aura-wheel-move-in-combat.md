Aura Wheel appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Aura Wheel Normal DB 11 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 11 → 13, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "The user's Speed is increased 1 CS."

The DB shows "11 → 13" because STAB adds +2 to the damage base. The Speed combat stage increase is described in text and applied manually by the GM. Scene x2 frequency.
