Apple Acid appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Apple Acid GRASS DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, AC: 2, Range: Cone 2
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "The target's Special Defense is lowered by 1 CS."

The Cone 2 AoE range allows hitting multiple targets. The guaranteed -1 SP.DEF CS is similar to [[acid-spray-move-in-combat]] but at -1 instead of -2.
