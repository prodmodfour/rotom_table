The [[gm-character-detail-page]] uses a horizontal tab bar with seven tabs:

1. **Stats** — [[gm-character-detail-stats-tab]]
2. **Classes** — [[gm-character-detail-classes-tab]]
3. **Skills** — [[gm-character-detail-skills-tab]]
4. **Equipment** — [[gm-character-detail-equipment-tab]]
5. **Pokemon** — [[gm-character-detail-pokemon-tab]]
6. **Healing** — [[gm-character-detail-healing-tab]]
7. **Notes** — [[gm-character-detail-notes-tab]]

The active tab is visually highlighted. The tab selection persists while navigating between view and [[gm-character-detail-edit-mode]].
