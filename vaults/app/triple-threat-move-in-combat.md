Triple Threat appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Triple Threat NORMAL DB 7 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 3 Targets
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Limitation: Large Melee Weapons and Long-Range Weapons Only"

Triple Threat is a trainer Physical weapon move that hits three separate targets at Weapon Range. Its only displayed effect is the weapon limitation — it has no additional mechanical effects beyond its multi-target damage.
