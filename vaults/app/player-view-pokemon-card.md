# Player View Pokemon Card

A card in the [[player-view-team-tab]] representing a single Pokemon. It has a collapsed and expanded state, toggled by tapping the card header.

**Collapsed** — shows the Pokemon's sprite on the left, then its name, type badges (colored labels like "Dragon", "Ground"), level, and an HP bar with numeric values (e.g. "47 / 47"). A chevron icon on the right indicates it can expand.

**Expanded** — reveals additional sections below the header:

- [[player-view-pokemon-stats]] — six combat stats in a grid
- [[player-view-pokemon-abilities]] — abilities with descriptions
- [[player-view-pokemon-moves]] — move list
- [[player-view-pokemon-capabilities]] — movement and physical capabilities

The HP bar in the header uses a green/teal color and a pink/red endpoint marker.
