Clangorous Soul appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Clangorous Soul Dragon Scene". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "The user loses 1/3rd of their Max Hit Points and has each of its stats raised by +1 Combat Stage."

Clangorous Soul is the ultimate self-buff: every stat gets +1 CS in a single action, at the cost of a third of the user's maximum HP. Scene frequency means it can only be used once per encounter, reinforcing its role as a one-time setup. The HP cost is substantial and cannot be avoided, creating a risk/reward decision — the stat boosts are comprehensive but leave the user closer to fainting.
