Counter appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Counter Fighting Scene x2". No DB or AC values are displayed despite being classified as Physical.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Reaction, Trigger
- Effect text: "Counter may be used as a Reaction when the user is hit by a damaging Physical Attack. Resolve the Triggering Attack, with Counter's user resisting the attack one step further. After the attack is resolved, if Counter's user was not Fainted, the triggering foe then loses Hit Points equal to twice the amount of Hit Points lost by the user from the triggering attack. Note that Counter is Physical, and while it cannot miss, it cannot hit targets immune to Fighting-Type Moves."

Counter is a Reaction move that triggers in response to being hit by a Physical attack. The user gains one step of additional resistance to the triggering hit, then reflects double the HP lost back at the attacker. This makes it scale with the opponent's power — stronger attacks produce stronger counterattacks. The Reaction and Trigger keywords in the range indicate it operates outside normal turn flow. Scene x2 frequency limits it to two uses per encounter. Being Fighting-type means Ghost-types are immune to the reflected damage, and the Melee range requires the attacker to be adjacent. Similar to [[bide-move-in-combat]] in concept — both store and reflect damage — but Counter is immediate and specific to Physical attacks.
