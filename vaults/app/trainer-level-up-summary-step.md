The final step of the [[trainer-level-up-modal]], labeled "Summary". Displays a "Level-Up Summary" heading with a subtitle like "Aurora: Level 1 -> Level 2".

If milestones were crossed and choices made, a **Milestones** section appears first, showing each milestone name, level, and chosen option label.

A **Stats** section lists all six combat stats. Stats that changed show "X -> Y (+N)" in green. Unchanged stats show their value followed by "(unchanged)" in muted text. Below the individual stats, Max HP shows its before -> after values.

If edges were chosen, a **New Edges** section shows them as tags. If any Skill Edges were chosen (bonus or regular), a [[trainer-level-up-summary-skill-rank-ups]] section shows each rank-up with source attribution.

If features or classes were chosen, they appear in their own sections.

If any allocations are incomplete, a [[trainer-level-up-incomplete-warnings]] section appears.

The "Apply Level Up" button replaces the "Next" button on this step. It remains clickable even when warnings are present, allowing the GM to apply a partial level-up.
