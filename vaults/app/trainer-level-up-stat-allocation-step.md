The first step of the [[trainer-level-up-modal]], labeled "Stats". Presents a "Stat Allocation" section with a "Points Remaining: X / Y" counter.

Six stat rows appear: HP, Attack, Defense, Sp. Attack, Sp. Defense, Speed. Each row shows the current stat value, a "+" sign, minus/plus allocation buttons, the allocated amount (starting at 0), an "=" sign, and the resulting total. When a point is allocated to a stat, the total updates and turns green.

The minus button is disabled when allocation is 0. The plus buttons on all stats become disabled when no points remain. Allocating a point to one stat disables plus on all others if the budget is exhausted.

Below the stat rows is a [[trainer-level-up-derived-stats-preview]] showing how Max HP and evasions will change.

The layout mirrors the [[full-create-combat-stats-section]] but with fewer points (1 per level instead of 10 at creation).
