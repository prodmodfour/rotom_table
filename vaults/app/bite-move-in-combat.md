Bite appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark-colored (Dark-type) button showing "Bite Dark DB 6 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Bite Flinches the target on 15+."

Both Target Dummy and Move Tester appear as valid targets. The "Use Bite" button is disabled until a target is selected. As a Melee-range Physical move, the panel shows the ATK stat for damage calculation. At-Will frequency means it can be used every turn without restriction.
