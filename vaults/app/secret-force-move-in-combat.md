Secret Force appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Secret Force NORMAL DB 4 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4 → 6 (with STAB), AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Smite
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "When calculating damage, the target subtracts their Defense from Secret Force's damage instead of their Special Defense. Secret Force is still otherwise Special. Limitation: Melee Weapons Only"

Secret Force is a trainer Special weapon move with the Smite keyword, which the range line displays. Despite being Special class, the target uses their Defense stat (not SP.DEF) for damage reduction — the GM must handle this manually when resolving damage. The "Limitation: Melee Weapons Only" restriction is shown in the effect text but not enforced by the app.
