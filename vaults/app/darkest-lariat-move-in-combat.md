Darkest Lariat appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark (Dark-type) button showing "Darkest Lariat Dark DB 9 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "This Move ignores the target's positive Defense Combat Stages and all Damage Reduction."

Darkest Lariat bypasses two common defensive measures: positive Defense Combat Stages and all Damage Reduction. This makes it a hard counter to setup-heavy opponents who stack Defense boosts via moves like [[cotton-guard-move-in-combat]], [[acid-armor-move-in-combat]], or [[cosmic-power-move-in-combat]]. Unlike [[chip-away-move-in-combat]] which also ignores Combat Stages, Darkest Lariat additionally bypasses all DR. DB 9 with AC 2 makes it both powerful and accurate. EOT frequency allows one use per round.
