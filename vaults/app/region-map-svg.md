A hand-drawn-style SVG map displayed on the [[gm-map-page]]. The map shows the campaign title in large spaced lettering (e.g. "Pokemon Ranger Campaign") above the illustrated locations.

Locations appear as labeled nodes connected by named paths. Each location has a name in bold capitals and a parenthetical descriptor:

- **Rivermere** — "(stone bridge + waterwheels)"
- **Bramblewick** — "(berries, honey, timber + thatch)"
- **Thickerby Forest** — "(dim, berry-thick woodland)" with tree emoji decorations
- **Castle Grounds** — "(overgrown)" nested inside Thickerby Forest
- **Phasmosa's Castle** — "(haunted keep on the hill)"

Named routes connect the locations: Rivermere Causeway, Bramblewick Road, and Greywater Aqueduct. A stylized river with the text "~ ~ ~ R I V E R ~ ~ ~" runs across the top. Additional terrain notes like "(banks/fishing)" and "(puddles/mud patches)" annotate specific areas.
