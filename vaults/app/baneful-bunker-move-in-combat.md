Baneful Bunker appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Baneful Bunker Poison Scene".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self, Interrupt, Shield, Trigger
- No AC, DB, or attack stat fields
- Effect text: "If the user is hit by an attack, the user may use Baneful Bunker. The user is instead not hit by the Move. You do not take any damage nor are you affected by any of the Move's effects. In addition, if the triggering attack was Melee ranged, the attacker is Poisoned."

Baneful Bunker has Interrupt, Shield, and Trigger keywords in its range string, indicating it is a reactive protection move declared as an interrupt. The Poison infliction on melee attackers is an additional penalty beyond standard protection. Scene frequency.
