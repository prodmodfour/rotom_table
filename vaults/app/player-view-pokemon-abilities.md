# Player View Pokemon Abilities

A section within an expanded [[player-view-pokemon-card]], labeled "Abilities." Each ability is shown with its name in bold followed by a paragraph describing its effect.

For example, Sand Veil shows: "The user gains +1 Evasion. This is increased to +2 Evasion while in a Sandstorm or sandy terrain. While in a Sandstorm, the user and adjacent allies do not lose Hit Points due to the Sandstorm."
