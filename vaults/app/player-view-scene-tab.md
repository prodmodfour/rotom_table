# Player View Scene Tab

Accessed from the [[player-view-bottom-navigation]]. Shows a top bar with a "Group View" label, a "Current Tab: Scene" indicator, and a [[player-view-request-lobby-button]].

When no scene is active, the main area displays a location pin icon, the heading "No active scene", and the message "The GM has not started a scene yet." When a scene is active, the [[player-view-scene-display]] renders a list-based view of the scene content.
