# Pokemon Creation Tab

When "Pokemon" is selected on the [[create-character-page]], a single-mode form appears (no Quick/Full toggle). It has four sections: [[pokemon-basic-info-section]], [[pokemon-types-section]], [[pokemon-base-stats-section]], and a Notes textarea.

A "Create Pokemon" submit button appears at the bottom.
