Dynamic Punch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Dynamic Punch Fighting DB 10 At-Will AC 9".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dynamic Punch Confuses the target. Dynamic Punch ignores the target's Evasion if they are Flanked."

AC 9 is the highest (worst) accuracy check observed across all moves surveyed in the A-D range, making Dynamic Punch extremely likely to miss under normal circumstances. However, the guaranteed Confusion on hit (no roll threshold — it always Confuses) and the Flanking clause that bypasses Evasion create a high-risk/high-reward profile. DB 10 with At-Will frequency would be overwhelmingly powerful without the severe accuracy penalty. The Flanking interaction encourages tactical positioning — with allies adjacent to the target, the Evasion bypass makes hitting much more reliable.
