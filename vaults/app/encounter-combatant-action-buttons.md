# Encounter Combatant Action Buttons

A row of buttons at the bottom of each [[encounter-combatant-card]]:

- **+T** — adds temporary HP
- **CS** — opens the [[encounter-combat-stages-modal]]
- **ST** — opens the [[encounter-status-conditions-modal]]
- **Item** — opens the [[encounter-use-item-modal]] (green, with an icon)
- **Act** — opens the [[encounter-act-modal]] (magenta/pink)
- **Switch** — initiates a Pokemon switch
- **Force Switch** — forces a Pokemon switch (GM override)
- **Remove** — removes the combatant from the encounter
