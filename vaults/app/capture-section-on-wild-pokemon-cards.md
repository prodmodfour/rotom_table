Each wild Pokemon [[encounter-combatant-card]] in GM encounter view displays a **Capture** section beneath the card's basic info (name, level, type, HP, initiative). This section only appears when at least one trainer combatant is present in the encounter. It contains a [[capture-trainer-selector]], a [[capture-ball-type-selector]], [[capture-context-condition-checkboxes]], a [[capture-rate-display]], and a [[capture-throw-button]]. Each wild Pokemon card has its own independent capture section with its own settings.
