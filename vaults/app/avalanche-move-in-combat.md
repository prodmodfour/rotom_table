Avalanche appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a light blue (Ice-type) button showing "Avalanche Ice DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "When declaring Avalanche, the user does nothing and may not Shift. At the end of the round, the user may Shift and use Avalanche. If the target damaged the user this round, Avalanche has a Damage Base of 12 (4d10+15 / 40)."

Avalanche has a delayed-execution mechanic: the user waits until end of round to act. The conditional DB doubling (6 → 12) if the user was damaged is described in the effect text and tracked manually by the GM. This is the first observed move with a delayed-action timing mechanic. EOT frequency.
