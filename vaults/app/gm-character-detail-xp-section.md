The XP section on the [[gm-character-detail-page]] shows a "Trainer Experience" heading with a progress display (e.g., "0 / 10 XP") and a [[trainer-xp-progress-bar]].

Below the progress bar are XP grant buttons: -1, +1, +2, +3, +5, and Custom. The -1 button is disabled when XP is already at zero.

These buttons allow the GM to grant or remove trainer XP directly from the character detail page without entering [[gm-character-detail-edit-mode]]. When an award causes XP to reach 10, the [[trainer-level-up-modal]] opens automatically.
