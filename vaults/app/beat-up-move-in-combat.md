Beat Up appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark grey (Dark-type) button showing "Beat Up Dark EOT".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- No DB or AC fields displayed
- Effect text: "The user and up to two allies adjacent to the target may each make a Struggle Attack against the target. These Struggle Attacks hit for Dark-Type Damage instead of their usual Type. Beat Up may trigger Pack Hunt only once, no matter the number of attacks."

Beat Up is unusual in that it has no DB or AC values shown in the target panel. Instead of dealing damage directly, it enables the user and allies to perform Struggle Attacks as Dark-type. The multi-combatant attack mechanic and Pack Hunt restriction are described in the effect text. EOT frequency.
