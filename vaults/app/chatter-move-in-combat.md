Chatter appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a light blue (Flying-type) button showing "Chatter Flying DB 7 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 7, AC: 2, Range: 4, 1 Target, Sonic, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Chatter confuses all targets on 16+."

Chatter is an At-Will Special Flying attack with the Sonic keyword, meaning it bypasses Substitutes and hits through soundproofing interactions. DB 7 and AC 2 provide solid baseline damage with easy accuracy. The confusion chance on 16+ (25%) gives it utility beyond raw damage on repeated use. The 4-meter range is modest but serviceable for a ranged option.
