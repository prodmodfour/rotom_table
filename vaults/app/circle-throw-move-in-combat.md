Circle Throw appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Circle Throw Fighting DB 6 At-Will AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Push, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The target is Pushed 6 meters minus their Weight Class. The target is also Tripped on 15+."

Circle Throw combines damage with battlefield repositioning. The Push keyword means the target is forcibly moved, with the distance reduced by their Weight Class — lighter targets get pushed further. The 15+ Trip chance (30%) adds a prone condition on top of displacement. At-Will frequency allows repeated use for zone control. AC 4 is relatively low accuracy, reflecting the move's difficulty to land. DB 6 is modest but the utility comes from repositioning rather than raw damage.
