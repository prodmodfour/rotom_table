When the [[capture-ball-type-selector]] button is clicked, a dropdown panel appears listing all available Poke Ball types. The balls are organized into three category groups, each with a header icon and label:

- **Basic Balls** — [[capture-basic-ball-category]]
- **Apricorn Balls** — [[capture-apricorn-ball-category]]
- **Special Balls** — [[capture-special-ball-category]]

Each ball entry is a button showing the ball name, its base modifier as a signed number, and optionally a [[capture-ball-conditional-modifier-preview]] or [[capture-ball-post-capture-effect-badge]]. Clicking a ball button selects it and closes the dropdown.

The 25 ball types and their modifiers are defined in [[poke-ball-catalog-constant]].
