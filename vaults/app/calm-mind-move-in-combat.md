Calm Mind appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Psychic-type) button showing "Calm Mind Psychic EOT". No DB or AC values are displayed on the button since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Special Attack 1 Combat Stage and raise the user's Special Defense 1 Combat Stage."

Calm Mind is the Special counterpart to [[bulk-up-move-in-combat]], raising both offensive and defensive Special stats by +1 CS each. EOT frequency allows repeated stacking. The target panel omits DB and AC fields for Status moves, consistent with the pattern observed in other self-buff moves.
