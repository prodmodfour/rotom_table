Cheap Shot appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Cheap Shot NORMAL DB 5 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5 → 7 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Cheap Shot cannot miss."

Cheap Shot is a trainer Physical weapon move that auto-hits — the "cannot miss" effect means the accuracy roll is skipped entirely. The GM must enforce this manually. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Small Melee and Short Ranged Weapons Only" but this limitation is truncated from the displayed effect.
