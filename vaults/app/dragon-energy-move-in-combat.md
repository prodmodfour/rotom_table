Dragon Energy appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Energy Dragon DB 15 Daily x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 15, AC: 2
- Range: Cone 3 or Line 8
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "For each 10% of HP the user is missing, Dragon Energy's Damage Base is reduced by 1."

Dragon Energy has the highest observed base DB (15) among moves surveyed so far. The range offers a choice between "Cone 3 or Line 8" — another dual-mode range displayed verbatim with no UI toggle. Daily x2 frequency is the most restrictive non-Daily frequency, limiting it to two uses per day. The HP-scaling penalty means DB drops by 1 for every 10% HP lost — at half health it would be DB 10, still strong. Combined with AC 2, Dragon Energy is devastating when used at full HP early in combat. The area coverage from Cone or Line makes it effective against groups.
