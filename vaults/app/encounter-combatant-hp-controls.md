# Encounter Combatant HP Controls

Each [[encounter-combatant-card]] has two sets of HP modification controls:

**HP reduction (left side):**
- A spinbutton for entering the amount (defaults to 0)
- A **-HP** button to apply the reduction
- An [[encounter-hp-reduction-type-selector]] dropdown

**HP gain (right side):**
- A spinbutton for entering the amount (defaults to 0)
- A **+HP** button to apply the gain

Applying damage immediately updates the HP display and the visual HP bar on the card.
