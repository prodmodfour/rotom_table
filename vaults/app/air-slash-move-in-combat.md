Air Slash appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink/magenta (Flying-type) button showing "Air Slash FLYING DB 8 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, AC: 3, Range: 6, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Air Slash Flinches the target on 15+."

The Flinch condition would need to be applied manually by the GM via the [[encounter-status-conditions-modal]] if the accuracy roll meets the 15+ threshold.
