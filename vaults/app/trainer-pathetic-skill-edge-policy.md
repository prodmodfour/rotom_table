During level-up, Pathetic skills can be raised via Skill Edges. This differs from character creation, where the Pathetic rank restriction is enforced. The [[trainer-level-up-composable]] notes this as a policy from decree-027: "Pathetic skill restriction is creation-only."

The `getEffectiveSkillRank()` function in the composable applies rank-ups starting from whatever the character's current rank is, including Pathetic. A Pathetic skill raised by one Skill Edge becomes Untrained; raised by two becomes Novice.
