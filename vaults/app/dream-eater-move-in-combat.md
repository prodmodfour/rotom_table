Dream Eater appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Psychic-type) button showing "Dream Eater Psychic DB 10 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 10, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dream Eater can only target Sleeping Pokémon or Trainers. After the target takes damage, the user gains Hit Points equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target. Dream Eater does not wake up sleeping targets."

Dream Eater is the most powerful draining move observed, with DB 10, but carries a strict prerequisite: the target must be Sleeping. This condition is described in the effect text but not enforced by the UI — the target selection panel does not filter out non-sleeping targets. The effect also explicitly notes that Dream Eater does not wake up sleeping targets, allowing repeated use against a sleeping foe. Melee range on a Special move is again unusual, requiring close proximity despite being stat-based on SP.ATK.
