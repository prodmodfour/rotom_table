Resonance Beam appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Resonance Beam NORMAL DB 4 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4 → 6 (with STAB), AC: 3, Range: Line 4
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "All targets have their Special Defense lowered by 1 Combat Stage on 20+. This Effect Range is extended by +1 for each foe targeted by this Move."

Resonance Beam is a trainer Special weapon move that fires in a Line 4 area. The range extension effect (Line grows longer per foe hit) and the SP.DEF debuff on a high roll (20+) must both be tracked manually by the GM.
