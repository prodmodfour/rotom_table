Chip Away appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Chip Away Normal DB 7 EOT AC 2". The button text shows the base DB value; the target panel reveals the STAB-boosted value.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9 (STAB), AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, ATK: 80
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge because the Move Tester is Normal-type
- Effect text: "Ignore any Armor, Damage Reduction, or changes in the target's Defense or Special Defense (such as from Combat Stages) when calculating damage."

Chip Away ignores all defensive modifications — Armor, DR, and Defense/SpDef combat stage changes. This makes it effective against targets that have buffed their defensive stats or wear heavy armor. The STAB boost from 7 to 9 DB gives it strong consistent damage. EOT frequency prevents spamming but its defense-ignoring property makes each use count.
