Crush Claw appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Crush Claw Normal DB 7 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9, AC: 3
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
- ATK: 80
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge — the DB display shows the STAB-boosted value (7 → 9) inline
- Effect text: "Crush Claw lowers the target's Defense by -1 CS on Even-Numbered Rolls."

Crush Claw has a generous Defense-lowering trigger: any even-numbered accuracy roll (roughly 50% of the time) reduces the target's Defense by 1 Combat Stage. This is much more reliable than [[crunch-move-in-combat]] at 17+ (~20%). The Dash keyword in the range means the user can move and attack in a single action. The STAB boost from the Normal-type Move Tester raises the effective DB from 7 to 9.
