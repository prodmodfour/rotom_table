Aqua Tail appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Aqua Tail WATER DB 9 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 4, Range: Melee, Pass
- ATK stat of the attacker
- No special effect text

AC 4 is notably higher than the typical AC 2, making this a less accurate but more powerful move. The "Pass" keyword in the range allows the attack to pass through allies without hitting them. DB 9 is high (3d8+10 dice).
