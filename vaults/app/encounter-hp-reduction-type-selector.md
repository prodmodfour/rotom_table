# Encounter HP Reduction Type Selector

A dropdown next to the -HP button on each [[encounter-combatant-card]]. Its accessible label reads "HP reduction type: Damage (injuries), HP Loss (no massive damage), Set HP (no massive damage)."

Options:

- **DMG** (default) — standard damage that can trigger injury rules
- **Loss** — HP loss without massive damage checks
- **Set** — directly sets HP to a specific value, without massive damage checks
