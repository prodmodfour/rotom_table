Blaze Kick appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red (Fire-type) button showing "Blaze Kick Fire DB 9 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Blaze Kick Burns the target on 19+ and is a Critical Hit on 18+."

Blaze Kick has dual conditional effects on high accuracy rolls: Burn on 19+ and Critical Hit on 18+. The critical hit threshold (18+) is lower than the status effect threshold (19+), so a roll of 18 would crit without burning, while 19+ would both crit and burn. EOT frequency limits it to once per round.
