Dark Void appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark (Dark-type) button showing "Dark Void Dark EOT AC 4". No DB value is displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 4
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- Effect text: "The target falls Asleep. Once per Scene, Dark Void may be used as if its range were 'Burst 5, Friendly' instead."

Dark Void inflicts Sleep on the target, one of the most disabling status conditions. Its default range is Melee with 1 Target, but once per Scene it can switch to Burst 5 Friendly — hitting all enemies in a massive area while avoiding allies. The AC 4 makes it unreliable, balancing the power of guaranteed Sleep on hit. EOT frequency allows repeated attempts, which offsets the low accuracy somewhat. The dual-mode range (single Melee or area Burst 5) gives it tactical flexibility — use the Burst mode for the opening mass-sleep attempt, then fall back to single-target Melee for subsequent uses.
