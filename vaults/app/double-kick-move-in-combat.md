Double Kick appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Double Kick Fighting DB 3 At-Will AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 3, AC: 3
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Double Kick is a straightforward Double Strike move with no secondary effect. DB 3 per strike is low, but the two hits provide decent cumulative damage and two chances for critical hits. AC 3 makes each strike slightly less accurate. At-Will frequency allows unlimited use, making it a reliable if modest option. Compared to [[double-hit-move-in-combat]] which also has Double Strike and no secondary effect, Double Kick has lower DB (3 vs 4) and the same AC 3, but gains Fighting typing which provides super-effective coverage against Normal, Rock, Steel, Ice, and Dark types.
