The Notes tab on the [[gm-pokemon-detail-page]] contains two text fields:

- **Notes** — a general-purpose text area. For imported Pokemon, this contains "Imported from PTU sheet". The [[evolution-service]] also appends evolution history notes here.
- **Held Item** — a text field for the Pokemon's held item.

Both fields are disabled in view mode and editable in [[gm-pokemon-detail-edit-mode]].
