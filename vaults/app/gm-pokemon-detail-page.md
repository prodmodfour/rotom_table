The Pokemon detail page lives at `/gm/pokemon/:id`. It displays a single Pokemon's full sheet.

The header area contains a "Back to Sheets" link, an [[gm-pokemon-detail-evolve-button]], and an [[gm-pokemon-detail-edit-mode]] toggle.

Below the header, the [[gm-pokemon-detail-identity-section]] shows the Pokemon's sprite, species, nickname, level, EXP, gender, shiny status, location, and type badges.

The lower half is organized into seven tabs: [[gm-pokemon-detail-stats-tab]], [[gm-pokemon-detail-moves-tab]], [[gm-pokemon-detail-abilities-tab]], [[gm-pokemon-detail-capabilities-tab]], [[gm-pokemon-detail-skills-tab]], [[gm-pokemon-detail-healing-tab]], and [[gm-pokemon-detail-notes-tab]]. Stats is the default tab.

The page title reflects the Pokemon's nickname (e.g., "GM - Sabre").
