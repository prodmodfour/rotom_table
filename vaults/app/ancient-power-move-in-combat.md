Ancient Power appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan/olive (Rock-type) button showing "Ancient Power ROCK DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target, Spirit Surge
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "The user has each of its stats raised by +1 CS on 19+."

The range includes "Spirit Surge", a PTU keyword. The all-stats +1 CS boost on 19+ is described in the effect text but not automatically applied. The GM must check the accuracy roll and manually boost all combat stages if the threshold is met.
