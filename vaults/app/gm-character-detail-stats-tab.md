The Stats tab on the [[gm-character-detail-page]] displays three groups of information.

The first group shows combat stats as derived values:
- HP (shown with a base stat number and current/max display, e.g., "11" and "45 / 45")
- Attack, Defense, Sp. Atk, Sp. Def, Speed (each shown as a single number)

In [[gm-character-detail-edit-mode]], HP gains an editable spinbutton for adjusting current HP. The other combat stats remain read-only since they are derived.

The second group shows physical attributes:
- Height (cm), Weight (kg), Money — all as number spinners, disabled in view mode, editable in edit mode

The third group shows [[gm-character-detail-capabilities-display]].
