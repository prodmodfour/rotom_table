The Special Balls category in the [[capture-ball-selector-dropdown]] contains twelve balls. Many have conditional modifiers shown via [[capture-ball-conditional-modifier-preview]]:

- Premier Ball: +0
- Repeat Ball: +0 (if you own the species → -20)
- Timer Ball: +5 (degrades -5 per round, to -20)
- Nest Ball: +0 (if target under level 10 → -20)
- Net Ball: +0 (if target is Water or Bug → -20)
- Dive Ball: +0 (if underwater/underground → -20)
- Luxury Ball: -5, with [[capture-ball-post-capture-effect-badge]] "Happiness"
- Heal Ball: -5, with [[capture-ball-post-capture-effect-badge]] "Heal Max"
- Quick Ball: -20 (degrades over time: +5 after round 1, +10 after round 2, +20 after round 3)
- Dusk Ball: +0 (if dark → -20)
- Cherish Ball: -5
