Arm Thrust appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as an orange/red (Fighting-type) button showing "Arm Thrust FIGHTING DB 2 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 2, AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Five Strike
- ATK stat of the attacker
- No special effect text

The "Five Strike" keyword in the range means the move hits multiple times — in PTU, Five Strike rolls a d20 to determine how many hits (2-5). The app displays this keyword but each strike uses the same DB 2 per hit. The multi-hit resolution is handled manually by the GM.
