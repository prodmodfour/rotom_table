Brutal Swing appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark (Dark-type) button showing "Brutal Swing Dark DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Burst 1, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

Brutal Swing is a Burst 1 area move centered on the user, hitting all adjacent targets. It has no secondary effect, making it a pure damage AoE. DB 6 with EOT frequency is modest but the Burst 1 range can hit multiple targets. Compared to [[breaking-swipe-move-in-combat]] which is also DB 6 EOT AoE, Brutal Swing trades the ATK debuff for a different area shape (Burst vs Close Blast).
