When a trainer's XP crosses the 10 XP threshold via the [[gm-character-detail-xp-section]], a modal wizard titled "Level Up: [Name]" appears with a "Level X -> Y" badge.

The modal is a multi-step wizard with a step indicator at the top right (e.g., "Stats (1/3)"). Steps are navigated with Back and Next buttons at the bottom. The final step replaces Next with "Apply Level Up". A Cancel button is available on every step, and an X button sits in the header.

The [[trainer-level-up-step-order]] is: [[trainer-level-up-milestone-step|Milestones]], [[trainer-level-up-stat-allocation-step|Stats]], [[trainer-level-up-edges-step|Edges]], [[trainer-level-up-feature-step|Features]], [[trainer-level-up-class-step|Classes]], [[trainer-level-up-summary-step|Summary]]. Steps are omitted when their budget is zero — at level 1 -> 2, only Stats, Edges, and Summary appear.

A [[trainer-level-up-gains-banner]] appears at the top of every step showing what the new level grants.
