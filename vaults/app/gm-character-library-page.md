The `/gm/sheets` route presents the Character Library, a unified listing of all human characters and Pokemon in the campaign. The page title is "Character Library" and it is accessible from the "Sheets" link in the [[gm-navigation-bar]].

The page has a header with a "Manage" button that toggles [[gm-character-library-manage-mode]] and a "+ New Character" link that navigates to the [[create-character-page]].

Below the header sits a [[gm-character-library-filters]] bar for searching and filtering the list.

Characters are divided into two sections. The first section lists human characters grouped by role under a heading with count (e.g., "Players (9)"). Each character appears as a [[gm-character-library-player-card]]. The second section lists Pokemon under a heading with count (e.g., "Pokemon (183)"), grouped by location using collapsible sections. Each Pokemon appears as a [[gm-character-library-pokemon-card]].

Clicking a character card navigates to its [[gm-character-detail-page]].
