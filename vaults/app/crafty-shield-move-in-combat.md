Crafty Shield appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Crafty Shield Fairy Scene". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status
- Range: Burst 2, Trigger, Interrupt, Shield
- Effect text: "If the user or an Ally within 2 meters of Crafty Shield's user is hit by a Status Move, you may use Crafty Shield as an Interrupt. All targets in Crafty Shield's area-of-effect including the user, are instead not hit by the triggering Move and do not suffer any of its effects."

Crafty Shield is an Interrupt move that protects against Status moves specifically. The Trigger, Interrupt, and Shield keywords in the range indicate it operates reactively outside normal turn flow — similar to [[counter-move-in-combat]] being a Reaction. The Burst 2 area means it protects the user and all allies within 2 meters from the triggering Status move. Scene frequency limits it to one use per encounter. This is narrower than general protection moves like [[baneful-bunker-move-in-combat]] since it only blocks Status moves, but broader in that it shields multiple allies simultaneously.
