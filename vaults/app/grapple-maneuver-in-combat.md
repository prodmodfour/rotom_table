Grapple appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Grapple", a "Standard" action-type badge, an "AC 4" badge, and the description "Initiate grapple (opposed Combat/Athletics)". Its icon is `hand-grabbing.svg`.

Clicking Grapple fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]] — no target-selection step. The GM resolves the accuracy check (AC 4), opposed skill check, and Grappled status application manually.

Grapple carries the `maneuver_other` AoO trigger flag in the [[combat-maneuver-constants]], meaning it provokes an [[attack-of-opportunity-in-combat]] from adjacent foes when used against a non-adjacent target.
