Energy Vortex appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Energy Vortex NORMAL DB 2 Scene x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 2 → 4 (with STAB), AC: 4, Range: WR, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "The target is put in a Vortex."

Energy Vortex is a trainer Special weapon move with the lowest DB among weapon moves (DB 2) but carries the Vortex condition as its primary purpose. The Vortex status must be applied and tracked manually by the GM.
