Crabhammer appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Crabhammer Water DB 10 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 4
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Crabhammer is a Critical Hit on 18+."

Crabhammer is a high-power Physical Water move with an enhanced critical hit range. The standard critical threshold is 20, but Crabhammer crits on 18+, giving it roughly a 15% crit chance versus the usual 5%. The AC 4 makes it less accurate than most moves, balancing its high DB 10 power. EOT frequency allows one use per round. Melee range requires adjacency to the target.
