Pierce! appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Pierce! NORMAL DB 7 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Pierce deals an additional +10 damage against targets with Damage Reduction."

Pierce! is a trainer Physical weapon move with the highest DB among EOT single-target weapon moves (tied with Bash! at DB 7). The +10 bonus damage against Damage Reduction targets must be calculated and applied manually by the GM.
