The identity section at the top of the [[gm-character-detail-page]] displays:

- A circular avatar showing the character's first initial letter
- Name (text field, disabled in view mode)
- Played By (text field, disabled in view mode)
- Level (number spinner, disabled in view mode)
- Age (number spinner, disabled in view mode)
- Gender (text field, disabled in view mode)
- Type (dropdown with "Player" and "NPC" options, disabled in view mode)

In [[gm-character-detail-edit-mode]], all fields become editable. When the level is manually increased in edit mode, the [[trainer-level-up-modal]] opens to handle the allocation of gains.
