The `/gm/characters/:id` route displays a single human character's full sheet. The page title reflects the character name (e.g., "GM - Hassan").

At the top is a "Back to Sheets" link returning to the [[gm-character-library-page]] and an "Edit" button that toggles [[gm-character-detail-edit-mode]].

The page is divided into three main areas:
1. The [[gm-character-detail-identity-section]] showing the character's basic info
2. The [[gm-character-detail-xp-section]] for tracking and granting trainer experience
3. A tabbed content area with [[gm-character-detail-tab-navigation]] switching between Stats, Classes, Skills, Equipment, Pokemon, Healing, and Notes

The page also hosts the [[trainer-level-up-modal]], which opens when XP grants or manual level edits trigger a level-up.
