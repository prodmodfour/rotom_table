# Battle Grid Measurement Toolbar

A row of buttons below the [[battle-grid]] header for measuring distances and area-of-effect shapes on the grid:

- **Distance** — point-to-point distance measurement
- **Burst** — circular AoE centered on a point
- **Cone** — cone-shaped AoE
- **Line** — linear AoE
- **Close Blast** — close-range rectangular AoE

When a measurement tool is active, a "Clear" button appears at the right end of the toolbar to dismiss the overlay. The [[battle-grid-coordinate-display]] updates to show the active tool name alongside coordinates.
