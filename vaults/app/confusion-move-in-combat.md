Confusion appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Psychic-type) button showing "Confusion Psychic DB 5 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 5, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Confusion Confuses the target on 19+."

Confusion is a basic Psychic Special attack with a small chance (10%) to inflict the Confused condition. DB 5 and AC 2 are modest baseline values. The 6-meter range gives comfortable distance. At-Will frequency allows unlimited use, making it a reliable workhorse move. The confusion chance on 19+ is a minor bonus rather than the move's primary purpose — compare with [[confuse-ray-move-in-combat]] which guarantees confusion.
