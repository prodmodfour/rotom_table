The Abilities tab on the [[gm-pokemon-detail-page]] lists the Pokemon's abilities. Each ability shows its name as a heading and its effect description as a paragraph.

For example, Vulpix [Alola] shows "Cute Charm" with its trigger effect and "Snow Cloak" with its hail-related evasion bonus.

Abilities are assigned during creation (the [[pokemon-generator-service]] picks one random Basic Ability) or at milestone levels via [[pokemon-ability-milestone-assignment]].
