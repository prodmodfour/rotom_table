# Derived Stats Display

A sub-section of [[full-create-combat-stats-section]] headed "Derived Stats". Displays four computed values:

- Max HP: 42 (at default stats)
- Physical Evasion: 1
- Special Evasion: 1
- Speed Evasion: 1

These values update automatically as stat point allocations change. A similar display appears in the [[trainer-level-up-derived-stats-preview]] during level-up.
