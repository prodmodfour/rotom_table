Dual Chop appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dual Chop Dragon DB 5 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5, AC: 3
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Doublestrike
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

The range line displays "Doublestrike" as a single word, unlike [[dual-wingbeat-move-in-combat]] which displays "Double Strike" as two words. Both keywords mean the same thing — hit the target twice — but the data inconsistency is visible in the target panel. DB 5 per hit with AC 3 and EOT frequency makes Dual Chop a modest Double Strike option. Compared to [[dragon-darts-move-in-combat]] which also has DB 5 and Double Strike, Dual Chop has worse accuracy (AC 3 vs 2) and is Melee-only, but [[dragon-darts-move-in-combat]] also offers a split-target mode.
