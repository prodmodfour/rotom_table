Boomburst appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Boomburst Normal DB 14 Scene AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 14 → 16 (STAB boost), AC: 2, Range: Burst 1, Sonic
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

Boomburst has one of the highest damage bases observed (14, boosted to 16 with STAB). The Burst 1 range creates an AoE centered on the user hitting all adjacent targets. The Sonic keyword is a range modifier — Sonic moves cannot be blocked by Soundproof but can interact with other sound-related abilities. Scene frequency limits it to one use per scene. With AC 2 and no accuracy complications, this is an extremely powerful AoE nuke limited mainly by its once-per-scene restriction.
