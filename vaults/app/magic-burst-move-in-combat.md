Magic Burst appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Magic Burst NORMAL DB 6 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical (the CSV source marks this as Special — the Physical display is a seed parsing artifact from the multiline effect field), DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: Burst 1, Friendly
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Foes hit by Magic Burst can't make Attacks of Opportunity for 1 full round."

Magic Burst is a trainer Special weapon move with the Friendly keyword, meaning allies in the Burst 1 area are excluded. The AoO suppression effect must be tracked manually by the GM. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Melee Weapons Only" but this is truncated from the displayed effect.
