Bide appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Bide Normal Scene".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, Range: Burst 1, Friendly
- No DB or AC fields displayed
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "The user may use Bide as a Reaction Move upon being Hit by a Damaging Move. During their next available turn, the user may Shift and then use Bide, causing all Adjacent foes to lose X HP, where X is the amount of Damage taken since declaring use of Bide (Loss of life through effects such as Poison is not 'Damage')."

Bide is a reactive move with Burst 1, Friendly range — it hits all adjacent foes but excludes allies. It has no fixed DB because its damage equals the damage the user received. This is the first observed move with the "Friendly" range keyword, indicating it avoids hitting allied targets within the AoE. The delayed counter-damage mechanic is tracked manually by the GM. Scene frequency.
