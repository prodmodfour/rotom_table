Amnesia appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Psychic-type) button showing "Amnesia PSYCHIC EOT".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Special Defense 2 Combat Stages."

Functionally identical in UI flow to [[agility-move-in-combat]] — a Self-targeting status move with no AC, boosting a stat by +2 CS. The GM must manually apply the change through the [[encounter-combat-stages-modal]].
