Astral Barrage appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Ghost-type) button showing "Astral Barrage GHOST DB 12 Daily AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 12, AC: 2, Range: 8, Ranged Blast 2, Smite Friendly
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Legal targets hit by Astral Barrage are Slowed for one full round."

DB 12 is very high (4d12+10 dice). The range "Ranged Blast 2" is an AoE at a distance, and "Smite Friendly" means it hits allies in the blast as well. The Slowed condition is not automatically applied — the GM handles it manually. Daily frequency.
