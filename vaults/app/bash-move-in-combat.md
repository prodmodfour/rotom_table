Bash! appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Bash! NORMAL DB 7 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Bash! lowers the target's Initiative to 0 for 1 full round on 15+."

Bash! is a trainer Physical weapon move with high damage (DB 7) and a debilitating Initiative reset on a high roll (15+). The Initiative modification must be tracked manually by the GM.
