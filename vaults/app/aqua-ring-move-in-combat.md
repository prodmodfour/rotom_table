Aqua Ring appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Aqua Ring WATER Scene".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self, Coat
- Effect text: "Aqua Ring covers the user in a Coat that heals the user at the beginning of their turn. The user is healed a Tick of Hit Points each turn."

The "Coat" keyword in the range indicates the move applies a persistent layer that can be removed. The tick healing is not automated — the GM must manually heal at the start of each turn.
