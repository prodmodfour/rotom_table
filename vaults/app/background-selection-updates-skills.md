# Background Selection Updates Skills

When a background is chosen in the [[full-create-background-and-skills-section]], the [[skills-display]] immediately updates to reflect the assigned ranks. The background definitions come from the [[trainer-background-catalog-constant]]. For example, selecting "Fitness Training" sets Athletics to Adept, Acrobatics to Novice, and Guile, Intuition, and Focus to Pathetic. All other skills remain Untrained.

A description paragraph also appears below the dropdown explaining the background flavor text, along with a summary of the exact skill assignments.

If the assigned ranks violate the level cap, a [[skill-rank-cap-warning]] appears.
