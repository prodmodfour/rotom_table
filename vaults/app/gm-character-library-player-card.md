Each human character in the [[gm-character-library-page]] is displayed as a clickable card that links to the character's [[gm-character-detail-page]].

The card shows:
- A circular avatar with the character's first initial letter
- The character's name as a heading
- The character's role label (e.g., "Player")
- The character's level (e.g., "Level 1")
- HP as current/max (e.g., "HP: 45/45")
- Speed stat (e.g., "SPD: 11")
- If the character has a partner Pokemon, a small sprite of that Pokemon appears with its nickname
