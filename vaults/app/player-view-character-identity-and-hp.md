# Player View Character Identity and HP

The top region of the [[player-view-character-tab]]. On the left is a circular letter avatar (first letter of the character's name on a pink/magenta background), the character's name as a heading, their level (e.g. "Lv. 1"), and their trainer classes as a slash-separated list (e.g. "Ace Trainer / Elite Trainer").

On the right is the character's HP displayed as "current / max HP" (e.g. "45 / 45 HP") with a horizontal HP bar that fills proportionally. The bar is green/teal when full.
