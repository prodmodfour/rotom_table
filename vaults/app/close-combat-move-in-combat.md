Close Combat appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Close Combat Fighting DB 12 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 12, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Lower the user's Defense and Special Defense by -1 CS each after damage."

Close Combat is one of the strongest Physical Fighting attacks available, with DB 12 matching top-tier moves. The Dash keyword allows the user to close distance before striking, making it effective even when not adjacent to the target. The drawback is -1 CS to both Defense and Special Defense after use, leaving the user exposed. Scene x2 frequency allows two uses per encounter, though repeated use compounds the defensive penalty.
