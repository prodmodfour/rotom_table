There is no dedicated page or component for browsing or searching the full [[movedata-reference-table]]. Moves are only visible in context: on the [[gm-pokemon-detail-moves-tab]], in the [[move-learning-panel]] during level-up, in the [[player-view-pokemon-moves]] section, and in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] during combat (with full details shown in the [[encounter-move-target-panel]]).

The [[batch-move-lookup-api]] is the only general-purpose lookup mechanism, and it requires knowing move names in advance.
