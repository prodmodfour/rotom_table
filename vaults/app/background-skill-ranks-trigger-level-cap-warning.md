# Background Skill Ranks Trigger Level Cap Warning

When selecting "Fitness Training" background at level 1 in the [[full-create-background-and-skills-section]], the [[skill-rank-cap-warning]] fires: "Skills exceed rank cap for level 1 (max Novice): Athletics (Adept)". The background assigns Athletics to Adept, which is above the level 1 Novice cap. The rank cap validation does not distinguish between background-assigned ranks and manually trained ranks.
