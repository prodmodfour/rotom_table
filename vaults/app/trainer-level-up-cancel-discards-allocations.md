Clicking "Cancel" or the X button on the [[trainer-level-up-modal]] dismisses the modal and discards all allocation choices (stat points, edges, features, classes, milestones).

Because the [[xp-award-updates-level-immediately|level already incremented]] when the XP was awarded, cancelling leaves the character at the new level but without any of the corresponding stat or capability gains applied.

The character sheet then shows the higher level with unchanged stats, edges, and features.
