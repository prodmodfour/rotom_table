# Encounter Combat Stages Modal

A modal opened by clicking the "CS" button on an [[encounter-combatant-card]]. The title reads "Combat Stages - [Name]" (e.g. "Combat Stages - Ash").

**Combat Stages (stat multipliers)** — five stats arranged in a grid, each with a minus button, current value (default 0), and plus button:

- Atk, Def, SpA, SpD, Spe

**Accuracy & Evasion (additive modifiers)** — two values, same layout:

- Acc, Eva Bonus

Footer buttons: **Reset All** (resets all to 0) and **Save Changes**.
