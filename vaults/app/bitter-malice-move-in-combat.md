Bitter Malice appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Bitter Malice Normal DB 6 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6 → 8 (STAB boost), AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- STAB badge displayed (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: ": Legal targets hit by Bitter Malice are Stuck and Trapped until the end of their next turn on a 19+."

The effect text begins with a leading colon (":"), which appears to be a formatting artifact from the move data source. The move is typed as Normal in the database, though in the official Pokemon games Bitter Malice is a Ghost-type move — this is a data discrepancy in the [[movedata-reference-table]]. The STAB badge and DB boost (6 → 8) appear because the move's stored type (Normal) matches Move Tester's Normal type.

The range of 6 means the target can be up to 6 meters away, making it a ranged Physical attack despite using the ATK stat.
