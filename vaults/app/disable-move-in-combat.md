Disable appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Disable Normal Scene". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check. A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: 1 Target, Trigger
- Effect text: "Disable may be used as a Free Action that does not take up a Command whenever the user is hit by a Move. That Move becomes Disabled for the attacker."

Disable is a reactive move that triggers when the user is hit by an attack. Unlike most Status moves, it is a Free Action — it does not consume the user's turn or a Command, allowing them to still act normally. The Trigger keyword indicates it activates in response to being attacked. The Disabled status prevents the attacker from using that specific move again. Scene frequency limits it to one use per encounter. Compared to [[detect-move-in-combat]] which prevents damage entirely, Disable allows the hit but punishes the attacker by removing their move option.
