Cut appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Cut Normal DB 5 At-Will AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5 → 7, AC: 3
- Range: Melee, Pass
- ATK: 80
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge — the DB display shows the STAB-boosted value (5 → 7) inline
- Effect text: "Cut ignores up to 5 Damage Reduction (Defenses are not Damage Reduction.)"

Cut is a low-power Physical attack with a Damage Reduction penetration effect. The move ignores up to 5 points of DR, with the parenthetical clarifying that normal Defense stats are not considered DR — this targets specific abilities, items, or effects that grant Damage Reduction. The Pass keyword in the range means the user moves through the target's space. At-Will frequency makes it freely repeatable. DB 5 is modest but the STAB boost to 7 and DR bypass can make it effective against DR-heavy targets.
