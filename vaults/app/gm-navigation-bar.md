# GM Navigation Bar

A persistent banner at the top of every GM page. It shows the app title "Rotom Table" with a "GM View" label.

The navigation links are: Encounter (`/gm`), Encounters (`/gm/encounters`), Scenes (`/gm/scenes`), Habitats (`/gm/habitats`), Sheets (`/gm/sheets`), Create (`/gm/create`), and Map (`/gm/map`).

The right side of the bar contains a "Connect" button that opens the [[gm-connect-panel]], a "Group View" section with Lobby/Scene/Encounter/Map toggle buttons, an "Advance Day" button, and a "Group View" link to the [[group-view-page]].
