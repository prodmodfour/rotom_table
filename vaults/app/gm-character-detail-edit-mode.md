Clicking the "Edit" button on the [[gm-character-detail-page]] enters edit mode, adding `?edit=true` to the URL.

In edit mode:
- The "Edit" button is replaced by "Cancel" and "Save Changes" buttons
- Identity fields (name, played by, level, age, gender, type) become editable in the [[gm-character-detail-identity-section]]
- The avatar initial becomes clickable (for changing the trainer sprite)
- In the [[gm-character-detail-stats-tab]], current HP gains an editable spinbutton, and height, weight, money become editable
- In the [[gm-character-detail-notes-tab]], all text fields become editable
- Combat stats and capabilities remain read-only because they are derived values

Clicking "Cancel" discards changes and returns to view mode. Clicking "Save Changes" persists the edits.
