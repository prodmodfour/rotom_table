Beak Blast appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink/magenta (Flying-type) button showing "Beak Blast Flying DB 10 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target, Priority
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Beak Blast must be declared at the start of the round. If the user is hit by a Melee attack this round, the triggering attacker is Burned. At the end of the round, the user Shifts and attacks with Beak Blast."

Like [[avalanche-move-in-combat]], Beak Blast has a delayed-execution mechanic: declared at round start, executed at round end. The Burn infliction on melee attackers during the waiting period adds a defensive deterrent. The Priority keyword combined with delayed execution creates a unique timing interaction. Scene x2 frequency.
