Conversion2 appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Conversion2 Normal At-Will". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge, which is unusual for a Status move
- Effect text: "The user becomes the elemental Type of their choice as long as the Type resists the elemental Type of the Move it last took damage from until the end of the encounter. Replace all other Types."

Conversion2 is the reactive counterpart to [[conversion-move-in-combat]]. Instead of freely choosing any type the user has a move for, Conversion2 requires the chosen type to resist the type of the move that last dealt damage to the user. This makes it a defensive response — after being hit by a Fire move, the user could become Water or Dragon type, for example. At-Will frequency allows re-adaptation after each hit. The restriction makes it less flexible than Conversion but encourages tactical type-switching in response to the opponent's attacks.
