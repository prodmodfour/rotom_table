Dragon Ascent appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Flying-type) button showing "Dragon Ascent Flying DB 12 Scene x2 AC 2". No STAB badge appears since the user is Normal-type.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 12, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The user's Defense and Special Defense are each lowered by -1 Combat Stage."

Despite its name, Dragon Ascent is Flying-type, not Dragon-type — the type badge in the target panel confirms this. DB 12 with AC 2 and Dash makes it one of the most powerful physical moves available. The defensive cost of -1 DEF and -1 SPDEF per use is less punishing than Draco Meteor's -2 to one stat but affects both defensive stats. Scene x2 frequency allows two uses per scene.
