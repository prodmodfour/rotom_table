The Milestones step of the [[trainer-level-up-modal]], shown when the advancement crosses a milestone level (5, 10, 20, 30, or 40). This step appears first in the wizard because [[trainer-level-up-milestone-budget-effects|milestone choices alter the stat, edge, and feature budgets]] for subsequent steps.

Headed "Lifestyle Milestones". Each crossed milestone appears as a card with the milestone name and level (e.g., "Amateur Trainer", "Level 5"), a "Choose one bonus:" prompt, and radio-button options.

Amateur (level 5) offers two options: "Lifestyle Stat Points" (+1 Atk or SpAtk per even level 6-10, plus 2 retroactive for levels 2 and 4) or "General Feature". Capable and Veteran (levels 10, 20) offer "Lifestyle Stat Points" or "Bonus Edges" (2 edges). Elite and Champion (levels 30, 40) offer all three: "Lifestyle Stat Points", "Bonus Edges" (2 edges), or "General Feature".

Selecting a radio button immediately updates the [[trainer-level-up-gains-banner]] and the budgets for subsequent steps.
