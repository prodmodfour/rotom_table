Bon Mot is one of three sub-options of the [[manipulate-maneuver-in-combat]]. It does not appear in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid or anywhere in the encounter UI.

Bon Mot exists as a separate entry in the moves seed data (moves.csv, Maneuver section): Standard action, AC 2, range 6 (1 Target). On a successful opposed Guile check (vs target's Guile or Focus), the target is Enraged and cannot spend AP for one full round. The target does not get a Save Check against this effect.

Each Manipulation sub-option can only be used once per Scene per target. Since the app has no UI for Manipulate, the GM resolves this entirely manually.
