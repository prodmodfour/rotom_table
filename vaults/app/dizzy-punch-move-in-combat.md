Dizzy Punch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Dizzy Punch Normal DB 7 At-Will AC 2". A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge in the target panel.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dizzy Punch Confuses the target on 17+."

The DB field shows "7 → 9", demonstrating how the target panel displays STAB (Same-Type Attack Bonus): the base DB is shown with an arrow to the STAB-boosted value. This is the first observed move where STAB visually modifies the displayed DB. The Confusion effect on 17+ gives roughly a 20% chance per hit. At-Will frequency means unlimited uses, making Dizzy Punch a reliable workhorse attack with a useful secondary effect. Confusion forces the target to roll each turn to act normally, providing lasting disruption.
