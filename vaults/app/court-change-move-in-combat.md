Court Change appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Court Change Normal Daily". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Field
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge, which is unusual for a Status move
- Effect text: "All Blessings and Hazards swap which side that they belong to."

Court Change is a Field-range move that swaps all Blessings and Hazards between sides. This makes it powerful in encounters where one side has set up field effects — the user can steal the opponent's Reflect, Light Screen, or Tailwind while simultaneously burdening them with Stealth Rock, Spikes, or Toxic Spikes the user's side had received. Daily frequency limits it to one use per day. Field range means it requires no specific target proximity, and both Target Dummy and Move Tester appeared as selectable targets unlike most moves where out-of-range targets are greyed out.
