The Skills tab on the [[gm-character-detail-page]] lists all PTU trainer skills with their current rank names. The skills shown are:

Acrobatics, Athletics, Charm, Combat, Command, General Ed, Medicine Ed, Occult Ed, Pokemon Ed, Technology Ed, Focus, Guile, Intimidate, Intuition, Perception, Stealth, Survival.

Each skill displays its name and rank as a label (e.g., "Untrained", "Pathetic", "Novice", "Adept"). The list is flat without the Body/Mind/Spirit grouping used in the [[skills-display]] during character creation.

The skills are read-only in both view and edit modes.
