Coil appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Coil Poison Scene x2". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Attack and Defense by +1 CS, and the user gains +1 Accuracy."

Coil provides a triple benefit: +1 CS to Attack, +1 CS to Defense, and +1 Accuracy bonus. The Accuracy bonus is distinct from combat stage modifiers — it is an additive bonus to accuracy rolls, making attacks more reliable. This combination of offensive, defensive, and accuracy improvement makes it one of the more comprehensive setup moves. Scene x2 frequency allows stacking twice per encounter.
