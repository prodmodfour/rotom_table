Cross Chop appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Cross Chop Fighting DB 10 Scene x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 4
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Cross Chop is a Critical Hit on 16+."

Cross Chop is a high-power Fighting-type Physical attack with a notably wide critical hit range. Critical hits on 16+ give approximately a 25% crit chance, more generous than [[crabhammer-move-in-combat]] at 18+ or the standard 20. The AC 4 makes accuracy a concern, and Scene x2 frequency limits it to two uses per encounter. The combination of high DB, wide crit range, and limited uses positions it as a burst-damage option for decisive moments.
