Autotomize appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Steel-type) button showing "Autotomize Steel EOT".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- No AC, DB, or attack stat fields
- Effect text: "For the remainder of the Encounter, the user's Weight Class is one value lower, to a minimum of 1. If the user can, the user's Speed is raised by +2 Combat Stages."

Autotomize is a Self-targeting Status move with two effects: reducing weight class and raising Speed by 2 combat stages. Both effects are described in text and applied manually by the GM. The "+2 Combat Stages" speed boost is the largest single-move Speed increase observed so far. EOT frequency.
