Cross Poison appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Cross Poison Poison DB 7 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, Pass
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Cross Poison is a Critical Hit on 18+ and Poisons the target on 19+."

Cross Poison combines an enhanced critical hit range with a Poison infliction chance. The 18+ crit threshold matches [[crabhammer-move-in-combat]], while the 19+ Poison chance adds a secondary debilitating effect. The "Pass" keyword in the range is distinctive — it means the user moves through the target's space during the attack. EOT frequency allows one use per round. The dual effect roll thresholds (18+ crit, 19+ Poison) mean that a roll of 19 or 20 produces both a critical hit and Poison simultaneously.
