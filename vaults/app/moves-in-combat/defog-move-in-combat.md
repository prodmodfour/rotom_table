Defog appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a light blue (Flying-type) button showing "Defog Flying Daily x2". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Field, Weather
- Effect text: "The Weather becomes Clear, and all Blessings, Coats, and Hazards are destroyed. Clear Weather is the default weather, conferring no bonuses or penalties of any sort."

Defog is a comprehensive field-clearing move that resets both weather and all persistent field effects. It destroys Blessings (like [[aurora-veil-move-in-combat]]), Coats, and Hazards simultaneously, making it the broadest field reset observed. Unlike [[court-change-move-in-combat]] which swaps Blessings and Hazards between sides, Defog simply removes everything. The Range shows "Field, Weather" — a dual range tag indicating it affects both the field layer and weather layer. Daily x2 frequency allows two uses per day. Both combatants appeared as selectable targets in the target list, consistent with Field-range moves.
