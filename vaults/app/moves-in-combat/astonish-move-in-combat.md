Astonish appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Ghost-type) button showing "Astonish GHOST DB 3 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 3, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Astonish Flinches the target on 15+. Once per Scene, if the target is unaware of the user's presence, Astonish automatically Flinches."

Like [[air-slash-move-in-combat]], has a conditional Flinch effect on a roll threshold. The awareness-based auto-Flinch is a unique modifier.
