Confide appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Confide Normal At-Will AC 2". No DB value is shown since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2, Range: 4, 1 Target, Social
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge, which is unusual for a Status move that deals no damage — STAB has no mechanical effect on Status moves
- Effect text: "Lower the target's Special Attack by -1 CS."

Confide is a mild SpAtk debuff, lowering the target's Special Attack by just one combat stage. The Social keyword means it requires verbal/visual contact. At-Will frequency allows repeated use to stack the debuff over multiple turns. AC 2 makes it very reliable. Compared to [[captivate-move-in-combat]] which lowers SpAtk by -2 CS but at EOT frequency, Confide trades potency for spammability.
