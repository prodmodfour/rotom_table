Arcane Storm appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Arcane Storm NORMAL DB 6 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical (the CSV source marks this as Special — the Physical display is a seed parsing artifact from the multiline effect field), DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, Blast 3
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "All targets of Arcane Storm are Slowed and Vulnerable for 1 Full Round."

Arcane Storm is a trainer Special weapon move with Scene x2 frequency targeting a large Blast 3 area at Weapon Range. The Slowed and Vulnerable conditions on all targets must be applied manually by the GM. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Ranged Weapons only" but this limitation text is truncated from the displayed effect.
