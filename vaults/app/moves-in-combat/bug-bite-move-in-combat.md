Bug Bite appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Bug-type) button showing "Bug Bite Bug DB 6 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "If the target has a stored Digestion/Food Buff or has traded in a Digestion/Food Buff this Scene, the user may gain the effects of the Digestion/Food Buff. This does no count towards the Usual limit on the user's Digestion/Food Buffs."

Bug Bite is a melee Physical attack that interacts with the Digestion/Food Buff system. The effect text contains a typo ("no" instead of "not") carried from the [[movedata-reference-table]]. At-Will frequency and DB 6 make it a serviceable main attack. The food-stealing mechanic is highly situational, only relevant against targets that have consumed berries or similar items.
