Blast Burn appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red (Fire-type) button showing "Blast Burn Fire DB 15 Daily x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 15, AC: 4, Range: Close Blast 3, Smite, Exhaust
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

The range field shows three keywords: Close Blast 3 (a cone-shaped AoE originating from the user covering 3 squares), Smite (additional accuracy penalty for targets who haven't acted yet in the round), and Exhaust (the user cannot act on the following turn after using this move). Daily x2 frequency means it can be used twice per day. DB 15 is the highest damage base observed so far among the moves tested.

Both Target Dummy and Move Tester appear as valid targets despite Close Blast 3 being an AoE — the target selection still lists individual combatants rather than showing a blast template.
