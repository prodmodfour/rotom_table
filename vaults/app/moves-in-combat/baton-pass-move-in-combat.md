Baton Pass appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Baton Pass Normal At-Will".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- No AC, DB, or attack stat fields
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "The user is replaced with another Pokémon from their trainer's roster. All Combat Stage, Coats, and [Stratagems] on Baton Pass' user are transferred to the replacement. Baton Pass may be used to switch even if the user is Trapped."

Baton Pass is a switching move that transfers accumulated combat stages and other buffs to the replacement Pokemon. It bypasses Trapping effects that would normally prevent switching. The switching and transfer mechanics are described in the effect text and handled manually by the GM. At-Will frequency.
