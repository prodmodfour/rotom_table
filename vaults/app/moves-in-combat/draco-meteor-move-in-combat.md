Draco Meteor appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Draco Meteor Dragon DB 13 Scene AC 4". No STAB badge appears since the user is Normal-type.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 13, AC: 4
- Range: 8, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Lower the user's Special Attack by -2 CS after damage."

Draco Meteor is a Ranged Blast 3 move with Smite keyword, making it an area-of-effect attack that also hits harder against certain targets. DB 13 is extremely high for a Special move, but AC 4 is notably poor and the -2 Special Attack combat stage penalty after use severely limits follow-up Special damage. Scene frequency restricts it to one use per scene. The combination of high power, area coverage, and heavy self-penalty positions Draco Meteor as a decisive opening salvo rather than a sustained damage option.
