Chloroblast appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Chloroblast Grass DB 12 Daily AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 12, AC: 3, Range: Line 8, Smite, Spirit Surge, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The user's Hit Points are reduced by 50% of their full Hit Point value. This Hit Point loss cannot be prevented or reduced in any way."

Chloroblast is a high-risk, high-reward Grass Special attack. DB 12 is very high for a single move, but the user loses half their maximum HP as an unavoidable cost. The Line 8 range means it fires in a straight line hitting everything along 8 meters, with Smite and Spirit Surge keywords. Daily frequency means it can only be used once per day, reinforcing its role as a desperation nuke. AC 3 gives it slightly lower accuracy than standard moves.
