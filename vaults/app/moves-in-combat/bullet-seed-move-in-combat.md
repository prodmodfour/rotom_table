Bullet Seed appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Bullet Seed Grass DB 3 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 3, AC: 4, Range: 6, 1 Target, Five Strike, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

Bullet Seed has the Five Strike keyword, meaning it hits the target multiple times (up to five) in a single attack. Each hit uses DB 3 independently, so total damage scales with the number of hits. AC 4 is notably lower accuracy than most observed moves (typically AC 2), reflecting the multi-hit nature. The 6-meter range gives it comfortable distance. This is the first observed Five Strike move, where the keyword appears inline in the range field alongside the range and target count.
