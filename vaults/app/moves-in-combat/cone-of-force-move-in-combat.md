Cone of Force appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Cone of Force NORMAL DB 6 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical (the CSV source marks this as Special — the Physical display is a seed parsing artifact from the multiline effect field), DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: Cone 2, Push
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Cone of Force Pushes all targets 2 meters, and lowers their Evasion by -2 for 1 full round.."

Cone of Force is a trainer Special weapon move with the Push keyword in its range. The 2-meter Push and Evasion debuff must be applied manually by the GM. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Melee Weapons Only" but this is truncated from the displayed effect. The double period in the effect text ("round..") is present in the source data.
