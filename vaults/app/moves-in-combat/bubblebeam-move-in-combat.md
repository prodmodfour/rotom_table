Bubblebeam appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Bubblebeam Water DB 8 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, AC: 2, Range: 4, 1 Target, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Bubblebeam lowers the target's Speed by -1 CS on 18+."

Bubblebeam is a solid At-Will Special attack with 4-meter range. DB 8 makes it a reliable main attack option, and the -1 Speed CS on 18+ is a modest bonus effect. Compared to [[bubble-move-in-combat]], Bubblebeam doubles the DB (4→8) but loses the AoE (Burst 1→single target) and has a stricter trigger threshold (16+→18+). This is a direct upgrade path from Bubble for single-target damage.
