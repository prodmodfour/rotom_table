Assurance appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark grey (Dark-type) button showing "Assurance DARK DB 6 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "If Assurance's target has already been damaged by a Move on the same round Assurance is being used, Assurance has a Damage Base of 12 (3d12+10 / 30) instead. This effect may trigger only once per Scene per Target."

The conditional DB doubling (6 → 12) is described in the effect text. The app uses the base DB 6 for the damage dice — the GM must manually recalculate if the boost condition applies.
