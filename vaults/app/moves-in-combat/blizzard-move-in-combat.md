Blizzard appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a light blue (Ice-type) button showing "Blizzard Ice DB 11 Scene x2 AC 7".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 11, AC: 7, Range: 4, Ranged Blast 2, Smite
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Blizzard Freezes all legal target on 15+. If the target is in Hailing Weather, Blizzard cannot miss."

Blizzard has the highest AC observed so far (7), making it difficult to land. The range combines a 4-meter distance with Ranged Blast 2 and Smite. Scene x2 frequency allows two uses per scene. The effect has a weather interaction — Blizzard cannot miss during Hail, which is the first observed move with a weather-dependent accuracy override. The Freeze condition on 15+ applies to "all legal target" (the effect text has a minor grammar issue with singular "target" for an AoE move).
