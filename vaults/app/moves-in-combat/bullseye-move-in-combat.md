Bullseye appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Bullseye NORMAL DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Bullseye is a Critical Hit on 16+."

Bullseye is a trainer Physical weapon move that becomes a Critical Hit on a roll of 16+ (normally crits only on NAT 20). The critical hit threshold expansion must be tracked manually by the GM. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Ranged Weapons Only" but this limitation is truncated from the displayed effect.
