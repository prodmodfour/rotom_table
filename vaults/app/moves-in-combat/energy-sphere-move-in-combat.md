Energy Sphere appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Energy Sphere NORMAL DB 4 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4 → 6 (with STAB), AC: 3, Range: Burst 1
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "You gain +1 Special Defense Combat Stage on 19+."

Energy Sphere is a trainer Special weapon move that targets a Burst 1 area centered on the user. The +1 Special Defense Combat Stage buff on a high roll (19+) applies to the user and must be tracked manually by the GM.
