Dirty Trick appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Dirty Trick", a "Standard" action-type badge, an "AC 2" badge, and the description "Hinder, Blind, or Low Blow (once per Scene per target)". Its icon is `eye-slash.svg`.

Clicking Dirty Trick fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]] — there is no sub-menu to choose between [[hinder-maneuver-in-combat]], [[blind-maneuver-in-combat]], or [[low-blow-maneuver-in-combat]]. The GM must decide which sub-trick to apply and resolve the opposed check manually.

Dirty Trick carries the `maneuver_other` AoO trigger flag in the [[combat-maneuver-constants]], meaning it provokes an [[attack-of-opportunity-in-combat]] from adjacent foes when used against a non-adjacent target.
