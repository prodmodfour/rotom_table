Drum Beating appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Drum Beating Grass DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: 4, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The target's Speed is lowered by 1 CS."

Drum Beating is a Physical move with a guaranteed Speed debuff — unlike many secondary effects that require a high roll (15+ or 17+), the -1 Speed CS applies every time the move hits. Range 4 gives it modest reach beyond melee, which is unusual for a Physical attack. DB 8 and AC 2 provide solid damage and accuracy. EOT frequency allows regular use. The guaranteed Speed reduction makes Drum Beating valuable for slowing fast opponents, and the effect stacks with repeated use.
