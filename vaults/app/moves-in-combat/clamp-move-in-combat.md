Clamp appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Clamp Water Static". The button is disabled because Static-frequency moves cannot be actively selected for use — they provide passive effects.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] is not accessible for Clamp because the disabled button prevents opening it. The move's details from the move list button show:
- Class: Status, Frequency: Static, Range: --
- Effect: "The user gains a +1 Bonus to Accuracy Rolls made to initiate Grapple Maneuvers, and +2 to Skill Checks made to initiate Grapple Maneuvers or gain Dominance. Whenever the user gains Dominance in a Grapple, the target of the Grapple loses a Tick of Hit Points."

Clamp is a passive grapple enhancer. It improves both the accuracy and skill checks for initiating grapples, and adds HP drain when the user gains dominance. The Static frequency means it is always in effect as long as the Pokemon knows the move. The disabled state in the UI correctly prevents attempting to "use" a passive ability.
