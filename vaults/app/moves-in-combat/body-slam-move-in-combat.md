Body Slam appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Body Slam Normal DB 9 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9 → 11 (STAB boost), AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "Body Slam Paralyzes the target on 15+."

The STAB boost raises DB from 9 to 11. Scene x2 frequency allows two uses per scene. Body Slam's Paralysis on 15+ is a strong status effect on a reasonably accurate move (AC 2). This is a straightforward Physical melee attack with a conditional status effect.
