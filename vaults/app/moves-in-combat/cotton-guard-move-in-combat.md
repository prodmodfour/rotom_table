Cotton Guard appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Cotton Guard Grass Scene". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Defense 3 Combat Stages."

Cotton Guard provides an enormous +3 Defense boost in a single action, making it the most dramatic physical Defense increase among observed moves. By comparison, [[acid-armor-move-in-combat]] and [[barrier-move-in-combat]] raise Defense by 2 Combat Stages, and [[cosmic-power-move-in-combat]] raises it by only 1. The trade-off is Scene frequency — Cotton Guard can only be used once per encounter, while the others have EOT frequency. This makes it a one-shot defensive spike rather than a gradual buildup.
