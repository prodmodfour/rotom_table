Bleed! appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Bleed! NORMAL DB 9 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9 → 11 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "The target loses a Tick of Hit Points at the start of their next three turns."

Bleed! is a trainer Physical weapon move with the highest DB among all trainer weapon moves (DB 9). In addition to heavy direct damage, it inflicts a three-turn damage-over-time effect (Tick loss each turn). The ongoing Tick loss must be tracked manually by the GM.
