Anchor Shot appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Steel-type) button showing "Anchor Shot STEEL DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "The Target is Trapped for 2 rounds. Anchor Shot may only be used from the user's Anchor Token."

The Trapped condition and the Anchor Token restriction are described in the effect text but not enforced by the app. The GM must manually apply the Trapped status via the [[encounter-status-conditions-modal]] and track the 2-round duration.
