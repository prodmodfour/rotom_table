Captivate appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a gray (Normal-type) button showing "Captivate Normal EOT AC 2". A STAB badge appears on the target panel header.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2, Range: Cone 2, Friendly, Social
- Effect text: "Captivate lowers the target's Special Attack by -2 CS. Captivate may not affect something that is the same gender as the user or something that is genderless."

Captivate is a Status move that requires an accuracy check (AC 2), unlike most self-targeting Status moves. The Cone 2 AoE can hit multiple targets, and the -2 SpAtk debuff is a strong debuff. The Friendly keyword means it can target allies, and the Social keyword indicates it involves social interaction. The gender restriction is a unique constraint — the target must be a different gender from the user and not genderless.
