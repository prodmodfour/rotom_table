Confuse Ray appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark purple (Ghost-type) button showing "Confuse Ray Ghost Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target
- Effect text: "The target is Confused."

Confuse Ray is a dedicated confusion-inflicting move. Unlike moves that have confusion as a secondary effect on a roll threshold, Confuse Ray guarantees the Confused condition on a successful hit. AC 2 makes it very reliable. The 6-meter range provides safe distance. Scene x2 frequency limits it to two uses per encounter, preventing it from being overly oppressive. Ghost typing means Normal-type targets are immune.
