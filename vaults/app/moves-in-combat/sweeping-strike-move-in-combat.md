Sweeping Strike appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Sweeping Strike NORMAL DB 9 Scene x2 AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9 → 11 (with STAB), AC: 3, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "You may attempt a Trip Maneuver against the target as a free action."

Sweeping Strike is a trainer Physical weapon move that combines high damage (DB 9) with a free [[trip-maneuver-in-combat]] attempt. The free Trip Maneuver still requires its own accuracy check and opposed roll, all resolved manually by the GM. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Short-Range Weapons or Weapons with the Reach Quality Only" but this is truncated from the displayed effect.
