Dragon Dance appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Dance Dragon EOT". No DB or AC is displayed, consistent with Status moves.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status
- Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Attack and Speed by +1 CS."

Because the range is "Self", only the user is a valid target. The opposing combatant appears greyed out with "Out of range". Dragon Dance is one of the most powerful setup moves, boosting both Attack and Speed simultaneously. EOT frequency allows use every other turn, enabling rapid stacking. Like [[agility-move-in-combat]], the combat stage changes are described in text but must be applied manually by the GM via the [[encounter-combat-stages-modal]].
