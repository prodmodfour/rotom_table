Bubble appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Bubble Water DB 4 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: Burst 1, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Bubble lowers the target's Speed by -1 CS on 16+.."

Bubble is a low-power AoE with a speed-lowering secondary effect. Burst 1 range hits all adjacent targets, and the -1 Speed CS trigger on 16+ provides a modest chance of slowing enemies. At-Will frequency means it can be spammed, but DB 4 limits its damage output. The effect text has a double period at the end, suggesting a data formatting artifact in the [[movedata-reference-table]].
