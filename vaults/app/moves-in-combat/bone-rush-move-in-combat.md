Bone Rush appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Ground-type) button showing "Bone Rush Ground DB 3 EOT AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 3, AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Five Strike
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

The Five Strike keyword in the range indicates the move hits up to five times, with each hit using DB 3. This is the first observed move with Five Strike — the total potential damage is significantly higher than the base DB suggests (up to 5 × DB 3 worth of damage rolls). The multi-hit mechanic is tracked manually by the GM. EOT frequency limits use to once per round.
