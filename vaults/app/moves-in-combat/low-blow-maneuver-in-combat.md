Low Blow is one of three sub-options of the [[dirty-trick-maneuver-in-combat]]. It does not have its own button in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid — the GM selects "Dirty Trick" and decides to apply Low Blow manually.

Low Blow exists as a separate entry in the moves seed data (moves.csv, Maneuver section): Standard action, AC 2, Melee 1 Target. On a successful opposed Acrobatics check, the target is Vulnerable and has their Initiative set to 0 until the end of the user's next turn. Each Dirty Trick variant can only be used once per Scene per target.

The app does not distinguish between the three Dirty Trick variants in its action logging or UI.
