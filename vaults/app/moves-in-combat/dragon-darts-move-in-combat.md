Dragon Darts appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Darts Dragon DB 5 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5, AC: 2
- Range: 6, 1 Target, Double Strike; or 6, 2 Targets
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Dragon Darts has a unique dual-mode range line: "6, 1 Target, Double Strike; or 6, 2 Targets". This means the user can either hit one target twice (Double Strike) or split the hits between two different targets. The semicolon-separated range format is displayed verbatim in the target panel — the UI does not offer a toggle between the two modes. DB 5 per hit is modest, but the flexibility to concentrate damage or spread it across two enemies is tactically valuable. EOT frequency and AC 2 make it a reliable mid-power option.
