Double Team appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Double Team Normal Scene". No DB or AC is displayed, consistent with Status moves. A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge in the target panel since the user is Normal-type.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status
- Range: Self, Illusion, Coat
- Effect text: "The user gains 3 activations of Double Team. The user may either activate Double Team when being targeted by an attack to increase their Evasion by +2 against that attack or when making an attack to increase their Accuracy by +2 for that attack."

Because the range includes "Self", only the user is a valid target. The opposing combatant appears greyed out with "Out of range". Double Team's range also carries the Illusion and Coat keywords — these are displayed in the target panel but have no visible mechanical enforcement in the UI. The Scene frequency allows one use per encounter scene. The effect creates a versatile resource: the three activations can be spent defensively (+2 Evasion) or offensively (+2 Accuracy), offering tactical flexibility that most self-buff moves lack.
