Double Hit appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Double Hit Normal DB 4 EOT AC 3". A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge in the target panel.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4 → 6, AC: 3
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Double Hit features the Double Strike keyword in its range, indicating it hits the target twice in a single action. The base DB 4 is boosted to 6 by STAB (shown as "4 → 6" in the panel). Despite the low per-hit DB, the Double Strike property effectively doubles the total damage output and doubles the chance to trigger critical hits. No secondary effect text is listed. AC 3 makes each strike slightly less accurate than the standard AC 2. EOT frequency allows one use per round.
