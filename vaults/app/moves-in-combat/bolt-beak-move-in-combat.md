Bolt Beak appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a yellow (Electric-type) button showing "Bolt Beak Electric DB 9 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Against targets with a lower initiative that have not yet acted this round, Bolt Beak deals +10 damage."

Bolt Beak's effect rewards acting before the target — if the target has lower initiative and hasn't acted yet, the move deals +10 flat damage on top of the normal calculation. This initiative-based bonus is tracked manually by the GM using the encounter's initiative order. The +10 bonus is significant and incentivizes high-speed Pokemon to use this move early in the round.
