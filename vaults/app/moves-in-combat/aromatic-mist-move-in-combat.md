Aromatic Mist appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Aromatic Mist FAIRY EOT".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Burst 1
- Effect text: "Raise the Special Defense of all allied legal targets by +1 CS."

The Burst 1 AoE affects all allies in range. The +1 SP.DEF CS effect is not automated. Unlike [[aromatherapy-move-in-combat]] which also uses Burst 1, this move buffs rather than heals.
