Barb Barrage appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Barb Barrage Poison DB 2 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 2, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target, Five Strike
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "The target is Poisoned on a 17+. Once a Scene, if Barb Barrage's target has a Status Condition, you may choose to not roll for Five Strike. Instead, treat Barb Barrage as though it automatically rolled an 8 for Five Strike."

Barb Barrage is a Five Strike move with a low base DB of 2 that hits multiple times. The Five Strike keyword means the user rolls 1d8 to determine how many hits land (similar to [[arm-thrust-move-in-combat]]). The conditional automatic max roll against status-afflicted targets is a once-per-Scene bonus described in the effect text. EOT frequency.
