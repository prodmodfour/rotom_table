Dragon Pulse appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Pulse Dragon DB 9 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 9, AC: 2
- Range: 8, 1 Target, Aura
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Dragon Pulse is the Special counterpart to [[dragon-hammer-move-in-combat]], sharing DB 9, AC 2, and EOT frequency but operating at range 8 instead of Melee. The Aura keyword in the range line indicates the move bypasses certain defensive abilities. No secondary effect. DB 9 at range 8 with AC 2 makes Dragon Pulse a reliable workhorse for Special attackers, analogous to what [[dragon-claw-move-in-combat]] is for Physical attackers but with better range and slightly restricted frequency.
