Aura Sphere appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink/red (Fighting-type) button showing "Aura Sphere Fighting DB 8 EOT". No AC is displayed because the move auto-hits.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, Range: 8, 1 Target, Aura
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- No AC field in the info card
- Effect text: "Aura Sphere cannot miss."

Because Aura Sphere has no AC value (null in the data), the target panel skips the accuracy roll phase entirely, showing the damage section (DB 8: 2d8+10) and "Roll Damage" button immediately. The "Aura" keyword in the range string indicates the move ignores line-of-sight restrictions. EOT frequency.
