Push appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Push", a "Standard" action-type badge, an "AC 4" badge, and the description "Push target 1m away (opposed Combat/Athletics)". Its icon is `arrow-fat-right.svg`.

Clicking Push fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]] — there is no target-selection step or damage panel. The GM resolves the opposed check and movement manually.

Push carries the `maneuver_other` AoO trigger flag in the [[combat-maneuver-constants]], meaning it provokes an [[attack-of-opportunity-in-combat]] from adjacent foes when used against a non-adjacent target.
