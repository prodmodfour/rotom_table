Charm appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Charm Fairy EOT AC 2". No DB value is displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target, Social
- Effect text: "Lower the target's Attack by -2 CS."

Charm is a debuff move that lowers the target's Attack by two combat stages at once, making it one of the stronger single-stat debuffs available. The Social keyword means it requires verbal/visual contact rather than physical. EOT frequency limits repeated stacking but a single use already inflicts a significant penalty. The 6-meter range allows safe use from a distance without needing to enter melee.
