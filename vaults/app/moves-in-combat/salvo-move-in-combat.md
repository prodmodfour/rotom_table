Salvo appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Salvo NORMAL DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, Blast 2
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Limitation: Ranged Weapons Only"

Salvo is a trainer Physical weapon move that targets a Blast 2 area at Weapon Range. Its only effect text is the weapon limitation — it has no additional mechanical effects beyond area damage.
