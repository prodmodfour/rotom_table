Acid appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Acid POISON DB 4 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: Cone 2
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Acid lowers the target's Special Defense by -1 Combat Stage on 18+."

Acid has an AoE range (Cone 2), and the target list allows selecting multiple targets. The secondary effect (SP.DEF -1 CS on a roll of 18+) is displayed as text but not automatically applied — the GM checks the accuracy roll and manually adjusts combat stages if the roll meets the threshold.
