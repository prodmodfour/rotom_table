Destiny Bond appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Ghost-type) button showing "Destiny Bond Ghost EOT". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Burst 10, Friendly
- Effect text: "All enemy targets in the burst become Bound to the user until the end of your next turn. If a Bound target causes the user to Faint through a Damaging Attack, the Bound target immediately faints after their attack is resolved."

Destiny Bond is a deterrence move with an enormous Burst 10 Friendly range, ensuring it covers most or all enemies on the field. The Bound status creates a mutual-destruction threat: if any Bound enemy causes the user to faint via a damaging attack, that enemy also faints. This makes it a powerful tool for low-HP combatants who are likely to be knocked out, threatening to take an attacker down with them. The "Friendly" keyword means it targets enemies while avoiding allies. EOT frequency allows reapplication each round to maintain pressure. The binding lasts only until the end of the user's next turn, so opponents can wait it out.
