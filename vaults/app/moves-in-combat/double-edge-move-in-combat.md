Double-Edge appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Double-Edge Normal DB 12 Scene x2 AC 2". A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge in the target panel since the user is Normal-type.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 12 → 14, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Recoil 1/3
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

The DB display shows "12 → 14", reflecting the STAB boost from 12 to 14. Double-Edge carries both Dash and Recoil 1/3 keywords in its range line. Dash allows the user to move before attacking, while Recoil 1/3 means the user takes damage equal to a third of the damage dealt. Scene x2 frequency limits it to two uses per scene. AC 2 is highly accurate. DB 12 (14 with STAB) makes it one of the hardest-hitting Normal-type physical moves, balanced by the recoil cost.
