Dragon Claw appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Claw Dragon DB 8 At-Will AC 2". No STAB badge appears since the user is Normal-type.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Dragon Claw is a straightforward Dragon-type physical attack with no secondary effect. DB 8 with AC 2 and At-Will frequency makes it a reliable workhorse move — usable every turn with good accuracy and solid damage. Melee range with 1 Target is the simplest targeting profile. The absence of any keyword (no Dash, Push, or multi-strike) or secondary effect keeps it clean and predictable. Dragon Claw fills the same role in the Dragon type that moves like Crunch and Brick Break fill in their respective types: a dependable At-Will attacker.
