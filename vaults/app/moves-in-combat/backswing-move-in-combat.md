Backswing appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Backswing NORMAL DB 7 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7 → 9 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: Melee, 2 Targets
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Limitation: Large Melee Weapons Only"

Backswing is a trainer Physical weapon move that hits two targets in Melee range. It has the highest DB among EOT-frequency weapon moves (DB 7). The only effect text shown is the weapon limitation — Backswing has no additional mechanical effects beyond its multi-target damage.
