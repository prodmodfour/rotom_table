Take Aim appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Take Aim NORMAL EOT" — with no DB or AC badges (it is a Status move).

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- No DB or AC fields shown
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Accuracy by +1. If the user performs an Weapon Move on their next turn that deals damage, add its Damage Dice Roll an extra time to the damage."

Take Aim is the only trainer Status weapon move. It is self-targeting and has no accuracy check. The +1 Accuracy buff and the doubled Damage Dice Roll on the next weapon move must both be tracked manually by the GM.
