Bleakwind Storm appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Flying-type) button showing "Bleakwind Storm Flying DB 10 Scene AC 5".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 10, AC: 5, Range: 6, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "The target is Flinched on a 15+ and Frozen on a 19+."

The range combines a 6-meter maximum distance with Ranged Blast 3 (a 3-square blast that originates at a point within 6 meters of the user, unlike Close Blast which originates from the user). The Smite keyword imposes additional accuracy penalties on targets who haven't acted yet. Scene frequency limits it to one use per scene. AC 5 is the highest accuracy check observed so far, making it harder to land.

Bleakwind Storm has dual conditional status effects: Flinch at 15+ and Freeze at 19+, giving it both crowd control and a powerful status condition on high rolls.
