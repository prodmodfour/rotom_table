Brine appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Water-type) button showing "Brine Water DB 7 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 7, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "If the target's Hit Points are under 50%, Brine's Damage Base is increased to Damage Base 13 (4d10+10 / 35)."

Brine is a conditional finisher move. At base DB 7 it is modest, but against targets below half HP it nearly doubles to DB 13, making it one of the highest effective DBs observed. The 6-meter range gives it comfortable distance. Scene x2 frequency limits its use, but the conditional DB spike rewards timing — using it after softening a target with other attacks.
