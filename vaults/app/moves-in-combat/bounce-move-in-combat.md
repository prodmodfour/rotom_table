Bounce appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Flying-type) button showing "Bounce Flying DB 9 Scene x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Full Action
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "The user first Shifts, gaining a +1 Bonus to Movement Speed and to their Jump Capabilities. After the user Shifts, they may attack with Bounce. The target becomes Vulnerable, and is Paralyzed on 16+. Grants High Jump +1"

Bounce has two range keywords not seen in previous observations: Dash (the user Shifts before attacking with enhanced movement) and Full Action (consuming both Standard and Shift actions for the turn). The move combines movement and attack — the user gains +1 to Movement Speed and Jump capabilities during the Shift, then attacks. The target becomes Vulnerable (takes extra damage from subsequent attacks), and Paralysis procs on 16+. Scene x2 frequency.
