Maul appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Maul NORMAL DB 5 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5 → 7 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: 1 Target, Melee
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "The target is Flinched."

Maul is a trainer Physical weapon move that inflicts the Flinch condition on hit. The GM must apply Flinched manually. The CSV also notes "Limitation: Melee Weapons Only" but this limitation is truncated from the displayed effect.
