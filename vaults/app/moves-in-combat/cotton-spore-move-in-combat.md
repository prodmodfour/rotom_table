Cotton Spore appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Cotton Spore Grass EOT AC 2". No DB value is displayed since it is a Status move.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2
- Range: Burst 1, Powder
- Effect text: "All legal targets have their Speed lowered 2 Combat Stages."

Cotton Spore is a potent Speed-lowering move that hits all legal targets in Burst 1 for a -2 Speed drop. The Powder keyword means Grass-type targets and those with Overcoat are immune. Compared to [[bulldoze-move-in-combat]] which lowers Speed by 1 as a side effect of dealing damage, Cotton Spore trades damage for a stronger Speed reduction. The Burst 1 area and "all legal targets" wording means it hits everyone adjacent — including allies unless they are immune — requiring careful positioning.
