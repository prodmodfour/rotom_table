Dragon Rush appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Rush Dragon DB 10 Scene x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 4
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Smite
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The target is Pushed 3 Meters. Dragon Rush Flinches the target on 17+."

Dragon Rush carries three keywords: Dash (move before attacking), Push (forced movement), and Smite (bonus against certain targets). The effect text specifies a 3-meter Push and a Flinch chance on 17+. DB 10 is strong but AC 4 is notably poor, making each use a gamble between high damage with displacement and a complete miss. Scene x2 frequency limits it to two uses per scene. The combination of Dash and Push gives Dragon Rush significant battlefield control potential — the user can close distance and then shove the target away.
