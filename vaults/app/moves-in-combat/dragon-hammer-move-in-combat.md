Dragon Hammer appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Hammer Dragon DB 9 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 9, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target or Line 3
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Dragon Hammer features a dual-mode range: "Melee, 1 Target or Line 3". The user can either attack a single adjacent target or hit in a Line 3 pattern, displayed verbatim in the target panel with no UI toggle. DB 9 with AC 2 and EOT frequency makes it a strong Physical option. The Line 3 mode adds area potential for a melee move, which is unusual — most melee moves are strictly single-target. No secondary effect keeps the move straightforward.
