Riposte appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Riposte NORMAL DB 12 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 12 → 14 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target, Reaction, Trigger
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Trigger: Your Target misses you with a melee Attack."

Riposte has the highest DB of any trainer weapon move (DB 12). It is a Reaction move with a Trigger condition — it can only be used when the target misses the user with a melee attack. The Range line shows "Reaction, Trigger" as keywords. The GM must verify the trigger condition is met before allowing this move. The CSV also notes "Limitations: Melee or Short-Ranged Weapons Only" but this is truncated from the displayed effect.
