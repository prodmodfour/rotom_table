Slice appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Slice NORMAL DB 10 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10 → 12 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: Melee, Pass
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "Limitation: Melee Weapons Only"

Slice is a trainer Physical weapon move with the Pass keyword in its range, meaning the user moves through the target's space when attacking. It has DB 10, making it one of the highest-damage trainer weapon moves. Its only displayed effect is the weapon limitation — it has no additional mechanical effects beyond its high damage and Pass movement.
