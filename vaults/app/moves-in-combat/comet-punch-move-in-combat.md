Comet Punch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Comet Punch Normal DB 2 At-Will AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 2 → 4 (STAB), AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Five Strike, ATK: 80
- A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

Comet Punch is a Five Strike move, meaning it hits multiple times in a single action. Each hit uses the listed DB independently. Despite the low per-hit DB of 2 (4 with STAB), five hits accumulate significant total damage. AC 4 is relatively low, meaning each individual hit is harder to land. The "--" effect text indicates no secondary effect beyond the multi-strike property. At-Will frequency allows unlimited use.
