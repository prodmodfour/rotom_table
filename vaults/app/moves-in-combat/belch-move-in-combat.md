Belch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Belch Poison DB 12 Scene x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 12, AC: 4, Range: Cone 2
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Belch cannot be used if the user has not traded in a Digestion/Food Buff during this Scene."

Belch is a high-power Special AoE move with Cone 2 range, but has a usage prerequisite: the user must have consumed a food item during the Scene. The AC of 4 is higher than most moves, making it less accurate. The Digestion/Food Buff prerequisite is described in effect text and verified manually by the GM. Scene x2 frequency.
