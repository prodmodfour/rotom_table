Branch Poke appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Branch Poke Grass DB 4 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: 2, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

Branch Poke is a simple Physical attack with 2-meter range, placing it between Melee (adjacent) and typical ranged moves. At-Will frequency and AC 2 make it reliable and repeatable. No special effects or keywords. This is a basic Grass-type poke with minimal complexity.
