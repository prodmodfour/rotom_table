Core Enforcer appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Dragon-type) button showing "Core Enforcer Dragon DB 10 Daily x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 10, AC: 2
- Range: 6, Ranged Blast 3, Spirit Surge
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Each Target has its Ability disabled until the end of the encounter. If a target has more than one Ability, you choose one of them to disable."

Core Enforcer is a powerful Dragon-type area attack that also suppresses Abilities for the rest of the encounter. The Ranged Blast 3 hits a wide area from 6 meters away, and Spirit Surge is a keyword modifier. The Ability-disabling effect is permanent for the encounter duration, making it a strategic tool against Ability-dependent foes. Daily x2 frequency means it can only be used twice per day, so the GM must choose carefully when to deploy it. The high DB 10 and SP.ATK 80 make it a serious damage threat on top of its disabling utility.
