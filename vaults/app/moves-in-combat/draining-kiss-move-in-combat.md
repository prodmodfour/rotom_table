Draining Kiss appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Draining Kiss Fairy DB 5 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 5, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The user gains HP equal to half of the damage the user dealt to the target."

Draining Kiss is a Special draining move — the user heals for half the damage dealt. Despite being a Special move, the range is Melee rather than ranged, which is unusual for Special attacks. DB 5 is modest, making the raw damage low, but the healing sustain from EOT frequency (usable every other turn) provides consistent recovery. Compared to [[drain-punch-move-in-combat]], Draining Kiss trades raw power (DB 5 vs 8) and the Aura keyword for more frequent use (EOT vs Scene x2).
