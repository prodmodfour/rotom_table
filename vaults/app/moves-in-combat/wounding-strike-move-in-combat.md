Wounding Strike appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Wounding Strike NORMAL DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6 → 8 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "The target loses a Tick of Hit Points."

Wounding Strike is a trainer Physical weapon move that inflicts an immediate Tick of HP loss on hit, in addition to its regular damage. The Tick loss is applied unconditionally (no roll threshold) and must be tracked manually by the GM.
