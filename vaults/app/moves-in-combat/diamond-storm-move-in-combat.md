Diamond Storm appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Rock-type) button showing "Diamond Storm Rock DB 10 Scene AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 3
- Range: Close Blast 3, Friendly, Smite
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Diamond Storm raises the User's Defense by +1 CS on an Even-Numbered Roll."

Diamond Storm is a powerful area attack with DB 10, making it one of the highest observed damage bases. The Close Blast 3 range with Friendly keyword means it hits a 3-meter area in front of the user while avoiding allies. The Smite keyword is a range modifier not seen on previously observed moves. AC 3 balances the high damage output. Scene frequency restricts it to one use per encounter. The secondary effect — +1 Defense CS on even rolls — gives roughly a 50% chance of a defensive bonus, providing both offensive and defensive value in one action.
