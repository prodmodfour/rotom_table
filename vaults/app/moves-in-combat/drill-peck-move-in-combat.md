Drill Peck appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Flying-type) button showing "Drill Peck Flying DB 8 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Drill Peck is a straightforward Flying-type physical attack with no secondary effect. DB 8, AC 2, and At-Will frequency make it a reliable workhorse — the same stat profile as [[dragon-claw-move-in-combat]] but with the added Dash keyword allowing movement before attacking. This makes Drill Peck strictly better than moves with the same stats but no Dash. Flying-type coverage and Dash combine well for approaching ground-bound targets.
