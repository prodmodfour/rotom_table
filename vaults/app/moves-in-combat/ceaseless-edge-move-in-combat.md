Ceaseless Edge appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark (Dark-type) button showing "Ceaseless Edge Dark DB 7 Scene x2 AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7, AC: 4, Range: Melee, 1 Target, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The target is put in a Vortex. Ceaseless Edge is a Critical Hit on a 19+."

Ceaseless Edge combines two effects: it applies the Vortex condition (trapping and dealing residual damage over time) and has an enhanced critical hit range (19+ instead of the default). AC 4 is notably lower accuracy than most moves (typically AC 2), offsetting its powerful effects. Scene x2 frequency limits its use. The Vortex application makes this both a damage and control move.
