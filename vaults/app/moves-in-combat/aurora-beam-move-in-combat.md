Aurora Beam appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a light blue (Ice-type) button showing "Aurora Beam Ice DB 7 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 7, AC: 2, Range: 6, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "Aurora Beam lowers the target's Attack 1 Combat Stage on 18+. *Grants: Freezer"

The conditional Attack drop on 18+ is described in the effect text and checked manually by the GM against the accuracy roll. The "*Grants: Freezer" note indicates the move teaches the Freezer capability to the user as a permanent unlock. At-Will frequency.
