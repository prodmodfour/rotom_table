Crunch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark (Dark-type) button showing "Crunch Dark DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Crunch lowers the target's Defense by -1 CS on 17+."

Crunch is a solid Physical Dark-type attack with a secondary Defense-lowering effect. The 17+ threshold for the Defense drop gives roughly a 20% chance per hit of softening the target for subsequent Physical attacks. DB 8 with AC 2 provides a good balance of power and accuracy. EOT frequency allows one use per round. The Defense lowering synergizes well with Physical teammates who can exploit the weakened target.
