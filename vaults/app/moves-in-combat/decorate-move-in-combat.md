Decorate appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Decorate Fairy Scene". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- Effect text: "The target gains +2 CS in both Attack and Special Attack."

Decorate grants a massive +2 to both Attack and Special Attack in a single action, making it the most potent observed offensive buff. Unlike [[coaching-move-in-combat]] which raises Attack and Defense by +1 CS each for the user and nearby allies, Decorate focuses entirely on offense with double the Attack boost. The Melee range and 1 Target limitation means it must be used adjacent to the recipient. Scene frequency restricts it to one use per encounter, but the sheer magnitude of the boost makes it a powerful opening play for an offensive partner.
