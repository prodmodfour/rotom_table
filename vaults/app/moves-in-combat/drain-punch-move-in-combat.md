Drain Punch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a red/brown (Fighting-type) button showing "Drain Punch Fighting DB 8 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Aura
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage it dealt to the target."

Drain Punch is a draining move — the user heals for half the damage dealt. The Aura keyword in the range line means it bypasses certain defensive abilities. DB 8 with AC 2 provides solid base damage, and the HP recovery makes it especially valuable for sustaining in prolonged encounters. Scene x2 frequency limits use to twice per scene. The healing is described in the effect text and must be applied manually by the GM.
