Sprint appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Sprint", a "Standard" action-type badge, and the description "+50% Movement Speed this turn". It has no AC badge. Its icon is `person-simple-run.svg`.

Clicking Sprint fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]]. There is no target selection — Sprint is self-targeting. The GM applies the movement speed increase manually.

Sprint does not provoke Attacks of Opportunity (no `provokesAoO` flag in the [[combat-maneuver-constants]]).
