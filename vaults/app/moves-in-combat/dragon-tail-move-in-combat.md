Dragon Tail appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Tail Dragon DB 6 At-Will AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 3
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Push
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The target is Pushed 6 meters minus their Weight Class. The target is also Tripped on 15+."

Dragon Tail's Push distance scales inversely with the target's Weight Class — heavier targets are pushed less. This Weight Class interaction is described in the effect text but not enforced by the UI; the GM must calculate the push distance manually. The Trip chance on 15+ adds a debilitating secondary effect. At-Will frequency and DB 6 position it as a reliable battlefield control tool rather than a primary damage dealer. AC 3 is slightly less accurate than the standard AC 2.
