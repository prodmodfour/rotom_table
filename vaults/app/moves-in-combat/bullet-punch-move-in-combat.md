Bullet Punch appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a gray (Steel-type) button showing "Bullet Punch Steel DB 4 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Priority, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

Bullet Punch has the Priority keyword in its range field, meaning it acts before normal-speed moves in the turn order. This is a key tactical feature despite the low DB 4. At-Will frequency and AC 2 make it reliable for finishing off weakened targets before they can act. The Priority keyword appears inline in the range text alongside "Melee, 1 Target", following the same pattern as other keyword-bearing moves like [[aqua-jet-move-in-combat]].
