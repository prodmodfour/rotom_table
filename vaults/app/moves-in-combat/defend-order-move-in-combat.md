Defend Order appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green-yellow (Bug-type) button showing "Defend Order Bug EOT". No DB or AC values are displayed since it is a Status move with no accuracy check.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Self
- Effect text: "Raise the user's Defense and Special Defense by +1 CS each."

Defend Order mirrors [[cosmic-power-move-in-combat]] exactly — both raise Defense and Special Defense by +1 CS each with Self range and EOT frequency. The only difference is typing: Defend Order is Bug-type while Cosmic Power is Psychic-type, which affects type-based interactions but not the mechanical effect. Both provide balanced defensive setup, trading the higher single-stat boost of [[amnesia-move-in-combat]] (+2 SpDef) or [[cotton-guard-move-in-combat]] (+3 Def) for dual-stat coverage.
