Barrage appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a beige (Normal-type) button showing "Barrage Normal DB 2 At-Will AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 2 → 4, AC: 4, Range: 6, 1 Target, Five Strike
- ATK stat of the attacker
- STAB badge (Normal-type move used by Normal-type Move Tester)
- Effect text: "--" (no effect)

Barrage is a Five Strike move with STAB applied (DB 2 → 4). It has a higher AC of 4 compared to most moves, making it harder to land. The "--" effect text indicates the move has no secondary effects beyond its multi-hit damage. At-Will frequency.
