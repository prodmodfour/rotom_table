Bulldoze appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Ground-type) button showing "Bulldoze Ground DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Burst 1, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "All Legal Targets are lowered 1 Speed Combat Stage."

Bulldoze is a Burst 1 AoE that guarantees a -1 Speed CS debuff on all legal targets (no roll threshold — it always applies). This makes it a reliable speed control tool. DB 6 is modest but the guaranteed Speed reduction on every hit is valuable for turn order manipulation. Compare to [[bubble-move-in-combat]] which only triggers its Speed debuff on 16+.
