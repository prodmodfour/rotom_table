Bug Buzz appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Bug-type) button showing "Bug Buzz Bug DB 9 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 9, AC: 2, Range: Cone 2 or Close Blast 2; Sonic, Smite, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Bug Buzz lowers the Special Defense of all legal targets by -1 CS on 19+."

Bug Buzz has two range keywords (Sonic and Smite) and offers a choice between Cone 2 or Close Blast 2 AoE shapes. This is the first observed move with an explicit choice of AoE shape. The Sonic keyword typically means the move bypasses Substitute, and Smite means it can hit through certain protections. DB 9 with AoE and a SpDef debuff on 19+ makes this a powerful Bug-type option despite Scene x2 frequency.
