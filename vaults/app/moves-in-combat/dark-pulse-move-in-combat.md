Dark Pulse appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a dark (Dark-type) button showing "Dark Pulse Dark DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: 8, 1 Target, Aura
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dark Pulse Flinches the target on 17+."

Dark Pulse is the Special counterpart to [[crunch-move-in-combat]] — same type, same DB 8, same AC 2, same EOT frequency, but Special class instead of Physical. The 17+ Flinch effect disrupts the target's next action rather than lowering Defense. The 8-meter range with Aura keyword makes it a ranged attack, unlike Crunch's Melee requirement. The Aura keyword typically means the move bypasses certain protective effects. This makes Dark Pulse the preferred option for Special attackers or when the target is beyond Melee range.
