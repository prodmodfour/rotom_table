Hinder is one of three sub-options of the [[dirty-trick-maneuver-in-combat]]. It does not have its own button in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid — the GM selects "Dirty Trick" and decides to apply Hinder manually.

Hinder exists as a separate entry in the moves seed data (moves.csv, Maneuver section): Standard action, AC 2, Melee 1 Target. On a successful opposed Athletics check, the target is Slowed and takes a -2 penalty to all Skill Checks for one full round. Each Dirty Trick variant can only be used once per Scene per target.

The app does not distinguish between the three Dirty Trick variants in its action logging or UI.
