Breaking Swipe appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Breaking Swipe Dragon DB 6 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 6, AC: 2, Range: Close Blast 2, ATK: 80
- Effect text: "The target's Attack is lowered 1 CS."

Breaking Swipe is a Close Blast 2 area move, hitting all targets in a cone-shaped area. As a Physical Dragon-type move with EOT frequency, it trades single-target power for multi-target coverage. The guaranteed -1 ATK combat stage debuff makes it a control-oriented option, weakening enemy Physical attackers across an area. DB 6 is moderate but the AoE and debuff compensate.
