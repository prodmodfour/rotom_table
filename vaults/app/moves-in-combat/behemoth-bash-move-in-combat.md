Behemoth Bash appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Steel-type) button showing "Behemoth Bash Steel DB 10 Scene x2 AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 10, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker
- Effect text: "The DB of Behemoth Bash increases by +2 for each positive CS the target has, to a maximum of DB 20."

Behemoth Bash is a counter-buff move that punishes targets with positive combat stages. The conditional DB scaling (+2 per positive CS, max DB 20) is described in effect text and calculated manually by the GM. Scene x2 frequency.
