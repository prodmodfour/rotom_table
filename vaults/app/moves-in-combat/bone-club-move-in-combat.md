Bone Club appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Ground-type) button showing "Bone Club Ground DB 7 At-Will AC 5".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 7, AC: 5, Range: Melee, 1 Target
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "Bone Club Flinches the target on 18+."

Bone Club is a straightforward melee Physical attack. AC 5 is relatively high for a DB 7 move, making it harder to land than many At-Will options. The Flinch on 18+ has a narrow trigger window. At-Will frequency allows unlimited use.
