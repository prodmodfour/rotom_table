Clear Smog appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a purple (Poison-type) button showing "Clear Smog Poison DB 5 Scene x2". No AC value is displayed on the button.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 5, Range: 6, 1 Target, SP.ATK: 80
- No AC field is shown because the move cannot miss
- Effect text: "The target's Combat Stages are reset to their defaults, and all Coats on the target are destroyed. Clear Smog cannot miss."
- A damage section appears immediately showing "Damage (DB 5): 1d8+8" with a "Roll Damage" button, bypassing the normal accuracy check phase

Clear Smog is a guaranteed-hit utility attack that resets all combat stage modifications on the target. The "cannot miss" property is reflected in the UI by omitting the AC field entirely and showing the damage roll section immediately rather than requiring an accuracy check first. DB 5 provides modest damage — the value is in stripping away an opponent's stat boosts. It also destroys Coats (protective layers). Scene x2 allows two uses per encounter.
