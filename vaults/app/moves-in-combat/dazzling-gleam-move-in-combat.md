Dazzling Gleam appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Dazzling Gleam Fairy DB 8 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 8, AC: 2
- Range: Cone 2
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Dazzling Gleam is a Cone 2 area attack with no secondary effect. The Cone range hits multiple targets in a conical area, making it one of the few Fairy-type moves with multi-target potential. DB 8 with AC 2 provides a strong balance of power and accuracy. EOT frequency allows one use per round. The lack of any secondary effect is offset by the area coverage — most single-target moves of similar DB have additional effects.
