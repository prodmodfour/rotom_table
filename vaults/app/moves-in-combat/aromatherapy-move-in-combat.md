Aromatherapy appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a green (Grass-type) button showing "Aromatherapy GRASS Scene".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Burst 1, Healing
- Effect text: "All allies in the burst are cured of one status condition of their choice."

The "Burst 1" range is an AoE centered on the user, and the "Healing" keyword indicates it restores or removes negative effects. The status condition removal is not automated — the GM uses the [[encounter-status-conditions-modal]] to remove conditions manually.
