Baby-Doll Eyes appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a pink (Fairy-type) button showing "Baby-Doll Eyes Fairy EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, AC: 2, Range: 4, 1 Target, Priority, Social
- Effect text: "Lower the target's Attack by -1 CS."

Baby-Doll Eyes is a Status move with both Priority and Social keywords in its range string. Priority means it acts before other moves in the same speed bracket, and Social indicates it is a social-category move. The Attack combat stage reduction is described in effect text and applied manually by the GM. EOT frequency.
