Bonemerang appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Ground-type) button showing "Bonemerang Ground DB 5 EOT AC 3".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 5, AC: 3, Range: 6, 1 Target, Doublestrike
- ATK stat of the attacker displayed (80)
- Effect text: "--" (no additional effect)

The Doublestrike keyword means the move hits twice, each at DB 5. Unlike the Melee-range [[bone-rush-move-in-combat]], Bonemerang has a 6-meter range, making it a ranged Physical attack despite using ATK. The two-hit structure (DB 5 × 2) offers higher total damage than a single DB 9 hit because each hit rolls separately and applies the attacker's ATK stat independently. EOT frequency.
