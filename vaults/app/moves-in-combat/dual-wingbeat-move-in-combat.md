Dual Wingbeat appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue (Flying-type) button showing "Dual Wingbeat Flying DB 4 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4, AC: 2
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Dual Wingbeat is a straightforward Double Strike move with no secondary effect. DB 4 per hit is low, but two hits produce decent cumulative damage and two independent chances for critical hits. AC 2 is highly accurate, and EOT frequency allows regular use. Compared to [[dual-chop-move-in-combat]], Dual Wingbeat has lower per-hit DB (4 vs 5) but better accuracy (AC 2 vs 3) and Flying typing instead of Dragon. The "Double Strike" keyword here is spelled as two words, unlike Dual Chop's "Doublestrike" — a data inconsistency visible in the target panel.
