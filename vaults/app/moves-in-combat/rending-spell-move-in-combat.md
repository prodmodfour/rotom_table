Rending Spell appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan (Normal-type) button showing "Rending Spell NORMAL DB 3 EOT AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 3 → 5 (with STAB), AC: 2, Range: WR, 1 Target
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- Effect text: "The target loses a Tick of Hit Points on 16+."

Rending Spell is a trainer Special weapon move with the lowest AC among the batch 1 moves (AC 2), making it easier to hit. The Tick of HP loss on 16+ is an additional damage-over-time effect the GM must track manually.
