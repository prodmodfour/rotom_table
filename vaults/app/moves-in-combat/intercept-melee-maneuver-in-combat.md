Intercept Melee appears in the [[encounter-combat-maneuvers]] grid as a button showing the name "Intercept Melee", a "Full + Interrupt" action-type badge, and the description "Take melee hit meant for adjacent ally". It has no AC badge. Its icon is `shield.svg`.

Clicking Intercept Melee fires the maneuver action immediately and closes the [[encounter-act-modal]]. The GM resolves the Acrobatics/Athletics check, ally displacement, and damage redirection manually.

Intercept Melee is flagged as an `isInterrupt` maneuver in the [[combat-maneuver-constants]], meaning it can be used outside the combatant's normal turn in response to a trigger.
