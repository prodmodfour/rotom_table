The Capabilities section within the [[gm-character-detail-stats-tab]] shows a "Capabilities" heading followed by derived movement and physical values:

- Power (e.g., "4")
- High Jump (e.g., "0m")
- Long Jump (e.g., "1m")
- Overland (e.g., "5")
- Swimming (e.g., "2")
- Throwing Range (e.g., "6m")
- Weight Class (e.g., "4")

These values are always read-only, even in [[gm-character-detail-edit-mode]], because they are derived from the character's stats and features.
