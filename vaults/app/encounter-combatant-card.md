# Encounter Combatant Card

Each combatant in the [[encounter-participant-sections]] is represented by a card. Human cards show an avatar circle with the character's initial letter; Pokemon cards show the species sprite.

Common elements on every card:

- **Name** and **Level** (e.g. "Ash Lv.30")
- **HP display** — label "HP" followed by current/max (e.g. "7/10"), with a colored bar that visually tracks the ratio
- **Init** value (e.g. "Init: 5")
- [[encounter-combatant-hp-controls]]
- [[encounter-combatant-action-buttons]]

Enemy Pokemon cards additionally show [[encounter-enemy-card-extras]].

During the Resolution or Pokemon Phase, the current combatant's card appears in a "Current Turn" panel with a green border highlight and the phase badge from the [[encounter-header]].
