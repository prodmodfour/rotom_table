Dragon Rage appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a blue/purple (Dragon-type) button showing "Dragon Rage Dragon DB 15 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 15, AC: 2
- Range: 4, 1 Target
- SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "Dragon Rage causes the target to lose 15 HP. Dragon Rage is Special and interacts with other moves and effects as such (Special Evasion may be applied to avoid it, Mirror Coat can reflect it, etc.)."

The target panel displays DB 15, but this is misleading — the effect text clarifies that Dragon Rage deals a flat 15 HP of damage regardless of stats, not DB-based damage. The DB field appears to be populated from the database value rather than reflecting the move's actual damage mechanics. At-Will frequency and AC 2 make it usable every turn with high accuracy. Range 4 is short for a ranged move. The effect text explicitly notes it interacts as a Special move for purposes of evasion and reflection, which is important since fixed-damage moves could otherwise be ambiguous.
