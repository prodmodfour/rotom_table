Charge Beam appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a yellow (Electric-type) button showing "Charge Beam Electric DB 5 At-Will AC 4".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 5, AC: 4, Range: 6, 1 Target, SP.ATK: 80
- Effect text: "If Charge Beam successfully hits a target, roll 1d20. On a roll of 7+, the user's Special Attack is raised by +1 Combat Stage."

Charge Beam has a high probability (70%) of boosting the user's Special Attack on each successful hit. At-Will frequency means repeated use can stack SpAtk combat stages quickly. DB 5 and AC 4 are modest — the value comes from the self-buffing effect rather than raw damage. The 6-meter range gives it comfortable distance. Pairs well with [[charge-move-in-combat]] which doubles the damage dice of the next Electric attack.
