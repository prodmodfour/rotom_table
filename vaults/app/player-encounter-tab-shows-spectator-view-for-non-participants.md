When the selected character is not a combatant in the active encounter, the [[player-view-encounter-tab]] renders a spectator-only view. The encounter header (name, round number, current turn) and the combatant lists (Players and Enemies sections) are visible, along with the map thumbnail and zoom controls.

The "Your Turn" action panel — with Shift, Struggle, Pass Turn buttons and the GM-approval request buttons (Use Item, Switch, Maneuver, Capture, Heal) — does not appear. The player can observe the encounter's progress but cannot take any actions.

When the selected character is a participant and it is their turn, the full action panel appears with available actions and requests.
