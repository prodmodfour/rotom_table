# Player View Pokemon Stats

A section within an expanded [[player-view-pokemon-card]]. Displays the Pokemon's six combat stats in a two-row grid:

- Top row: **HP**, **ATK**, **DEF**
- Bottom row: **SPA**, **SPD**, **SPE**

Each cell shows the stat label above and its numeric value below. Unlike the [[player-view-stats-section]] for trainers, the Pokemon stats show HP as a final calculated value rather than "HP Base."
