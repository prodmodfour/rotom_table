Barrier appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a magenta (Psychic-type) button showing "Barrier Psychic Scene x2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Status, Range: Hazard
- No AC, DB, or attack stat fields
- Effect text describes creating up to 4 segments of blocking terrain, each continuous and adjacent to the user. Each segment is 2 meters tall, 1 meter wide, with 20 HP and 15 Damage Reduction, taking damage as Psychic-typed.
- Note: "(ignores stats & type effectiveness)"

Barrier is the first observed Hazard-range move. The Hazard range means it places a persistent environmental effect on the battlefield rather than targeting a combatant. The psychic-energy wall segments function as blocking terrain with their own HP and DR values. Scene x2 frequency.
