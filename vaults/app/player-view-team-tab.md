# Player View Team Tab

Accessed from the [[player-view-bottom-navigation]]. Displays the selected character's Pokemon team as a vertical list of expandable cards.

Each Pokemon appears as a [[player-view-pokemon-card]] that can be tapped to expand or collapse its details.
