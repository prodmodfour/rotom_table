The Healing tab on the [[gm-character-detail-page]] has two areas: a status summary and healing actions.

The status summary shows:
- Current HP (e.g., "45 / 45")
- Injuries (e.g., "0")
- Rest Today (e.g., "0m / 8h")
- HP per Rest (e.g., "2 HP")
- Injuries Healed Today (e.g., "0 / 3")
- Current AP (e.g., "5")
- Drained AP (e.g., "0")

The healing actions section offers four options:

1. **Rest (30 min)** — heals a fixed amount of HP. Requires fewer than 5 injuries and under 8 hours rest today. Button is disabled when the character is at full HP.
2. **Extended Rest (4-8 hours)** — heals HP over a chosen duration with a spinner to set hours. Each 30 minutes heals 1/16th max HP. Subject to the daily 8-hour cap.
3. **Pokemon Center** — full HP, all status cleared, AP restored. Heals up to 3 injuries per day. Takes 1 hour plus 30 minutes per injury.
4. **New Day** — resets daily healing limits: rest time, injuries healed counter, drained AP.
