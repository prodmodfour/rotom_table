Sandstorm Sear appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a brown (Ground-type) button showing "Sandstorm Sear GROUND DB 10 Scene AC 5".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Special, DB: 10, AC: 5, Range: 6, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
- SP.ATK stat of the attacker displayed (e.g. 80)
- No STAB indicator (the Move Tester is Normal-type, Sandstorm Sear is Ground-type)
- Effect text: "The target is Burned on a 15+"

Sandstorm Sear is the only non-Normal trainer weapon move on the checklist — it is Ground-type and Special class. It has Scene frequency (once per scene, not Scene x2). The Smite keyword means the target uses their Defense stat (not SP.DEF) for damage reduction despite the move being Special. The Burn condition on 15+ must be applied manually by the GM. It has the highest AC among trainer weapon moves (AC 5), making it the hardest to land.
