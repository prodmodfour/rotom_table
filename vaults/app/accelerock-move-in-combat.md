Accelerock appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a tan/olive (Rock-type) button showing "Accelerock ROCK DB 4 At-Will AC 2".

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 4, AC: 2, Range: Melee, 1 Target, Priority
- ATK stat of the attacker (Physical-class moves show ATK instead of SP.ATK)
- Effect text: "--" (no special effect text)

The range field includes "Priority" as part of the range string. Priority is a PTU keyword indicating the move can be used during a Priority action rather than the normal Pokemon Phase. The app displays this keyword but does not enforce priority timing — the GM decides when to allow priority moves.

When a target is selected, the panel shows Speed Evasion (the target's best defensive evasion against Physical moves is compared between Physical Evasion and Speed Evasion, and the higher is used).
