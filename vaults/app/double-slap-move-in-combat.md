Double Slap appears in the [[encounter-act-modal-move-list]] as a grey (Normal-type) button showing "Double Slap Normal DB 2 At-Will AC 4". A "STAB" badge appears next to the type badge in the target panel.

The [[encounter-move-target-panel]] displays:
- Class: Physical, DB: 2 → 4, AC: 4
- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Five Strike
- ATK: 80
- Effect text: "--"

Double Slap features the Five Strike keyword, indicating it hits the target five times in a single action — the highest observed hit count among multi-strike moves. Despite the extremely low base DB 2 (boosted to 4 by STAB), five hits produce meaningful cumulative damage and five independent chances for critical hits. AC 4 is notably poor, meaning each individual strike has a higher miss chance. At-Will frequency allows unlimited use. The contrast between Double Slap's Five Strike and [[double-hit-move-in-combat]]'s Double Strike illustrates the multi-strike spectrum: fewer hits with higher per-hit damage versus more hits with lower per-hit damage.
