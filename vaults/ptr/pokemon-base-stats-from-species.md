Each Pokemon species defines seven base stats in the Pokedex: HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, Speed, and [[stamina-stat|Stamina]]. These are the starting point for all stat calculations. Their sum is the [[base-stat-total]]. Base stats [[base-stats-unmodifiable-by-traits|cannot be modified by traits]].
