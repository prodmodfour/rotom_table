[[Alolan Muk]] is unexpectedly quiet and friendly compared to its Kantonian counterpart — a temperament shift likely resulting from its domesticated origin as a [[alolan-grimer-line-chemical-diet|waste management species]]. However, this docility is conditional: if not fed trash regularly, its hunger compels it to destroy furnishings and surroundings in search of food. It also hunts Trubbish and Garbodor, positioning it as an apex predator within the garbage-ecosystem niche.

This combination of friendliness and compulsive destructiveness makes Alolan Muk a high-maintenance partner that requires consistent access to waste material — a practical constraint for any trainer working with the species.
