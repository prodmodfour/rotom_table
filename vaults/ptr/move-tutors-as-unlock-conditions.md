Move Tutors are a type of [[unlock-conditions|unlock condition]] for moves. Unlike [[tms-as-unlock-conditions|TMs]], Move Tutors are not instantaneous — they require X [[training-session-one-hour|training sessions]] to complete.

A character must have the [[move-tutor-trait]] for the relevant move to serve as the tutor.
