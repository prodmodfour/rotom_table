Upon evolving, a Pokemon's stats are completely rebuilt: take the new form's [[pokemon-base-stats-from-species]], reapply [[natures-raise-one-lower-one]], reapply any [[vitamins-raise-base-stats]], then redistribute all [[stat-points-equal-level-plus-ten]] from scratch. The [[nature-adjusted-base-relations]] constraint still applies.

This is a [[clear-then-reapply-pattern]] — the entire stat block is reconstructed, not patched.
