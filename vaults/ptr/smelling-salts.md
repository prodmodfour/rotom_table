Smelling Salts is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

If the target is Paralyzed, Smelling Salt’s Damage Base is doubled to 14 (4d10+15 / 40), and cures the target of Paralysis.
