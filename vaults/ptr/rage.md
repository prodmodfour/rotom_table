Rage is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 2, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, [[spirit-surge|Spirit Surge]].

The user becomes Enraged. Until the end of the user’s next turn, if the user is Enraged, the user gains +1 Attack Combat Stage whenever they are damaged by an Damaging Move or Attack.
