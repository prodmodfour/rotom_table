Fatigue is a stacking debuff and a [[fatigued-is-its-own-condition-category|condition in its own category]]. For each level of Fatigue:
- −2 to attack rolls
- −2 to Evasions (see [[evasion-from-defensive-stats]])
- −2 [[movement-points|Movement Points]] (floor is 0)

Fatigue is gained from [[zero-energy-causes-fatigue|reaching 0 Energy]] or from [[energy-overdraft|overdrafting Energy]]. It can be recovered by [[take-a-breather-recovers-fatigue|Taking a Breather]] in combat, or fully cured by [[rest-cures-fatigue|any period of rest]].

At 5 levels, the character is [[fatigue-causes-unconsciousness|unconscious]].
