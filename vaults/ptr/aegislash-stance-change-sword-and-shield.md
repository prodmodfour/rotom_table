[[Aegislash]] alternates between Shield Forme and Blade Forme based on its combat intent. In Shield Forme (its default), it holds its ornate shield forward and can block and reduce damage through a combination of steel and spectral force fields. In Blade Forme, the shield is carried in one arm while the blade is exposed, using its full weight and edge to cut through any opponent. The stance change is instantaneous and occurs the moment Aegislash commits to an action -- attacking shifts to Blade Forme, while using King's Shield reverts to Shield Forme. This is a fundamentally different combat model from [[Doublade]]'s dual-sword coordination; Aegislash trades paired offense for the ability to be an impenetrable wall or an overwhelming blade, but never both at once. The [[stance-change]] trait converts this into a stat swap that occurs automatically based on combat intent.
