Snore is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 5, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1, Sonic.

Snore Flinches all legal targets on 15+. Snore may only be used by Sleeping users. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
