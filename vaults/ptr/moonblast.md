Moonblast is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Moonblast lowers the target’s Special Attack by -1 CS on 15+.
