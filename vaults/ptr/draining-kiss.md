Draining Kiss is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 5, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The user gains HP equal to half of the damage the user dealt to the target.
