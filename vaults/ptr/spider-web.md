Spider Web is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 5, 1 Target.

Spider Web cannot miss. The target is Stuck and Trapped. If the target is freed of the Stuck condition, it is freed of Trapped as well.

Spider Web grants the Threaded [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
