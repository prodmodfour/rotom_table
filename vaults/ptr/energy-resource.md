Energy is a combat resource derived from [[stamina-stat]], analogous to how health is derived from HP (see [[pokemon-hp-formula]]). Energy goes up slowly compared to health as Stamina increases — see [[energy-stamina-scaling]] for the mapping.

Energy is spent to use [[move-energy-cost|moves]] and can optionally be spent on [[energy-for-extra-movement|extra movement]]. It regenerates at a [[energy-regain-rate|base rate each turn]].

Running out of Energy causes [[zero-energy-causes-fatigue|fatigue]], and spending beyond zero is possible via [[energy-overdraft]].
