Echoed Voice is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 3, 1 Target, Sonic.

If Echoed Voice was used by any Pokémon or Trainer in the Encounter on the previous round, increase its Damage Base by +4. If Echoed Voice was used by any Pokémon or Trainers during both the previous two rounds, increase its Damage Base by +8.
