Poisoned and Badly Poisoned have lower save DCs than Burning (DC 10/15 vs DC 18), balanced by triggering more frequently in practice. This gives Burning and Poison a different tactical identity: Burning is harder to shake but rarer, while Poison is easier to cure per check but applied and stacked more often.
