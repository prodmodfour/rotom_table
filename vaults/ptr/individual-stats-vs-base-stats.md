Individual stats are the stats that a specific instance of a character has. They differ from [[pokemon-base-stats-from-species|base stats]] because they are modified through various sources. Both Pokemon and [[trainers-are-human-species|trainers]] have individual stats.

The seven individual stats are: HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, Speed, and [[stamina-stat|Stamina]].

Individual stats feed into [[current-stat-formula|current stat calculations]] and [[trainer-hp-formula|Health formulas]].
