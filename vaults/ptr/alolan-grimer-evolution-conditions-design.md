The [[Alolan Grimer]] → [[Alolan Muk]] evolution condition requires consuming 5 unique Poison-type Pokemon (excluding the Grimer and Muk lines). This gates on the [[alolan-grimer-line-chemical-diet|chemical accumulation]] that defines the line's identity — Muk contains over a hundred different kinds of poison, and the reactions between diverse toxins are the source of its vitality.

**Why "unique"** — eating five of the same species doesn't produce chemical diversity. The condition forces the Grimer to seek out varied poison sources, mirroring the lore that Muk's power comes from the *interactions between different toxins*, not the volume of any single one.

**Why Poison-type specifically** — Grimer starts as an Omnivore, but Muk is a Toxivore. The evolution represents the shift from a generalist diet to obligate chemical dependency. Requiring Poison-type prey ensures the Grimer is accumulating the toxic material that triggers the [[alolan-grimer-line-toxin-crystallization|crystallization transformation]], not just eating anything available.

**Why exclude own line** — Cannibalism is attrition, not ecological mastery. [[alolan-muk-docile-but-destructive|Muk hunts Trubbish and Garbodor]] — it's an apex predator within the garbage ecosystem, not a cannibal. The exclusion also prevents degenerate scenarios in play where a trainer feeds Grimer to Grimer.

**Why "consume" not "defeat"** — Grimer's ecological role is eating things. The condition describes what the species naturally does, not a combat milestone. A wild Grimer in an urban waste habitat consumes poison-types as part of its normal feeding — this is dietary behavior, not a fight scene.

**BST calibration** — the jump from 36 to 51 (15 points) is moderate-to-large for a two-stage line. Five unique species is a meaningful gate that takes time and access to varied prey, but it's not as demanding as [[shroomish-evolution-conditions-design|Shroomish's four skill thresholds]] (BST jump of 19). The difficulty is proportional.

**Wild viability** — Alolan Grimer's habitat is Urban, where Trubbish, Garbodor, and other poison-types are common in waste sites. A Grimer feeding in its natural environment will encounter and consume different poison-type species over time. No trainer required — just sustained access to the garbage ecosystem the line was [[alolan-grimer-line-chemical-diet|introduced to inhabit]].
