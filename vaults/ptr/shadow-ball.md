Shadow Ball is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 8, 1 Target.

Shadow Ball lowers the target's Special Defense by -1 CS on 17+.
