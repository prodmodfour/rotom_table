There can only be one Weather Effect in place at a time. New Weather Effects replace old Weather Effects. This makes weather control a contested resource — setting up your preferred weather destroys your opponent's, creating tactical tension around weather moves.

Combined with [[weather-lasts-five-rounds]], this means weather is a temporary, exclusive battlefield modifier that both sides compete over.
