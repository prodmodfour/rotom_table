The Reach [[trait-definition|trait]] extends melee attack range beyond [[melee-range-is-adjacency|standard adjacency]]. Small and Medium Pokemon with Reach can make melee attacks from up to 2 meters away. Large and bigger Pokemon with Reach can attack from up to 3 meters away.

Reach is a [[innate-traits|species trait]] from the Pokedex. The app must check for Reach when validating melee attack range and extend the targeting radius accordingly.
