Noble Roar is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1, Sonic, Friendly, Social.

Noble Roar lowers all legal targets’ Attack and Special Attack by +1 CS.
