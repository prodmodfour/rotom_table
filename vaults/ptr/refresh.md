Refresh is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user is cured of all Poison, Burns, and Paralysis.
