Wild encounters should have motivated triggers, not random occurrences. PTU suggests several: an ongoing fight between Pokemon on the road, Pokemon protecting eggs/children/wounded pack members, Pokemon agitated by an external source (like a radio broadcast riling up wildlife), territorial disputes, or resource competition.

These motivations connect to [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven]] — the first step is always *why* this fight is happening. Motivated encounters also inform [[wild-pokemon-six-dispositions]] (a protective mother is Very Hostile) and [[pokemon-intelligence-scales-with-niche]] (intelligent Pokemon have comprehensible reasons for fighting).
