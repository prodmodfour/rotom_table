A Pokemon or Trainer at 0 HP or lower is Fainted (Knocked Out) — unconscious, unable to use any Actions, [[trait-definition|Traits]], or Features unless specifically stated otherwise.

Recovery from Fainted requires specific items (Revive) or healing moves/features that bring HP to a positive value (like Wish, Heal Pulse). Regular healing items (Potions) can restore HP above 0 but the target remains Fainted for an additional 10 minutes.

When a Pokemon Faints, it is automatically cured of all Persistent and Volatile status conditions.
