When threatened, [[Shroomish]] shakes its body and scatters toxic spores from the top of its head. These spores are potent enough to make trees and weeds wilt, and cause pain throughout the body if inhaled. This is Shroomish's only real defense -- it has no claws, arms, or combat capability, so spore dispersal is its survival strategy. The Effect Spore trait in the redesign directly models this: any physical contact with Shroomish risks exposure to its defensive spore cloud.
