Leech Life [SM] is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.
