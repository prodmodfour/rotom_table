Hyperspace Fury is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 2.

Interrupts may not be triggered against Hyperspace Fury. The user's Defense is lowered by -1 Combat Stage.

As a Daily move, Hyperspace Fury can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
