Rock Climb is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, At-Will frequency, AC 5, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

Rock Climb Confuses the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
