Dragon Ascent is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

The user’s Defense and Special Defense are each lowered by -1 Combat Stage.
