[[no-guard|No Guard]] has a low Pokemon DC (12 Combat) because reckless all-in aggression is a natural instinct — many Pokemon can learn to fight this way on their own. The higher Trainer DC (18) reflects that coaching a Pokemon into deliberately dropping its guard is counterintuitive and requires real tactical understanding.
