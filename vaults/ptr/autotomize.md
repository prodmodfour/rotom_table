Autotomize is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

For the remainder of the Encounter, the user’s Weight Class is one value lower, to a minimum of 1. If the user can, the user’s Speed is raised by +2 Combat Stages.
