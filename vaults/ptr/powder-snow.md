Powder Snow is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Line 4.

Powder Snow Freezes all Legal Targets on 19+. *Grants: Freezer The 19+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
