Water Spout is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 15, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Burst 1*.

For each 10% of HP the user is missing, Water Spout's Damage Base is reduced by -1. Water Spout creates a 1 meter burst, but also affects an area 10 meters tall straight up.

As a Daily move, Water Spout can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
