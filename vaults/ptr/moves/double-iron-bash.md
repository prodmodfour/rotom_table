Double Iron Bash is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike.

Double Iron Bash Flinches the targets on 15+. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
