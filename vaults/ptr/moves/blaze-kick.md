Blaze Kick is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Blaze Kick Burns the target on 19+ and is a Critical Hit on 18+. The 19+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
