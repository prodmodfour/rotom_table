Flip Turn is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

If Flip Turn successfully hits its target, the user deals damage and then immediately is returned to its Poké Ball in the same turn. A New Pokémon may immediately be sent out. Using Flip Turn lets a Trapped user be recalled.
