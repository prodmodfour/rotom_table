Freeze-Dry is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

When calculating Weakness and Resistance for Freeze-Dry, Water-Typed targets calculate damage as if Water was weak to Ice.
