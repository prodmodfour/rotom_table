Strange Steam is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Burst 1.

The target is confused on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
