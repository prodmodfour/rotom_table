Yawn is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 2, 1 Target, Social.

The target falls Asleep at the end of its next turn. Yawn cannot miss.
