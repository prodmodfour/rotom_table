Thunder Shock is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Thunder Shock Paralyzes the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].

Thunder Shock grants the Zapper [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
