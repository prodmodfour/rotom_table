Heal Block is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Until the end of the encounter, the target may not gain HP or Temporary HP from any source. This effect ends if the target is switched out or Takes a Breather.
