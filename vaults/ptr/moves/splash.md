Splash is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, At-Will frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Shift Action - The user may make a single Jump, adding +1 to their Long Jump and High Jump values, and gains +2 Evasion until the end of their next turn.

Splash grants the +1 Long Jump [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
