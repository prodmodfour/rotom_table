Phantom Force is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Set-Up.

Set-Up Effect: The user is removed from the field, and their turn ends. Resolution Effect: Phantom Force’s user appears adjacent to any legal target on the field, ignoring movement [[trait-definition|traits]], and then uses Phantom Force’s attack. Phantom Force cannot be avoided by Moves with the Shield Keyword, the Dodge trait, or similar effects, and Intercepts may not be attempted in response.
