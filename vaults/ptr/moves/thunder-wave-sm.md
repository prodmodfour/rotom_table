Thunder Wave [SM] is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Thunder Wave Paralyzes the target. Pokémon immune to Electric Attacks are immune to Thunder Wave's effects.
