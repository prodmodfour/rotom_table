Magma Storm is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 6, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

The target is put in a Vortex; this effect occurs even if Magma Storm misses its target.
