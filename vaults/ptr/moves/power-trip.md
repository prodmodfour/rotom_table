Power Trip is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 2, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

This Move gains +2 to its Damage Base for every positive Combat Stage held by the user, to a maximum of DB20.
