Psych Up is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

The user's Combat Stages are changed to match the target's Combat Stages. Psych Up cannot miss.
