Snap Trap is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target is trapped in a Vortex. The DC to escape the Vortex is increased by 3.
