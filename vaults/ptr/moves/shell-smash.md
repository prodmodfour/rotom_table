Shell Smash is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Raise the user's Attack, Special Attack, and Speed by +2 CS each. Lower the user's Defense and Special Defense by -1 CS each.
