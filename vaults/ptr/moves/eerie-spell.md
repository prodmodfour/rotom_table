Eerie Spell is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

The user may choose to Disable the target’s last Move used. This effect may only be activated once per Scene.
