Aurora Beam is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Aurora Beam lowers the target's Attack 1 Combat Stage on 18+. *Grants: Freezer The 18+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
