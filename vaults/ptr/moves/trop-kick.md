Trop Kick is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target receives a -5 penalty to damage rolls for 1 round.
