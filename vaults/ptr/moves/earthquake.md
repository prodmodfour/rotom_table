Earthquake is a Ground-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 3, Groundsource.

Earthquake can hit targets that are underground, including those using the Move Dig.

Earthquake grants the Groundshaper [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
