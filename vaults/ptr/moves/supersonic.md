Supersonic is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 6, and a range of 4, 1 Target, Sonic.

The target becomes Confused. On miss, the target suffers a -2 penalty to Accuracy Rolls for one full round.
