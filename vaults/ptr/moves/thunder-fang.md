Thunder Fang is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 3, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Thunder Fang Paralyzes or Flinches on 18-19; flip a coin to determine whether the foe becomes Paralyzed or Flinched. On 20, the foe is both Paralyzed and Flinched.
