Darkest Lariat is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

This Move ignores the target’s positive Defense Combat Stages and all Damage Reduction.
