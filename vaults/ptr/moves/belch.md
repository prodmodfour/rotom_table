Belch is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Cone 2.

Belch cannot be used if the user has not traded in a Digestion/Food Buff during this Scene.
