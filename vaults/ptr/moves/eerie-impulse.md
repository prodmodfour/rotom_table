Eerie Impulse is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Lower the target's Special Attack by -2 CS.

Eerie Impulse grants the Glow. [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
