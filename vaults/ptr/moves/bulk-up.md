Bulk Up is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Raise the user’s Attack and Defense by +1 CS each.
