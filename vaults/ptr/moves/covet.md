Covet is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Covet takes the target’s Held Item or Accessory Slot Item and attaches it to Covet’s user, if the user is not holding anything.
