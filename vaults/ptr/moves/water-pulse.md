Water Pulse is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 8, 1 Target, Aura.

Water Pulse Confuses the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
