Roar of Time is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 15, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Burst 8, Smite, Exhaust.

Roar of Time Slows all legal targets, even if the attack misses.

As a Daily x2 move, Roar of Time can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
