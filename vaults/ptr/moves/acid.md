Acid is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Cone 2.

Acid lowers the target’s Special Defense by -1 Combat Stage on 18+.
