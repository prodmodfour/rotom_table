Horn Drill is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Execute.

Roll 1d100. This roll may not be modified in any way. If you roll X or lower, the target Faints. X is equal to 30 + The User's Level - The Target's Level.

As a Daily move, Horn Drill can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
