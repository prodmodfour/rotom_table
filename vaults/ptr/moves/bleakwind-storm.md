Bleakwind Storm is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 5, and a range of 6, Ranged Blast 3, Smite.

The target is Flinched on a 15+ and Frozen on a 19+.
