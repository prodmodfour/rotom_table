Venoshock is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

If the target is Poisoned, Venoshock has a Damage Base of 13 (4d10+10 / 35) instead.
