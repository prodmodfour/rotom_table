Sunsteel Strike is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Close Blast 2.

This Move ignores any Abilities that would cause it to fail or miss; those Abilities cannot be activated in response to Sunsteel Strike (ex: Dodge, Wonder Guard)
