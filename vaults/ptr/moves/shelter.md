Shelter is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user gains +1 CS in Defense and Special Defense, and +2 Evasion.
