Bleed! is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of WR, 1 Target.

The target loses a Tick of Hit Points at the start of their next three turns.
