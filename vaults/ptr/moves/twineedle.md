Twineedle is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 3, At-Will frequency, AC 3, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Doublestrike.

Twineedle Poisons the target on 18+. The 18+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
