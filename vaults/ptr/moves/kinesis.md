Kinesis is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Trigger, Interrupt.

If the user or an Ally within 6 meters is about to be hit by an attack, the user may use Kinesis as an interrupt. The triggering Accuracy Roll receives a -4 penalty. This may cause Moves to miss.
