Gastro Acid is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4. 1 Target.

The target's Ability is disabled until the end of the encounter. If the target has more than one ability, you choose one of them to disable.
