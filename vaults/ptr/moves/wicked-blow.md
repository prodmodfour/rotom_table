Wicked Blow is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Push.

If Wicked Blow hits, it is a Critical Hit. The target is Pushed 6 meters minus their Weight Class. On a roll of 15+, the target is also Tripped.
