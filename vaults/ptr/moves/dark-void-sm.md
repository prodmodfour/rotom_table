Dark Void [SM] is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 10, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target falls Asleep. Once per Scene, Dark Void may be used as if its range were “Burst 5, Friendly” instead.
