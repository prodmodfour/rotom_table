Psychic is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 5, 1 Target, Push.

The target is Pushed 1 meter in any direction. Psychic lowers the target’s Special Defense 1 Combat Stage on 17+.  Grants Telekinetic.
