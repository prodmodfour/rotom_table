Thrash is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Melee, all adjacent foes, Smite.

After damage is dealt, the user becomes Enraged and Confused.
