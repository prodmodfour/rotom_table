Glare is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target, Social.

Glare Paralyzes the target.
