Ember is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Ember Burns the target on 18+. *Grants: Firestarter The 18+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
