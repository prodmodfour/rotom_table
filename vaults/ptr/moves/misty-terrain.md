Misty Terrain is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field.

The area becomes Misty for 5 turns, While Misty, all Pokemon and Traners standing on the ground ignore the first turn of all Status Afflictions, and Dragon-type attacks targeting or origination from a grounded Pokemon or Trainer take a -10 Penalty to Damage Rolls.

As a Daily x2 move, Misty Terrain can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
