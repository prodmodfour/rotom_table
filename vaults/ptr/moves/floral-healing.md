Floral Healing is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Healing.

The target recovers 1/2 their maximum Hit Points. If the Field is Grassy Terrain, the target instead recovers 2/3 their maximum Hit Points.

As a Daily x2 move, Floral Healing can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
