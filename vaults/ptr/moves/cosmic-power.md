Cosmic Power is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Raise the user’s Defense 1 Combat Stage and raise the user’s Special Defense 1 Combat Stage.
