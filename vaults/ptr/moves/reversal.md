Reversal is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

For each Injury the user has, Reversal's Damage Base is increased by +1.
