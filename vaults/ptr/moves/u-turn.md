U-Turn is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

The user deals damage and then is immediately recalled to its Poké Ball in the same turn. A New Pokémon may immediately be sent out. Using U-Turn lets a Trapped user be recalled.
