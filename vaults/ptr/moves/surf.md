Surf is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Line 6.

As a Shift Action, the user may Move to any open square in Surf ’s area of effect without provoking any Attacks of Opportunity.
