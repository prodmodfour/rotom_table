Hydro Pump is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 11, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Push.

The target is pushed away from the user 3 meters.
