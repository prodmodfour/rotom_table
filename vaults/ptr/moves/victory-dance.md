Victory Dance is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

If the user performs a Fighting Move on their next turn that deals damage, add its Damage Dice Roll an extra time to the damage. Raise the user’s Defense 1 Combat Stage.
