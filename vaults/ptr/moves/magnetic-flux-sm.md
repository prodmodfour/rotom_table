Magnetic Flux [SM] is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Burst 4.

Raise the Defense and Special Defense of all legal targets with the Minus or Plus Abilities by +1 CS.
