Snatch is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Trigger, Interrupt.

If the target uses a Self-Targeting Move, you may use Snatch as an Interrupt. You gain the benefits of the Self-Targeting Move instead of the target.
