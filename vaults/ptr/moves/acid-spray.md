Acid Spray is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Acid Spray lowers the target’s Special Defense by -2 CS.
