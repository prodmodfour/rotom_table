Follow Me is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Burst 5, Social.

Until the end of the user's next turn, all Foes must target the user when using a Move that targets their opponents. This effect ends if the user is Fainted or Switched out.
