Hyper Voice is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Close Blast 3, Sonic, Smite.

All legal targets are pushed back to the squares immediately outside the blast, away from the user.
