Stomping Tantrum is a Ground-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

If the user’s last attack failed or missed, Stomping Tantrum is DB15 instead.
