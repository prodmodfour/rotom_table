Frustration is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of X, See Effect, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Frustration's Damage Base is equal to 9 minus the user's Loyalty Value. Using Frustration may make your Pokémon dislike you.
