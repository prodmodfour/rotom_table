Moongeist Beam is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Line 6.

This Move ignores any Abilities that would cause it to fail or miss; those Abilities cannot be activated in response to Moongeist Beam (ex: Dodge, Wonder Guard)
