Sonic Boom is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 15 Damage, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 6, and a range of 8, 1 Target.

Sonicboom causes the target to lose 15 HP. Sonicboom is Special and interacts with other moves and effects as such (Special Evasion may be applied to avoid it, Mirror Coat can reflect it, etc.)
