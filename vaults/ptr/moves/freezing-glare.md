Freezing Glare is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Freezing Glare Freezes the target on a 19+. Once per Scene, Freezing Glare may instead be used as an Ice-Type Move.
