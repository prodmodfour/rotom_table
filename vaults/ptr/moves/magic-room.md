Magic Room is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field.

The area becomes Useless for 5 rounds. While Useless, Pokémon may not benefit from the effects of any Held Items, and Trainers cannot benefit from any Accessory-Slot equipment. This does not affect consumable or activated items, only Items with Static effects or Triggers.

As a Daily x2 move, Magic Room can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
