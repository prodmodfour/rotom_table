Springtide Storm is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 5, and a range of 6, Ranged Blast 3, Smite.

If the user is not in Therian Forme, the user has each of its stats raised by +1 CS on a 16+. If the user is in Therian Form, the target has each of its stats lowered by -1 CS on a 16+.
