Anchor Shot is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The Target is Trapped for 2 rounds. Anchor Shot may only be used from the user's Anchor Token.
