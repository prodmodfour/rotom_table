Skull Bash is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 13, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Set-Up.

Set-Up Effect: Raise the user's Defense by +1 CS. Resolution Effect: The user may attack with Skull Bash. The target is pushed 3 meters.
