Power Swap is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The user and the target trade Combat Stage values for the Attack Stat, and then for the Special Attack Stat.
