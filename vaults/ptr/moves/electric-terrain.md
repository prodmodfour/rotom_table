Electric Terrain is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field.

The field becomes Electrified for 5 rounds. While the field is Electrified, Pokemon and Trainers touching the ground are immune to Sleep, and Electric-Type attacks used by Pokemon and Trainers touching the ground gain a +10 Bonus to Damage Rolls.

As a Daily x2 move, Electric Terrain can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
