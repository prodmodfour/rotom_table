Future Sight is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 10, 1 Target.

Future Sight does nothing on the turn it is used. At the end of the user's next turn, Future Sight hits, even if the user is no longer on the field. Future Sight cannot miss.
