Night Daze is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Night Daze lowers the target's Accuracy by -1 on 13+.
