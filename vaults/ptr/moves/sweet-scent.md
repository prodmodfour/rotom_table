Sweet Scent is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 2, Friendly.

Targets hit by Sweet Scent gain a -2 Penalty to Evasion. (Total Evasion may not be lowered to a negative value.)

Sweet Scent grants the Alluring [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
