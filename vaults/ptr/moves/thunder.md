Thunder is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 11, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 7, and a range of 12, 1 Target, Smite.

Thunder Paralyzes the target on 15+. If the target is in Sunny Weather, Thunder's Accuracy Check is 11. If the target is in Rainy Weather, Thunder cannot miss. If the target is airborne as a result of Bounce, Fly, or Sky Drop, Thunder cannot miss. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
