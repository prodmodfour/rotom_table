Scorching Sands is a Ground-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 5, 1 Target.

Scorching Sands Burns the target on 15+. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
