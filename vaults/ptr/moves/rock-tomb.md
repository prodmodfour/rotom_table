Rock Tomb is a Rock-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, AC 5, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Rock Tomb lowers the target's Speed by -1 CS.

Rock Tomb grants the Materializer [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
