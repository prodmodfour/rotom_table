Quiver Dance is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Raise the user's Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed by +1 CS each.
