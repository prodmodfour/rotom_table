Spacial Rend is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of 10, 1 Target.

Spacial Rend is a Critical Hit on Even-Numbered Rolls.

As a Daily x2 move, Spacial Rend can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
