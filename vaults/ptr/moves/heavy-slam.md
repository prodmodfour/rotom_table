Heavy Slam is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

For each weight class the user is above the target, increase Heavy Slam's Damage Base by +2.
