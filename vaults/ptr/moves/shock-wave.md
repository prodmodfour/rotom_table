Shock Wave is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Shock Wave cannot miss.

Shock Wave grants the Zapper [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
