Brick Break is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Light Screen and Reflect may not be activated in response to Brick Break.
