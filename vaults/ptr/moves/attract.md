Attract is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 3, 1 Target, Social.

Attract Infatuates the target if its gender is the opposite of the user’s. Attract fails when used by or against Genderless targets.
