Minimize is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user gains +4 Evasion, and the user's size is lowered to Small for the remainder of the encounter.

Minimize grants the Shrinkable [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
