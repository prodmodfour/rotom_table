Endeavor is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a variable Damage Base (see effect), [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

The target loses a Tick of Hit Points for each Injury the user has.
