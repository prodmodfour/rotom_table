Doom Desire is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 14, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 10, 1 Target.

Doom Desire does nothing on the turn it is used. At the end of the user's next turn, Doom Desire hits, even if the user if no longer on the field. Doom Desire cannot miss.
