Assist is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Randomly select another Pokémon on the user’s roster and then randomly select a Move that Pokémon knows. Assist’s user uses that Move immediately.
