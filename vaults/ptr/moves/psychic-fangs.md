Psychic Fangs is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Light Screen, Reflect and Aurora Veil cannot be declared in response to Psychic Fangs.
