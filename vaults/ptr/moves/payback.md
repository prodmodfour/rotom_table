Payback is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 5, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

If the target hit the user with a Damaging Move on the previous turn, Payback has a Damage Base of 10 (3d8+10 / 24) instead.
