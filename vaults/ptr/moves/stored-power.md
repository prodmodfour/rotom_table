Stored Power is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 2, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 10, 1 Target.

For every Combat Stage the user has above 0, add +2 to Stored Power’s Damage Base, up to a maximum of Damage Base 20.
