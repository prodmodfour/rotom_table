Psycho Boost is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 14, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 8, Ranged Blast 3, Smite.

Lower the user's Special Attack by -2 Combat Stages after damage is resolved.
