Razor Shell is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

Razor Shell lowers the target's Defense by -1 CS on an Even-Numbered Roll.
