Bubblebeam is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Bubblebeam lowers the target's Speed by -1 CS on 18+.
