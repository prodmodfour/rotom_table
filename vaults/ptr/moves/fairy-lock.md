Fairy Lock is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Burst 3, Friendly.

All legal targets become Trapped and Slowed while the user remains in the encounter. If the user is switched or knocked out, this effect ends.
