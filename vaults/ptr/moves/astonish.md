Astonish is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 3, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Astonish Flinches the target on 15+. Once per Scene, if the target is unaware of the user's presence, Astonish automatically Flinches. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
