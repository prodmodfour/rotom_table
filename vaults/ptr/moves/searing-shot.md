Searing Shot is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1.

Searing Shot Burns all targets on 15+. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
