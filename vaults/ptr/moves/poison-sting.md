Poison Sting is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 2, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Poison Sting Poisons the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
