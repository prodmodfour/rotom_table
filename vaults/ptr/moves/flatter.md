Flatter is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Social.

Raise the target's Special Attack by +1 CS. Flatter Confuses the target.
