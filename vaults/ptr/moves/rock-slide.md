Rock Slide is a Rock-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 6, Ranged Blast 3.

Rock Slide Flinches all legal targets on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
