Charge is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

If the user performs an Electric Attack on its next turn, add its Damage Dice Roll an extra time to the damage. Raise the user's Special Defense by +1 CS.
