Super Fang is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a variable Damage Base (see effect), [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target loses 1/2 of its current HP.
