Toxic Threads is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

The target is Poisoned and receives -1 Speed Combat Stage. If the target is already Poisoned, they instead lose a Tick of Hit Points and receive -2 Speed Combat Stages.
