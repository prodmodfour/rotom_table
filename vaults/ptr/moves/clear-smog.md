Clear Smog is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 5, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

The target's Combat Stages are reset to their defaults, and all Coats on the target are destroyed. Clear Smog cannot miss.
