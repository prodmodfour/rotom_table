Bolt Strike is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 13, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 5, and a range of 10, 1 Target, Smite.

Bolt Strike Paralyzes the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
