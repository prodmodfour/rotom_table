Brine is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6,  1 Target.

If the target’s Hit Points are under 50%, Brine’s Damage Base is increased to Damage Base 13 (4d10+10 / 35).
