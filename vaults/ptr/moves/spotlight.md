Spotlight is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Priority.

Until the end of the user’s next turn, the target is Blinded and Vulnerable.

As a Daily move, Spotlight can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
