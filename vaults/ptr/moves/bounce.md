Bounce is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Full Action.

The user first Shifts, gaining a +1 Bonus to Movement Speed and to their Jump [[trait-definition|traits]]. After the user Shifts, they may attack with Bounce. The target becomes Vulnerable, and is Paralyzed on 16+. Grants High Jump +1 The 16+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
