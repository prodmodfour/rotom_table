Aeroblast is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Line 6.

Aeroblast is a Critical Hit on an Even-Numbered Roll.

As a Daily move, Aeroblast can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
