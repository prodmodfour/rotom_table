Sucker Punch [SM] is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Trigger, Interrupt.

If an adjacent foe targets the user with a Damaging Attack, Sucker Punch may be used as an Interrupt Move against the triggering foe.
