Outrage is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Melee, All Adjacent Foes, Smite.

Outrage makes the user becomes Enraged and Confused after damage is dealt.
