Oblivion Wing is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The user gains HP equal to Oblivion Wing’s Damage Roll.

As a Daily move, Oblivion Wing can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
