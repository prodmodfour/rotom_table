Fiery Dance is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

If Fiery Dance successfully hits a foe, it raises the user's Special Attack by 1 Combat Stage on Even-Numbered Rolls.
