Defend Order is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

Raise the user's Defense and Special Defense by +1 CS each.
