Leaf Tornado is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 6, Ranged Blast 3.

Small or Medium targets in the central square of the blast are not hit. On 15+, all legal targets have their Accuracy lowered by -1.
