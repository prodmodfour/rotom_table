Bane is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of WR, 1 Target.

The target loses a Tick of Hit Points at the start of their next three turns and suffers a -2 penalty to all Save Checks on those turns.
