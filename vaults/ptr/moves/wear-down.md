Wear Down is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 5, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of WR, 1 Target.

Wear Down lowers the target’s Defense by 1 Combat Stage on Even-Numbered Rolls.
