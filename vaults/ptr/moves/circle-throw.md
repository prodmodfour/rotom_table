Circle Throw is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Push.

The target is Pushed 6 meters minus their Weight Class. The target is also Tripped on 15+.
