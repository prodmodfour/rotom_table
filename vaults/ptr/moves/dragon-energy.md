Dragon Energy is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 15, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Cone 3 or Line 8.

For each 10% of HP the user is missing, Dragon Energy’s Damage Base is reduced by 1.

As a Daily x2 move, Dragon Energy can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
