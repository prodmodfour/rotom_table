Magnetic Flux [SS] is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Burst 4.

Choose +1 or -1. All targets that are Electric-Type or have the Magnetic Capability receive that many Defense and Special Defense Combat Stages.
