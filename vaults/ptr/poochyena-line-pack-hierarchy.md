[[Mightyena]] packs operate under strict hierarchical obedience. Wild packs of roughly ten individuals follow a single leader whose commands are never defied. This instinct is so deeply embedded that it transfers directly to trainer relationships — a Mightyena will always obey a skilled trainer, treating them as pack leader. The hierarchy also explains why [[Poochyena]] has Run Away and Rattled; juveniles without pack protection default to flight.

The pack communicates over long distances through coordinated howling, relaying positional and tactical information between separated members. This combination of absolute obedience and long-range communication enables the line's coordinated hunting style. The Pack Hunt and Pack Mon traits reflect this biology — these are not merely social animals but ones whose survival strategy is built entirely around group discipline.
