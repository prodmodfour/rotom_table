Traits gained through breeding are not immediately active. They follow the same [[unlock-conditions]] system as all other traits. Breeding adds a trait to the Pokemon's pool of unlockable innate traits — it does not bypass the unlock process.

This means a bred Pokemon with a desirable inherited trait still needs to meet that trait's conditions before it becomes active.
