[[Alolan Ninetales]] produces ice crystals from its fur that serve as both shields and projectiles, launched with enough force to shatter rock. When it sleeps, it bathes the surrounding area in powdery snow, creating a sparkling ambient effect. Those who anger it are frozen solid in an instant.

This combat application goes beyond the passive cooling described in [[vulpix-line-ice-crystal-fur]] — Alolan Ninetales weaponizes its ice generation into a formidable offensive and defensive toolkit. The crystal projectiles justify its high Special Attack, while the shielding behavior supports its solid Defense stat.
