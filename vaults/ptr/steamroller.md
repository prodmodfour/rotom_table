Steamroller is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, Pass.

Steamroller Flinches the target on 15+. If the target is Small, Steamroller deals an additional +5 Damage. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
