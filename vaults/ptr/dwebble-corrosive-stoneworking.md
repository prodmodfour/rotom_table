[[Dwebble]] produces a corrosive spray from its mouth that dissolves rock, allowing it to carve cavities in stones for habitation and to repair cracks in its shell. This acid is specialized for mineral dissolution rather than combat — it functions as a biological tool for stoneworking. Dwebble also maintains a mutualistic relationship with Hippowdon: homeless Dwebble relocate to Hippowdon's nasal passages and dislodge stuck stones in exchange for temporary shelter. Competition with Roggenrola and Rolycoly over suitable stones creates natural territorial conflict in rocky habitats.
