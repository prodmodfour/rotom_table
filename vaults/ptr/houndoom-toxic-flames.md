[[Houndoom]]'s gut produces toxins that mix with the fire it breathes, creating uniquely poisonous flames with a foul odor. Burns inflicted by these flames never heal — the pain persists indefinitely, a supernatural quality that elevates Houndoom's fire beyond simple combustion.

This toxic fire is the basis for the Brimstone trait on [[Houndoom]], distinguishing it from other Fire-type Pokemon whose flames merely burn. It also explains why Houndoom is Dark/Fire rather than pure Fire — its flames are not just destructive but malicious, carrying a permanent consequence. The toxin-flame mixture connects thematically to the [[houndoom-hellhound-folklore|hellhound mythology]] surrounding the species.
