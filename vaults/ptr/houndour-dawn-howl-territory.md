Around dawn, [[Houndour]] packs howl in unison to announce territorial boundaries. This ominous chorus is distinct from the [[houndour-pack-hunting-tactics|tactical barking]] used during hunts — howling is a broadcast, not a conversation. Houndour even howls in its sleep, believed to be dreaming of hunting alongside its packmates.

The dawn howl establishes Houndour as a crepuscular-to-nocturnal predator that hunts through the night and stakes its claim at first light. This behavioral pattern supports the Early Bird trait (it is active and alert at dawn) and the Darkvision trait (it operates primarily in darkness).
