Parabolic Charge [SM] is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Cone 2.

The user gains HP equal to half of the total damage the user dealt to all legal targets.
