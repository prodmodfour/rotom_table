Spirit Lance is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 6, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Line 6.

Spirit Lance deals +3 damage to all targets for each target beyond the first that it successfully hits.
