Encounter XP is calculated as: total enemy levels (trainers count as 2× their level) × significance multiplier ÷ number of players gaining XP. Fainted Pokemon can still gain experience from an encounter.

The significance multiplier ranges from x1 to x5 per [[significance-cap-x5]], with additional adjustments from [[difficulty-adjusts-significance]]. This formula feeds into [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]] — the budget works backwards from desired XP reward to determine total enemy levels. Pokemon level up per [[experience-chart-level-thresholds]].
