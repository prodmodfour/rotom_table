Fatigued is a condition in PTR, classified in its own standalone category — separate from Persistent, Volatile, and Other. It has its own rules distinct from those category groupings.

This formalizes [[fatigue-levels|Fatigue]] (previously just a stacking debuff) as a recognized condition within the condition system, while keeping it outside the standard categories because its stacking behavior and mechanics don't fit any of them.
