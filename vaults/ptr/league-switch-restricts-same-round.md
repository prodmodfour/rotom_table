In League Battles, a Pokemon released as part of a Switch cannot be commanded for the rest of that round. Exceptions: the Switch was forced by a move like Roar, or the Switch replaced a Fainted Pokemon.

This restriction adds strategic weight to voluntary switching in League Battles and must be tracked by the app's turn system.
