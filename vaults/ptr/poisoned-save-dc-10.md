Poisoned has a DC 10 save check at the end of the afflicted target's turn. On success, Poisoned is cured.

The tick of damage also moves to end of turn (see [[persistent-tick-timing-end-of-turn]]). All other Poisoned mechanics (1 [[tick-value-one-tenth-max-hp|tick]] of damage, -2 SpDef CS) are unchanged.

The DC is lower than [[burning-save-dc-18|Burning's DC 18]], balanced by Poison triggering more often (see [[poison-identity-through-trigger-frequency]]).
