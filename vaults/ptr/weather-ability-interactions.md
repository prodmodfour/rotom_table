Certain [[trait-definition|traits]] and moves change based on which [[weather-as-game-keyword]] is active. Forecast changes the user's Type to match the weather: Fire in Sunny, Water in Rainy, Ice in Hail, Rock in Sandstorm. Weather Ball similarly changes its move Type to match the active weather.

These interactions mean weather is not just a passive modifier — it enables entirely different tactical identities for Pokemon with weather-reactive traits.
