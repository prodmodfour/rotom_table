Pay Day is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Cone 2.

Pay Day scatters metal coins equal in value to 1d8 times the user's level. If it is a trainer battle, the winner of the battle gets to pick up the coins.

As a Daily move, Pay Day can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
