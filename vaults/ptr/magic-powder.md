Magic Powder is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 5, 1 Target.

The target gains the Psychic Type in addition to its other Types for 5 turns.

As a Daily move, Magic Powder can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
