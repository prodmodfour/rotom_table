A maximum of 3 injuries can be healed per day per individual, across all sources combined — Pokemon Centers, [[natural-injury-healing-24h-timer]], [[bandages-heal-injury-after-full-duration]], [[trainer-ap-drain-heals-injury]], and Features all share this cap.

The app must track injury healing per day and enforce this limit regardless of the source.
