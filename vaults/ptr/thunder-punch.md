Thunder Punch is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Thunder Punch Paralyzes the target on 19+. The 19+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
