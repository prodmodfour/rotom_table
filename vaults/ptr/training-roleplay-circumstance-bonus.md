During a [[training-dual-check-system|training session]], the GM can award [[skill-modifiers-from-traits-or-circumstance|Circumstance Bonuses]] to both the Trainer and Pokemon checks when the player describes clever roleplay or creative ideas that would make the training easier.

GMs should keep these bonuses modest — training should still feel like a meaningful investment. A +1 or +2 for a genuinely good idea is appropriate; larger bonuses risk making training trivially easy and undermining the tension of the [[training-dual-check-system|dual check system]].
