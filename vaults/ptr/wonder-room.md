Wonder Room is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field.

For 5 rounds, the area is considered Wondered. While Wondered, each individual Pokémon's Defense and Special Defense are switched.

As a Daily x2 move, Wonder Room can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
