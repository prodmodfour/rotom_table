Bone Club is a Ground-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 5, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Bone Club Flinches the target on 18+. The 18+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
