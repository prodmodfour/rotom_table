Clanging Scales is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 11, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target or Burst 1, Sonic.

The user adds half their Defense Stat to Clanging Scale's Damage Roll. After the Move is resolved, the user's Defense is lowered by 1 CS.
