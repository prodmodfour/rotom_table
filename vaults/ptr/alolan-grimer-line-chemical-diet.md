[[Alolan Grimer]] was introduced to Alola specifically to consume the excess waste generated by the region's growing population. Over time, the line adapted from consuming sludge to feeding on solid garbage and chemical waste, fundamentally altering its physiology. This dietary shift produced crystallized toxin formations — hard lumps of concentrated poison that protrude from the body like teeth or shards. If these crystals break off, lethal poisons leak out.

[[Alolan Muk]] contains over a hundred different kinds of poison from its varied chemical diet. The reactions between these diverse toxins are the source of its vitality — it literally cannot maintain its energy without a constant supply of toxic material. This chemical dependency parallels how the line was [[corphish-line-invasive-adaptability|introduced to fill an ecological niche]], though the Grimer line was brought deliberately rather than escaping captivity.
