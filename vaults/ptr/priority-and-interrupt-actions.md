Priority and Interrupt actions break normal initiative flow:

- **Priority:** If you haven't acted this turn, declare between turns to act immediately (counts as your turn). Priority (Limited) only allows the priority action itself. Priority (Advanced) works even if you've already acted, at the cost of your next round's turn.
- **Interrupt:** Declared during another combatant's turn, like Priority (Advanced, Limited). Only the interrupt action is taken, not a full turn.

Both require the app to pause normal initiative processing to resolve out-of-order actions, then resume.
