Reflect Type is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Reflect Type changes one of the user’s Types into one Type of your choice that the target has for the rest of the scene.
