[[Vulpix]] is known to feign injury to escape opponents too powerful for it to defeat. Rather than fighting to exhaustion, it plays dead or pretends to be hurt, then slips away when the threat loses interest.

This cunning survival instinct reflects the fox archetype — clever and evasive rather than confrontational. It justifies [[Vulpix]]'s Competitive trait (turning disadvantage into opportunity) and its high Stealth skill. The behavior also suggests a Pokemon that evaluates threats intelligently rather than fighting on instinct alone.
