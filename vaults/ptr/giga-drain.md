Giga Drain is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.
