Gust is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

If the target is airborne as a result of Bounce, Fly, or Sky Drop, Gust can hit them, ignoring Range and has a Damage Base of 8 instead. *Grants: Guster
