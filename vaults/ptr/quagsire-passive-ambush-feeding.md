[[Quagsire]] hunts by leaving its mouth wide open underwater and simply waiting for prey to swim in. Because it barely moves during this process, it expends almost no energy and rarely gets hungry. This passive feeding strategy extends even into sleep — Quagsire keeps its mouth agape while resting. The behavior explains its Unaware trait (it genuinely does not register much of what happens around it) and its sluggish, carefree temperament, which causes it to bump into boulders and boat hulls while swimming without any apparent concern.
