Punishment is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Punishment's Damage Base is raised by +1 for each Combat Stage the target has, to a maximum of DB 12.
