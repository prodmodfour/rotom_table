[[Stunfisk]] developed its electricity-generating organs through a symbiotic relationship with bacteria living in the mud alongside it. This is unusual among Electric-type Pokemon — most generate electricity through internal biological processes, but Stunfisk's electrical capacity is partly external in origin, tied to the microbial environment of coastal mudflats. The bacterial symbiosis explains why Stunfisk is Ground/Electric rather than pure Electric: its electrical nature is inseparable from the substrate it lives in. It also explains the species' inexplicable smile when shocking prey — a behavioral quirk still not understood.
