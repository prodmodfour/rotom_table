Charge Beam is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 5, At-Will frequency, AC 4, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

If Charge Beam successfully hits a target, roll 1d20. On a roll of 7+, the user's Special Attack is raised by +1 Combat Stage.
