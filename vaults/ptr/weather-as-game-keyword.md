Weather in PTU is a defined game keyword with four specific types: Hail, Rain, Sandstorm, and Sunny. Moves with the Weather keyword change the rules of battle for an area, altering damage and move effects.

Each weather type has distinct mechanical consequences: [[hail-weather-effects]], [[rain-weather-effects]], [[sandstorm-weather-effects]], [[sunny-weather-effects]]. Only one weather can be active at a time per [[weather-exclusivity-constraint]], and weather conditions last a fixed duration per [[weather-lasts-five-rounds]].

Importantly, [[natural-weather-not-game-weather]] — a normal sunny day is not "Sunny Weather" mechanically.
