In Pokemon, "poison" encompasses both poison and venom — there is no mechanical or thematic distinction between the two. A Poison Fang delivering venom and a Poison Gas releasing airborne toxin are both just "Poison-type" effects. When the system refers to poison (the type, the status, or the damage), it covers all toxic substances regardless of delivery method.
