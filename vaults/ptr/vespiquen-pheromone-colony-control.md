[[Vespiquen]] controls her [[Combee]] colony through pheromone production. The volume and potency of her pheromone output directly determines colony size — a stronger Vespiquen commands a larger hive. This chemical authority is the biological basis for her Rally and Pressure traits: Rally reflects the ability to marshal Combee through pheromone signals, while Pressure reflects the intimidating aura of a queen whose entire colony will die to defend her. Vespiquen only enters direct combat when her workers cannot handle a threat themselves.
