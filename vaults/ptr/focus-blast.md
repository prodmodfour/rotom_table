Focus Blast is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 7, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Smite, Aura.

Focus Blast lowers the target's Special Defense by -1 CS on 18+.
