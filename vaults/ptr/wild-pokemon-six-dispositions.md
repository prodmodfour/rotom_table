Wild Pokemon have 6 Dispositions toward Trainers, ranging from Very Friendly to Very Hostile. Disposition determines how a wild Pokemon reacts to approach and influences [[disposition-charm-check-dcs]] for calming or befriending it. Disposition is [[disposition-is-per-entity|tracked per entity]] — a Pokemon may have different dispositions toward different trainers.
