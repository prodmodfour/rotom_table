The [[Wooper]]-[[Quagsire]] line are cold-water amphibians modeled after axolotls. Wooper lives in cold water most of the time and emerges to search for food when temperatures drop further. Quagsire avoids sunlight by staying submerged during the day, surfacing at dusk and night. Both are timid as juveniles — Wooper stays close to Quagsire for protection against predators. Their shared Rain Dish and Damp traits reflect this dependence on wet, cool environments, while their Water/Ground typing captures the line's life split between freshwater and the muddy substrate beneath it.
