First Impression is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Priority.

You may only use First Impression with Priority upon joining an encounter; if you do, First Impression Flinches the target. Switching out resets the requirement of joining an encounter. .
