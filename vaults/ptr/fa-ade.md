Façade is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

If the user is afflicted with a Persistent Status Affliction, Façade’s Damage Base is doubled to DB 14 (4d10+15 / 40).
