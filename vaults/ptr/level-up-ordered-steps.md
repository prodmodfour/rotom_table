Whenever a Pokemon levels up, follow this sequence: first [[level-up-grants-one-stat-point]], then [[level-up-move-check]] and [[evolution-check-on-level-up]] (checking the Pokedex entry), and finally check for new [[trait-definition|traits]] at Level 20 and 40 per [[trait-unlock-schedule]].

If the Pokemon evolves, the new form's move list is checked for any moves learned at that level. The app should automate this workflow per [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]].
