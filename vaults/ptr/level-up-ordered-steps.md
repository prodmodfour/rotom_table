Whenever a Pokemon levels up, follow this sequence: first [[level-up-grants-one-stat-point]], then [[evolution-check-on-level-up]] (checking the Pokedex entry), and finally check for new [[trait-definition|traits]] at Level 20 and 40 per [[trait-unlock-schedule]].

Moves are not part of the level-up sequence — [[moves-are-universally-available|they are unlocked through their own conditions]] per [[unlock-level-up-split]]. The app should automate this workflow per [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]].
