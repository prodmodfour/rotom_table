Heal Pulse is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Aura, Healing.

Restores 50% of the target’s max Hit Points. Heal Pulse’s user may not target itself with Heal Pulse.

As a Daily x2 move, Heal Pulse can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
