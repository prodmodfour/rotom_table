The current value of a combat stat is calculated as:

((Stat × Multipliers) × [[combat-stage-asymmetric-scaling|Combat Stage Factor]]) + Modifiers

This applies to Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed. The Stat is the character's [[individual-stats-vs-base-stats|individual stat]] value. Multipliers and Modifiers come from various sources (traits, items, conditions, etc.).
