Rototiller is a Ground-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Burst 2.

All Grass-type Pokémon in the area raise their Attack and Special Attack 1 Combat Stage.
