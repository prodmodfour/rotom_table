Leech Life is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 2, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.
