Temporary Hit Points from multiple sources do not stack — only the highest value applies. If a Pokemon has 30 temp HP from one source and gains 20 from another, it retains 30.

Temp HP is always lost before real HP from any damage or effect. This interacts with [[massive-damage-after-temp-hp]] (only real HP lost counts toward the injury threshold) and [[real-max-hp-for-all-percentages]] (temp HP doesn't count toward HP percentage calculations).
