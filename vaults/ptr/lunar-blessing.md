Lunar Blessing is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user regains hit points equal to 50% of its Hit Point total, cures all of its Status conditions, and gains +2 Evasion.

As a Daily move, Lunar Blessing can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
