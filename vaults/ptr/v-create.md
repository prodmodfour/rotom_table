V-Create is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 18, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 5, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Smite.

Lowers the user's Defense, Special Defense, and Speed by -1 CS each.

As a Daily move, V-Create can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
