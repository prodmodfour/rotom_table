Each move has an Energy Cost that determines how much [[energy-resource|Energy]] it consumes when used. Cost balances the move's power — stronger moves cost more.

Energy Cost is also a design lever: costs can deviate from raw power for specific reasons. See [[low-distribution-energy-discount]] and [[downside-moves-energy-discount]].
