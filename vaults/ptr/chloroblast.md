Chloroblast is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Line 8, Smite, [[spirit-surge|Spirit Surge]].

The user’s Hit Points are reduced by 50% of their full Hit Point value. This Hit Point loss cannot be prevented or reduced in any way.

As a Daily move, Chloroblast can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
