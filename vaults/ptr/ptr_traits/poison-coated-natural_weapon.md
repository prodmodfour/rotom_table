# Poison Coated [Natural Weapon]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Natural Weapon, Trait Modifier
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect
- The user has a poison coated natural weapon that they use in battle.
- Contact moves using the natural weapon inflict poison on 18+
- The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Poison-type (Physical) instead of Normal-type.

## Unlock Conditions
- The relevant [Natural Weapon] Trait.

## Styling

This trait belongs to a [[trait-families|trait family]]. The correct format is `Poison Coated [Subtype]` where the subtype matches the specific [[natural-weapons|Natural Weapon]] — e.g. `Poison Coated [Stinger]`, `Poison Coated [Claws]`. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Poison Coated Claws` is incorrect).

## Notes
- Examples:
	 - Nidoran♂/Nidorino/Nidoking — venomous horn and dorsal barbs
	 - Nidoran♀/Nidorina/Nidoqueen — venomous spines
	 - Beedrill — arm and abdomen stingers
	 - Weedle — head stinger
	 - Skorupi/Drapion — tail stinger
	 - Qwilfish/Overqwil — venomous spines all over
	 - Venipede/Whirlipede/Scolipede — venomous horns
	 - Mareanie/Toxapex — venomous spike-tentacles
	 - Roselia/Roserade — poison thorns 
	 - Croagunk/Toxicroak — venomous knuckle spikes
