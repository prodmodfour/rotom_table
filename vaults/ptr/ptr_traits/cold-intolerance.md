# Cold Intolerance

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

In cold weather (0 degrees Celsius or lower), the user:
- Gets -2 to all attack rolls
- Is [[slowed-halves-movement|Slowed]].

Suppressed for three rounds if the user uses or is hit by a Fire type move.
