# Guts

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

While the user is Burned, Poisoned, Paralyzed, Frozen, or Asleep, their Attack is raised by 2 Combat Stages. These Combat Stages are lost when the user is no longer suffering from any of these conditions.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Represents a fighting spirit that burns hotter under adversity — pain and debilitation fuel raw physical power rather than hindering it. In PTU, this was the Guts Ability.
