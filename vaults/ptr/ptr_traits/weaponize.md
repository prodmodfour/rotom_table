# Weaponize

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Voluntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

While being wielded as a [[living-weapon|Living Weapon]] and being actively Commanded as a Pokemon, the user may Intercept for its wielder as a Free Action.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

The spirit inhabiting the blade shields the hand that wields it. This only functions when the weapon is both wielded and Commanded — a sheathed or disengaged Living Weapon cannot Intercept. The Intercept follows normal Intercept rules (the user takes the hit instead of the wielder).
