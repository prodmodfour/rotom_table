# Gas Production

Gas Production traits describe how a Pokemon produces and releases airborne substances — gas, spores, powder, pollen, vapor, scent, or smoke. They are the biological "hardware" that enables moves which disperse substances through the air.

Most Pokemon may incidentally emit odors or shed skin, but a Gas Production trait means the Pokemon can produce airborne substances deliberately and with enough volume or potency to use them in battle.

## Gas Production Traits

- [[gas-sacs]] — internal organs that produce, store, and expel gaseous substances
- [[spore-release]] — body produces and ejects spores, pollen, or powder from surface structures
- [[pleasant-scent]] — glands that emit aromatic or noxious chemical compounds
