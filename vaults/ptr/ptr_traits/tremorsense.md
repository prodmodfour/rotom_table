# Tremorsense X

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user can detect creatures and objects within X meters by sensing vibrations through the ground. Works in total darkness, through obscuring terrain, or while Blinded. Does not require line of sight.

Only detects creatures and objects in contact with the same solid surface as the user, or moving through water the user is also in contact with. Cannot detect airborne or levitating creatures.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

X is set per species. A burrowing ambush predator that hunts by vibration has a larger X than a fish that supplements poor eyesight with whisker feedback.
