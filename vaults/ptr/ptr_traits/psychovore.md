# Psychovore

Psychovores are Pokemon that feed on the emotional energy of other creatures. Rather than consuming physical food, they sustain themselves by provoking and absorbing specific emotions. The emotion they feed on shapes their behavior — a fear-feeder terrorizes, an annoyance-feeder pesters.

## Psychovore Traits

- [[phobovore]] — feeds on fear (see [[misdreavus-fear-feeding-cycle]])
- Annoyance — feeds on frustration and irritation (see [[impidimp-line-emotion-feeding]])
