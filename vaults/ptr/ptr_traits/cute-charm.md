# Cute Charm

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

When the user is hit by a melee attack, the attacker must make a Focus check against DC 10 + the user's Charm modifier. On a failure, the attacker becomes Infatuated with the user. Gender does not matter.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Unlike [[attract]], which requires opposite genders, Cute Charm's infatuation is not romantic — it's an involuntary captivation by the user's sheer appeal. The Focus check represents the attacker's ability to stay composed after close contact.
