# Battle Armor

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user is immune to Critical Hits. Attacks that would critically hit instead deal normal damage.

This does not protect against [[coup-de-grace-auto-crit|Coup de Grace]], which explicitly ignores critical hit immunities.
