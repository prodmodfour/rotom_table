# Wing Buzz

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Sound
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user produces sound through rapid wing vibration — a buzzing, humming, or droning that can be controlled in pitch and intensity. This is distinct from stridulation; the sound comes from the wings beating the air, not from friction between body parts.
