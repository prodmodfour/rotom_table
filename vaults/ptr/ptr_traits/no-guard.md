# No Guard

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Inheritable, Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user gains a +3 bonus to all [[one-evasion-per-accuracy-check|Accuracy Checks]]. However, all foes gain a +3 bonus to Accuracy Checks against the user.

## Unlock Conditions (Learned):

- Pokemon Test: DC 12 Combat
- Trainer Test: DC 18

## Notes

No Guard represents a fighting philosophy of total commitment — the user throws everything into offense with no regard for self-preservation. This creates a glass-cannon dynamic: the user lands hits far more reliably, but is equally exposed. The +3 is significant in a system where [[evasion-from-defensive-stats|evasion caps at +6]], effectively halving a defender's evasion advantage while equally undermining the user's own defenses.

See [[no-guard-dc-rationale]] for the design reasoning behind the DC split.
