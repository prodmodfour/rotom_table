# Synchronize

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect

When the user is inflicted with Paralysis, Freeze, Burn, Poison, or Sleep by a foe, that foe receives the same Status Condition.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

A psychic feedback loop — the attacker's hostile intent rebounds through an empathic or magical connection. Primarily a Psychic-type trait. The energy cost represents the mental strain of maintaining the synchronous link. In PTU, this was the Synchronize Ability.
