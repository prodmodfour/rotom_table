- We should aim to be exhaustive in making traits that are needed to describe what Pokemon *can do*. There shouldn't be a notable capability that a Pokemon has that we don't have a Trait for.
- However, [[lore-does-not-require-traits|not every lore detail needs a trait]] — cosmetic features and narrative flavor don't need mechanical expression.
- Traits that modify skills [[skill-traits-must-gate-behaviors|must also serve as unlock conditions]] — don't create traits purely for stat bonuses.
