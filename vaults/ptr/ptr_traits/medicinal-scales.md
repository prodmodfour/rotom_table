# Medicinal Scales

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Extended Action)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user periodically molts and sheds its scales. As an Extended Action, shed scales can be harvested and ground into a medicinal powder called Dragon Salve.

Preparing Dragon Salve requires a DC 15 Medicine check. On failure, the scales are wasted.

Dragon Salve can be applied to a target as an Extended Action:
- Removes all [[fatigue-levels|Fatigue]]
- Cures incurable illness and any Persistent or Volatile Status Conditions

One dose can be prepared per day. Prepared Dragon Salve spoils after 24 hours.
