# Light Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can bend and shape light through innate photokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Fairy-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using focused beams of light.

**Light Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates light to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Intensify or suppress light in an X+1-square radius area, raising bright light to blinding or dimming it to darkness. Blinding light in the area imposes -2 to Accuracy for creatures that rely on sight. The change lasts until the end of the user's next turn.
- Focus a beam of light at one creature, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Bend light around an object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter, rendering it invisible until it is moved or touched.
- Produce a harmless sensory light effect — shifting colors, prismatic refractions, glowing patterns, faint shimmering. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Light Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Light Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Light Manipulation represents ambient photokinetic control — an innate connection to light and radiance. It is distinct from combat Moves like Dazzling Gleam or Flash, which are specific offensive or tactical applications of light.
