# Rock Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can shape and hurl rock and earth through innate geokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Rock-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using hurled rock fragments.

**Rock Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates rock and earth to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Hurl one unattended rock or stone object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares in a direction of the user's choice.
- Launch stone at one creature, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Reshape earthen or stone ground in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing rough terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory effect — a faint tremor, shifting pebbles, grinding stone, rising dust. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Rock Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Rock Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Rock Manipulation represents ambient geokinetic control — an innate connection to rock and earth. It is distinct from combat Moves like Rock Throw or Stone Edge, which are specific offensive applications of stone.
