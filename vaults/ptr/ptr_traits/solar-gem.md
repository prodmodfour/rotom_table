# Solar Gem

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

+5 damage to Psychic-type attacks during the day. -5 damage to Psychic-type attacks during the night.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

[[espeon-solar-psychic-power|Espeon's forehead gem]] stores solar energy as psychic fuel. During daylight, the gem glows brightly and amplifies psychic output. At night, the gem darkens and Espeon's psychic power wanes.
