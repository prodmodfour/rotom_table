# Mega Launcher

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user's moves with the Aura keyword have their Damage Base increased by +3.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Adapted from the PTU ability of the same name. The original boosted Aura Sphere, Dark Pulse, Dragon Pulse, and Water Pulse by +3 DB. In PTR, all four of those moves carry the Aura keyword, so the trait references the keyword directly — making it automatically apply to any future Aura-keyword moves as well.

Heal Pulse also has the Aura keyword but deals no damage, so the DB boost has no effect on it.
