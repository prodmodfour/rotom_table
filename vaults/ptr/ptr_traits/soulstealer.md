# Soulstealer

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

When the user's attack kills a creature, the user removes one Injury and recovers 25% of their maximum Hit Points.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

The [[honedge-sash-drains-life-force|sash-based life drain]] made literal in combat — the blade drinks deeply from a felled opponent. No usage limit; every kill feeds the weapon. This makes the Honedge line increasingly dangerous the longer a fight drags on, as each downed foe restores the weapon's vitality. The Injury removal is particularly potent, as Injuries are otherwise difficult to clear in combat.
