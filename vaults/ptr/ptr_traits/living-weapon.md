# Living Weapon

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Standard Action)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user is both a Pokemon and a melee weapon. A willing trainer may engage the user as a Standard Action, wielding it as equipment. The user must also be willing.

While wielded:
- The wielder and the Living Weapon share the wielder's Movement Speed. The total distance Shifted across both their turns in a round cannot exceed the wielder's Movement Speed.
- The user grants weapon moves to the wielder based on the wielder's [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier|Combat Skill]] Rank (see [[living-weapon-gates-moves]]).
- Those granted moves are also added to the user's own Move List while wielded, so long as the wielder qualifies.
- The user cannot benefit from [[no-guard]] while wielded.

Either party may disengage as a Swift Action during their turn. Re-engaging is a Standard Action that may be taken by either party.

## Notes

Any trainer can engage a willing Living Weapon regardless of Combat rank — the rank only gates which weapon moves become available, not whether the trainer can wield it. This is a species-defining trait for the [[Honedge]] line, whose [[honedge-line-spirit-inhabited-weapon|spirit-inhabited-weapon]] nature makes them simultaneously creature and armament.
