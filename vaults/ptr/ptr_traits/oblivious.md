# Oblivious

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user is immune to the Infatuated and Enraged conditions.

## Unlock Conditions (Learned):

- Pokemon Test: DC 10 Focus
- Trainer Test: DC 20

## Notes

Represents a Pokemon that is simply too dim, detached, or single-minded to be provoked or charmed. Where [[inner-focus]] is disciplined concentration, Oblivious is the absence of the emotional hooks that Infatuation and Rage exploit — the user doesn't resist these conditions so much as fail to notice them.

See [[oblivious-dc-rationale]] for the design reasoning behind the DC split.
