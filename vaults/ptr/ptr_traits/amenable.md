# Amenable

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The Trainer of a Pokemon with Amenable gains +4 to all social skill checks (Command, Charm, Intimidate, Persuasion, Deception) targeting that Pokemon.

This bonus only applies when the Pokemon is owned by the Trainer making the check. It does not apply to other Trainers or to wild interactions.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Amenable represents a Pokemon that is naturally receptive to human social cues — it reads tone, body language, and intent more easily than others of its kind. This isn't obedience or submissiveness; the Pokemon simply has a disposition that makes the trainer-Pokemon social channel clearer in both directions.

The bonus modifies the Trainer's roll, not the Pokemon's [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|social skill hierarchy]] DC. The two stack normally — a Trainer using the Pokemon's top-ranked social skill against an Amenable Pokemon benefits from both +0 DC and the +4 bonus.
