# Wanderer [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

When the user has remained in the same settlement, camp, or resting location for more than a day, the user will attempt to leave and travel to a new location at the first opportunity — wandering off during rest, slipping away at night, or simply walking toward the horizon when unattended.

This can be suppressed for 1 hour with a DC 10 + X Command, Charm, Persuasion or Intimidate check, modified by the Pokemon's [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|social skill hierarchy]]. If you fail this check, you cannot try again for 1 hour.

## Unlock Conditions


## Styling

The correct format is `Wanderer [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Wanderer 1` is incorrect).

## Notes

Wanderer represents a deep migratory instinct — the Pokemon is not unhappy or disloyal, it simply cannot stay still. Trainers who travel frequently may never notice the compulsion, while those who settle in one place will need to manage it constantly.
