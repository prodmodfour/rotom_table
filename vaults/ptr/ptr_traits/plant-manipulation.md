# Plant Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can shape and direct plant matter through innate phytokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Grass-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using lashing vines or launched plant matter.

**Plant Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates plant matter to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Grow, extend, or move plant matter up to X+2 squares in a direction of the user's choice, or hurl one unattended object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares using vines or roots.
- Entangle one creature with plant growth, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Grow or clear vegetation in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing overgrown terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory plant effect — rustling leaves, blooming flowers, swaying grass, creaking branches. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Plant Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Plant Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Plant Manipulation represents ambient phytokinetic control — an innate connection to plant life. It is distinct from combat Moves like Razor Leaf or Leaf Storm, which are specific offensive applications of plant matter, and from [[plant-matter-production]], which describes the Pokemon's ability to grow plant tissue from its own body.
