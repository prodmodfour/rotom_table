# Likes Shiny

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

Is drawn to shiny objects, gems, and crystals. It will attempt to collect and guard them. Offering a coveted sparkly object grants +4 to Charm checks against this Pokemon.
