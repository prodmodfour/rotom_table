# Traits

~197 custom trait definitions for PTR. Traits are the unified progression mechanic replacing PTU's abilities, features, edges, and capabilities.

## What you can't know without exploring here

- What a specific trait does (effect, prerequisites, rank scaling)
- The full trait taxonomy — hub traits with subtypes (Natural Weapon [Fangs, Claws], etc.)
- Which traits represent which type identities (e.g. Opportunist = Dark, Light Manipulation = Fairy)
- Design philosophy behind the trait system

- Trait *design rationale* notes (e.g. `effect-spore-design.md`, `technician-design.md`) live in `../rules/`, not here.

When designing traits or modifying traits, first read trait_philosophy.md
