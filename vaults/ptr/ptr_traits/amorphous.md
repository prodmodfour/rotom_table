# Amorphous

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive

## Effect

The user has an inconsistent body shape. It can flatten and reform itself like putty, stretching out its body material and condensing itself as well. It is able to squeeze through tight spaces, such as under a door.
