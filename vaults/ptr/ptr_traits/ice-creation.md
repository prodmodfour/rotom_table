# Ice Creation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect

The user generates ice from ambient moisture or innate cryokinetic energy. X is the maximum [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] of ice the user can create.

**Create Ice (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user creates a mass of ice of Weight Class X or lighter in an unoccupied adjacent square or in their own space. The ice is a solid object that persists until it melts naturally or is destroyed — it can be used as a barrier, platform, or projectile and can be interacted with normally.

## Unlock Conditions


## Styling

The correct format is `Ice Creation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Ice Creation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Ice Creation represents the ability to conjure or condense ice from ambient moisture — flash-freezing humidity from the air, crystallizing breath, or solidifying cryokinetic energy into solid ice. The created ice is indistinguishable from natural ice.

This trait pairs naturally with [[ice-manipulation]] — one creates the material, the other controls it.
