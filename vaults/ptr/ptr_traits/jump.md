# Jump X/Y

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (part of movement)
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user can jump X squares vertically (high jump) and Y squares horizontally (long jump) as part of its movement. Jump distance counts against the user's total movement for the turn.

## Styling

The correct format is `Jump X/Y` where X is the high jump value and Y is the long jump value (e.g. `Jump 2/3`).

## Notes

Adapted from PTU's Jump capability. Values typically range from 1/1 (minimal hopping) to 3/3 (strong leapers). Most Pokemon have at least Jump 1/1.
