# Mind Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can directly interface with the minds of other creatures through innate psychic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Psychic-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using projected psychic force.

**Mind Reach (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user extends its psychic awareness to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Send a telepathic message (up to one sentence) to a creature. The creature can reply if willing. No check required.
- Read the emotional state of a creature (calm, hostile, afraid, etc.). No check required.
- Detect the presence and number of minds within an X+1-square radius, even through walls. The user cannot identify specific creatures this way unless it recognizes their psychic signature (familiar creatures). No check required.
- Impose psychic pressure on one creature, giving it -1 to its next check. The creature resists with a Focus check (DC 8 + 2X). This does not stack with itself.
- Produce a harmless sensory psychic effect — a prickling sensation on the scalp, a feeling of being watched, a brief flash of color behind the eyes. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Mind Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Mind Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Mind Manipulation represents ambient psychokinetic awareness — an innate connection to the mental landscape. It is distinct from combat Moves like Psychic or Confusion, which are specific offensive applications of psychic force. It is also distinct from [[telekinetic]], which manipulates physical objects rather than minds, and from [[sorcery]], which requires vocalization.
