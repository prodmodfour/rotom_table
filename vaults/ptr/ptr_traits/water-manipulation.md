# Water Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can shape and direct water through innate hydrokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Water-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using projected jets of water.

**Water Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates water to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Move a volume of water up to X+2 squares in a direction of the user's choice, or hurl one unattended object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares using a water current.
- Blast one creature with water, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Flood or drain earthen ground in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing muddy terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory water effect — rippling puddles, gentle misting, splashing, condensation on surfaces. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Water Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Water Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Water Manipulation represents ambient hydrokinetic control — an innate connection to water. It is distinct from combat Moves like Water Gun or Surf, which are specific offensive applications of water.
