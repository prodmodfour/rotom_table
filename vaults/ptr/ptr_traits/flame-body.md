# Flame Body

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

If hit by a contact move, the attacker becomes Burned.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Defensive trait. The user's body radiates extreme heat — for Pokemon like [[Flareon]], this is a product of [[flareon-fur-thermoregulation|thermoregulatory fur]] that continuously vents heat from an [[flareon-internal-flame-sac|internal flame sac]]. Any physical contact risks burns.
