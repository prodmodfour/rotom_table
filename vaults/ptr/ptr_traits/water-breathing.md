# Water Breathing

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The Pokemon can breathe underwater indefinitely and does not [[suffocation-rules|suffocate]] from being submerged.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Replaces the PTU "Gilled" capability. Water Breathing is standard for fish, aquatic crustaceans, and other species whose biology allows them to extract oxygen from water. Semi-aquatic species that can hold their breath for extended periods but don't have gills (e.g. beavers, frogs) do not necessarily have this trait.
