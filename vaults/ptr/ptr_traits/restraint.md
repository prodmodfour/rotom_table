# Restraint

## Properties
	- Category: Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

Increases the Z value of the user's [[commitment|Commitment]] trait by 1. This trait can be trained multiple times; each successful training adds 1 to Z.

## Unlock Conditions (Learned)

- Pokemon Test: DC 15 + X + Y + Z Focus (where X, Y, Z are the current Commitment values)
- Trainer Test: DC 15

## Notes

Restraint represents martial discipline — learning to wait for the right moment rather than throwing the Committed technique at the first opportunity. The Pokemon Test uses Focus (mental discipline) rather than Combat (fighting skill), reflecting that patience is a mental achievement, not a physical one.

The training DC scales with the Pokemon's current Commitment values. A more powerful committed fighter (high X and Y) finds it harder to learn restraint — the drive to use the technique grows with its mastery. And a Pokemon that has already learned restraint (high Z) finds further restraint increasingly difficult.
