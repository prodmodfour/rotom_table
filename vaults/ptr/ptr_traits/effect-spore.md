# Effect Spore

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Trigger, Involuntary
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
	- Trigger: Hit by a Melee Attack
	- The user involuntarily releases a burst of toxic spores. Roll 1d6: on 1–2 the attacker is Poisoned, on 3–4 the attacker is Paralyzed, on 5–6 the attacker falls Asleep.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Effect Spore is the reactive defense enabled by [[spore-release]] — when physically struck, the user's spore structures rupture or discharge automatically, exposing the attacker to whatever toxic particulates the species produces. The randomness reflects the unpredictable mix of spore types released under duress.
