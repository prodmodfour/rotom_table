# Brimstone

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

Whenever the user inflicts [[burned|Burn]] or [[poisoned|Poison]] with a damaging Fire-Type or Poison-Type attack, the target becomes both Burned and Poisoned.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Brimstone represents a creature whose toxic and incendiary biology are deeply intertwined — venom that ignites on contact with air, or flames hot enough to catalyze tissue-destroying toxins. The dual affliction makes the user exceptionally dangerous over sustained engagements, as both status conditions compound damage each round.
