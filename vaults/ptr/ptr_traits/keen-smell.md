# Keen Smell

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

The user has a heightened olfactory sense, allowing it to detect and track creatures, food sources, or specific scents over long distances and through obscuring conditions. The user can track by scent alone and detect hidden or invisible creatures within close range if they have a scent.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Keen Smell is a subtype of the [[senses|Sense]] hub trait. It represents biological scent hardware — enlarged nasal passages, chemoreceptive antennae, forked tongues with Jacobson's organs — that goes beyond what ordinary Pokemon can detect by smell.
