# Mettle

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Learnable, Inheritable
	- Activation: Triggered (Voluntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user has a store of Mettle Points (maximum 3). Whenever the user faints, they gain 1 Mettle Point (stored across encounters, up to the maximum).

The user may spend 1 Mettle Point to reroll any roll they have just made. The user must accept the new result.

## Unlock Conditions (Learned):

- Pokemon Test: DC 25 Focus
- Trainer Test: DC 25

## Notes

Mettle represents sheer tenacity — the kind of resolve that comes from being knocked down and getting back up. Each defeat sharpens determination, and that stored resolve can be called upon at a critical moment to push past bad luck. The resource is scarce and self-replenishing only through failure, making it a comeback mechanic rather than a grindable advantage.
