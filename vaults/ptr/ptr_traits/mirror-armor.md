# Mirror Armor

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

When a foe's move or trait would lower the user's [[combat-stage-asymmetric-scaling|Combat Stages]], the stage decrease is instead applied to the foe. The user's Combat Stages are unaffected.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Corviknight's polished steel plumage acts as a reflective ward — hostile debuffing energy rebounds off the surface. In PTU, this was the Mirror Armor Ability (Corviknight's High Ability).
