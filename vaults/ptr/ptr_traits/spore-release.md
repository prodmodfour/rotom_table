# Spore Release

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Gas Production
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user produces and ejects spores, pollen, powder, or similar particulates from surface structures on its body — caps, gills, petals, wing scales, or skin pores.
