# Seed Sower

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

If the user is hit by a damaging move, the field becomes Grassy for 5 rounds. While Grassy, all Pokemon and Trainers standing on the ground recover 1/10th of their maximum Hit Points at the start of every turn, and Grass-Type attacks performed by grounded Pokemon and Trainers gain a +10 bonus to Damage Rolls.

If the field is already Grassy, this trait does not refresh the duration.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Based on the Seed Sower ability. The user involuntarily scatters seeds on impact, rapidly sprouting into lush terrain. This makes the user a natural battlefield supporter — even taking hits benefits allies who can leverage Grass-Type moves or need passive healing.
