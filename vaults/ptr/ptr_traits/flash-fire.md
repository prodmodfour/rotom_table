# Flash Fire

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user is immune to the damage and effects of Fire-Type moves. If the user is hit by a Fire-Type move, they gain a +5 bonus to their next damage roll with a Fire-Type move. This bonus fizzles at the end of the user's next turn if unused.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Defensive trait. The immunity applies to all Fire-Type attacks regardless of whether they would normally deal damage — status moves with Fire typing are also negated. The +5 bonus does not stack with itself; a second Fire-Type hit while the bonus is active simply refreshes the duration. Compare [[volt-absorb]], which converts absorbed hits into energy recovery, and [[water-absorb]], which converts absorbed hits into health recovery.
