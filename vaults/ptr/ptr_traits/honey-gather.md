# Honey Gather

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Extended Action)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

Once per day, the user can produce a Honey item as an Extended Action, provided it has access to an environment with abundant plant life (forests, meadows, flower fields, etc.). The Honey may be collected with a Collection Jar.

Honey attracts wild Pokemon when spread in a suitable habitat, making it a valuable resource for luring encounters.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Derived from PTU's Honey Gather capability. Common among pollinator Bug-types ([[Combee]], [[Cutiefly]], [[Ribombee]]) and nectar-foraging species — the trait reflects a lifestyle of constant nectar collection and processing.
