# Dry Skin

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user's skin is highly permeable, making it sensitive to heat and moisture.

- The user is immune to the damage and effects of Water-Type Moves. Whenever the user would be hit by a Water-Type Move, they instead recover 1 Tick of HP.
- Whenever the user is hit by a damaging Fire-Type Move, they lose an additional 1 Tick of HP beyond the move's damage.
- While in [[rain-weather-effects|Rain]], the user recovers 1 Tick of HP at the beginning of each of their turns.
- While in [[sunny-weather-effects|Sunny]] weather, the user loses 1 Tick of HP at the beginning of each of their turns.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

A double-edged environmental trait. The Water immunity and Rain healing make Dry Skin Pokemon strong in wet environments, but Sunny weather and Fire attacks punish them disproportionately. This creates strong weather-dependency and incentivizes teams to control weather.

Species with Dry Skin tend to live in marshes, freshwater, or other consistently damp habitats where sun exposure is limited.
