# Electricity Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can generate and direct electricity through innate electrokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Electric-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using projected arcs of electricity.

**Electricity Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates electricity to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Generate an electromagnetic pulse that pushes or pulls one metallic or conductive object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares in a direction of the user's choice.
- Jolt one creature with electricity, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Electrify or discharge ground in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing charged terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory electrical effect — sparks arcing between surfaces, static raising fur or hair, the scent of ozone, faint crackling. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Electricity Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Electricity Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Electricity Manipulation represents ambient electrokinetic control — an innate connection to electrical energy. It is distinct from combat Moves like Thunderbolt or Thunder Wave, which are specific offensive applications of electricity.
