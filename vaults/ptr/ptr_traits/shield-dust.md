# Shield Dust

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user ignores secondary effects of damaging moves that target it. Effects that trigger on specific Accuracy Check rolls (such as [[fire-fang|Fire Fang's]] Burn/Flinch or [[crunch|Crunch's]] Defense drop) do not apply to the user.

This does not prevent the move's primary damage or any effect that is the move's main purpose (e.g. a move whose sole function is inflicting a status condition is not a "secondary effect").

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Derived from PTU's Shield Dust ability. Common among Bug-types, particularly Lepidoptera (moths, butterflies) — the trait represents fine scales or dust that shed on contact, disrupting the mechanisms behind secondary effects.
