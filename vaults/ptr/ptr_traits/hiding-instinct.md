# Hiding Instinct [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

When the user feels threatened — by a predator, loud noise, or unfamiliar creature — it compulsively hides rather than fighting or fleeing. The user will seek the nearest cover (leaf litter, foliage, burrow, or its own body wrapping) and remain motionless until the threat passes. While hiding, the user refuses to attack or move, even if commanded.

This can be suppressed for 1 hour with a DC 10 + X Command, Charm, Persuasion or Intimidate check, modified by the Pokemon's [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|social skill hierarchy]]. If you fail this check, you cannot try again for 1 hour.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Reflects the freeze-and-conceal survival strategy of small prey insects. [[Sewaddle]] folds its leaf hood over its face to blend into foliage; [[Swadloon]] wraps itself tightly in leaves on the forest floor and goes still. This instinct is effective in the wild — motionless, camouflaged prey is hard for predators to detect — but in combat it can be a liability, as the Pokemon may shut down at a critical moment.

[[Leavanny]] outgrows this instinct entirely, transitioning from prey to protector (see [[parental-guardian-instinct]]).
