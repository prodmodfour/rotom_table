# Naturewalk (Terrain)

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user ignores movement penalties and difficult terrain effects caused by the specified natural terrain type. A Pokemon with Naturewalk (Forest) moves through dense undergrowth, roots, and fallen branches without slowing down.

## Styling

The correct format is `Naturewalk (Terrain)` where Terrain is one or more terrain types in parentheses. Multiple terrains are comma-separated: `Naturewalk (Forest, Mountain)`.

## Notes

Adapted from PTU's Naturewalk capability. Common terrain types include: Forest, Grassland, Mountain, Ocean, Wetlands, Tundra, Desert, Cave, Urban. The terrain type should match the species' natural habitat.
