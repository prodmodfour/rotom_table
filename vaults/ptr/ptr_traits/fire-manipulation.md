# Fire Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can shape and direct fire through innate pyrokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Fire-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using projected jets of flame.

**Fire Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates fire and heat to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Hurl one unattended object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares using a jet of flame or heat updraft.
- Blast one creature with fire, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Ignite or extinguish ground in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing scorched terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory fire effect — flickering shadows, heat shimmer, embers drifting on the breeze, warmth radiating from surfaces. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Fire Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Fire Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Fire Manipulation represents ambient pyrokinetic control — an innate connection to fire and heat. It is distinct from combat Moves like Flamethrower or Fire Blast, which are specific offensive applications of fire.
