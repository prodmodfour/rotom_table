# Earth Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can shift and shape soil, sand, and loose earth through innate terrakinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Ground-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using launched clods of earth or erupting pillars of soil.

**Earth Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates earth and soil to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Shift a mass of loose earth, sand, or soil up to X+2 squares in a direction of the user's choice, or hurl one unattended object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares by launching it from the ground.
- Erupt earth beneath one creature, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Churn or compact earthen ground in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing rough terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory earth effect — shifting soil, rising dust, cracking dry ground, faint tremors underfoot. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Earth Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Earth Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Earth Manipulation represents ambient terrakinetic control — an innate connection to the ground and soil. It is distinct from combat Moves like Earthquake or Earth Power, which are specific offensive applications of earth, and from [[rock-manipulation]], which governs solid stone and minerals rather than loose earth.
