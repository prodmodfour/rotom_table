# Telepath

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user can communicate mind-to-mind with creatures it can see. It can send and receive thoughts — words, images, or simple concepts — without vocalization, gesture, or shared language. Both parties understand the intended meaning, though nuance and abstraction are limited by the recipient's intelligence.

Telepathic communication is silent and invisible to outside observers. A creature receiving a telepathic message is aware it is being communicated with and can choose not to respond, but cannot block reception.

## Notes

Telepath is a communication trait, not a combat trait. It does not grant the ability to read unwilling minds, detect creatures, or impose mental effects — those are functions of [[mind-manipulation]].

Telepath does not require a Sound trait to communicate. A Pokemon with Telepath but no Sound trait can still "speak" through projected thought. This is relevant for Pokemon that lack vocalization entirely — they are not mute, they simply communicate differently.
