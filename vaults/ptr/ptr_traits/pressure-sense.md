# Pressure Sense

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user detects subtle changes in air pressure, movement, and environmental conditions through specialized sensory structures — typically fur, whiskers, or similar appendages. This provides a general awareness of nearby activity even when vision or hearing is impaired.

- The user can sense the presence and approximate location of creatures within 3 meters, even if they are [[invisible]] or in [[obscuring-terrain]].

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Distinct from [[tremorsense]], which detects vibrations through the ground. Pressure Sense works through the air — it reads displaced air currents, body heat differentials, and barometric micro-shifts caused by nearby movement. It is less precise than Tremorsense at range but works regardless of terrain type.

Distinct from [[aura-reader]], which senses the emotional and vital state of living things. Pressure Sense is purely physical — it detects that something is there and moving, not what it is feeling.
