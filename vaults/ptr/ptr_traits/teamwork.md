# Teamwork

## Properties
	- Category: Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

- While the user is adjacent to an opponent, allies using Melee attacks against that opponent receive a +2 bonus to Accuracy Checks.

## Unlock Conditions

- Pokemon Test: DC 10 Command, DC 10 Persuasion, DC 10 Charm
- Trainer Test: DC 15
- Focus 3

## Notes

A cooperative fighting instinct — the user positions itself to create openings for allies. The accuracy bonus stacks with the Evasion penalty from [[flanking]] if both conditions are met.
