# Moxie

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Static
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

Whenever the user's Move causes a target to faint, the user may raise its Attack Combat Stage by +1. Moxie can only trigger once per Move, even if the Move causes multiple targets to faint.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Adapted from the PTU ability of the same name. Represents a predatory drive that intensifies through successful combat — the thrill of the kill fueling greater aggression.
