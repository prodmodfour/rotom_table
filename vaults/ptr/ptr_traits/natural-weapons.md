# Natural Weapons

Natural Weapons are biological structures a Pokemon grows that can be used offensively in battle. They are the physical "hardware" that enables certain moves — a move that requires claws can only be learned by a Pokemon with the [[claws]] trait.

Nearly every Pokemon has at least one Natural Weapon. They are notable when they are a defining feature of the species' combat style.



## Natural Weapon Traits

- [[fangs]] — teeth and jaw strength for biting
- [[blade]] — rigid flat-edged body structure for slashing
- [[claws]] — sharp nails or talons on limbs
- [[head]] — skull used as a blunt weapon
- [[horn]] — rigid head-mounted weapon
- [[beak]] — hard pointed bill
- [[tail]] — tail used as a striking weapon
- [[wings]] — wings used offensively
- [[pincers]] — gripping and crushing appendages
- [[stinger]] — piercing organ for injection
- [[spines]] — multiple rigid sharp projections
- [[tentacles]] — flexible grasping and striking limbs
- [[body]] — whole-body mass used for tackling and slamming
- [[thorns]] — sharp plant-based growths
- [[arms]] — arms for holding, pushing, bracing, blocking
- [[hands]] — grasping digits for holding and throwing, for punching
- [[legs]] — legs for kicking and bracing
- [[feet]] — feet for stomping and trampling

## Styling

When listing a Natural Weapon on a Pokemon, the correct format is `Natural Weapon [Subtype]` where the subtype is one of the entries listed above. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Natural Weapon Fangs` is incorrect).

## Design

- [[natural-weapons-are-appendages]] — Natural Weapons are for physical body structures, not for hunting capability

## Modifiers

Natural Weapons can have modifiers that add effects when the weapon is used in battle.

- [[poison-coated-natural-weapon]] — the natural weapon is coated in toxin
