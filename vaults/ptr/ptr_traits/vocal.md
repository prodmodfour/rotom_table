# Vocal

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Sound
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user can produce controlled sounds through its throat, mouth, or vocal organs — growling, roaring, screeching, howling, or similar. This is the most common Sound trait.
