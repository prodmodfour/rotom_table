# Volatile [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

When the user is startled, provoked, or experiences a sudden strong emotion (anger, fear, pain, excitement), the user involuntarily discharges a burst of energy in its immediate vicinity, dealing Damage Base X damage of the user's primary type (Special, auto-hit) to all adjacent creatures and objects — including allies and its own Trainer.

This can be suppressed for 1 hour with a DC 10 + X Command, Charm, Persuasion or Intimidate check, modified by the Pokemon's [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|social skill hierarchy]]. If you fail this check, you cannot try again for 1 hour.

## Styling

The correct format is `Volatile [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Volatile 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Volatile represents an involuntary coupling between emotion and power. The Pokemon doesn't choose to lash out — its body converts emotional spikes into dangerous energy release. The nature of the discharge matches the user's primary type: electrical arcs, flame bursts, psychic pulses, ice shards, etc.
