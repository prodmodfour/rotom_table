# Natural Weapons Are Appendages

A [[natural-weapons|Natural Weapon]] represents a physical body structure — not hunting capability or combat skill. But having the structure alone is not enough. The appendage must be significant enough in size or power to actually function as a weapon.

A Pokemon with small teeth doesn't get [[fangs]]. A Pokemon with tiny nubs doesn't get [[horn]]. The body part must be prominent or powerful enough that it's a meaningful tool in combat — something the Pokemon would actually strike with.

This keeps the Natural Weapon list honest: it describes what a species is physically equipped to do, not what it technically possesses.
