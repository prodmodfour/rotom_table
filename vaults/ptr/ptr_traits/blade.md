# Blade

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Natural Weapon, Slashing
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user has a rigid, flat, edged body structure that can be used to slash and cut in battle. Blades include sword-like limbs, scythe-shaped fins, and sharpened leaf edges.
