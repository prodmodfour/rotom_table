# Supersonic Wind Blade

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Voluntary)
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect

If the user moves 8 squares in a line using their [[movement-traits|Flier]] trait, they may use the move [[air-slash|Air Slash]] as a swift action on a target in front of them and within range for Air Slash.

The user unlocks the move Air Slash, even if it does not meet that move's [[unlock-conditions]].

The energy cost of using this trait is in addition to the [[move-energy-cost|energy cost]] of using Air Slash.
