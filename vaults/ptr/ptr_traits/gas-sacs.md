# Gas Sacs

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Gas Production
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user has internal organs that chemically produce, store, and expel gaseous substances — toxic fumes, smoke, vapor, or mist. This covers both hot exhaust (smoke, ash) and cold emission (icy vapor, haze).
