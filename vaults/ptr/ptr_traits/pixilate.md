# Pixilate

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Voluntary)
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect

When the user uses a Normal-Type damaging move, they may spend 3 energy to change the move's type to Fairy for that use.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

The energy cost represents the effort of channeling fairy energy through an attack that isn't naturally fairy-typed. The value is in type coverage — turning a Normal move into a Fairy move lets the user exploit Fairy weaknesses and gain STAB on a move it otherwise wouldn't.
