# Technician

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

Moves with a Damage Base of 6 or lower have their Damage Base increased by +2. This bonus always applies to Moves with the Double Strike or Fivestrike Keywords.

## Unlock Conditions (Learned):

- Pokemon Test: DC 20 Athletics, DC 20 Acrobatics, DC 25 Focus
- Trainer Test: DC 15

## Notes

Technician represents mastery of precise, efficient technique over raw power. Rather than swinging harder, the user optimizes the mechanics of smaller strikes — perfect timing, ideal angle, maximum transfer of force. This makes weak moves punch above their weight and rewards multi-hit fighting styles that rely on volume and accuracy rather than brute force.
