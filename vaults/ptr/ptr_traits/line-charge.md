# Line Charge

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

During a [[shift-renamed-to-movement-action|Movement Action]], each movement segment must be a straight line in a single cardinal direction (north, south, east, or west). The user cannot move diagonally or change direction within a segment. Between segments (via [[movement-splitting]]), the user may choose a new cardinal direction.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Represents the biomechanical tradeoff of a body built for raw linear speed — extreme acceleration in exchange for zero mid-stride maneuverability. The user must stop and reorient to change heading.
