# Water Creation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect

The user generates water from ambient moisture or innate hydrokinetic energy. X is the maximum [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] of water the user can create.

**Create Water (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user creates a volume of water of Weight Class X or lighter in an unoccupied adjacent square or in their own space. The water is mundane and persists indefinitely — it flows, pools, and can be interacted with normally.

## Unlock Conditions


## Styling

The correct format is `Water Creation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Water Creation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Water Creation represents the ability to conjure or condense water from ambient moisture — drawing humidity from the air, pulling groundwater upward, or condensing hydrokinetic energy into liquid. The created water is indistinguishable from natural water.

This trait pairs naturally with [[water-manipulation]] — one creates the material, the other controls it.
