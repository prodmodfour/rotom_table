# Fur Fluffing

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user gains a +2 bonus to Intimidate.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

The user can fluff out its fur to appear larger and more threatening. For [[Flareon]], this doubles as its [[flareon-fur-thermoregulation|thermoregulation mechanism]] — the fluffed fur radiates visible heat distortion, making the display genuinely dangerous rather than a bluff.
