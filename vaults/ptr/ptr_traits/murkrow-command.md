# Murkrow Command

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user can command a murder of [[Murkrow]] through vocal cries and intimidation. The user controls up to 2 Murkrow per point of Command. Murkrow under this control must be at or below one quarter of the user's level.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

This trait represents [[Honchkrow]]'s role as mob boss of a Murkrow murder. Its vocal authority determines flock size — a stronger Honchkrow commands a larger murder. The level restriction reflects that a boss's authority only extends to subordinates significantly less developed than itself.
