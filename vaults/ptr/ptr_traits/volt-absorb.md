# Volt Absorb

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user is immune to the damage and effects of Electric-Type moves. If the user is hit by an Electric-Type move, they restore 5 [[energy-for-extra-movement|Energy]].

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Defensive trait. The immunity applies to all Electric-Type attacks regardless of whether they would normally deal damage — status moves with Electric typing are also negated. Compare [[flash-fire]], which converts absorbed hits into offensive power, and [[water-absorb]], which converts absorbed hits into health recovery; Volt Absorb instead recovers energy, giving it a sustain role rather than a burst-damage role.
