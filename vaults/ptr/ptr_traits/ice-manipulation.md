# Ice Manipulation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive / Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1 (active effects only)

## Effect

The user can shape and direct ice through innate cryokinetic ability. X is the trait's potency.

**Struggle Modification (Passive):** The user's [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] are Ice-type instead of Normal-type. They may be made at a range of X+3 squares instead of Melee, using projected shards of ice.

**Ice Control (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user manipulates ice and frost to produce one of the following effects within 2X+4 squares:
- Hurl one unattended ice or frozen object of [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] X or lighter up to X+2 squares in a direction of the user's choice.
- Blast one creature with ice, pushing them X squares. The creature resists with an Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 8 + 2X).
- Freeze or thaw ground in an X+1-square radius area, creating or clearing icy terrain.
- Produce a harmless sensory cold effect — frost spreading across surfaces, visible breath, rime forming on nearby objects, condensation freezing on contact. This does not cost energy.

## Styling

The correct format is `Ice Manipulation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Ice Manipulation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Ice Manipulation represents ambient cryokinetic control — an innate connection to ice and cold. It is distinct from combat Moves like Ice Beam or Blizzard, which are specific offensive applications of cold.
