# Queen's Proxy [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user refuses to engage in direct combat, instead directing others to fight on its behalf. When threatened, the user will attempt to position allied Pokemon (especially [[Combee]]) between itself and the threat rather than attacking directly. If no allies are available, the user will attempt to flee rather than fight.

In league or tournament settings, a Pokemon that refuses to fight on its own will be disqualified. Trainers must suppress this instinct before entering sanctioned matches.

This can be suppressed for 1 hour with a DC 10 + X Command, Charm, Persuasion or Intimidate check, modified by the Pokemon's [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|social skill hierarchy]]. If you fail this check, you cannot try again for 1 hour.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

This reflects [[Vespiquen]]'s role as a colony queen — she is too valuable to risk in direct combat when expendable workers exist. In the wild, a Vespiquen only fights personally as an absolute last resort. This instinct is deeply ingrained and difficult to override, requiring significant trust between trainer and Pokemon.
