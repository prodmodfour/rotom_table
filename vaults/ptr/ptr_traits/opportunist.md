# Opportunist [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Inheritable, Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

- The user may make X additional Attacks of Opportunity per round beyond the base 1.
- Grants Dark Type Struggle Attacks.
- The Learned version of this Trait is limited to X = 1

## Unlock Conditions
- Pokemon Test: DC 15 Insight, DC 15 Perception, DC 15 Combat
- Trainer Test: DC 15 

## Styling

The correct format is `Opportunist [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Opportunist 1` is incorrect).

## Notes

Primarily a Dark-type trait. Reflects an instinct for exploiting openings — the predator that strikes when prey stumbles, the bully that kicks when you're down. X is typically 1; X of 2 is reserved for apex predators and exceptionally aggressive hunters.
