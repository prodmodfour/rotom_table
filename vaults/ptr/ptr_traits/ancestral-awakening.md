# Ancestral Awakening

## Properties
	- Category: Learnable
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

The user has a deep spiritual connection to its ancient ancestors. The user gains +3 [[skill-modifier-scale|Focus]].

## Unlock Conditions (Learned)


## Notes

Ancestral Awakening is an [[unlock-conditions|unlock condition]] for [[ancient-power|Ancient Power]]. It also serves as an [[evolution-trigger-conditions|evolution trigger condition]] for certain species (e.g., Piloswine, Tangela, Yanma).

Ancient species such as fossil Pokemon have this trait innately.
