# Threaded

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user produces thread-like material — silk, webbing, string, or fine vine-like filaments — and can direct it with precision. The user can spin, anchor, shoot, and weave its threads at will.

Outside of combat, a Threaded Pokemon can create simple web structures, rappel or swing from anchor points, bind objects together, or trail a line behind itself. The threads are strong enough to support the user's weight and durable enough to persist until deliberately removed or destroyed.

## Notes

Threaded describes the production of flexible, filament-like material that can be anchored and woven. Bug-types typically produce silk from spinnerets; Grass-types may produce fine vine filaments that serve the same mechanical purpose (see [[vine-whip]], [[power-whip]]).

This is distinct from [[plant-matter-production]], which covers solid plant structures like leaves, seeds, and wood. A Leavanny has both — it produces leaves (Plant Matter Production [Leaves]) and spins silk to cut and sew them (Threaded).
