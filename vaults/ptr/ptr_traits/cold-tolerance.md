# Cold Tolerance

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user is naturally adapted to freezing environments.

- The user is immune to [[hail-weather-effects|Hail]] damage.
- The user is unaffected by cold weather penalties (cold weather does not impose Slowed or attack roll penalties on the user).
- Ice-Type moves that hit the user are treated as one step less effective on the [[damage-formula-step-order|type effectiveness chart]] (super effective becomes neutral, neutral becomes resisted, resisted becomes double-resisted).

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

The inverse of [[cold-intolerance]]. Where Cold Intolerance represents species that suffer in the cold, Cold Tolerance represents species that thrive in it — thick fur, insulating blubber, antifreeze blood, or an icy body that simply doesn't register cold as a threat.

Distinct from [[ice-body]], which actively heals in Hail, and [[snow-cloak]], which gains evasion in Hail. Cold Tolerance is purely defensive — surviving the cold rather than exploiting it.
