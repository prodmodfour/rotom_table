# Light Creation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect

The user generates light from innate photokinetic energy. X is the trait's potency.

**Create Light (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user creates a light source in an unoccupied adjacent square or in their own space. The light source sheds bright light in an X+2-square radius and dim light in a 2X+4-square radius. The light persists until dismissed (free action) or until the user falls unconscious.

The user may maintain up to X light sources at a time. Creating a light source beyond this limit automatically dismisses the oldest one.

## Unlock Conditions


## Styling

The correct format is `Light Creation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Light Creation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Light Creation represents the ability to produce radiance from innate energy — bioluminescence, conjured orbs of light, or glowing auras. Unlike fire, the light produces no heat and cannot ignite materials.

This trait pairs naturally with [[light-manipulation]] — one creates the light, the other controls it.
