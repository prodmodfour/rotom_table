# Rock Creation [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Active (Swift Action)
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect

The user generates solid rock from ambient minerals or innate geological energy. X is the maximum [[weight-classes-one-through-six|Weight Class]] of rock the user can create.

**Create Rock (Active, Swift Action, 1 Energy):** The user creates a rock or stone object of Weight Class X or lighter in an unoccupied adjacent square or in their own space. The rock is a mundane, solid object that persists indefinitely and can be interacted with normally — hurled, shaped, climbed on, or used as cover.

## Unlock Conditions


## Styling

The correct format is `Rock Creation [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Rock Creation 2` is incorrect).

## Notes

Rock Creation represents the ability to conjure or condense stone from ambient material — drawing minerals from the ground, compressing earth, or crystallizing geological energy. The created rock is indistinguishable from natural stone.

This trait pairs naturally with [[rock-manipulation]] — one creates the material, the other controls it.
