# Sweet Veil

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user and allies within 3 meters are immune to Sleep.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Derived from PTU's Sweet Veil ability. Represents a soothing aroma or sugar-laden pollen cloud that keeps nearby allies alert and prevents drowsiness. Found on nectar-producing species like [[Cutiefly]] and [[Ribombee]].
