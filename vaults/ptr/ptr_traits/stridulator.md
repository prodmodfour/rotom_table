# Stridulator

## Properties
	- Category: Innate, Sound
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

The user produces sound by rubbing body parts together — legs against wings, segments against each other, or similar friction-based mechanisms. This is the way crickets, grasshoppers, and similar creatures make their characteristic music.
