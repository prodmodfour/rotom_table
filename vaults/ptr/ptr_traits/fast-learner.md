# Fast Learner

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

The user gains +2 to all checks made during [[training-dual-check-system|training sessions]].

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Represents Pokemon that pick up new skills quickly — whether through high intelligence, eagerness to please, or sheer adaptability. In PTU, this maps loosely to abilities that aided skill acquisition. The bonus applies to the [[training-pokemon-check|Pokemon's check]], not the Trainer's.
