# Sniper

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

Critical hits deal +5 bonus damage that is multiplied as part of the crit. This stacks with other sources of bonus crit damage (e.g. [[coup-de-grace-auto-crit|Coup de Grace's]] +5, for a total of +10 before multiplication).
