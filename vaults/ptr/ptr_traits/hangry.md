# Hangry [X]

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost:

## Effect

When the user becomes hungry, it enters a violent tantrum lasting 1 hour. During the tantrum, the user attacks any creature or object nearby indiscriminately — ally or foe — and cannot be reasoned with through normal means.

This can be suppressed for 1 hour with a DC 10 + X Command, Charm, Persuasion or Intimidate check, modified by the Pokemon's [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|social skill hierarchy]]. If you fail this check, you cannot try again for 1 hour.

## Styling

The correct format is `Hangry [X]` where X is a number. Do not omit the brackets (e.g. `Hangry 2` is incorrect).
