# Rapid Development

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

The user gains 20% increased experience from all sources.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Represents the [[bug-type-accelerated-development|accelerated life cycle]] common to Bug-type Pokemon. In nature, insects develop through their life stages far faster than most other creatures — hatching, growing, and reaching maturity in a fraction of the time. This translates mechanically to faster level progression.

Rapid Development is the Bug type's [[type-identity-traits|identity trait]]. Where other types express identity through elemental control or behavioral tendencies, Bug types are defined by their compressed life cycles — reaching combat readiness sooner than similarly sized Pokemon of other types.
