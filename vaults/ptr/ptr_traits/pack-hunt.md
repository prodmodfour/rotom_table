# Pack Hunt

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive
	- Energy Cost: —

## Effect

- When an adjacent foe is damaged by an ally's Melee Attack, the user may make an [[attack-of-opportunity-trigger-list|Attack of Opportunity]] against that foe. This counts against the user's AoO limit for the round.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

A pack-fighting instinct — the user reflexively strikes when an ally lands a blow on a nearby target. Distinct from [[opportunist]], which grants additional AoO per round; Pack Hunt adds a new *trigger* for AoO rather than increasing the limit.
