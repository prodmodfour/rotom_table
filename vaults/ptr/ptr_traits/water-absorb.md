# Water Absorb

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect

The user is immune to the damage and effects of Water-Type moves. If the user is hit by a Water-Type move, they restore 1 [[tick-value-one-tenth-max-hp|Tick]] of HP.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

Defensive trait. The immunity applies to all Water-Type attacks regardless of whether they would normally deal damage — status moves with Water typing are also negated. Compare [[volt-absorb]], which converts absorbed hits into energy recovery, and [[flash-fire]], which converts absorbed hits into offensive power; Water Absorb instead recovers health, giving it a tank/sustain role.
