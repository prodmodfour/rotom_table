# Magic Bounce

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Triggered (Involuntary)
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect

When the user is hit by a Status Move, the Move is reflected back at the attacker. The user may also redirect the placement and affiliation of any Hazards being set within 10 meters of the user.

## Unlock Conditions


## Notes

A magical ward that turns hostile status effects back on the caster. In PTU, this was the Magic Bounce Ability.
