# Dead Silent

## Properties
	- Category: Innate
	- Activation: Passive

## Effect

- The user does not breathe or have a heartbeat, and does not shuffle or make noise as it moves unless it chooses to. This grants a passive bonus to Stealth in situations where sound would give the user away.
- + 5 Stealth
