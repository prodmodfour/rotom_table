# Trait Families

Trait families group related innate traits that describe a Pokemon's biological hardware. Each family has a hub note listing its subtypes.

## Families

- [[natural-weapons]] — biological structures used offensively in battle
- [[sound]] — mechanisms for producing controlled sound
- [[gas-production]] — mechanisms for producing and releasing airborne substances
- [[senses]] — specialized perceptive capabilities
- [[psychovore]] — Pokemon that feed on the emotional energy of others
