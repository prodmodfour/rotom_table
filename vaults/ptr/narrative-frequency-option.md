PTU offers an optional Narrative Frequency rule: "per day" can be reinterpreted as "per session" for games with frequent in-character time-skips between sessions. If a session ends mid-encounter, the session refresh can be deferred until the encounter finishes.

This is an explicit GM override for tracking convenience, not a mechanical change. It simplifies bookkeeping when the fictional day boundary doesn't align with play sessions.
