Thousand Arrows is a Ground-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

When calculating Weakness and Resistance for Thousand Arrows, Flying-Typed targets calculate damage as if Ground was neutral to Ice. The target is knocked down to ground level, and loses all Sky Speed for 3 turns. During this time, they may be hit by Ground-Type Moves even if normally immune.
