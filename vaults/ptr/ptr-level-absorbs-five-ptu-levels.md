Each PTR level absorbs 5 PTU levels. Blocks 1–15 map cleanly to 5-level PTU blocks. The remaining 20 PTU levels (81–100) are distributed 6-6-6-7 across blocks 16–19 to keep the XP curve smooth.

This is why the [[total-xp-unchanged|total XP to max is unchanged from PTU]] despite the [[pokemon-level-range-1-to-20|compressed 1–20 range]].
