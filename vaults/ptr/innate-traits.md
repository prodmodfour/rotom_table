Innate Traits are [[trait-definition|traits]] that a Pokemon has or can unlock due to its species. They are part of what a Pokemon's [[species-determines-vs-informs|species determines]].

A Pokemon's innate traits can also include traits gained through breeding — see [[inheritable-traits-list]]. These are not part of the species' default set but become part of the individual Pokemon's unlockable pool via [[hatched-pokemon-trait-pool]].
