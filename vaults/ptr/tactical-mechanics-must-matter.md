Combat mechanics that provide tactical options should always be meaningful. If a mechanic has no effect against strong opponents (where it matters most), it feels broken.

This is why [[flanking-penalty-post-cap]] applies the -2 evasion penalty AFTER the evasion cap — otherwise flanking would be useless against high-evasion targets, defeating its purpose as a positioning reward.

Similarly, [[dynamic-initiative-on-speed-change]] keeps initiative responsive to Speed changes, maintaining the tactical value of speed-altering moves and conditions.
