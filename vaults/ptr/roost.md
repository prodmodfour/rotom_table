Roost is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user regains HP equal to half of its full HP. If the user is a Flying Type, it loses the Flying Type until the start of their next turn.

As a Daily x2 move, Roost can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
