The [[alolan-grimer-line-chemical-diet|chemical diet]] of [[Alolan Grimer]] and [[Alolan Muk]] causes toxins to crystallize into solid formations on their bodies. In Alolan Grimer, these appear as two blunt tooth-like structures in the upper jaw. In Alolan Muk, white crystallized poison shards protrude from across the body, especially from the mouth and fingers, creating a fanged appearance.

These crystals are extremely hazardous — they break off easily and contain concentrated toxins. This crystallization also eliminates the foul stench associated with the Kantonian forms, because the toxins are locked into solid structures rather than being exuded as gas. The vivid green, pink, and yellow coloration of the Alolan forms is similarly a byproduct of chemical reactions from their garbage diet.
