Dragon Rush is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Smite.

The target is Pushed 3 Meters. Dragon Rush Flinches the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
