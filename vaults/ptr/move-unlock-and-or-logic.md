Move [[unlock-conditions]] typically use AND/OR composition. A move may require multiple conditions to all be met (AND), or any one of several conditions (OR), or a combination of both.

For example, a move might be unlocked by reaching a certain level OR by using a [[tms-as-unlock-conditions|TM]], or it might require a level AND a [[move-tutors-as-unlock-conditions|Move Tutor]].
