PTU defines these basic HP-restoring items: Potion (20 HP), Super Potion (35 HP), Hyper Potion (70 HP), Full Restore (80 HP + cures status), Revive (un-faints + 20 HP), Energy Powder (25 HP, Repulsive), Energy Root (70 HP, Repulsive), Revival Herb (un-faints + 50% HP, Repulsive).

Only Revive and Revival Herb actually cure [[fainted-at-zero-hp]] — regular potions bring HP above 0 but the target stays Fainted for 10 more minutes. See [[applying-items-action-economy]] for action costs.
