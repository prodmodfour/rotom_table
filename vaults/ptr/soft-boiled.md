Soft-Boiled is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target regains Hit Points equal to half of its full Hit Points. The user may target themselves with Soft-Boiled.

As a Daily x2 move, Soft-Boiled can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
