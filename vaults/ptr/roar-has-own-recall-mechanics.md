Roar (PTU p.406) has its own built-in recall mechanic distinct from the standard 8m recall beam. Targets shift away using their highest movement capability, and if an owned Pokemon ends within 6 meters of their Poke Ball, they are immediately recalled. The replacement sent out does not lose their Pokemon turn.

Move-based switching mechanics use the move's own text, not the general switching rules.
