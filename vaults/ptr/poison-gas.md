Poison Gas is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 6, and a range of Burst 1 or Cone 2.

Poison Gas Poisons all legal targets.
