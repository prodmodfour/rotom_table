Any character can unlock any move, gated only by the move's own [[unlock-conditions]]. There are no species-specific move lists — no Natural Moves, no Egg Moves, no TM-compatibility lists.

This replaces PTU's system where each species had a curated move pool (Level-Up, Egg, TM, Tutor). In PTR, the unlock conditions on the move itself do all the filtering. A move's conditions may still reference stats, types, or other factors that naturally favor certain Pokemon, but nothing prevents a Pokemon from meeting those conditions.
