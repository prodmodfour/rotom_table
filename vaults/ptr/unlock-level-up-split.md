In PTR, unlocking [[trait-definition|traits]] and moves is separate from leveling up and gaining stats. A character gains stat points from [[five-stat-points-per-level|leveling]], but unlocks traits and moves through [[unlock-conditions]] during play.

This split means a character can have low stats while having learned a lot, or high stats while knowing little. In PTU, stat growth and ability/move acquisition were tied together. The separation makes more narrative sense.
