[[Umbreon]] is a nocturnal ambush predator that conceals itself in total darkness using its black fur, waiting motionlessly for prey to move. Its large eyes grant exceptional night vision, allowing it to spot targets that cannot see it. When prey appears, Umbreon strikes at the throat. This hunting style -- patience, concealment, decisive lethality -- is reflected in its redesign through the Ambush, Opportunist, Pressure, and Darkvision traits. Unlike the more reactive [[jolteon-volatile-temperament|Jolteon]], Umbreon's predatory approach is about control and timing.
