The [[bibarel_line|Bidoof -> Bibarel]] evolution condition is **Technology Education 1** — a single skill rank.

**Technology Education** captures the behavioral shift that defines evolution in this line: [[bidoof-unflappable-temperament|aimless gnawing]] becoming [[bibarel-dam-building|deliberate construction]]. Bidoof gnaws on everything indiscriminately. The moment a Bidoof starts solving problems with its gnawing — choosing which tree to fell, where to place debris, how to redirect water — it has crossed the threshold into Bibarel. Technology Education is the skill that represents this engineering instinct, and Bibarel's +4 in the stat shows how far the aptitude develops after evolution.

**Why so easy?** The BST jump is significant (26 → 43, delta 17), which would normally justify a harder condition per [[evolution-difficulty-scales-with-bst-jump]]. But Bibarel is a weak, undesirable Pokemon in the broader competitive landscape — those stats look large relative to Bidoof but are modest in absolute terms. A hard evolution gate on a Pokemon nobody is racing to evolve creates friction without purpose. The condition should feel like a natural coming-of-age, not an achievement.

**Wild viability:** Bidoof colonies live near water and gnaw constantly. A young Bidoof that starts arranging debris purposefully — even crudely — rather than gnawing at random has developed Technology Education 1. This happens naturally as Bidoof mature within their [[bidoof-line-communal-nesting|communal colonies]], especially when older Bibarel model construction behavior nearby.
