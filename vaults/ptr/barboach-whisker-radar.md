[[Barboach]] uses its barbels (whiskers) as a multi-sensory radar system. The whiskers detect prey movement in murky water where vision is useless, and can also function as taste organs — sensing chemical signatures the way a tongue or nose would. When Barboach buries itself in mud to ambush prey, it leaves only its whiskers exposed. This sensory specialization explains the Anticipation trait (detecting incoming threats through vibration) and the line's reliance on perception over sight, which [[Whiscash]] inherits as full Tremorsense.
