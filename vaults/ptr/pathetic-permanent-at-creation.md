PTR removes skill ranks and edges — see [[skill-ranks-removed]] and [[ptr-removes-features-edges-classes]].

In PTU, Pathetic skills chosen during character creation were permanent at creation time. No mechanism — including Skill Edges — could raise them during the creation process.

PTU pp.14 and 18 both explicitly prohibit raising Pathetic skills during creation, with p.18 specifically calling out Edges by name. This repeated, specific prohibition controls over p.41's general rank progression description.

The Pathetic-to-Untrained progression via Basic Skills Edge becomes available only after character creation (during leveling), when the Pathetic lock is no longer in effect.
