Shadow Force is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x3]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Set-Up.

Set-Up Effect: The user is removed from the field, and their turn ends. Resolution Effect: Shadow Force’s user appears adjacent to any legal target on the field, ignoring movement [[trait-definition|traits]], and then uses Shadow Force’s attack. Shadow Force cannot be avoided by Moves with the Shield Keyword, the Dodge trait, or similar effects, and Intercepts may not be attempted in response.

As a Daily x3 move, Shadow Force can be used in 3 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
