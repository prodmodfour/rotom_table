While Sandstorming, all non-Ground, Rock, or Steel Type Pokemon lose a Tick of HP at the beginning of their turn. Ability interactions: Sand Force grants +5 damage to Ground/Rock/Steel moves, Sand Rush grants +4 Speed CS, Desert Weather grants immunity to Sandstorm damage.

Sandstorm is one of four [[weather-as-game-keyword]] types. Like [[hail-weather-effects]], it deals passive damage to unprotected types, but protects a broader set of types (three vs one).
