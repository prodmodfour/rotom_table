Dynamax Cannon is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

The DB of Dynamax Cannon increases by +2 for each positive CS the target has, to a maximum of DB 20.
