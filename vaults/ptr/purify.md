Purify is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target is cured of all Permanent and Volatile Statuses. For every status removed, the user recovers a Tick of Hit Points.
