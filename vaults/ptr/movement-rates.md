Movement Rates are [[trait-definition|traits]] that define how far a character moves per [[movement-points|Movement Point]] spent in a given mode. Without a trait granting a movement mode, the rate is 0 and that mode is unavailable.

The six movement modes are:
- Land
- Sky
- Water
- Phase
- Burrow
- Teleporter

These replace PTU's movement capabilities (Overland, Swim, Sky, Levitate, Burrow, Teleporter).
