Nature's Madness is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 4, 1 Target, HP Loss.

The target loses half of their current Hit Points.
