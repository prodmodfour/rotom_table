Trainers have seven combat stats: HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, Speed, and [[stamina-stat|Stamina]]. These define all derived [[trait-definition|traits]] -- [[trainer-hp-formula]], [[evasion-from-defensive-stats]], and combat damage calculations all flow from these stats.
