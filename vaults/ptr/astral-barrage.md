Astral Barrage is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 8, Ranged Blast 2, Smite Friendly.

Legal targets hit by Astral Barrage are Slowed for one full round.

As a Daily move, Astral Barrage can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
