A Standard Action can be traded down: give up your Standard to take an extra Swift Action, or give up your Standard to take an extra Shift Action. However, the extra Shift cannot be used for movement if you already used your regular Shift for movement.

A Full Action consumes both the Standard and Shift Actions for the turn. Take a Breather, Coup de Grâce, and Intercept maneuvers are Full Actions.
