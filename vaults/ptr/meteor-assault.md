Meteor Assault is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 15, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1, Smite, Exhaust.

As a Daily x2 move, Meteor Assault can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
