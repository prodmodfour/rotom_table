Bound AP is not a daily counter — it is a persistent effect tied to specific game mechanics. It persists across extended rest (per [[extended-rest-drained-ap-only]]) and across New Day (per [[new-day-is-counter-reset-only]]). The GM must manually clear bound AP when the binding effect narratively ends.

This maintains the modular design where each mechanic is explicitly invoked, consistent with [[separate-mechanics-stay-separate]].
