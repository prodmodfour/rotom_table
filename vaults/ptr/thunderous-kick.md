Thunderous Kick is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

All legal targets hit lose 1 Defense Combat Stage. Once per Scene, Thunderous Kick may instead be used as an Electric-Type Move.
