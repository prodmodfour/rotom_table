Trainer skill does not influence what a bred Pokemon becomes. There are no education rank gates or skill checks that let a breeder choose the baby's traits, gender, or any other attribute. The breeding outcome is not controlled by the trainer.

This supersedes the PTU system where a breeder's Pokemon Education Rank determined whether they could choose Nature (Adept), Ability (Expert), or Gender (Master).
