Telekinesis is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

The target becomes Lifted. While Lifted, they gain the [[movement-traits|Flier]] [[trait-definition|trait]], are Slowed, and lose all movement traits except for the Sky 4 granted by Flier (reduced to 2 by the Slow condition). While Lifted, the user may not apply any Evasion bonuses to determine whether they are hit by Moves or not. The Lifted target may use a Shift Action to roll 1d20; on a result of 16+, they stop being Lifted.

Telekinesis grants the Telekinetic [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
