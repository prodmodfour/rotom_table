Explosion is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 25, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 2.

The user's HP is set to -50% of their full HP. This HP loss cannot be prevented or reduced in any way. The user's loyalty toward its trainer may be lowered.

As a Daily move, Explosion can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
