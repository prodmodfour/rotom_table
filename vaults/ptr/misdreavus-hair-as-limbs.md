Misdreavus lacks visible arms or legs — its lower body trails off into a ruffled, dress-like form. To compensate, it uses its long, flowing hair as functional appendages, grasping objects and even other Pokemon as if the hair were hands. This makes [[Misdreavus]] deceptively capable of physical manipulation despite its ethereal appearance.

The hair-as-limbs adaptation explains why Misdreavus has Natural Weapon [Tentacles] in its trait list rather than claws or arms — its primary physical interaction with the world is through prehensile hair tendrils.
