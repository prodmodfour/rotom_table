Haze is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field.

The Combat Stages of the user and all Pokémon and Trainers in the encounter are set to their default state (usually 0).
