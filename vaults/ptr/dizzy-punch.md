Dizzy Punch is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Dizzy Punch Confuses the target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
