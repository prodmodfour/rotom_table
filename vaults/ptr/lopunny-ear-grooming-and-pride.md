[[Lopunny]] is fastidious about grooming its long ears and never neglects their maintenance. The ears are sensitive both physically and as a point of dignity -- if anyone handles them roughly, Lopunny retaliates with powerful kicks described as comparable to a karate master's strikes. This behavioral pattern creates a clear social boundary: Lopunny is otherwise timid and cautious, cloaking its body with its ears or fleeing when threatened, but ear-handling crosses a line that triggers immediate aggression. The connection between [[buneary-ear-coil-mechanism|Buneary's ear-weapons]] and Lopunny's ear-pride suggests the ears remain central to the line's identity even as the primary combat style shifts from ear strikes to kicks.
