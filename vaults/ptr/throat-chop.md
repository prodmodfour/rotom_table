Throat Chop is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target cannot use Moves with the Sonic keyword for the next two turns.
