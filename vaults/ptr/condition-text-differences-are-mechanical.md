When PTU status conditions have different wording for their tick triggers, the exact text is respected. In base PTU, Burn and Poison fire on "takes or is prevented from taking a Standard Action." Cursed fires only on "takes a Standard Action" — the omission of the "prevented" clause is deliberate, not editorial.

PTR changes the timing for Burning and Poison to end of turn (see [[persistent-tick-timing-end-of-turn]]), but Cursed retains its original "takes a Standard Action" trigger. Cursed is a Volatile affliction (weaker persistence) that deals 2 ticks instead of 1. The tradeoff is that incapacitated targets don't take Cursed damage. This textual difference remains mechanical, not a drafting oversight.
