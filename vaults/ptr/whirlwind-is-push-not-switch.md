Whirlwind (PTU p.412) pushes targets, disperses Smokescreen, and destroys hazards. It has NO recall or forced-switch mechanic whatsoever. It is purely a push/displacement move.

Do not conflate displacement moves with forced-switch moves unless the move text explicitly includes recall mechanics. See [[roar-has-own-recall-mechanics]] for a move that does include them.
