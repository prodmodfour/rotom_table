Stone Axe is a Rock-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Stone Axe is a critical hit on an 18+. Once per scene, on a hit, the target may be put in a Vortex.
