Alolan Vulpix and Ninetales produce ice crystals from their fur. In Vulpix, the six tails generate crystals that glitter in the light — a visual display and a cooling mechanism. In Ninetales, the crystals serve as both insulation and weapons, projected as diamond dust to freeze intruders.

Alolan Vulpix also exhales air colder than -58°F (-50°C), and can freeze its own fur as a protective layer.

This biology is the basis for several of the line's traits: [[ice-creation]] (generating solid ice from their body), and the cryokinetic ambient control of [[ice-manipulation]].
