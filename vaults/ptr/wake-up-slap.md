Wake-Up Slap is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 5, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

If the target is Asleep, Wake-Up Slap has a Damage Base of 10 (3d8+10 / 24) instead, and cures the target of Sleep.
