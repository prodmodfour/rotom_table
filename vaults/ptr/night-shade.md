Night Shade is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 8, 1 Target.

The target loses HP equal to the level of Night Shade's user. Do not apply weakness or resistance. Do not apply stats.
