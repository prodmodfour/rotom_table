When [[Garchomp]] folds up its body and extends its wings, it resembles a jet plane and can fly at sonic speeds. It never allows prey to escape once a chase begins, and it hunts voraciously -- consuming entire flocks of bird Pokemon in a single outing. Its speed underground is also remarkable. When running, its wings generate wind blades that cut down nearby trees, reflected in the Supersonic Wind Blade trait. Garchomp is described as feral and extremely dangerous in the wild, justifying both its Apex Predator and Very Scary traits.
