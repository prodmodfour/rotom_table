Knock Off is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Choose one of the target's Held Items or Accessory Slot Items. It is knocked to the ground.
