Venom Drench is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Cone 2.

All Poisoned targets have their Attack, Special Attack, and Speed lowered by -1 CS. Venom Drench cannot miss.
