People once believed [[Houndoom]]'s howl was the call of the grim reaper. Its eerie vocalizations cause other Pokemon to shiver and flee to their nests — a supernatural intimidation effect that goes beyond simple loudness. Combined with its skull-marked appearance, curved horns, and [[houndoom-toxic-flames|flames that inflict permanent burns]], Houndoom embodies the hellhound archetype.

This sinister reputation is the basis for Houndoom's Intimidate-adjacent presence. In [[wild-encounter-motivations|encounter design]], a Houndoom pack encounter should feel ominous — the howling warns of their approach, and NPCs may associate them with death omens or curses.
