A Pokemon's Natural Move List includes all Moves gained from Level Up, all Egg Moves, and all Tutor Moves marked with (N). A Pokemon may fill as many of its [[six-move-slot-limit]] as it likes with Natural Moves. The [[three-tm-tutor-move-limit]] does not count against Natural Moves.

TM/Tutor moves can be reclassified as Natural via [[tm-to-natural-reclassification]].
