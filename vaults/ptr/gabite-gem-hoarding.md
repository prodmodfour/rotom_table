[[Gabite]] is obsessively attracted to shiny objects and hoards gems, treasures, and even [[Carbink]] in its nest. It will jealously attack [[Sableye]] that try to take Carbink as food, creating a three-way predator-prey-competitor dynamic. Despite this fierce possessiveness, offering Gabite a sparkly item like a glass bead can turn it docile -- a useful behavioral lever for trainers. This hoarding instinct is reflected in the Likes Shiny behavior trait shared across the line.
