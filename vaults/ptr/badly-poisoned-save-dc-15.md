Badly Poisoned has a DC 15 save check at the end of the afflicted target's turn. On success, Badly Poisoned is cured.

HP loss also moves to end of turn (see [[persistent-tick-timing-end-of-turn]]). All other Badly Poisoned mechanics are unchanged.

The DC sits between [[poisoned-save-dc-10|Poisoned's DC 10]] and [[burning-save-dc-18|Burning's DC 18]], reflecting its severity.
