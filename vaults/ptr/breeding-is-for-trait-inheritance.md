The purpose of breeding in PTR is to get specific [[innate-traits]] onto a baby Pokemon. A bred Pokemon can unlock traits that wouldn't be available to its species alone, drawn from its [[inheritable-traits-list]]. This replaces PTU's breeding model which focused on move inheritance and trainer-influenced trait selection.
