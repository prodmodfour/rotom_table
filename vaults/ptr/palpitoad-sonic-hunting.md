[[Palpitoad]] hunts by blasting prey with sound waves intense enough to cause headaches and disorientation, then snatching the weakened target with its long, sticky tongue. The larger a Palpitoad's head bumps, the wider the range of sounds it can produce — bump size directly correlates with acoustic versatility. This two-phase hunting method (debilitate with sound, capture with tongue) explains why Palpitoad gains the Vocal trait upon evolving from [[Tympole]]: its sound production shifts from communication to weaponized output, powerful enough to function as an offensive tool rather than just a signaling system.
