The Frozen status has a unique cure interaction: being hit by a damaging Fire, Fighting, Rock, or Steel attack automatically cures Frozen. This is in addition to the standard DC 16 save check at end of turn (DC 11 for Fire-types).

While Frozen, the target cannot act and receives no evasion bonuses — making them extremely vulnerable. The app should automate the Frozen cure check when damage of the qualifying types is applied.
