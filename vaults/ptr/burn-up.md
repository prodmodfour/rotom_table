Burn Up is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 13, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1, Smite.

Until the end of the encounter, the user loses its Fire-Type (pure Fire-Types become Normal-Type).

As a Daily x2 move, Burn Up can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
