Encounters serve two fundamental roles: taxing resources or threatening defeat. Tax encounters are unlikely to cause a party wipe but cost HP, PP, items, and move frequencies — they drain resources over time. Threat encounters genuinely risk defeat, capture, or incapacitation.

Designing tax and threat encounters in concert creates interesting player choices about resource conservation. This interacts with [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]] (tax encounters use lower budgets) and [[level-distribution-shapes-difficulty]] (concentrated distributions create threats; spread distributions create taxes).
