A Pokemon released mid-round can act that same round if the player has a Pokemon turn available. If the Pokemon's initiative count has already passed, it acts immediately after release.

This means tactical mid-round switching can enable a fresh Pokemon to act before opponents expect it. The app's initiative tracker must handle inserting a newly released Pokemon into the current round's turn order.
