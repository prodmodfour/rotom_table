Combat stages range from -6 to +6 but scale asymmetrically: each positive stage adds +20% to the stat, each negative stage subtracts only -10%. This means buffs are more impactful than debuffs — going from 0 to +6 multiplies by x2.2, while going from 0 to -6 only reduces to x0.4.

The full multiplier table: -6=x0.4, -5=x0.5, -4=x0.6, -3=x0.7, -2=x0.8, -1=x0.9, 0=x1.0, +1=x1.2, +2=x1.4, +3=x1.6, +4=x1.8, +5=x2.0, +6=x2.2.

This applies to Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed only — never HP. The app multiplies the base stat by the stage multiplier and rounds down per [[always-round-down]].
