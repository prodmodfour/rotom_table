While Hailing, all non-Ice Type Pokemon lose a Tick of HP at the beginning of their turn. Blizzard cannot miss in Hail. Ability interactions: Ice Body recovers a Tick per turn, Snow Cloak grants +2 Evasion and protects adjacent allies from Hail damage, Thermosensitive halves movement.

Hail is one of four [[weather-as-game-keyword]] types. It interacts with Frozen status via [[frozen-save-modified-by-weather]] (imposing a -2 penalty on save checks to cure Frozen).
