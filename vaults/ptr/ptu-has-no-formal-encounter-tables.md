PTU 1.05 does not define a mechanical system for random encounter tables. There are no weighted probability percentages, density tiers, random roll generation (no d100 tables), formalized level range formulas for specific areas, or sub-habitat modification mechanics.

What PTU provides: [[fourteen-canonical-habitats]] (qualitative reference), [[fun-game-progression]] + [[sensible-ecosystems]] (qualitative principles), [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]] (formulaic but GM-driven), and [[quick-stat-workflow]] (fast stat distribution).

The app's encounter table system (weighted spawn tables, density tiers, sub-habitat modifications, level range overrides) is GM tooling that operationalizes PTU's qualitative guidance into structured, reusable data. Per [[raw-fidelity-as-default]], these should be clearly labeled as app-specific heuristics rather than claimed as RAW.
