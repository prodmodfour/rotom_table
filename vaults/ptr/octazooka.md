Octazooka is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 3, and a range of 6,  1 Target.

Octazooka lowers the target's Accuracy by -1 on an Even-Numbered Roll.
