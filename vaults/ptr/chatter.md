Chatter is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target, Sonic.

Chatter confuses all targets on 16+. The 16+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
