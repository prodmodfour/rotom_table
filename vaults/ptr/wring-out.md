Wring Out is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

For every 10% the target is below their full HP, Wring Out's Damage Base is reduced by -1.
