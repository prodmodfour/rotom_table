At 5 or more injuries, a combatant becomes Heavily Injured. While Heavily Injured, taking a Standard Action or receiving damage from an attack causes HP loss equal to the current injury count.

This creates a death spiral: the more injured you are, the more each action costs. The app should automate this HP deduction whenever a Heavily Injured combatant takes a Standard Action or is hit.

Heavily Injured status also blocks rest healing — you cannot restore HP through rest with 5+ injuries.
