Constrict is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 1, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Lower the target’s Speed by -1 CS. Constrict may be used as a Swift Action against targets the user is Grappling and automatically hits when performed this way.
