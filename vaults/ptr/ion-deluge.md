Ion Deluge is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 5, Ranged Blast 3, Interrupt.

An ion cloud is dispersed in the targeted area. All Normal-Type Moves targeting into or originating from the area become Electric-Type Moves.
