Stamina is a stat added in PTR. It exists as both a base stat (species-defined) and an individual stat (modified through various sources). It is included in the [[base-stat-total]]. Base Stamina operates on a similar scale to other base stats.

Stamina derives [[energy-resource|Energy]], a combat resource — analogous to how HP derives health (see [[pokemon-hp-formula]]).
