The Slowed condition halves a Pokemon's Movement (minimum 1). It can be removed by switching out or at the end of a Scene as an Extended Action.

Slowed is distinct from [[slow-terrain-doubles-movement]] — Slow Terrain doubles movement cost per cell, while Slowed halves total movement budget. Both can stack, creating severe movement restriction. The app should apply Slowed as a modifier to the combatant's movement [[trait-definition|traits]], separate from terrain cost calculations.
