Imprison is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 10, 1 Target.

The target is Locked for the rest of the Scene. A Locked target may not use any Moves the user knows. Imprison cannot miss.
