The base capture rate starts at 100 and subtracts the target's level × 2. All other modifiers — [[hp-tiered-capture-modifiers]], [[evolution-stage-capture-modifiers]], [[status-capture-bonus-hierarchy]], [[rarity-capture-penalty]] — adjust from this base.

PTU p.214: "A Pokemon's Capture Rate depends on its Level, Hit Points, Status Afflictions, Evolutionary Stage, and Rarity. First, begin with 100. Then subtract the Pokemon's Level x2."
