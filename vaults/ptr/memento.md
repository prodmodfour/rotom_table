Memento is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 8, 1 Target, Trigger, Free Action.

Memento may be used as a Free Action that does not consume a Command action when the user becomes Fainted. Lower each of the target's stats by -2 CS.
