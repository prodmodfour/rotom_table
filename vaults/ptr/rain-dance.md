Rain Dance is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field, Weather.

The weather becomes Rainy for 5 rounds. While Rainy, Water-Type Attacks gain a +5 bonus to Damage Rolls, and Fire-Type Attacks suffer a -5 Damage penalty.

As a Daily x2 move, Rain Dance can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
