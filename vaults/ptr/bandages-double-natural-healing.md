Bandages are applied as Extended Actions. While applied, they double the [[natural-healing-rate]] — healing 1/8th of max HP per half hour instead of 1/16th. Bandages last for 6 hours.

This stacking is explicit in PTU. See [[bandages-heal-injury-after-full-duration]] for the injury benefit and [[bandages-break-on-damage]] for the fragility constraint.
