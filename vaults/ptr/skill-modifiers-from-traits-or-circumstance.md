Skill modifiers in PTR are gained through [[trait-definition|Traits]] or Circumstance Bonuses. [[ptr-removes-features-edges-classes|Features and Edges no longer exist]], so they no longer serve as prerequisites for skills.

This replaces the old rank-based progression where [[skill-ranks-removed|skill ranks]] determined dice pools.
