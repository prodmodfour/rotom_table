During a [[training-dual-check-system|training session]], the Trainer chooses one of five social skills for their check: Command, Charm, Intimidate, Persuasion, or Deception.

The [[learned-traits|Learned Trait]] defines a base Trainer DC. This DC is then modified by the [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|Pokemon's social skill hierarchy]], which adjusts the DC based on which social skill the Trainer chose.
