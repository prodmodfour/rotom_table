"Scene X" means a move can be performed X times per Scene. A move with just "Scene" frequency (no number) can be used once per Scene. Scene is the middle tier of PTU's frequency hierarchy, between At-Will/EOT and Daily.

Scene frequency interacts with [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction]] (multi-use scene moves still alternate turns) and [[daily-moves-once-per-scene]] (daily moves are capped at once per scene). The definition of when a scene begins and ends is governed by [[scene-boundary-resets-frequencies]].
