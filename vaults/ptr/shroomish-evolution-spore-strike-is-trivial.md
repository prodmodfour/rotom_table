"Deal damage to a target you inflicted a status condition on" is a [[evolution-easy-not-trivial|trivial]] evolution condition for [[Shroomish]]. All Pokemon combat involves attacking, and Shroomish's [[shroomish-spore-defense-mechanism|spore defense]] means it will inflict status conditions on anything that touches it via [[effect-spore-design|Effect Spore]]. This condition would be met in virtually every combat encounter — making wild Shroomish populations ecologically impossible, since every specimen would evolve after its first real fight.
