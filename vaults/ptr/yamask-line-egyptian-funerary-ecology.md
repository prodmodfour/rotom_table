The [[Yamask]] line is ecologically tied to ancient tombs, ruins, and desert burial sites. Yamask wanders the ruins of ancient civilizations, possibly seeking traces of its past identity. [[Cofagrigus]] inhabits tombs where it waits for intruders, sustaining itself on gold nuggets. The entire line draws from Egyptian funerary culture -- Yamask embodies the ba (the soul's wandering aspect) while Cofagrigus embodies the sarcophagus that houses and protects the dead. Their Desert habitat and Terravore diet in the redesigns reflect this tomb-dwelling ecological niche.
