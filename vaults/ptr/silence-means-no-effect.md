When PTU is silent on a mechanic, it does not apply. If PTU does not say extended rest clears Bound AP, then extended rest does not clear Bound AP. If PTU does not say New Day includes an implicit extended rest, then it does not.

This is why [[new-day-is-counter-reset-only]] and [[bound-ap-persists-until-unbound]] and [[extended-rest-drained-ap-only]] work the way they do — PTU's silence is itself informative.

Related: when PTU wants a move to bypass the Trapped condition, it says so explicitly. Absence of such text means Trapped applies — see [[roar-blocked-by-trapped]].
