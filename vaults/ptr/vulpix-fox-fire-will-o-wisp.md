[[Vulpix]] can manipulate fire with enough precision to create small, floating wisps of flame. These hovering lights are sometimes mistaken by humans for ghosts or spirits — the origin of the "fox fire" phenomenon in folklore.

This ability is the direct basis for the Fox Fire trait on both [[Vulpix]] and [[Ninetales]]. It also connects to the broader supernatural mystique of the line: even before evolution, Vulpix blurs the line between natural animal and something uncanny.
