A Tripped combatant must spend a Shift Action standing up before taking further actions. This makes Tripped a powerful tactical condition — it doesn't just slow movement, it consumes the entire Shift Action for the turn.

The app should mark Tripped combatants visually on the grid and enforce that their first Shift Action goes to standing. Standing up from Tripped also triggers an [[attack-of-opportunity-trigger-list|Attack of Opportunity]] from adjacent foes.
