Alolan Ninetales will guide lost travelers to safety on its mountain — but this behavior is territorial, not benevolent. It acts to prevent disturbance to its domain, not out of compassion. It creates ice crystals to block the path of anyone approaching uninvited, and freezes trespassers with diamond dust.

Even when partnered with a trainer who has earned its trust, Alolan Ninetales remains fundamentally wild and independent. It cooperates on its own terms.

This behavioral profile matters for [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven|encounter design]]: an Alolan Ninetales encounter on its home turf is a territorial confrontation, not a predator hunt. It warns before it attacks, and it escalates only when its boundaries are violated.
