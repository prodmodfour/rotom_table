Setting an encounter indoors or near fragile objects and innocent bystanders forces players to limit their powerful area-of-effect attacks. Even single-target attacks like Flamethrower become dangerous in a wooden shack. This is an encounter design tool that shifts difficulty through tactical constraint rather than enemy power.

Collateral damage environments work alongside [[environment-modifies-encounter-difficulty]] and [[tax-vs-threat-encounters]] — even a weak encounter becomes threatening when the players must fight with restraint.
