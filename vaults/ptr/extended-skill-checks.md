Extended skill checks model complex, multi-step tasks. The DC is set normally, then multiplied by a factor of 2–5 based on task complexity. The trainer must accumulate the total across multiple rolls, with attempts limited to half their rank in the tested skill.

This creates a natural tension: higher-ranked characters both roll better AND get more attempts, making expertise doubly impactful.
