String Shot is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, At-Will frequency, AC 3, and a range of Cone 2.

Lower the Speed of all legal targets by -1 CS. If this lowers their Speed CS to -6, or if their Speed CS was already at -6, they are instead Stuck.

String Shot grants the Threaded [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
