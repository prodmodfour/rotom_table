Enemy moveset quality should scale with encounter significance. Low-significance encounters use only level-up moves and basic [[trait-definition|traits]]. Higher-significance encounters feature Egg, TM, and Tutor moves plus strategically chosen traits that cover weaknesses.

This is a qualitative axis of [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven]] that operates alongside the quantitative [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]] and within [[significance-cap-x5]].
