Enemy moveset quality should scale with encounter significance. Low-significance encounters use simple, easy-to-unlock moves and basic [[trait-definition|traits]]. Higher-significance encounters feature moves with harder [[unlock-conditions]] plus strategically chosen traits that cover weaknesses.

This is a qualitative axis of [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven]] that operates alongside the quantitative [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]] and within [[significance-cap-x5]].
