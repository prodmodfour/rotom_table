In dark environments, light sources create defined radii of illumination. A standard lantern or Small/Medium Pokemon with Glow creates a Burst 2 of unimpeded visibility. Large Pokemon with Glow and powerful artificial sources generate Burst 3, Huge Pokemon generate Burst 4. The Illuminate ability adds 1 to any light source's Burst radius.

Beyond the illuminated area, every meter between a character and their target imposes -2 to Accuracy Rolls and Perception Checks — but illuminated squares do not count toward this total. This interacts with [[raw-darkness-penalties-with-presets]] for the overall darkness system.
