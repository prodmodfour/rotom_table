Mirror Move is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with a variable Damage Base (see effect), [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Illusion.

Use the Move the target has used on their last turn. You may choose new targets for the Move. Mirror Move cannot miss.
