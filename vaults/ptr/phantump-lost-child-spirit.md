[[Phantump]] is born when the spirit of a child who became lost in the forest and died possesses a tree stump. Its cries sound like a child's voice, and it imitates children's voices to lure people deeper into the forest -- but according to Pokedex entries, it does this because it is attempting to make friends, not out of malice. This tragic origin means Phantump is a confused, lonely ghost child that inadvertently causes harm through the only social behavior it remembers. For tabletop encounters, this creates a potent moral dilemma: the creature causing travelers to become hopelessly lost is itself a lost child seeking companionship.
