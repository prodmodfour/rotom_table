Dragon Breath is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 6, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Dragon Breath Paralyzes the target on 15+. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
