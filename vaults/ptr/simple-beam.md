Simple Beam is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

You choose one of the target's Abilities. Simple Beam changes that Ability to Simple for the remainder of the encounter.
