Alolan Ninetales is revered in Alolan culture as the incarnation of a mountain deity. It lives on the snowy peak of Mount Lanakila, and the people of the region treat it as sacred.

This divine or spiritual association is the basis for its Fairy typing — it is not fey in the folkloric sense, but a creature whose supernatural presence inspires awe and myth. Its [[telepath]] trait reflects this otherworldly nature, as does its [[alolan-ninetales-territorial-guardian|guardian behavior]] on the mountain.

Regular [[Ninetales]] shares this supernatural mystique — both forms are steeped in legend and possess abilities beyond what their biology alone would explain.
