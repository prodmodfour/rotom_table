Magikarp's signature leaping behavior connects to the East Asian legend of the Dragon Gate — a mythological waterfall that transforms any carp that successfully leaps over it into a dragon. A long-lived [[Magikarp]] can build enough splashing power to leap over mountains, and this persistent, desperate leaping is what eventually triggers evolution into [[Gyarados]]. The myth frames Magikarp's apparent uselessness as potential rather than weakness: every futile splash is practice for the one leap that changes everything. This is why Magikarp sits in the Dragon egg group despite being a pure Water type — the draconic potential is latent in every specimen.
