Each of [[Ninetales]]'s nine tails holds a distinct mystical power. Together, they store the energy that sustains its thousand-year lifespan. The tails are not merely ornamental — they are the source and reservoir of its supernatural abilities, from [[ninetales-thousand-year-curse|cursing]] to [[ninetales-kitsune-intelligence|hypnosis and telepathy]].

This is a direct reflection of the kitsune myth, where a fox's power is measured by the number of its tails. [[Vulpix]]'s [[vulpix-tail-growth-and-fire|tail-splitting process]] is the early stage of this accumulation — six tails becoming nine marks the threshold where a natural animal becomes something legendary.
