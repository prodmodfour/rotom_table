Murkrow have an compulsive attraction to shiny objects — gems, coins, jewelry, anything that glitters. They will steal rings from people's fingers and raid other species' stashes. [[Murkrow]] and Meowth are known to loot each other's hoards in overlapping territories. This obsessive collecting behavior is so consuming that Murkrow sometimes fall asleep before they can hide all their treasures.

Despite the compulsion, the behavior has a social dimension: a Murkrow that bonds with a trainer will gift its shiniest finds to them. In the wild, the hoards ultimately flow upward — [[Honchkrow]] sleep atop massive gem collections gathered by their subordinate Murkrow flocks.
