[[Smoliv]] produces a bitter, astringent oil that is too unpleasant for consumption but serves as an effective defensive mechanism. When startled or attacked, Smoliv shoots this oil at its opponent to slow them down, then flees. The oil is foul enough to make targets flinch on contact. This flee-and-spray tactic is Smoliv's primary survival strategy, compensating for its very low Speed and fragile body. As the line matures, the oil's composition shifts -- [[Dolliv]]'s oil becomes pleasant and shareable, and [[Arboliva]]'s becomes a healing resource, but Smoliv's version remains purely defensive.
