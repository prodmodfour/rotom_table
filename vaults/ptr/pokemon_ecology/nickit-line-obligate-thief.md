Unlike most Pokemon that steal opportunistically, the Nickit line has evolved to rely on theft as its primary survival strategy. [[Nickit]] follows other Pokemon that have already foraged and takes their food rather than finding its own. It lacks the combat ability to take food by force — its base Attack and Defense are among the lowest of any Dark-type — so stealth and timing are existential necessities, not optional tactics.

[[Thievul]] refines this into a professional operation: marking prey with scent, stalking patiently, and striking only when the target is completely unguarded. This obligate-thief niche is the biological basis for the line's [[kleptomaniac|Kleptomaniac]], Pickpocket, Stakeout, and Unburden traits — each one represents a different facet of a lifestyle built entirely around taking what others have gathered.
