The [[Skorupi]] line hunts primarily at night and in low-light environments. Skorupi's compound eyes are adapted for darkness — it emerges from its buried ambush position at dusk and is most active through the night when desert and forest floors cool and prey species become vulnerable. [[Drapion]] continues this nocturnal pattern, hunting through the dark hours when its darkvision gives it a significant advantage over prey that cannot see it coming.
