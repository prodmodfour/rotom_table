The [[barboach_line|Barboach -> Whiscash]] evolution conditions are **Earth Manipulation 2** AND **claim a pond for 3 days with no other Water-type Pokemon present**.

**Earth Manipulation 2** is a low trait gate — Barboach starts at Earth Manipulation 1, so it only needs one rank of growth. This represents the early development of the seismic ability that will eventually define Whiscash (Earth Manipulation 4, Tremorsense 10, the Namazu earthquake myth). The gate is intentionally modest because the territory claim carries the real difficulty.

**Claiming a pond for 3 days** is the primary condition and captures Whiscash's defining identity: a single predator that owns an entire body of water. The exclusion clause (no other Water-type Pokemon) means the Barboach must drive out or deter competitors — proof that it has the aggression and physical presence to transition from a hiding prey animal (Starter, Herbivore) to a territorial apex predator (Territorial 3 1, Carnivore, Gluttony). The 3-day duration prevents a lucky ambush from counting; the Barboach must sustain its claim.

**Wild viability:** wild Barboach naturally inhabit freshwater and marshes. As they grow, competition for resources intensifies. A Barboach that finds a small pond and successfully holds it against other water-dwellers is demonstrating exactly the behavioral shift that defines Whiscash. This explains why wild Whiscash are found alone in ponds — each one earned its territory before evolving.

**BST jump (30 → 48, jump of 18):** this is a large jump for a two-stage line, comparable to Shroomish → Breloom. The combined trait + duration + territorial defense condition is appropriately demanding without being exotic or trainer-dependent.
