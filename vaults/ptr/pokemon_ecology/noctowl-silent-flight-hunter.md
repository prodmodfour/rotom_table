[[Noctowl]]'s [[noctowl-nocturnal-hunter-senses|nocturnal hunting]] relies on near-silent flight. Like real owls, its wing structure dampens turbulence, allowing it to glide within striking distance of prey without alerting them. Combined with [[apex-predator|Apex Predator]] perception, this makes Noctowl one of the most effective ambush hunters among bird Pokemon. Stealth +4 reflects this silent-flight capability, up from [[Hoothoot]]'s +3.
