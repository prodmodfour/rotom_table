[[Hoothoot]] possesses a special internal organ that senses the Earth's rotation, giving it a precise and unfailing sense of time. It begins crying at exactly the same time every day and always tilts its head in the same rhythm. This biological clock is the basis for the line's Insomnia trait -- their awareness of temporal cycles makes them resistant to unnatural sleep. Historically, people raised Hoothoot to serve as living clocks, a practice some trainers continue today.
