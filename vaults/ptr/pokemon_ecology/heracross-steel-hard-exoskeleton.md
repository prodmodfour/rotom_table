[[Heracross]] possesses a blue exoskeleton described as hard as steel, providing natural armor across its entire body. This chitinous plating enables it to inhabit boreal, temperate, and tropical forests — a remarkably broad habitat range for a Bug-type — because the exoskeleton protects against both physical threats and varied environmental conditions. The [[shell|Shell]] 2 trait reflects this durable integument, though at a lower value than dedicated shell-bearers like [[Crustle]], since Heracross relies more on offensive power than passive defense.
