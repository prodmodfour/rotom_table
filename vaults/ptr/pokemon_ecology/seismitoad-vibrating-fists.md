[[Seismitoad]] can vibrate the bumps on its hands to dramatically increase its punching power, while the bumps on its head shoot a paralyzing liquid. This split specialization — offensive amplification in the limbs, crowd control from the head — makes Seismitoad a versatile close-range combatant. The species is also known to wield venom and is related to [[Croagunk]] and [[Toxicroak]], suggesting shared ancestry among ground-dwelling poisonous toads. This venomous connection explains the Poison Touch trait, while the vibrating fists justify its high Attack (10) and the Arms/Hands traits it gains at final evolution.
