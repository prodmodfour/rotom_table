[[Houndour]] hunts in coordinated packs with sophisticated communication. During pursuit, pack members use specialized barks that are intelligible only to other Houndour — a private tactical language that prey and rivals cannot decode. The pack works together to corner prey, using positioning and relay to exhaust targets.

This coordinated behavior is the basis for the Pack Hunt trait on both [[Houndour]] and [[Houndoom]]. It also justifies the line's high Stealth and Perception skills — effective pack hunting requires awareness of both prey and packmates. Unlike the cooperative-but-passive [[alolan-vulpix-skulk-social-structure|skulk structure]] of Alolan Vulpix, Houndour packs are actively predatory.
