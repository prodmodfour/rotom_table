[[Flareon]] possesses a flame chamber inside its body that maintains an average body temperature between 1,300 and 1,700 degrees Fahrenheit. By inhaling air and storing it in this sac, Flareon can exhale flames exceeding 3,000 degrees Fahrenheit. This organ is a product of [[eevee-line-unstable-genetics|Eevee's genetic restructuring]] upon exposure to a Fire Stone -- an entirely new internal system that did not exist in the base form. The [[flash-fire|Flash Fire]] and Firestarter traits in its redesign reflect this biological furnace, while [[flareon-fur-thermoregulation|its fur]] handles the critical problem of not cooking itself alive.
