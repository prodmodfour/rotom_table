The green leaves on [[Phantump]]'s head can be brewed into medicine capable of curing any illness. However, the leaves wither instantly when plucked, making them extremely difficult to harvest and process. This creates a natural resource tension -- Phantump's leaves are perhaps the most potent curative material in the Pokemon world, but obtaining them requires finding a reclusive forest ghost and harvesting from it before the leaves decay. The Natural Cure and Harvest traits both connect to this regenerative botanical property.
