[[Jolteon]]'s fur consists of electrically charged needles that serve both offensive and defensive roles. When angered or startled, each strand stiffens into a sharp quill capable of piercing flesh. Jolteon can also fire these electrified quills in small volleys, functioning as a ranged missile barrage. This fur doubles as the amplification stage for electricity produced by its [[jolteon-lung-electricity-organ|lung organ]] -- the static charge held in the needles magnifies the body's base electrical output into devastating attacks. The Spines trait directly reflects this quill-fur system.
