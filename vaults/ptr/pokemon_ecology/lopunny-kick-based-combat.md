[[Lopunny]] fights primarily with its legs, delivering kicks powerful enough to match a trained martial artist's strikes. Where [[Buneary]] relies on its [[buneary-ear-coil-mechanism|coiled ears]] as spring-loaded weapons, Lopunny's longer legs and higher center of gravity make kicks the more effective option. Its fighting style emphasizes speed and precision over raw force — rapid low kicks to sweep opponents off balance, followed by powerful leaping strikes. The ears shift from primary weapons to auxiliary tools: balancing counterweights during kicks, sensory organs for reading an opponent's movement, and defensive shields when Lopunny wraps them around its body to retreat.
