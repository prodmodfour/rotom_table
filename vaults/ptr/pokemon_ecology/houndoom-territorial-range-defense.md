[[Houndoom]] packs aggressively claim and defend defined territories — cave systems, mountain passes, and the surrounding hunting grounds. Once established, the [[houndoom-pack-alpha-hierarchy|alpha leads confrontation]] against any intruder that enters the range. Unlike [[Houndour]]'s [[houndour-dawn-howl-territory|dawn howling]], which announces boundaries passively, Houndoom will physically drive out trespassers that ignore the warning.

This territorial defense behavior is the basis for the Territorial [2] [1] trait on [[Houndoom]]. The territory is campsite-sized (Y = 1), reflecting the pack's core denning area rather than their full hunting range.
