[[Magikarp]] occupies the bottom of the aquatic food chain. Its leaping behavior — jumping out of the water with Splash — makes it conspicuously visible to aerial predators like Pidgeotto and Pidgeot. Underwater, Frillish prey on it as well. Because Magikarp cannot swim against currents, it drifts downstream into whatever waters will have it, making it ubiquitous in lakes, rivers, ponds, and even oceans. Its universal availability as prey and its extreme abundance make it a foundational species in aquatic ecosystems — the thing nearly everything else eats.
