[[Impidimp]]'s compulsive mischief is not mere personality -- it is a feeding strategy. Impidimp sneaks into homes, steals belongings, and pesters occupants specifically to provoke the annoyance it needs to survive. Groups of Impidimp practice troublemaking together, suggesting social learning of [[impidimp-line-emotion-feeding|emotion-harvesting]] techniques. This reframes what looks like behavioral disorder as predatory behavior: each stolen item and each broken rule is the equivalent of a predator stalking prey. The Kleptomaniac and Pickpocket traits on its [[Impidimp|species]] are mechanical expressions of this biological imperative, and its Frisk trait lets it identify which items are worth stealing to cause maximum frustration.
