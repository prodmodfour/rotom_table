Every member of the Gible line has horn-like appendages on its head that resemble jet or plane engines. These protrusions are not merely ornamental -- they function as sensory organs. In [[Gabite]], they emit ultrasound waves used for navigating pitch-dark caves, granting it Echolocation. In [[Garchomp]], they act as long-range sensors that detect distant prey, supporting its Apex Predator trait. The jet-engine shape foreshadows [[Garchomp]]'s eventual ability to fold its body into a jet-like silhouette and fly at sonic speeds.
