[[Eevee]] is extremely rare in the wild and is most commonly found in cities and towns rather than wilderness. This urban preference, combined with its rarity, makes it a species that is far more likely to be encountered through breeders or as a companion Pokemon than stumbled upon in the field. Its [[eevee-physiological-plasticity|physiological plasticity]] may contribute to this -- a species so responsive to environmental cues thrives best in stable, human-adjacent settings where it is not constantly bombarded by stimuli that could trigger unwanted change.
