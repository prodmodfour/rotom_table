[[Doublade]] consists of two swords that communicate via telepathy to coordinate complex attack patterns in tandem. Their synchronized strikes are described as unstoppable even for accomplished swordsmen. The blades can fight both offensively and defensively simultaneously, and produce an unnerving metallic sound when rubbed together. This telepathic coordination is the key distinction from [[Honedge]] -- evolution does not just add a second blade, it creates a unified combat intelligence distributed across two bodies.
