[[Glaceon]] can rapidly lower its body temperature to negative 75 degrees Fahrenheit, freezing its own fur into needle-sharp icicles that it fires at threats. This cryogenic self-weaponization parallels [[jolteon-electrified-quill-fur|Jolteon's electrified quill fur]] -- both Eeveelutions convert their fur into projectile weapons, but through entirely different physical mechanisms. The Frostbite and Winter's Kiss traits reflect this freezing-on-contact biology, while Snow Cloak captures how its icy body blends into cold environments.
