[[Vespiquen]] controls her [[Combee]] colony through pheromone production. The volume and potency of her pheromone output directly determines colony size — a stronger Vespiquen commands a larger hive. This chemical authority is the biological basis for her [[combee-command|Combee Command]] trait, which reflects the ability to marshal and direct Combee through pheromone signals. Beyond chemical signaling, Vespiquen can communicate telepathically with Combee that serve her within a 3 mile radius, allowing her to coordinate foraging parties and defensive responses far beyond the hive. Vespiquen only enters direct combat when her workers cannot handle a threat themselves.
