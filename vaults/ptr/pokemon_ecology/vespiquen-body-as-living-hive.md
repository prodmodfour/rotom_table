[[Vespiquen]]'s abdomen functions as a living nursery — seven hexagonal honeycomb cells house developing [[Combee]] grubs within her body. This is not merely cosmetic: the grubs emerge to counterattack when Vespiquen is threatened, which is the biological basis for her Order moves ([[attack-order]], [[defend-order]], [[heal-order]]). She feeds on flowers to produce honey that nourishes her offspring. Intruders caught entering the hive are destroyed and consumed, but Vespiquen may show mercy to outsiders who demonstrate loyalty by defending her colony — a rare conditional trust behavior among Bug-types.
