[[Mismagius]] is a natural deceiver and performer. Its [[mismagius-incantation-magic|cries function as spoken spells]] that can induce hallucinations, headaches, or euphoria — the line between performance and manipulation is nonexistent. Its [[mismagius-love-spell-reputation|love spell reputation]] means humans actively seek it out, which requires Mismagius to read and exploit emotional vulnerabilities.

The [[sorcery|Sorcery]] trait channels through vocalizations (Sound [Vocal, Singer]), making every spell an act of performance. Deception +3 reflects the illusory manipulation; Performance +3 reflects the vocal artistry that delivers it.

This is a significant shift from [[Misdreavus]], which relies on crude startles to feed on fear. Mismagius refines that into calculated misdirection.
