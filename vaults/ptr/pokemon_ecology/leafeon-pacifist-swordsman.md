[[Leafeon]] is temperamentally pacifistic and avoids conflict when possible. However, when its companions are threatened, it sharpens its leaf-like tail into a blade hard enough to fell a large tree in a single strike -- sharper than anything humans can forge. This paradox of a gentle creature with devastating cutting ability defines Leafeon's combat identity: it fights reluctantly but decisively. The Blade trait captures this, and its high Attack and Defense stats reflect a body built to both endure and deliver powerful physical blows when peace fails.
