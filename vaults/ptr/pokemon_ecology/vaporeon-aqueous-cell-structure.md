[[Vaporeon]]'s cellular structure is remarkably similar to water molecules, a direct result of [[eevee-line-unstable-genetics|Eevee's genetic restructuring]] upon exposure to a Water Stone. This adaptation allows Vaporeon to meld seamlessly into bodies of water, becoming effectively invisible. It exploits this camouflage as a hunting strategy, lurking unseen in clear freshwater to ambush fish Pokemon that swim too close. The Amorphous trait reflects this water-like cellular composition -- its body can partially dissolve its boundaries with surrounding water.
