[[Shiftry]]'s three broad leaves on each hand function as organic fans capable of generating winds approaching 100 feet per second (roughly 70 mph). This wind generation is the mechanical basis for its Wind Manipulation 3 trait and its signature role as a forest weather-maker. Shiftry leads groups of [[Seedot]] and [[Nuzleaf]] from the treetops of ancient forests, and its ability to read opponents' minds lets it deploy these gale-force winds preemptively. The progression from [[seedot-parasitic-hydration|Seedot clinging to branches]] to [[nuzleaf-leaf-flute|Nuzleaf playing eerie melodies]] to Shiftry commanding storms reflects the line's escalation from passive plant to active force of nature.
