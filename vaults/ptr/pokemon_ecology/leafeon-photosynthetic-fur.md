[[Leafeon]]'s fur cells are closer in nature to plant cells than animal cells, enabling it to photosynthesize. It gets its energy from sunlight rather than eating, sleeping in sunny spots to fuel itself. This is the most radical dietary adaptation among the Eeveelutions -- where other members of the line are omnivores or herbivores, Leafeon is essentially a phototroph. The Geovore and Photovore traits in its redesign reflect this dual plant-animal metabolism. Like [[espeon-solar-psychic-power|Espeon]], Leafeon is sun-dependent, but for nutrition rather than psychic power.
