The [[Honedge]] line originates when a [[honedge-spirit-is-killing-intent|warrior's residual killing intent]] inhabits a sword, animating it as a sentient weapon. This makes them tsukumogami -- objects that have gained a spirit and become alive. Unlike most Ghost-types that are spirits taking ethereal form, the Honedge line is anchored to a physical steel body, which is why they are Steel/Ghost rather than pure Ghost. This dual nature -- physical weapon inhabited by spectral will -- is what makes the [[living-weapon]] trait mechanically coherent: the spirit provides intent while the blade provides lethality.
