[[Espeon]] accumulates psychic energy by bathing in sunlight, storing it in the red gem embedded in its forehead. The gem glows when Espeon channels this energy and darkens when reserves are depleted. This solar dependency makes Espeon fundamentally diurnal -- it is significantly weaker at night when it cannot recharge. The gem functions as both a battery and a focusing lens for psychic output, which is why Espeon's power is concentrated and directed rather than diffuse. This sun-driven cycle may explain why [[eevee-line-unstable-genetics|Eevee's evolution]] into Espeon requires sustained daytime bonding rather than a stone.
