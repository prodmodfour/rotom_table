[[Drapion]] possesses hard, armor-like skin tough enough to deflect most strikes, and its head rotates a full 180 degrees to eliminate blind spots. Its claws can crush a car, and the tips secrete [[poison-includes-venom|poison]] — but Drapion takes pride in overpowering foes through raw physical strength, often foregoing venom entirely. This preference for brute force over toxins is unusual for a Poison-type and explains why the line gains the Dark type upon evolution from [[Skorupi]].

Known as the "Sand Demon" in its desert habitats, Drapion is a roaming predator that actively seeks out powerful prey. Where [[Skorupi]] waits patiently in ambush, Drapion travels long distances to find and challenge strong opponents, driven by an instinct to kill rather than to defend a fixed range. Its massive armored frame, gaping pincers, and aggressive posture make it one of the most visually intimidating predators in its ecosystem — most creatures flee on sight. Even Drapion is pragmatic enough to avoid picking fights with Hippowdon, suggesting it can gauge when a target outclasses it despite its aggression.
