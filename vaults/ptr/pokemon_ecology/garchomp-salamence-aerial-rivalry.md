[[Garchomp]] sometimes battles Salamence in the air as both species compete for the same food sources. This aerial rivalry between two pseudo-legendary Dragon-types creates a natural territorial tension in regions where their habitats overlap. Both are apex aerial predators -- Garchomp relying on jet-like speed and ground-type durability, Salamence on raw draconic aggression and maneuverability.
