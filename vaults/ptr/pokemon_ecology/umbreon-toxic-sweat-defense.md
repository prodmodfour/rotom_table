When threatened, [[Umbreon]] sprays poisonous sweat from its pores, targeting the attacker's eyes. This toxic secretion is its primary defensive mechanism -- rather than relying on raw offensive power, Umbreon uses chemical deterrence to create openings for escape or counterattack. This defensive poison biology is unusual for a Dark-type and explains the Synchronize trait in its redesign: an attacker who makes contact with Umbreon risks being afflicted by the same debilitating conditions it endures.
