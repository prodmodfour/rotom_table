[[Cofagrigus]] traps grave robbers who mistake it for a real coffin by sealing them inside its sarcophagus body and transforming them into mummies. Its body is said to be made of solid gold, which both attracts tomb raiders and serves as camouflage among genuine burial treasures. The four shadowy hands emerging from its body are used to grab and pull victims inside. This predatory behavior justifies the Mummy trait mechanically -- contact with Cofagrigus transfers the mummification curse, reflecting how physical proximity to the sarcophagus is inherently dangerous. The Shackle trait also maps directly to its ability to trap and hold creatures within its body.
