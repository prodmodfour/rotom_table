Both [[Barboach]] and [[Whiscash]] secrete a slimy film over their bodies. For Barboach, this coating serves two survival functions: it makes the body slippery enough to escape grasping predators like bird Pokemon, and it acts as a biological barrier preventing germs and parasites in muddy water from penetrating the skin. If the slime dries out, Barboach weakens rapidly — binding it to aquatic or at least damp environments. This explains the line's Hydration trait and its poor Landwalker speed (2 for Barboach, 3 for Whiscash).
