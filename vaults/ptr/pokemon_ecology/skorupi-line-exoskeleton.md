The [[Skorupi]] line possesses a rigid chitinous exoskeleton that covers the entire body. Even as a juvenile, Skorupi's carapace is thick enough to shrug off glancing blows — a necessity for a predator that buries itself in sand and must endure being stepped on or struck by thrashing prey while maintaining its grip. The exoskeleton hardens further as the line matures into [[Drapion]], whose armor-plated skin is described as tough enough to deflect most attacks outright.
