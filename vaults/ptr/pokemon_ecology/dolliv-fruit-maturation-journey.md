[[Dolliv]] exhibits a distinctive life-stage behavior: it basks in the sun near human settlements, sharing its pleasant-scented [[smoliv-line-oil-production|oil]] with people and Pokemon alike, until the olive fruits on its head fully ripen. Once maturation is complete, Dolliv departs the settlement and sets off on a solitary journey. This migratory instinct at reproductive maturity echoes real olive trees, which require years of growth before bearing fruit, and suggests that Dolliv's wandering phase is a necessary precursor to evolving into [[Arboliva]]. The pattern also explains why Dolliv is historically found near human communities but Arboliva tends to be encountered in wilder settings -- one is still growing, the other has already completed its pilgrimage. This migratory compulsion is represented by the [[wanderer]] trait.
