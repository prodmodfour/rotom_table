The [[Shroomish]] → [[Breloom]] evolution conditions require Combat 5, Athletics 5, Acrobatics 5, and Focus 7. These thresholds gate on [[shroomish-evolution-demands-discipline|discipline and trained combat behavior]], not a single moment of aggression.

Shroomish's species skills are Combat +2, Athletics +2, Acrobatics +2, and Focus +3. Every threshold is set well above the species baseline — a Shroomish cannot evolve just by being a Shroomish. It must develop beyond its innate capabilities through repeated practice or hard experience.

The four skills map to the four dimensions of Breloom's [[breloom-spore-then-strike-combat|boxing discipline]]:
- **Combat 5** — fighting technique, not flailing
- **Athletics 5** — the physical conditioning to close distance and deliver power
- **Acrobatics 5** — the light, springy footwork that Breloom is known for
- **Focus 7** — the highest threshold, because the core of the transformation is mental. A Shroomish that can concentrate under pressure — holding composure instead of panic-sporing — has crossed the line from reactive prey to deliberate fighter

Focus 7 is the hardest gate and the one most likely to be the bottleneck. This is intentional. The [[shroomish-line-fungal-growth-arc|fungal growth arc]] is fundamentally about a creature that survives by instinct developing the mental discipline to fight with purpose. The physical skills are prerequisites, but Focus is the signal.

The [[evolution-difficulty-scales-with-bst-jump|BST jump from 29 to 48]] justified setting these thresholds high for a two-stage line. The earlier rejected condition — [[shroomish-evolution-spore-strike-is-trivial|"deal damage to a statused target"]] — failed because Effect Spore made it trivially automatic. Skill thresholds avoid this problem entirely: they measure accumulated development, not a single lucky encounter.

In the wild, a Shroomish that survives enough territorial skirmishes and competitive pressure will gradually build these skills through experience. The condition is [[evolution-conditions-must-work-in-wild|ecologically coherent]] — no trainer required, just time and adversity.
