[[Sylveon]] emits a soothing aura from its [[sylveon-ribbon-feeler-empathy|ribbon-like feelers]] that erases hostility in nearby creatures, instantly stopping fights. This is not a psychic attack but an ambient emotional effect -- Sylveon's presence passively dampens aggression. It exploits this ability when hunting by swirling its feelers to distract prey while the aura weakens the target's will to resist, then pouncing. Despite this pacifying nature, Sylveon will charge boldly at Dragon-type opponents regardless of size difference. The [[cute-charm|Cute Charm]] trait reflects this aura -- close contact with Sylveon captivates aggressors involuntarily, an extension of its hostility-dampening biology.
