[[Jolteon]] is emotionally reactive -- it feels sadness and anger easily, and its body charges power whenever its mood shifts. This tight coupling between emotion and electricity generation makes Jolteon difficult for Trainers to bond with, as even minor emotional upsets produce dangerous electrical discharge. The volatility explains why Jolteon is rarely found in the wild and almost exclusively seen under Trainer ownership in urban settings: it needs the structure and management that comes with domestication. Its Quick Feet and Vanguard traits may reflect this hair-trigger responsiveness.
