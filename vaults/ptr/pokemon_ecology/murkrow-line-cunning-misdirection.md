[[Murkrow]] is a natural deceiver. Its [[murkrow-superstition-and-ill-omen|ill-omen reputation]] is partly earned — it deliberately lures pursuers onto dark, dangerous trails, exploiting their fear and disorientation. Within the [[murkrow-line-murder-hierarchy|murder hierarchy]], subordinate Murkrow use diversionary tactics to distract prey while others attack from behind.

[[Honchkrow]] applies this cunning at a leadership level. As boss of the murder, it delegates dirty work and maintains power through intimidation and strategic misdirection rather than direct confrontation. Both stages justify Deception +2 — Murkrow through streetwise trickery, Honchkrow through criminal cunning.
