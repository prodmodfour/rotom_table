[[Corvisquire]] occupies a middle rank in the Rookidee line's flock hierarchy. A [[Corviknight]] commands the flock as its apex, while Corvisquires serve as lieutenants -- coordinating hunts, flanking prey, and striking in concert when an ally lands a blow. [[Rookidee]] aspire to this role but fight as reckless solo challengers, lacking the discipline for coordinated tactics.

This hierarchy is why [[pack-hunt|Pack Hunt]] appears only on Corvisquire. Rookidee are too impulsive to coordinate, and Corviknight has outgrown pack tactics entirely, operating as an [[apex-predator|Apex Predator]] that fights alone. The flock structure also helps explain why Corvisquire's evolution to Corviknight requires broad skill development -- a lieutenant must demonstrate competence across combat, physical, and social dimensions before it can lead.
