The [[Tympole]]-[[Palpitoad]]-[[Seismitoad]] line demonstrates a progressive escalation of vibration-based abilities. Tympole vibrates its cheeks to produce ultrasonic signals inaudible to humans, used for group communication and danger warnings. Palpitoad's bumps grow larger and more numerous, generating sound waves intense enough to cause headaches and weaken prey before capture. Seismitoad's bumps specialize further — head bumps shoot paralyzing liquid while hand bumps vibrate to amplify punching power, and full-body vibration produces earthquake-like tremors. Each evolutionary stage repurposes the same biological mechanism (vibrating protuberances) for increasingly powerful applications, which the species reflects through the Resonant Body trait carried across all three stages and the addition of Groundshaper at the Seismitoad stage.
