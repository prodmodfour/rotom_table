[[Cofagrigus]] sustains itself on reverence -- the awe and respect that people feel when standing before ancient tombs and monuments. Its golden sarcophagus body is not camouflage or bait but a genuine monument, and the emotional weight it inspires in visitors is what feeds it. This is the same feeling humans have when they visit pyramids or enter sacred burial chambers: a quiet, overwhelming sense of standing in the presence of something ancient and worthy of respect.

This makes [[Cofagrigus]] a friendly Pokemon rather than a predator. It does not need to trap or frighten anyone to eat -- it simply needs to be visited. Cofagrigus that dwell in well-trafficked ruins or popular tombs are well-fed. Those in forgotten, unvisited sites may grow restless and seek out places where people gather to honor the dead.

The [[psychovore|Psychovore [Reverence]]] trait reflects this feeding behavior. Where [[misdreavus-fear-feeding-cycle|Misdreavus provokes fear to eat]], Cofagrigus passively draws sustenance from the reverence it naturally inspires by existing as a beautiful, ancient-looking monument.
