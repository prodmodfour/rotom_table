[[Morgrem]] compensates for its lack of physical strength through cunning and deception. Its signature tactic is dropping to all fours as if begging for forgiveness -- a false submission posture designed to lure opponents close enough to stab with its hair, which is shaped like a polearm. It also lures people deeper into the forests where it lives, isolating them from safety. This predatory deception is a direct escalation from [[impidimp-mischief-as-hunting|Impidimp's petty theft]]: where Impidimp steals and runs, Morgrem sets traps. Its [[impidimp-line-emotion-feeding|psychovore feeding]] benefits from prolonged psychological torment rather than brief flashes of annoyance, and its popularity among "people with gloomy thoughts" suggests a mutualistic relationship where both parties feed the other's darkness.
