[[Bidoof]] has nerves of steel. Despite its stout, naive appearance, it is rarely flustered by anything and remains calm in situations that would panic other species. It wanders into human villages and gnaws on houses without any apparent concern for the consequences, not out of aggression but out of genuine obliviousness to the disruption it causes.

This temperament is the biological basis for the Simple, Unaware, and Oblivious traits that define the [[Bidoof]]-[[Bibarel]] line. Where other species develop complex threat assessment, Bidoof's survival strategy is simply not registering threats at all. The unflappable nature persists through evolution — [[Bibarel]] channels the same imperturbable focus into industrious dam-building rather than aimless gnawing.
