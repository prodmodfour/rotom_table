[[Skorupi]]'s ambush hunting style demands precision. A buried Skorupi gets one chance to strike — if its stinger misses the nerve cluster or fails to inject venom cleanly, the prey escapes and another meal may not come for months. This selective pressure has produced an anatomy optimized for accurate, high-impact single strikes: the stinger is narrow and rigid for clean penetration, and the tail muscles can deliver force with minimal telegraphing. [[Drapion]] retains this precision despite its shift toward brute force, capable of targeting weak points in armor or hide with its pincers and stinger when it chooses to.
