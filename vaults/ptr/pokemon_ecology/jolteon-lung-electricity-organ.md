[[Jolteon]] possesses a specialized organ within its lungs that generates electrical energy, producing an audible crackling sound with each exhalation. This organ works by accumulating negative ions from the atmosphere and concentrating the weak electrical charges emitted by Jolteon's individual cells. The resulting charge is then amplified by the static electricity in its [[jolteon-electrified-quill-fur|needle-like fur]], creating a biological circuit that can produce thunderbolts exceeding 10,000 volts. This lung-based generation system is a product of [[eevee-line-unstable-genetics|Eevee's genetic restructuring]] -- an entirely new organ that did not exist in the pre-evolution form.
