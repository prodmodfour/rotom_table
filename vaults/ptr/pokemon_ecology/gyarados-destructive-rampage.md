When [[Magikarp]] evolves into [[Gyarados]], its brain cells undergo a structural transformation that rewires its temperament toward extreme violence. Once a rampage begins, Gyarados's blood does not calm until it has burned everything in its path — records exist of rampages lasting an entire month. Some regions call it the "deity of destruction," and folklore holds that any human settlement where a Gyarados appears is fated to be destroyed. This neurological rage explains the Very Scary, Violent Instincts, and Moxie traits: Gyarados is not merely aggressive, it is neurologically incapable of restraint once triggered.
