Wild Pokemon that navigate harsh terrain, forage or hunt in challenging environments, build shelters, or endure extreme conditions develop practical survival skills. In PTR, Survival +0 means a Pokemon has no wilderness competence at all — appropriate for domestic, urban, or hive-dependent species, but not for wild predators, foragers, and habitat specialists.

Survival values should reflect:
- **+1**: Basic wilderness competence. The Pokemon finds food, water, and shelter in its native habitat without difficulty. Most wild Pokemon that aren't purely urban or domestic should have at least this.
- **+2**: Adapted survivalist. The Pokemon navigates harsh or competitive environments, finds resources under pressure, and endures conditions that would challenge others.
- **+3**: Specialist or engineer. The Pokemon actively shapes its environment (dam building, nest engineering), thrives in extreme conditions (tundra, deep desert), or has survival as a core part of its ecological identity.

Species that remain at Survival +0:
- Urban-dependent species (Alolan Grimer/Muk, Impidimp line)
- Ghost-type Nullivores/Psychovores that don't forage (Honedge line, Yamask line, Misdreavus line)
- Hive-dependent species where the queen handles survival (Combee)
- Rare domestic species (Eevee)
- Species whose survival strategy is purely "flee" with no wilderness skill (Magikarp)
