Sableye feeds exclusively on raw gemstones, which it excavates from cave walls using its sharp claws. The consumed minerals crystallize and rise to the surface of its body, producing the gem-like eyes and the red, blue, and green stones embedded in its torso. Because the crystallization pattern depends on which gems it eats, every individual [[Sableye]] has a unique arrangement of body gems — no two look exactly alike.

This diet is the biological basis for both its Terravore dietary classification and its [[kleptomaniac|Kleptomaniac]] trait. It doesn't steal shiny objects out of greed — it eats them. The gem eyes that result from this diet allow it to see in total darkness, which explains its Darkvision trait.
