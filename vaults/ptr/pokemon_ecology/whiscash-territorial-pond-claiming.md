A single [[Whiscash]] claims an entire pond as its exclusive territory. It builds a nest at the bottom and violently repels any intruder. This extreme territorial behavior, combined with its gluttonous appetite — it will eat anything alive, sometimes swallowing prey whole — means a Whiscash-occupied body of water is functionally a single-predator ecosystem. For tabletop encounters, a Whiscash lair is a defined hazard zone: entering its pond means entering combat on its terms, in terrain shaped by its Burrower and Mud Dweller traits.
