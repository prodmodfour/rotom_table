In the Kitakami region, [[Noctowl]] is used for the local Flying Taxi service, much as [[Corviknight]] serves Galar's Galar Taxi. This demonstrates Noctowl's combination of size, strength, trainability, and reliable navigation -- especially useful in the mountainous terrain of Kitakami where its silent flight and night vision make it safer than alternatives. The parallel with [[corviknight-flying-taxi-service]] shows that multiple Pokemon fill this ecological-cultural niche in different regions.
