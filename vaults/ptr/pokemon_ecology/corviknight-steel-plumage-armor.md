[[Corviknight]]'s feathers are tempered with steel, giving its mantle, breast, belly, and scapular feathers the smooth appearance of plate armor. Its talons, head, and upper beak are similarly armored, with a crown-like crest above its eyes and a beard-like ruff at its throat. The extreme toughness of this steel plumage means Corviknight can fly unbothered even while being attacked mid-flight. This natural armor is reflected in its Shell 3 trait and Flying/Steel dual typing, making it one of the most defensively resilient bird Pokemon. The polished surface of its plumage also serves as a reflective ward — hostile debuffing energy rebounds off it, manifesting as [[mirror-armor]].
