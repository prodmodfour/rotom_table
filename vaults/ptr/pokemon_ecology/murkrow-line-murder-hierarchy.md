Murkrow live in large flocks called murders, typically in forests and swamps. These murders are organized around a single [[Honchkrow]], which commands absolute authority — a single cry summons over a hundred Murkrow, earning it the title "Summoner of Night." This is a strict dominance hierarchy: Honchkrow delegates most fighting to its subordinates and swoops in only to deliver finishing blows.

The hierarchy is enforced through punishment. A Honchkrow will ruthlessly hunt down any Murkrow that fails or shows disloyalty, pursuing offenders across great distances. This creates a mob-boss dynamic where [[Murkrow]] serve as loyal soldiers in exchange for protection and organized foraging. The social structure matters for [[wild-pokemon-social-hierarchy|encounter design]] — a Murkrow encounter almost always implies a Honchkrow leader nearby.
