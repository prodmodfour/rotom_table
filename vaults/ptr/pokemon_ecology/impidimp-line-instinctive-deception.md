The [[Impidimp]] line relies on deception as a core survival strategy, not merely an incidental behavior. [[Impidimp]] pesters and steals through misdirection — its [[pickpocket|Pickpocket]] and [[prankster|Prankster]] traits depend on reading targets and timing distractions. [[Morgrem]] refines this into [[morgrem-deceptive-predation|predatory deception]], feigning submission to lure victims within striking range of its hair-polearm. By [[Grimmsnarl]], the deception is sophisticated enough to manipulate social dynamics, using its imposing appearance as a distraction while its hair-tentacles operate independently.

This progression from petty mischief to calculated manipulation justifies Deception scaling from +2 (Impidimp) to +3 (Morgrem) to +4 (Grimmsnarl).
