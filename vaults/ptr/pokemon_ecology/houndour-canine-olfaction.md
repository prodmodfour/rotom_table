[[Houndour]] and [[Houndoom]] possess an exceptionally acute sense of smell, typical of canine Pokemon. This olfactory sensitivity is central to their pack hunting strategy — they track prey over long distances by scent before the pack begins its coordinated pursuit. Individual pack members can also identify each other by smell, maintaining pack cohesion during nighttime hunts when visual contact is limited.

This is the basis for the Keen Smell sense trait across the line.
