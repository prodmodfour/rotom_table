[[Buneary]]'s ears function as a second pair of arms. The ears are muscular, prehensile, and strong enough to grip objects, brace against surfaces, and deliver devastating strikes. This is why the species' Natural Weapons include [Arms] despite its forelegs being short — the ears serve the same mechanical role. When coiled, each ear stores kinetic energy like a spring ([[buneary-ear-coil-mechanism|coil mechanism]]), and when extended, they have the reach and dexterity of limbs. [[Lopunny]] retains this ear-arm duality but shifts its primary combat style to [[lopunny-kick-based-combat|kicks]], using the ears more for balance and grooming than offense.
