[[Wooper]] partially buries itself in mud at the bottom of ponds and rivers when sleeping. This behavior keeps its skin wet, prevents dehydration, and conceals it from predators like [[Arbok]]. On land, Wooper seeks out muddy ground for the same purpose. The instinct to burrow into substrate during rest explains Wooper's high Stealth skill (+3) and its Naturewalk (Wetlands) trait — it is deeply adapted to navigating and hiding within marshy terrain.
