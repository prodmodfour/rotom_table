[[Smoliv]] and [[Dolliv]] shake off debilitating conditions -- sleep, confusion, paralysis -- noticeably faster than most Pokemon their size. This resilience comes from sheer spirit, not physical hardiness. Smoliv is determined to keep going despite being small and slow, and [[Dolliv]] carries that same restless drive amplified by its migratory compulsion -- a Dolliv mid-journey simply refuses to stay down. They push through afflictions the way a stubborn traveler pushes through bad weather: not because it doesn't affect them, but because stopping isn't in their nature. Once the line matures into [[Arboliva]] and settles into place, this fierce drive mellows. Arboliva is no longer fighting to survive or compelled to wander -- it has arrived, and its temperament shifts from restless determination to patient endurance. The urgency that once burned through status conditions gives way to a calmer, more rooted disposition. This is represented by the [[early-bird|Early Bird]] trait on Smoliv and Dolliv.
