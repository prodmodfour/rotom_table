[[Noctowl]] is called "the emperor of dark nights" for good reason. Its eyes concentrate the faintest light to grant exceptional night vision, its supple wings enable completely silent flight, and it can rotate its head 180 degrees to sharpen its awareness. These combined senses allow it to catch prey without fail in total darkness. Head rotation also serves a communicative purpose -- turning its head upside down signals distress, and disturbing it in this state provokes aggressive pecking. These adaptations justify Noctowl's high Perception skill and Darkvision trait. As a top-of-chain nocturnal predator with no natural rivals in its forest habitat, Noctowl qualifies as an [[apex-predator|Apex Predator]] -- its hunting dominance translates into both heightened awareness and an intimidating presence that lesser creatures instinctively defer to.
