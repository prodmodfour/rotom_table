[[Corvisquire]]'s [[corvisquire-tool-use-intelligence|tool use]] reflects genuine general intelligence, not just combat instinct. It selects appropriate tools for specific problems — rocks for hard targets, vines for entanglement — and learns new tool applications through observation. This problem-solving ability is rare among bird Pokemon and justifies General Education +2.

[[Corviknight]] inherits and extends this intelligence. Its role as a [[corviknight-flying-taxi-service|Flying Taxi operator]] requires route memory, passenger assessment, and navigation across regions — practical general knowledge that justifies General Education +2 at the final stage as well.
