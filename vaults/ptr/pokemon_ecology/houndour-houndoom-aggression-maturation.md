[[Houndour]]'s aggression is impulsive — it attacks unfamiliar or hostile creatures on sight, a juvenile predatory instinct that prioritizes immediate action over strategy. This manifests as the Violent Instincts [1] trait.

Upon evolving into [[Houndoom]], this impulsive aggression matures into territorial defense. Rather than attacking everything it encounters, Houndoom channels its aggression into protecting a claimed territory and its pack. The Violent Instincts trait is replaced by Territorial [2] [1] — the same aggressive energy, now directed and purposeful.

This behavioral shift mirrors the [[houndoom-pack-alpha-hierarchy|transition from pack member to pack leader]]. A Houndour that evolves has proven itself capable of strategic thinking — claiming territory, commanding subordinates — and no longer wastes energy on impulsive confrontations outside its domain.
