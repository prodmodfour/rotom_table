[[Seaking]] males court females with elaborate swimming dances, and the female selects the most graceful performer. After mating, the male uses its horn to bore a sheltered nest cavity in riverbed boulders. The mated pair then take turns patrolling around the nest and eggs for roughly a month, protecting them from predators and water currents. This cooperative parental investment is unusual among fish Pokemon and gives Seaking a strong territorial motivation during breeding season — an encounter hook where disturbing a Seaking nest provokes both parents simultaneously.
