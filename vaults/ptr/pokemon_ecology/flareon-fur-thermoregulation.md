[[Flareon]]'s voluminous fur collar is not decorative -- it is a thermoregulation system. With an [[flareon-internal-flame-sac|internal flame sac]] maintaining body temperatures above 1,300 degrees Fahrenheit, Flareon must continuously vent excess heat to survive. It does this by fluffing out its fur to increase surface area, releasing heat into the surrounding air. This mechanism operates even during sleep. The Flame Body trait in its redesign reflects this -- physical contact with Flareon means contact with fur that is actively radiating extreme heat.
