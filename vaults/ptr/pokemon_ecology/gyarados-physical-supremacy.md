[[Gyarados]] possesses fangs that crush stones and scales harder than steel. It inhabits both freshwater and saltwater but is rarely seen in the wild — when it does appear, it dominates. As a Huge-sized Pokemon (6.5m) with Power 13 and Carnivore diet, it sits at the apex of any aquatic food chain it enters. The transformation from [[Magikarp]] — small, weak, universally preyed upon — into this apex predator represents one of the most dramatic power shifts in the Pokemon world, and the Dragon egg group shared by both stages hints at the mythological scale of the change.
