[[Heracross]] can lift and throw objects 100 times its own body weight, making it one of the most physically powerful Pokemon relative to its size. Its pronged horn — cross-shaped on males, heart-shaped on females — is the primary tool for this, used both in combat throws and in territorial food competition. The claws provide sturdy footing for leverage during lifts and enable climbing. This extraordinary power-to-weight ratio is the biological justification for Heracross's exceptional Attack stat (13) and the Power 8 trait, placing it among the strongest non-legendary physical attackers despite being only Medium-sized.
