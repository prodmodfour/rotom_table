[[Cofagrigus]] levitates above the ground rather than walking, despite being a massive golden sarcophagus. Where [[Yamask]] floats as a weightless spirit -- the ba drifting between worlds -- Cofagrigus hovers with the slow, deliberate gravity of a monument that refuses to touch the earth. It does not bob or drift like a ghost; it hangs in place like something enshrined, as though the air itself holds it in reverence. This is how it moves through the tombs and burial chambers it inhabits, gliding silently over sand and stone without disturbing either.
