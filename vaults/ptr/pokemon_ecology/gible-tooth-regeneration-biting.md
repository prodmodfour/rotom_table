[[Gible]] has tremendously strong jaws but is clumsy when biting, often chipping or losing teeth in the process. This is never a problem because its teeth regenerate fully within a few days. Gible drags prey back to its nest using its bite and can chew through most types of metal. This biological mechanism underpins the Tooth Regeneration trait shared across the line and explains why the entire Gible line favors bite-based combat despite the physical cost.
