[[Honedge]] uses the blue cloth extending from its pommel as a prehensile appendage to drain life energy. If anyone grasps its hilt, the sash wraps around their arm and siphons their life force completely. This predatory mechanism is passive and opportunistic -- Honedge does not need to actively hunt, it simply waits to be picked up. The sash-based draining justifies the [[soulstealer]] trait in [[Honedge]]'s species and explains why handling a living sword without its consent is lethal rather than merely dangerous. This predatory reflex follows directly from what [[honedge-spirit-is-killing-intent|animates the blade]] — pure hunger with no restraint.
