Beyond its [[eevee-line-unstable-genetics|unstable genetics]], [[Eevee]] displays a remarkable degree of physiological plasticity even without evolving. Its facial features can subtly shift to resemble those of its Trainer over time, suggesting that its body continuously responds to social bonds and environmental input at a level deeper than behavior. This plasticity is a softer expression of the same genetic instability that enables its dramatic evolutionary branching -- the same mutability that allows full-body restructuring also permits minor, ongoing physical adaptation.
