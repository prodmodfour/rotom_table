Because of its [[hoothoot-internal-clock-organ]], [[Hoothoot]] was once considered a divine messenger sent to tell the time. Some cultures regard it as a deity of wisdom. This cultural reverence gives Hoothoot significance beyond its combat capabilities -- in a tabletop setting, Hoothoot may be found in temples, libraries, or clocktowers as a sacred or utilitarian presence. The line's association with wisdom and time perception extends into [[Noctowl]]'s superior intellect.
