[[Vulpix]] is born with a single white tail that splits from its tip as it ages, eventually producing six distinct tails. As it approaches evolution into [[Ninetales]], the tails grow intensely hot. Inside its body burns a flame that never goes out — during hot days, it expels fire from its mouth to vent excess heat, a behavior it performs even while sleeping.

This internal flame is the biological basis for [[Vulpix]]'s Fire typing and its [[flash-fire]] trait. The tail-splitting process foreshadows the [[ninetales-nine-tails-mystical-energy|nine mystically-charged tails]] of its evolved form.
