The [[Dwebble]]-[[Crustle]] line depends entirely on carried rock shells for protection. Dwebble carefully selects a suitable stone, then hollows it out using a corrosive acid spray from its mouth — the same secretion it uses to repair shell damage. If its rock breaks, Dwebble becomes visibly agitated and will not calm down until it finds a replacement. Crustle escalates this dependency: its enormous layered sedimentary boulder is both home and status symbol, and losing it in a territorial fight means losing the contest outright. The [[shell|Shell]] trait reflects this relationship — the shell is not incidental equipment but the core of the line's survival strategy.
