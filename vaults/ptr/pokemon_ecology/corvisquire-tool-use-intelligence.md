[[Corvisquire]] has an unusually large brain relative to other bird Pokemon, making it one of the most intelligent avian species. It can use tools in battle -- throwing rocks at opponents, using vines to entangle them, and quickly learning to wield any object it can hold in its beak or talons. Through many tough battles, Corvisquire learns to assess an opponent's strength before engaging, a sharp contrast to [[Rookidee]]'s reckless bravery. This intelligence is a prerequisite for the tactical sophistication that [[Corviknight]] later demonstrates as a Flying Taxi operator.
