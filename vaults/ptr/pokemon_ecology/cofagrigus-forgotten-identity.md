While [[Yamask]] retains vivid memories of its former human life, [[Cofagrigus]] has forgotten its memories from when it was alive. Evolution into Cofagrigus represents a transition from grieving personhood to something that has moved beyond grief -- the spirit that once wept over its death mask has become the tomb itself, a monument that others come to honor. The mask from Yamask is still visible on Cofagrigus's forehead, but it is now ornamental rather than personal.

This loss of identity through evolution is a rare and narratively powerful concept: the Pokemon's growth comes at the cost of its humanity, but what replaces it is not malice -- it is peace. Cofagrigus no longer mourns because it no longer remembers what it lost. It has become the kind of thing that living people stand before in quiet awe, feeding on their [[cofagrigus-reverence-feeding|reverence]] without needing to cause harm.
