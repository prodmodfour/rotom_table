While [[Yamask]] retains vivid memories of its former human life, [[Cofagrigus]] has forgotten its memories from when it was alive. Evolution into Cofagrigus represents a transition from grieving personhood to something wholly inhuman -- the spirit that once wept over its death mask has become the tomb itself, concerned only with guarding treasure and trapping intruders. The mask from Yamask is still visible on Cofagrigus's forehead, but it is now ornamental rather than personal. This loss of identity through evolution is a rare and narratively powerful concept: the Pokemon's growth comes at the cost of its humanity.
