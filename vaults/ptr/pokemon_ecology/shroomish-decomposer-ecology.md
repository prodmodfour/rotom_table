[[Shroomish]] feeds on compost made of fallen, rotted leaves and plays an active role in forest decomposition cycles. It inhabits dark, damp environments and remains still in forest shade during daylight, concealing itself under fallen leaves. This fungal decomposer niche explains why Shroomish is found in marshes and rainforests rather than open grassland -- it requires the constant moisture and organic decay of a dense forest floor. The Geovore trait and Phototroph diet in the redesign reflect this: Shroomish processes decaying organic material from the soil rather than hunting prey or photosynthesizing like a typical Grass-type.
