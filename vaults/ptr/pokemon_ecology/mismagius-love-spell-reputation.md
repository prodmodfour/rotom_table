Mismagius has gained notoriety for its ability to cast love-inducing spells, driving desperate people to seek one out "as if their life depends on it." This reputation makes [[Mismagius]] one of the few Ghost-types that humans actively pursue rather than avoid. The dynamic creates a natural encounter hook — a trainer or NPC obsessively hunting a Mismagius for personal reasons, willing to take dangerous risks.

The love spell itself is a specific application of [[sorcery|Sorcery]] at the Emotion scope — shifting a target's disposition. It highlights how Mismagius's magic spans both harmful and beneficial effects, making it unpredictable and morally ambiguous rather than purely malevolent like many Ghost-types.
