[[Leavanny]]'s [[sewaddle-line-silk-and-leaf-tailoring|silk-and-leaf tailoring]] is a genuine engineering behavior, not simple instinct. It selects specific leaf types for different garments, adjusts cuts to fit the recipient's body shape, and uses its own silk thread as both adhesive and structural reinforcement. The clothing it produces serves functional purposes — warmth, camouflage, waterproofing — not just decoration.

This is the same class of material engineering as [[bibarel-dam-building|Bibarel's dam construction]], and justifies Technology Education +3. The earlier stages ([[Sewaddle]], [[Swadloon]]) produce cruder versions — Sewaddle wraps a single leaf hood, Swadloon bundles itself in a leaf blanket — but Leavanny's output is deliberate, tailored craft.
