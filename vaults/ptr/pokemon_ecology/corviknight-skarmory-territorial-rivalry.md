[[Corviknight]] has violent territorial clashes with Skarmory, both being Steel-type armored bird Pokemon competing for similar ecological niches. This rivalry parallels [[garchomp-salamence-aerial-rivalry]] as another example of apex Pokemon within the same type combination fighting for dominance. In regions where both species are present, encounters between them are aggressive and dangerous for bystanders. Corviknight also preys on Bunnelby, placing it firmly as both a territorial defender and an active predator.
