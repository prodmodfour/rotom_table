The [[Zigzagoon]] line has a powerful foraging instinct that drives both stages of the evolutionary line. Zigzagoon is exceptionally good at finding items in grass and underground, using its nose-to-ground zigzag searching to locate things other species miss. It marks territory by rubbing its bristly back fur against trees, establishing foraging routes.

[[Linoone]] channels this instinct into active collection. It is attracted to round, orb-shaped objects and carries snatched items in expandable cheek pouches. It maintains multiple secret stash sites scattered throughout its territory, naturally accumulating found objects as a byproduct of its restless foraging.
