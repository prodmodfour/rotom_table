[[Vespiquen]] exercises total authority over her hive through [[combee-command|Combee Command]] and [[vespiquen-pheromone-colony-control|pheromone signaling]]. Every Combee in the colony responds to her directives without hesitation — foraging routes, defensive formations, and attack targets are all dictated by the queen. This is not cooperation or persuasion; it is unilateral command over hundreds of subordinates, justified by a Command skill of +4.

A Vespiquen that cannot command is not a queen. The hive collapses without her direction.
