Misdreavus sustains itself entirely on fear absorbed through the five red gems encircling its neck. These gems are not decorative — they are its feeding organs, drawing in the emotional energy of frightened creatures. When critically weakened, Misdreavus's body vanishes entirely and only the neck gems remain, suggesting the gems are the true core of the creature and the ghostly body is projected from them.

This dietary requirement drives all of [[Misdreavus]]'s behavior: it yanks hair, bites from behind, and shrieks specifically to provoke terror in victims, then watches with satisfaction as it absorbs the resulting fear. The feeding mechanism is the biological basis for the [[psychovore|Psychovore [Fear]]] trait — Misdreavus doesn't scare for fun, it scares to eat.
