[[Glaceon]] can instantly freeze atmospheric moisture with a single cry, producing small ice crystals that form diamond dust -- tiny, glittering ice particles that shimmer as they drift. This ability has practical value at ski resorts, where Glaceon is popular for creating powdery snow. However, the beauty of the display is deceptive: onlookers captivated by the sparkling ice can be frozen without realizing it. The Freezer trait reflects this ambient cold generation, and the mechanic implications are clear -- Glaceon's presence alone alters the battlefield environment.
