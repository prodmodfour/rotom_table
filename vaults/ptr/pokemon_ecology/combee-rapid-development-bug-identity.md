[[Combee]] and [[Vespiquen]] carry the [[rapid-development|Rapid Development]] trait as part of their bug identity. Bug-type Pokemon as a group tend toward fast growth cycles — short egg incubation, quick level progression, and early evolution thresholds. For the Combee line specifically, rapid development serves the hive: a colony that can replace lost workers quickly has a survival advantage, and a female Combee that reaches evolution faster can establish her own hive sooner.
