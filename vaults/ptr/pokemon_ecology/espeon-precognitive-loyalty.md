[[Espeon]]'s precognitive abilities are said to have developed specifically to protect its Trainer from harm. Its fine, velvety fur can sense minuscule shifts in air currents, allowing it to predict weather changes and enemy movements -- when it does so, the tip of its forked tail quivers. This makes Espeon's combat precognition an extension of its loyalty rather than an abstract psychic talent. The Premonition and Perception traits in its redesign reflect this: Espeon does not merely see the future, it reads the immediate physical environment with extraordinary sensitivity to keep its companions safe.
