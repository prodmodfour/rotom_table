The [[Corvisquire]] -> [[Corviknight]] evolution conditions require broad skill development: Combat 5, Athletics 4, Command 2, Focus 4, Insight 2, and Mettle [0/3]. This was a deliberate contrast to [[Rookidee]] -> [[Corvisquire]], which requires Mettle [3/3] — getting beaten up and enduring.

Corvisquire's defining trait is its intelligence. The evolution conditions reflect that intelligence being put to work through disciplined, well-rounded training rather than a single dramatic moment. Five different skill thresholds mean the Pokemon can't brute-force its way forward — it has to develop across combat, physical, and social dimensions.

Mettle [0/3] means Mettle stacks were accumulated and then spent — problems were encountered and solutions were found. Where Rookidee just absorbs punishment, Corvisquire applies intelligence to adversity. The empty Mettle is evidence of that process.

An earlier design attempt used Mettle [3/3] again for the second evolution. This was rejected as repetitive — it told the same story twice. The shift from "fill Mettle" to "use Mettle" creates a narrative arc across the line: endure, then adapt.
