The [[Poochyena]]-[[Mightyena]] line hunts through coordinated pursuit to exhaustion. Poochyena has a keen sense of smell that lets it lock onto prey and track it relentlessly, and the pack chases as a unit until the target collapses from fatigue. Once a Mightyena pack selects prey, they never allow it to escape.

This hunting style has a distinctive weakness in Poochyena's juvenile form: it bites at anything that moves but immediately retreats if the prey fights back. The tenacity is there — Poochyena will resume the chase — but the individual courage is not. Only through evolution into [[Mightyena]] and integration into a full pack does the pursuit strategy become reliable. The line's high Stamina and [[sense|Keen Smell]] reflect this endurance-based approach to predation.
