[[Arboliva]]'s [[arboliva-nurturing-oil-sharing|healing oil sharing]] is not passive generosity — it is an active social behavior. Arboliva seeks out injured or distressed Pokemon and humans, approaching with a calm, reassuring presence that puts recipients at ease before administering healing oil. Its [[gas-production|Pleasant Scent]] gas production further disarms wariness. This nurturing charisma justifies Charm +3.

The earlier stages lack this social confidence. [[Smoliv]] is Cute but timid. [[Dolliv]] is a solitary wanderer. Arboliva's charm comes from maturity and purpose, not just appearance.
