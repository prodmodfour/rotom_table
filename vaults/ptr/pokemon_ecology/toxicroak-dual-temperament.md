[[Toxicroak]] displays a split behavioral profile — it can be either cruel and aggressive or surprisingly laid-back, lounging on rocks and in marshes. This variability makes Toxicroak encounters unpredictable for [[wild-encounter-motivations|encounter design]]. A Toxicroak found basking near water might be docile, while one guarding its territory could be immediately lethal.

Toxicroak even practices combat in its sleep, reflexively performing its [[commitment|Committed]] move — Poison Jab — while dreaming. The Croagunk line is one of the rare species where Commitment is species-determined rather than individual: the poison delivery system described in [[croagunk-line-poison-sac-system]] is so central to the line's fighting style that every member develops the same dedication to Poison Jab. Toxicroak uses its throat poison sac as a pillow, and approaching a sleeping Toxicroak triggers an instant defensive attack.
