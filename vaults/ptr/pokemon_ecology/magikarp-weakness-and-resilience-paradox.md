[[Magikarp]] is simultaneously one of the weakest and most resilient Pokemon. It cannot swim against even slow currents and does nothing but flop during battle, yet possesses an immune system strong enough to survive the most polluted waters. Scientists believe ancient Magikarp were far stronger than modern specimens, suggesting a degenerative process over generations — the opposite of typical evolutionary improvement. This paradox explains why Magikarp has high Defense (6) and Speed (8) but minimal Attack (1) and HP (2): the species is built to endure and flee, not fight. Its high Defense stat reflects the residual toughness of a once-mighty lineage.
