The [[Shroomish]] to [[Breloom]] evolution represents a dramatic shift from passive decomposer to active predator-fighter. Shroomish is armless, nearly immobile, and relies entirely on defensive spore clouds to survive. Breloom gains arms, legs, claws, and a tail -- becoming a fast, aggressive boxer that uses spores offensively to set up close-range strikes. This is one of the most extreme behavioral shifts in any evolutionary line: the equivalent of a mushroom learning to punch. This arc is reflected through trait changes -- Shroomish has no combat appendage traits, while Breloom gains Claws, Arms, Legs, Tail, Technician, and [[commitment|Commitment]] — the Fighting type's identity trait, representing total dedication to a single perfected technique.
