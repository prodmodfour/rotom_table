[[Shroomish]] has no arms, no legs, and no articulated joints — it is essentially a round mushroom cap on stubby feet. Movement means shuffling its entire body weight forward in a rocking waddle, with no ability to balance, brace, or correct mid-motion. Any offensive action — headbutting, body-checking, even shaking to release spores — risks toppling or stumbling, because Shroomish has no limbs to stabilize itself. The [[clumsy|Clumsy]] trait directly models this: a small chance of self-harm on any move reflects a body plan that was never built for coordinated action. This clumsiness disappears on evolution to [[Breloom]], which gains arms, legs, and the agile footwork of a trained boxer.
