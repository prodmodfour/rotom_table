[[Misdreavus]] does not simply startle — it manipulates. Its [[misdreavus-fear-feeding-cycle|fear feeding]] requires finding vulnerable targets and engineering the right moment to frighten them. It uses its [[cute|Cute]] appearance to approach unsuspecting victims, then switches to [[scary|Scary]] to maximize the fear response. This bait-and-switch is instinctive deception, not random spooking, and justifies Deception +2.

[[Mismagius]] refines this into [[mismagius-illusionist-performer|deliberate illusion and emotional manipulation]], escalating to Deception +3.
