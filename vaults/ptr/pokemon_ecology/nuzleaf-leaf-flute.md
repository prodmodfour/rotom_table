[[Nuzleaf]] uses the single leaf atop its head as a wind instrument, producing an eerie flute-like sound that unsettles anyone who hears it. People lost in forests report the sound striking fear and disorientation into their hearts, which Nuzleaf uses as territorial defense -- warning intruders away from its deep forest home without resorting to physical confrontation. The leaf-flute can also produce comforting melodies, suggesting Nuzleaf has fine control over the emotional impact of its music. This acoustic manipulation aligns with the line's Dark typing and its Singer trait, and foreshadows [[Shiftry]]'s more direct ability to read and influence minds.
