The [[Impidimp]] >> [[Morgrem]] >> [[Grimmsnarl]] line are [[psychovore|psychovores]] that sustain themselves on negative emotional energy, specifically annoyance and frustration. Impidimp sucks in these emanations through its nose, thriving on the irritation it provokes in others. If deprived of negative emotions to feed on, Impidimp itself becomes irritable -- a feedback loop where the creature's own distress could theoretically sustain another of its kind. This dietary requirement shapes the entire line's behavior: every stage has evolved increasingly sophisticated methods of provoking negative emotions, from Impidimp's petty theft to [[morgrem-deceptive-predation|Morgrem's manipulative ambushes]] to Grimmsnarl's continued pranks despite its immense physical power. Their Nullivore diet classification and Psychovore [Annoyance] trait reflect that conventional food plays no role in their survival.
