[[Leavanny]] displays strongly developed parental instincts that extend beyond its own offspring. It tailors leaf clothing for small Pokemon in its territory (see [[sewaddle-line-silk-and-leaf-tailoring]]), warms its eggs using heat from fermenting fallen leaves, and will aggressively attack anything that threatens young Pokemon in its care — even if those young are not its own species. This protective-nurturing combination makes Leavanny a natural guardian figure in forest ecosystems. Its arm blades, powerful enough to slice through tree trunks, serve both the craft of leaf-cutting and the defense of its charges.
