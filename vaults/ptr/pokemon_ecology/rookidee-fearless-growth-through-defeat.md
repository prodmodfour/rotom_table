[[Rookidee]] will bravely challenge any opponent regardless of size or strength, and it stores energy from every battle -- even losses make it slightly stronger. It uses its small body and nimble leg-based jumping to disorient larger foes, turning its size disadvantage into a tactical one. Female Rookidee are notably fussier about their plumage, relentlessly attacking anything that dirties their wings. This fearless-but-scrappy temperament is the foundation for the line's [[challenger|Challenger]] and [[mettle|Mettle]] traits -- Challenger captures the compulsion to fight anything bigger and stronger, while Mettle captures the growth-through-defeat pattern, converting each loss into stored resolve.
