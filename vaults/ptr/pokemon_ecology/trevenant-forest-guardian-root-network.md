[[Trevenant]] controls the trees in its forest through a root network that functions as a nervous system, allowing it to sense and manipulate the entire woodland. It uses this network to trap people who harm the forest so they can never leave, and a deadly curse falls upon anyone who cuts down trees where Trevenant dwell. Despite this fearsome territorial behavior, Trevenant is very kind to Pokemon living in its forest and tolerates creatures nesting in its own branches. This dual nature -- merciless to destroyers, nurturing to inhabitants -- makes Trevenant a true ecosystem guardian rather than a simple territorial predator. The Plant Manipulation 3 and Forest Lord traits directly reflect this root-network dominion.
