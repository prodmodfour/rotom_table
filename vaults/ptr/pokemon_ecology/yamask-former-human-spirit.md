[[Yamask]] arose from the spirits of people interred in graves in past ages. Each Yamask carries a golden mask that is literally the face it had when it was still human. The species retains memories of its former life and sometimes looks at its mask and cries in longing. It becomes furious when the mask is lost or taken away. This origin as a former human with persistent memory makes Yamask one of the most emotionally complex Ghost-types -- it is not a monster wearing a mask, it is a grieving person trapped in a spectral body, clinging to the last physical proof of who they once were.
