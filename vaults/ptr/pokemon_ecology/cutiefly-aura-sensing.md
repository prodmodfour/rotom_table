[[Cutiefly]] can detect the aura of living things — plants, people, and Pokemon alike. It uses this ability to locate flowers about to bloom (by reading the color and brightness of their auras) and to predict opponents' movements in battle. Strong emotions cause a living creature's aura to resemble a flower in full bloom, which draws Cutiefly toward emotionally excited beings. This explains why Cutiefly sometimes appears near towns and buildings rather than staying in meadows: it follows emotional intensity, not just floral scent. The Aura Reader trait in [[Cutiefly]]'s and [[Ribombee]]'s redesigns directly reflects this biological sense.
