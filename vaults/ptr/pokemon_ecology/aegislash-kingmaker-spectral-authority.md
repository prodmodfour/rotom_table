[[Aegislash]] possesses the ability to detect innate leadership qualities in people and Pokemon. According to legend, whoever it recognizes is destined to become king. The species has attended and served generations of royalty, and historically used its spectral powers to manipulate people and Pokemon into building kingdoms to its liking. One Aegislash drained the life of a king, causing his entire kingdom to fall. This kingmaker role -- part advisor, part puppeteer -- makes Aegislash far more than a weapon; it is a political force that selects and shapes rulers.
