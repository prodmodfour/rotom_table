The [[Phantump]] line draws from the Japanese concept of kodama -- spirits that inhabit old trees. Phantump represents the darker side of this folklore: a child's spirit bound to a stump, confused and dangerous. [[Trevenant]] represents the mature kodama -- a fully realized tree spirit that has grown into its role as forest protector. The progression from lost child to forest lord mirrors how kodama in folklore range from small, mischievous presences to ancient, powerful guardians of sacred groves. This mythological arc gives the evolution a narrative weight beyond simple stat growth: the child spirit finally finds purpose by becoming the forest itself.
