[[Crustle]] fight viciously with each other over territory and shell quality. The decisive rule is simple: the Crustle whose boulder breaks loses the contest. This makes shell integrity a direct proxy for social standing and territorial control. Crustle's legs are powerful enough to carry its massive stone slab across arid terrain for extended periods, and its claws can crack a Rhyperior's carapace — but the species avoids rain and will not emerge from its shell in wet weather. Victorious Crustle sometimes sleep atop their boulders in complete defenselessness, suggesting satisfaction and territorial security. The [[earth-manipulation|Earth Manipulation]] trait reflects how Crustle reshapes its environment through sheer physical power and geological interaction.
