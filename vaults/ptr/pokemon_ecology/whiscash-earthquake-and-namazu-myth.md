[[Whiscash]] can generate localized earthquakes by slamming its broad tail against swamp and lake beds, producing tremors that extend over three miles. It can also predict natural earthquakes before they occur, sensing geological vibrations through the substrate. Historically, people who witnessed Whiscash leaping from lakes before earthquakes mistakenly blamed the Pokemon for causing them. This mirrors the Japanese Namazu myth — a giant catfish beneath the earth whose thrashing causes earthquakes. The lore directly supports Whiscash's Tremorsense trait and its access to Earthquake as a signature move.
