[[Heracross]] is usually docile and feeds primarily on tree sap, using its two antennae to locate food sources by scent. It sometimes falls asleep while licking sap from a tree. However, this calm temperament shifts sharply if disturbed during feeding — Heracross will chase off intruders with its horn. It competes with Pinsir for sap-rich trees, though regional variation exists (Alolan populations coexist more peacefully with Pinsir). This context-dependent temperament — gentle by default, aggressive when its food is threatened — explains the [[moxie|Moxie]] trait: Heracross fights hardest when provoked, not when seeking conflict. The [[keen-smell|Keen Smell]] sense trait reflects its antennae-based olfactory specialization for locating sap-rich trees.
