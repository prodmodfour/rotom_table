[[Umbreon]]'s yellow ring markings glow faintly in darkness, when it is excited, or when it pounces to attack. The rings intensify on the night of a full moon, when lunar energy fills Umbreon with a mysterious power. This bioluminescence is paradoxical for a stealth predator -- it may serve as a warning display (similar to aposematic coloring in venomous animals) that signals [[umbreon-toxic-sweat-defense|its toxic defenses]] to would-be attackers. The moon's influence on Umbreon's power connects to its evolutionary origin: [[eevee-line-unstable-genetics|Eevee's genetic restructuring]] into Umbreon is triggered by prolonged moonlight exposure.
