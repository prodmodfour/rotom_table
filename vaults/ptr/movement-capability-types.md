In PTR, movement uses a [[movement-points]]-based system with six [[movement-rates]]: Land, Sky, Water, Phase, Burrow, and Teleporter. Each movement rate is a [[trait-definition|trait]].

This replaces PTU's capabilities (Overland, Swim, Sky, Levitate, Burrow, Teleporter).
