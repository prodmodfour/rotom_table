Diamond Storm is a Rock-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Close Blast 3, Friendly, Smite.

Diamond Storm raises the User’s Defense by +1 CS on an Even-Numbered Roll.
