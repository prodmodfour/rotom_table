Ice Beam is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Ice Beam Freezes on 19+. The 19+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
