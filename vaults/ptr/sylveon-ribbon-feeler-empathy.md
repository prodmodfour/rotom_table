[[Sylveon]]'s most distinctive feature is its ribbon-like feelers, which extend from bows at its ears and neck. These are not decorative -- they are sophisticated sensory organs that read emotional states through physical contact. When Sylveon wraps a feeler around a Trainer's arm, it can perceive their feelings directly. This tactile empathy is the biological foundation for Sylveon's [[sylveon-hostility-suppression-aura|hostility suppression]], its hunting strategy, and its bond-driven evolution from [[Eevee]]. The feelers also function as manipulable limbs, extending far beyond their resting length.
