PTR replaces this with [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier|1d20 + Modifier]].

In PTU, the core skill check mechanic was: roll a number of d6s equal to skill rank (per [[skill-rank-dice-progression]]), add modifiers from equipment and bonuses, and compare against a GM-set Difficulty Check (DC). Meeting or exceeding the DC succeeded.

This is the foundation for [[cooperative-skill-checks]], [[opposed-checks-defender-wins-ties]], [[extended-skill-checks]], and the [[ap-spend-for-roll-bonus]] option.
