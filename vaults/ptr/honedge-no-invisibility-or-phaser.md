Honedge lacks [[invisibility]] and [[phaser-movement-ignores-walls]] despite being a Ghost type. Its spirit is bound to a physical steel sword — it cannot dematerialize or pass through solid objects because the object it inhabits is tangible. Ghost typing reflects the spirit's nature, but the body it occupies constrains how that nature manifests.
