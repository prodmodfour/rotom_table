Sky Attack is a Flying-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 14, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, Pass, Set-Up, Full Action.

Set-Up Effect: The user is moved up 25 meters into the air. Resolution Effect: The user may shift until they are next to a legal target in the encounter. They may then shift again and pass through legal targets to attack with Sky Attack. Sky Attack Flinches a target on 17+. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
