When a Pokemon or Trainer cannot choose to Take a Breather themselves (Rage, unwillingness to risk Confusion save), another Trainer can assist them. This costs a Full Action from the assisting Trainer and an Interrupt from the target, and the assisting Trainer must be able to Shift to the target. The Trainer makes a Command Check with DC 12.

This provides a team-based escape from status lock, at the cost of two combatants' actions.
