[[Grimmsnarl]]'s most distinctive adaptation is that its dark purple hair functions as muscle fiber, dramatically amplifying its physical strength beyond what its actual body could produce. This hair-muscle system allows Grimmsnarl to overwhelm even [[Machamp]] in raw power despite being much smaller. The hair can also be actively controlled to ensnare opponents in a tentacle-like fashion, making it both an offensive weapon and a grappling tool. Beneath the hair, Grimmsnarl's true body is relatively lean -- the hulking, muscular silhouette is entirely constructed from hair wrapped around its frame. This biological mechanism is the basis for its Power 8 trait, the highest in the [[Impidimp]] line, and explains how a Fairy/Dark-type achieves physical dominance typically reserved for Fighting-types.
