The Push maneuver pushes a target 1 meter directly away. If the pusher has remaining movement, they can move into the vacated space and push again, repeating until they choose to stop or run out of movement.

This chaining mechanic creates a bulldozer effect that scales with the pusher's movement speed. Push requires an opposed Combat or Athletics check (AC 4) and only works against targets whose weight doesn't exceed the pusher's Heavy Lifting rating. The [[melee-range-is-adjacency]] requirement applies to each push in the chain.
