Dynamic Punch is a Fighting-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, At-Will frequency, AC 9, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Dynamic Punch Confuses the target. Dynamic Punch ignores the target's Evasion if they are Flanked.
