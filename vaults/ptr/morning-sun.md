Morning Sun is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user regains Hit Points equal to half of its full Hit Point value. If it is Sunny, the user gains 2/3 of its full Hit Point value. If it is Rainy, Sand Storming or Hailing the user gains 1/4 of their full Hit Point value.

As a Daily x2 move, Morning Sun can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
