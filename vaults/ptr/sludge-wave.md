Sludge Wave is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1 or Close Blast 2.

Sludge Wave Poisons all legal targets on 19+. The 19+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
