Ceaseless Edge is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target is put in a Vortex. Ceaseless Edge is a Critical Hit on a 19+.
