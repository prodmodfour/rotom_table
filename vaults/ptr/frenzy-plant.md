Frenzy Plant is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 15, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 3, 5 Targets, Smite, Exhaust.

As a Daily x2 move, Frenzy Plant can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
