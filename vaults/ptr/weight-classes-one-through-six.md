Weight Classes range from 1 to 6 and are used by several [[trait-definition|Traits]] and Moves (e.g., Low Kick, Heavy Slam). Each species has a weight and parenthetical weight class listed in its Pokedex entry.

| WC | lbs | kg |
|----|-----|-----|
| 1 | 0–25 | 0–11 |
| 2 | 25–55 | 11–25 |
| 3 | 55–110 | 25–50 |
| 4 | 110–220 | 50–100 |
| 5 | 220–440 | 100–200 |
| 6 | 440+ | 200+ |
