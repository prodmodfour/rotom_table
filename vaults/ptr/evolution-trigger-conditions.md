Evolution is triggered by species-specific unlock conditions. When a condition is met, evolution starts immediately.

Condition types include (not exhaustive):
- Item use — a specific item is used on the Pokemon (e.g., Fire Stone on Eevee)
- Held item + battle outcome — Pokemon wins a battle while holding a specific item
- Environmental — Pokemon spends time in a specific environment
- Level threshold — Pokemon reaches a specific level
- Move known — Pokemon knows a specific move
- Gender — Pokemon is a specific gender

Conditions can be combined with AND/OR logic, mirroring how [[unlock-conditions]] work for Traits and moves.
