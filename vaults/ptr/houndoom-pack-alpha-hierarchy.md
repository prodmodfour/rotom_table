[[Houndoom]] packs have a clear alpha hierarchy determined by combat. The leader is identifiable by horns raked sharply toward the back — horn shape is both a status marker and a product of fighting experience. After securing prey, the alpha divides food evenly among the pack, suggesting a leadership model based on responsibility rather than hoarding.

This pack structure is a matured version of [[houndour-pack-hunting-tactics|Houndour's cooperative hunting]]. The transition from Houndour to Houndoom involves not just physical growth but a shift in social role — a Houndoom must earn its place through dominance contests. The Pack Hunt trait carries forward, but Houndoom adds the Opportunist trait, reflecting the alpha's instinct to exploit openings in combat.
