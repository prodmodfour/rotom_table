Recall-blocking is exclusively governed by the Trapped condition in PTU. No phantom "Bound" condition exists — nothing in PTU produces it, and it should not be checked.

Grapple restricts shifting (movement), not recall (returning to Poke Ball). These are distinct mechanics. Vortex moves (Fire Spin, Whirlpool, Sand Tomb) inflict Trapped, which is the actual recall-blocking condition.
