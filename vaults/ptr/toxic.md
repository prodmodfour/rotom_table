Toxic is a Poison-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

The target is Badly Poisoned. If the user is Poison Type, Toxic cannot miss.
