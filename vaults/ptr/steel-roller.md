Steel Roller is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 13, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, Pass, Dash, Smite, Spirit Surge.

Legal targets hit by Steel Roller are Tripped on a roll of 15+. Any Hazards in spaces Passed through or adjacent to those spaces are removed (they are removed before they can affect the user). If the user moves through an area of Terrain, or a Field-range Terrain is active, those effects immediately end.

As a Daily move, Steel Roller can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
