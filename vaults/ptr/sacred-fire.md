Sacred Fire is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Sacred Fire Burns the target on Even-Numbered Rolls.
