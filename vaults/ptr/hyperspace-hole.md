Hyperspace Hole is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 3 Targets.

Make and resolve of Hyperspace Hole’s attacks separately. In between each attack, the user may Shift using their Teleporter capability, ending the Shift next to a legal target. Interrupts may not be triggered against Hyperspace Hole..

As a Daily move, Hyperspace Hole can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
