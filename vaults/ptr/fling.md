Fling is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a variable Damage Base (see effect), [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Fling.

The user throws a held item, determining the effect of Fling.
