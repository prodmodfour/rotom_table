[[Seedot]] closely resembles an acorn and exploits this resemblance as both camouflage and a dispersal strategy. When hanging motionless from a branch, it is easily mistaken for a nut, which protects it from predators that would otherwise target a small, defenseless Pokemon. More remarkably, Seedot deliberately invites bird Pokemon to pick it up and carry it away, using seed mimicry to hitchhike to new habitats. This dual-purpose mimicry -- passive defense and active range expansion -- explains how Seedot populations spread across distant forests despite having limited mobility of their own.
