Normal healing does not remove injuries. If a Pokemon is healed past an [[hp-marker-injury-thresholds]] and then damaged back through it, they gain another Injury for crossing that marker again.

This makes aggressive healing a calculated risk: pushing HP above a marker creates another opportunity to gain an injury if the combatant is hit again. The app should track which markers have been crossed and re-trigger injury checks on subsequent crossings.
