A Pokemon's species has two levels of influence on its attributes.

**Determines** (rigid, species-fixed):
- Base Stats
- [[innate-traits|Species Traits]]
- Evolution Chain
- Movement Traits
- Breeding Information

**Informs** (can vary from species defaults):
- Type
- Size Information
- Diet and Habitat

The "determines" attributes are fixed by species and cannot change without evolution. The "informs" attributes use species data as a starting point but can be modified.
