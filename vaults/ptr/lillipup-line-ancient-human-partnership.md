Cave paintings suggest that the [[Lillipup]] line was among the first Pokemon species to partner with humans. [[Herdier]] in particular appears in ancient wall art depicting cooperative relationships — herding, guarding, and working alongside early human communities. This deep history explains the line's exceptional loyalty and trainability.

The partnership runs both ways: Herdier assists trainers in raising other Pokemon and manages herds on ranches, while [[Stoutland]] serves as a babysitter trusted to watch human children. However, the line demands respect in return — [[Herdier]] will refuse to obey anyone who disrespects it, and [[Lillipup]] never forgets abuse it receives during training. This combination of loyalty and dignity makes the line a natural fit for working roles but a poor match for negligent trainers.
