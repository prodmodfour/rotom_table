PTR removes Action Points entirely. The AP pool, AP spending for roll bonuses, AP draining, AP binding, and AP-based injury healing no longer exist.

Mechanics that previously required AP spending are [[ap-gated-mechanics-become-traits|converted into Traits and rebalanced]] for the cost removal.

The old AP notes are kept for reference as superseded PTU mechanics: [[ap-refresh-per-scene]], [[ap-spend-for-roll-bonus]], [[extended-rest-drained-ap-only]], [[bound-ap-persists-until-unbound]], [[trainer-ap-drain-heals-injury]].
