Fire Blast is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 11, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 6, 1 Target, Smite.

Fire Blast burns the target on 19+. The 19+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
