The [[shift-renamed-to-movement-action|Movement Action]] can be split between other actions within a turn. The atomic unit of movement is a single [[movement-points|Movement Point]].

With 3 Movement Points, a combatant can potentially split movement three ways — move, take another action, move again, take another action, move a third time.

This supersedes the previous rule that movement was a single continuous path (see [[movement-is-atomic-per-shift]]).
