Relic Song is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 3, Friendly, Sonic.

All legal targets fall Asleep on 16+. As long as Meloetta knows Relic Song, it may change between Aria Form and Step Form as a Swift Action when using Relic Song or as a Standard Action otherwise. Both Aria and Step Form must be statted with the same HP Stat. The 16+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
