Iron Tail is a Steel-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 6, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Smite.

Iron Tail lowers the target’s Defense by -1 CS on 19+.
