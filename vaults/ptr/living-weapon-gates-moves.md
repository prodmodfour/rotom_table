Combat Skill Rank gates which weapon moves become available from a Living Weapon, not whether the trainer can engage (hold/wield) it. Any trainer can engage a willing Living Weapon regardless of Combat rank.

A trainer with Untrained Combat can wield Honedge but won't gain access to Wounding Strike until Adept Combat. This is consistent with how normal weapons work in PTU per [[gate-capabilities-not-permission]].
