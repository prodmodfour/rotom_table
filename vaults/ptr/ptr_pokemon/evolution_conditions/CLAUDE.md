# Evolution Conditions

~32 per-line evolution condition files. Each covers one evolutionary line's unlock requirements (level thresholds, item use, move known, etc.).

## What you can't know without exploring here

- The specific evolution triggers for a given evolutionary line
- How evolution gates are calibrated across lines (BST thresholds, timing)

## What you'd learn

Each file is named `{species}_line.md` (e.g. `eevee_line.md`, `honedge_line.md`). Files cover the full line, not individual stages.

- Ecology notes in `../../pokemon_ecology/` explain *why* these conditions were chosen (BST calibration, wild viability, lore fit).
- Species stat blocks in `../complete/` and `../incomplete/` have the stats these conditions gate access to.
