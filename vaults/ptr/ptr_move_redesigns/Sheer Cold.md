# Sheer Cold

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 4, 1 Target, Execute
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Roll 1d100. This roll may not be modified in any way. If you roll X or lower, the target Faints. X is equal to 30 + the user's Level - the target's Level.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 30

## Notes
OHKO move. Grants Freezer. Contest Type: Beauty. Big Show.
