# Mega Kick

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 6
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The target is Pushed 2 meters.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Legs]
- Individual Attack 35
- Athletics 6
- Combat 5

## Notes
High power with push effect.
