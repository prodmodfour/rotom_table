# Curse

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the user is not a Ghost Type, Curse has a Frequency of EOT. The user lowers its Speed by 1 Combat Stage, but raises Attack and Defense by 1 Combat Stage each. If the user is a Ghost Type, Curse has a Frequency of Scene. The user loses 1/3rd of their Max Hit Points (this loss cannot be prevented in any way) and a target within 8 meters becomes Cursed.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Dual-function move depending on user's type. Ghost-type version is much more costly but applies a powerful status. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Safe Option.
