# Fly

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, Dash, Set-Up
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Set-Up Effect: The user is moved up 25 meters into the air. Resolution Effect: The user may shift twice while in the air, using their overland or sky speed, and then comes down next to a legal target and attacks with Fly.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Two-turn move with Set-Up keyword. Grants Sky +3. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Special Attention.
