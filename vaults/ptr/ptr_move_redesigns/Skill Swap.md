# Skill Swap

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user loses one of their Abilities, selected by the user, and gains one of the target's Abilities, selected at random, for the remainder of the encounter. The target loses the copied Ability and gains the user's lost Ability. This effect ends if either the target or the user is Switched out or Fainted, but only for that Pokemon or Trainer.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Excitement.
