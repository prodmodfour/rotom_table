# Air Slash

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 3
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Air Slash Flinches the target on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Solid ranged special Flying attack with flinch chance. Contest Type: Cool.
