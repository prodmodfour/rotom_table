# Punishment

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Punishment's Damage Base is raised by +1 for each positive Combat Stage the target has, to a maximum of DB 12.

## Unlock Conditions
- Dark Type
- Insight 4
