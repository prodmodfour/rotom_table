# Smack Down

## Properties
	- Type: Rock
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target is knocked down to ground level and loses all Sky Speed for 3 turns. During this time, they may be hit by Ground-Type Moves even if normally immune.

## Unlock Conditions
- Rock Creation 2
- Individual Attack 10
- Insight 3
- Focus 3

## Notes
Anti-flying utility move.
