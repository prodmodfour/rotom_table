# Protect

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self, Interrupt, Shield, Trigger
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the user is hit by a Move, the user may use Protect. The user is instead not hit by the Move. The user does not take any damage nor is affected by any of the Move's effects.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Interrupt move. Shield keyword. Contest Type: Cute.
