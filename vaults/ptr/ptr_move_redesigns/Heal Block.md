# Heal Block

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Until the end of the encounter, the target may not gain Hit Points or Temporary Hit Points from any source. This effect ends if the target is switched out or Takes a Breather.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Powerful healing denial. Contest Type: Cute.
