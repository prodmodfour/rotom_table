# Skull Bash

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 13
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Set-Up
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Set-Up Effect: The user gains +1 Defense CS. Resolution Effect: The user may attack with Skull Bash. The target is pushed 3 meters.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Head]
- Athletics 6
- Individual Attack 30
- Individual HP 25
- Combat 5

## Notes
Two-turn move with Set-Up.
