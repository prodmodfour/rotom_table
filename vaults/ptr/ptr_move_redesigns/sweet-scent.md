## Sweet Scent

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 2, Friendly
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Targets hit by Sweet Scent gain a -2 Penalty to Evasion. Total Evasion may not be lowered to a negative value.

## Unlock Conditions
- Gas Production [Pleasant Scent]

## Notes
AoE Evasion debuff. Friendly keyword (does not hit allies). Grants Alluring.
