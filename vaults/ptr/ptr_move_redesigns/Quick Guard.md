# Quick Guard

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, Interrupt, Shield, Trigger
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the user or an adjacent ally is targeted by a Priority or Interrupt Attack, Quick Guard may be declared as an Interrupt, causing the triggering attack to have no effect.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

Interrupt move. Shield keyword. Contest Type: Cool.
