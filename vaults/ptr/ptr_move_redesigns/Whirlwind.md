# Whirlwind

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Line 6
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
All targets are pushed X meters, where X is 8 minus their weight class. If the Line targets into a Smokescreen, the smoke is dispersed. All hazards in the Whirlwind are destroyed.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Big Show. Phazing move that pushes targets and clears hazards and smoke.
