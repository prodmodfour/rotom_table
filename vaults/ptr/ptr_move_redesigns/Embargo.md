# Embargo

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect
The target cannot use or benefit from held items for the remainder of the encounter. Embargo may only affect one target at a time; if Embargo is used on a new target, the previous target is freed from the effect.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Item-denial utility move. Only one target can be under Embargo at a time. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Sabotage.
