# Jump Kick

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, Dash, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If Jump Kick misses, the user loses Hit Points equal to 1/4th of their Max Hit Points. A failure to hit due to a Move with the Shield keyword does not count as a miss. This Move cannot be used if Gravity is in effect.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Weaker version of High Jump Kick with same crash risk. Dash keyword. Contest Type: Cool.
