# Screech

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Burst 2, Friendly, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
All legal targets have their Defense lowered 2 Combat Stages.

## Unlock Conditions
- Vocal OR Resonant Body
- Intimidate 4

## Notes
Sonic keyword. Friendly keyword — hits allies too.
