# Facade

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If the user is afflicted with a Persistent Status Affliction, Facade's Damage Base is doubled to DB 14.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Double Time.
