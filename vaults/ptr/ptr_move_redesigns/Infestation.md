# Infestation

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 3, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target is put in a Vortex.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Trapping move. Vortex deals damage over time and prevents escape. Contest Type: Smart.
