# Charge Beam

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If Charge Beam successfully hits a target, roll 1d20. On a roll of 7+, the user's Special Attack is raised by +1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
— Electricity Generation 3
- Electricity Manipulation 3

## Notes
Damaging move with a high chance (70%) of boosting SpAtk.
