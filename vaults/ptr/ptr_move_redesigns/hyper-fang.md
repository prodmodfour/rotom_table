# Hyper Fang

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Hyper Fang Flinches the target on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Fangs]
- Individual Attack 30
- Combat 4

## Notes
Solid physical move with minor Flinch chance.
