# False Swipe

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, Pass
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
False Swipe's damage cannot bring a target lower than 1 Hit Point.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5 or TM

## Notes
Essential for capturing Pokemon. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal.
