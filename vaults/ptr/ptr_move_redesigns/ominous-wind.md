# Ominous Wind

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Spirit Surge
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
On 19+, the user has each of its stats raised by +1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Ghost Type
- Wind Manipulation 2
- Individual Special Attack 15

## Notes
Spirit Surge keyword. Omni-boost chance.
