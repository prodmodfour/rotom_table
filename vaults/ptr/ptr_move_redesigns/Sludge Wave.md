# Sludge Wave

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1 or Close Blast 2
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Sludge Wave Poisons targets on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or TM

## Notes
AoE move with flexible targeting. Contest Type: Cool. Steady Performance.
