# Razor Shell

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Razor Shell lowers the target's Defense 1 Combat Stage on an even-numbered roll.

## Unlock Conditions
- Species: Oshawott, Dewott, Samurott

Dash keyword.
