# Payback

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If the target hit the user with a Damaging Move on the previous turn, Payback has a Damage Base of 10 instead.

## Unlock Conditions
- Individual Attack 15
- Individual Health 20
- Opportunist 1

## Notes
Conditional power boost. Doubles effective DB when retaliating.
