# Sandstorm

## Properties
	- Type: Rock
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field, Weather
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The weather changes to a Sandstorm for 5 rounds. While it is Sandstorming, all non-Ground, Rock, or Steel Type Pokemon lose a Tick of Hit Points at the beginning of their turn.

## Unlock Conditions
- Earth Manipulation 3
- Rock Manipulation 3
- Rock Creation 1
- Focus 4

## Notes
Weather move. Damages non-Ground/Rock/Steel types.
