# Copycat

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The user copies the Move the target used on their last turn. The user may choose new targets for the copied Move. Copycat cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Auto-hit move mimic effect. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Attention Grabber.
