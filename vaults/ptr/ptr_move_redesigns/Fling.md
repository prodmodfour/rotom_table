# Fling

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Fling
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user throws a held item, determining the effect of Fling. Damage Base varies based on the item thrown.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Fling keyword. Damage depends on held item. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Catching Up.
