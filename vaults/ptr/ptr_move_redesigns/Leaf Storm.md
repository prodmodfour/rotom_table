# Leaf Storm

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 13
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 8, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 7

## Effect

## Unlock Conditions

## Notes
