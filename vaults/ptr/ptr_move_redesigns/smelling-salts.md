# Smelling Salts

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the target is Paralyzed, Smelling Salts' Damage Base is doubled to 14, and cures the target of Paralysis.

## Unlock Conditions
- Medicine 3
- Individual Attack 30

## Notes
Anti-Paralysis synergy move.
