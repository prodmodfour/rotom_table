# Pluck

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Pluck takes the target's Held Item or Accessory Slot Item and attaches it to Pluck's user, if the user is not holding anything.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Item-stealing attack. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Attention Grabber.
