# Waterfall

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Waterfall Flinches the target on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Water Creation 3
- Water Manipulation 3
- Individual Attack 20
- Acrobatics 4

## Notes
Premier physical Water-type attack with Flinch chance.
