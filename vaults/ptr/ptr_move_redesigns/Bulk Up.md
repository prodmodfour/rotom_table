# Bulk Up

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Raise the user's Attack 1 Combat Stage and raise the user's Defense 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Dual physical buff. One of the standard stat-boosting moves. Contest Type: Beauty.
