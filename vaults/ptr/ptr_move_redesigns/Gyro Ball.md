# Gyro Ball

## Properties
	- Type: Steel
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target reveals their Speed Stat (including Combat Stages). If it is higher than the user's (again, including Combat Stages), subtract the user's Speed Stat from the target's, and apply the difference as Bonus Damage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Favors slow users against fast targets. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Double Time.
