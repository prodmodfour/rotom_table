# Body Slam

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Body Slam Paralyzes the target on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions

## Notes
Strong Normal physical with paralysis chance. Contest Type: Tough.
