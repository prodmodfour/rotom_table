# Foresight

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self, Swift Action
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Swift Action. Foresight may be activated as a Swift Action on the user's turn. For the rest of the turn, the user's Normal-Type and Fighting-Type Moves can hit and affect Ghost-Type targets, and the user can see through the Illusion Ability, Moves with the Illusion keyword, and effects created by the Illusionist Capability, ignoring all effects from those.

## Unlock Conditions
- Perception 4
- Insight 4

## Notes
Ghost-type immunity bypass.
