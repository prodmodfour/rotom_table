# Dragon Tail

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Push
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target is Pushed 6 meters minus their Weight Class. On a roll of 15+, the target is also Tripped.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Displacement attack with weight-dependent Push distance and Trip chance. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Big Show.
