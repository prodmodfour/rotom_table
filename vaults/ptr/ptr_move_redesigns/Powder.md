# Powder

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Interrupt, Powder
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target is dusted with a Coat of flammable powder. If it uses a damaging Fire-Type attack, the attack is negated and instead creates a Blast 3 centered on itself as the powder explodes, and the Coat is removed. All targets within the Blast take damage equal to what the user of the Fire-Type attack would roll for the damage of their attack. This damage is Typeless or Fire-Type, whichever would be more effective.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Anti-Fire utility. Interrupt keyword. Powder keyword. Vivillon's signature move. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Sabotage.
