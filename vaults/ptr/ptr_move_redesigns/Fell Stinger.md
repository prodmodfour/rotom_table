# Fell Stinger

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 3
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the user successfully knocks out a target with Fell Stinger, raise their Attack by 2 Combat Stages.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Low base power but rewards KOs. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Desperation.
