# Waterfall

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Waterfall Flinches the target on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Steady Performance. Premier physical Water-type attack with Flinch chance.
