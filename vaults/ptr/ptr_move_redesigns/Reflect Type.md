# Reflect Type

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Reflect Type changes one of the user's Types into one Type of your choice that the target has for the rest of the scene.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Beauty.
