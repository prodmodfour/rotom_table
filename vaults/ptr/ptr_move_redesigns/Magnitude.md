# Magnitude

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: —
	- Range: Burst 2, Groundsource
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
When you use Magnitude, roll 1d6. Magnitude's Damage Base is equal to 5+X, where X is the value of the d6. Magnitude can hit targets that are underground, including those using the Move Dig.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Variable damage (DB 6-11). Grants Groundshaper capability. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Desperation.
