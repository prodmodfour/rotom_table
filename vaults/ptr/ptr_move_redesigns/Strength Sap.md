# Strength Sap

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user restores HP equal to the target's Attack stat. Then the target's Attack is lowered by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Gen 7 move. Healing scales with the target's Attack stat. Combines recovery with debuffing.
