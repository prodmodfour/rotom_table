# Mind Reader

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target becomes Read to the user until the end of the user's next turn. The user may end this effect when making an Attack on the target, causing that attack to automatically hit; or when the Read target uses an Attack against the user, causing that attack to automatically miss. If the user has the Telepathy Capability, the user automatically succeeds on a mind-reading attempt against the target, and may listen to the target's surface thoughts as long as they remain Read. Mind Reader automatically misses against targets with the Mindlock Capability.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15
