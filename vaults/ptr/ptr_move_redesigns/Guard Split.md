# Guard Split

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target loses 5 Defense and 5 Special Defense. If they do, the user gains 5 Damage Reduction. These effects last until the end of the scene.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

Stat redistribution move. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal.
