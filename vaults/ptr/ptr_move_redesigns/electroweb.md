# Electroweb

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: 4, Ranged Blast 2
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
All legal targets have their Speed lowered by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Electricity Generation 2
- Electricity Manipulation 2
- Focus 3

## Notes
AoE Electric attack with Speed-lowering effect on all targets hit.
