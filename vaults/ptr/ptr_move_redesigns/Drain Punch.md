# Drain Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or Move Tutor

## Notes
HP-draining physical Fighting attack. Aura keyword. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Good Show!
