# Discharge

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: All Cardinally Adjacent Targets
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Discharge Paralyzes all legal targets on a roll of 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Electricity Generation 3
- Individual Special Attack 20


## Notes
Hits all cardinally adjacent targets with a strong Paralysis chance.
