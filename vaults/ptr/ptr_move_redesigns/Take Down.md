# Take Down

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 5
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Recoil 1/3
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
You may perform a Trip Maneuver against the target as a Free Action. The user takes recoil damage equal to 1/3 of the damage dealt.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Steady Performance. Keywords: Dash, Recoil 1/3.
