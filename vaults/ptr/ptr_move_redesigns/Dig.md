# Dig

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1, Set-Up, Full Action, Groundsource
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Set-Up Effect: The user shifts 25 meters underground and their turn ends. Resolution Effect: The user may shift horizontally using their burrow or overland speed, then shifts 25 meters straight up. Upon reaching the surface, the user attacks with Dig, creating a Burst 1.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Two-turn attack with underground evasion phase. Grants Burrow +3. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Special Attention.
