# Venom Drench

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the target is Poisoned, lower the target's Attack, Special Attack, and Speed by 1 Combat Stage each.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Gen 6 move. Not in PTU 1.05. Adapted to PTU conventions (EOT, Status). Powerful triple debuff but conditional on the target already being Poisoned.
