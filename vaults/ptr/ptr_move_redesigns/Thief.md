# Thief

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Thief takes the target's Held Item or Accessory Slot Item and attaches it to Thief's user, if the user is not holding anything.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Attention Grabber. Combines damage with item theft.
