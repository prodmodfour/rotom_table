# Mind Reader

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 6

## Effect
Swift Action. The target becomes Read to the user until the end of the user's turn. The user may end this effect when making an Attack on the target, causing that attack to automatically hit.

## Unlock Conditions
- Telepath
- Focus 6

## Notes
Prediction/accuracy move.
