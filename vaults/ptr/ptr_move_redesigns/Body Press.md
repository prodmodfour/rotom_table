# Body Press

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Body Press uses the user's Defense stat instead of their Attack stat when calculating damage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Gen 8 move. Not in PTU 1.05. Unique damage calculation rewards defensive builds. Pairs well with Iron Defense, Bulk Up, etc.
