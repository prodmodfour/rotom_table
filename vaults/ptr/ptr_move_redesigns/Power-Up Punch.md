# Power-Up Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If Power-Up Punch successfully hits a target, the user's Attack is raised by +1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough.
