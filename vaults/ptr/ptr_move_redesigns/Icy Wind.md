# Icy Wind

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Cone 2
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
All Legal Targets have their Speed lowered 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
AoE Speed debuff with damage. Contest Type: Beauty.
