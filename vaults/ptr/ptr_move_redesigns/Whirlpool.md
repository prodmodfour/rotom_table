# Whirlpool

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 3, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target is put in a Vortex.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Safe Option. Trapping move that deals residual damage over time via Vortex status.
