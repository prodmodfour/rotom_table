# Assurance

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
When you hit with Assurance, if the target has already been damaged by a Move on the same round Assurance is being used, Assurance has a Damage Base of 12 instead. This effect may trigger only once per Scene per Target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Rewards coordinated attacks. DB doubles if an ally already hit the target this round. Contest Type: Beauty.
