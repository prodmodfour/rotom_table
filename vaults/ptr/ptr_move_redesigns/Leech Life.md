# Leech Life

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 2
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
After the target takes damage, the user gains Hit Points equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Draining move. Low power but sustain healing. Contest Type: Smart.
