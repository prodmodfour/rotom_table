# Muddy Water

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 5
	- Range: Close Blast 2
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
As a Shift Action, the user may Move to any open square in Muddy Water's area of effect without provoking any Attacks of Opportunity. On 16+, the Accuracy of all targets is lowered by 1.

## Unlock Conditions
- Water Manipulation 2
- Water Creation 2
- Eath Manipulation 2
- Individual Special Attack 25
- Focus 4

## Notes
AoE water attack with repositioning.
