# Swagger

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
The target's Attack is raised 2 Combat Stages. Swagger Confuses the target.

## Unlock Conditions
- Intimidate 3
- Deception 3

## Notes
Social keyword. Risk/reward debuff — boosts the target's Attack but Confuses them.
