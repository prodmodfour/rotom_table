# Lick

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 3
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Lick Paralyzes the target on 15+ during Accuracy Check.

## Notes
Weak Ghost physical with decent Paralysis chance. Contest Type: Tough.
