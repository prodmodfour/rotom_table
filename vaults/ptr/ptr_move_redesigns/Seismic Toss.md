# Seismic Toss

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target loses Hit Points equal to the level of Seismic Toss's user.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Fixed damage based on user's level. Contest Type: Tough.
