# Double Edge

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Recoil 1/3
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
No additional effect beyond damage. The user takes recoil damage equal to 1/3 of the damage dealt.

## Unlock Conditions
- Individual Attack 35
- Individual HP 25
- Athletics 6
- Combat 5

## Notes
High-power Normal attack with significant recoil. Same move as Double-Edge.
