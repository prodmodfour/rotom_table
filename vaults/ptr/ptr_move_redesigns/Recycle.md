# Recycle

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The effect of a consumable item used earlier in the encounter is used again as if it had not been destroyed. The item is still gone.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart.
