# Confusion

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Confusion Confuses the target on a roll of 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
— Mind Manipulation 1
- Individual Special Attack 10
