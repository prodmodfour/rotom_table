# Synchronoise

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 3
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Synchronoise can only hit targets that share a type with the user.

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 5
- Focus 5
- Individual Special Attack 30

## Notes
High power but highly conditional due to shared-type requirement.
