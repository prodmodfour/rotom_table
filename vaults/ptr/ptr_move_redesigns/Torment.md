# Torment

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 10, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target becomes Suppressed.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal. Social keyword. Suppressed targets cannot use the same move twice in a row.
