# Wonder Room

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
For 5 rounds, the area is considered Wondered. While Wondered, each individual Pokemon's Defense and Special Defense Stats are switched.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Tease. Field-wide stat swap that benefits teams with lopsided defensive matchups.
