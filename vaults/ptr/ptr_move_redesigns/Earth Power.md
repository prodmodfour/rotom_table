# Earth Power

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Groundsource
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Earth Power lowers the Special Defense of all legal targets by 1 Combat Stage on a roll of 16+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20

## Notes
Strong special Ground attack with SpDef-lowering secondary effect. Groundsource keyword. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Desperation.
