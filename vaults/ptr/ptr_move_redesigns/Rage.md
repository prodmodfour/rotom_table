# Rage

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 2
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Spirit Surge
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
The user becomes Enraged. Until the end of the user's next turn, if the user is Enraged, the user gains +1 Attack Combat Stage whenever they are damaged by a Damaging Move or Attack.

## Unlock Conditions
None

## Notes
Spirit Surge keyword. Contest Type: Cool.
