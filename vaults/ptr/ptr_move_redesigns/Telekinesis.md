# Telekinesis

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target becomes Lifted. While Lifted, they gain the Levitate Ability, are Slowed, and lose all Movement Capabilities except for the Levitate 4 granted by Levitate (reduced to 2 by the Slow condition). While Lifted, the target may not apply any Evasion bonuses to determine whether they are hit by Moves or not. The Lifted target may use a Shift Action to roll 1d20; on a result of 16+, they stop being Lifted.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Steady Performance. Unique status that grants levitation but removes evasion.
