# Bounce

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Full Action
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The user first Shifts, gaining a +1 Bonus to Movement Speed and to their Jump Capabilities. After the user Shifts, they may attack with Bounce. The target becomes Vulnerable, and is Paralyzed on 16+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Full Action move with enhanced mobility. Grants High Jump +1. Contest Type: Cute.
