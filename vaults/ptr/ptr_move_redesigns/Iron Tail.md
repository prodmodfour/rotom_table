# Iron Tail

## Properties
	- Type: Steel
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 6
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Iron Tail lowers the target's Defense 1 Combat Stage on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
High power but low accuracy. Defense debuff chance. Smite keyword. Contest Type: Cool.
