# Gunk Shot

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 5
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 6

## Effect
Gunk Shot Poisons the target on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or Move Tutor

## Notes
High-power Poison move with easy Poison proc. Smite keyword. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
