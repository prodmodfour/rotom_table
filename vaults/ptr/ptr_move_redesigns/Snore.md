# Snore

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Snore Flinches all legal targets on 15+. Snore may only be used by Sleeping users.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Can only be used while Asleep. Sonic keyword. Contest Type: Cute. Steady Performance.
