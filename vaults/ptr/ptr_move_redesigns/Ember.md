# Ember

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Ember Burns the target on a roll of 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Basic Fire-type attack with Burn chance. Grants Firestarter capability. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
