# Poison Tail

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Poison Tail is a Critical Hit on 18+, and Poisons the target on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Poison Coated [Tail]
- Individual Attack 20

## Notes
High crit rate with poison chance.
