# Last Resort

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 14
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Last Resort can only be used after the user has performed 5 other different Moves in its Move List during a single fight, without being switched out.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25

## Notes
Extremely high power but strict activation condition. Dash keyword. Contest Type: Cute.
