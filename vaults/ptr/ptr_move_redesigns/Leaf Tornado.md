# Leaf Tornado

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 6, Ranged Blast 3
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Small or Medium targets in the central square of the blast are not hit. On 15+, all legal targets have their Accuracy lowered by -1.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
AoE with Accuracy debuff. Central square safe zone for small/medium targets. Contest Type: Beauty.
