# Crunch

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Crunch lowers the target's Defense by 1 Combat Stage on a roll of 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Solid Dark-type physical attack with a defense-lowering secondary effect. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act.
