# Mud Sport

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Burst 2
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
All targets in the burst, including the user, gain a Coat which grants them 1 Step of Resistance to Electric Type Moves. After a target has been hit by a damaging Electric Type Move, the coat is removed.

## Unlock Conditions
None

## Notes
Protective coat move. Anti-Electric utility. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Sabotage.
