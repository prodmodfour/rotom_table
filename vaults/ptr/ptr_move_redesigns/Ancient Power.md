# Ancient Power

## Properties
	- Type: Rock
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Spirit Surge
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
On 19+, the user has each of its stats raised by +1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Spirit Surge keyword. The omni-boost chance makes this an evolution trigger move for several species. Contest Type: Tough.
