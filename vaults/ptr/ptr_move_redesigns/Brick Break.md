# Brick Break

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Light Screen and Reflect may not be activated in response to Brick Break.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Screen-piercing attack. Bypasses Light Screen and Reflect defenses. Contest Type: Cool.
