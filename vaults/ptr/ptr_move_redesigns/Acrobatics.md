# Acrobatics

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, Dash, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the user is not holding an item, Acrobatics instead has a Damage Base of 11.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Rewards unequipped users with nearly doubled damage. Dash keyword allows shifting before attacking. Contest Type: Smart.
