# Freeze-Dry

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
When calculating Weakness and Resistance for Freeze-Dry, Water-Typed targets calculate damage as if Water was weak to Ice.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Unique type effectiveness override against Water. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Saving Grace.
