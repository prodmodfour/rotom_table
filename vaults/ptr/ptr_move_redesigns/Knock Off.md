# Knock Off

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Choose one of the target's Held Items or Accessory Slot Items. It is knocked to the ground.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Item removal utility. Contest Type: Smart.
