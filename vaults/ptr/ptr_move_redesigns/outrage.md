# Outrage

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, all adjacent foes, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
After damage is dealt, the user becomes Enraged and Confused.

## Unlock Conditions
- Dragon Type
- Individual Attack 30
- Combat 5

## Notes
Powerful multi-target attack with self-inflicted drawback. Smite keyword.
