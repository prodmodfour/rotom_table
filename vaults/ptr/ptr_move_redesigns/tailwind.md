# Tailwind

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field (allies)
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
For the remainder of the encounter, all allied trainers and Pokemon gain +5 to their Initiative. Multiple instances of Tailwind cannot stack.

## Unlock Conditions
- Wind Manipulation 3
- Focus 3

Grants Guster. Powerful team-wide speed support.
