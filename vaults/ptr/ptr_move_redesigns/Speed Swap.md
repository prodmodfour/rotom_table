# Speed Swap

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user and the target swap their Speed stats. This includes base stats and any Combat Stage changes. The swap lasts for the remainder of the encounter.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Gen 7 move. Stat-swapping utility.
