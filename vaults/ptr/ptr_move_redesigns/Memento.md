# Memento

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 8, 1 Target, Trigger, Free Action
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Memento may be used as a Free Action when the user becomes Fainted. Memento's target has each of their stats lowered by -2 Combat Stages.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20

## Notes
Sacrificial debuff. Activated on fainting. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Big Show.
