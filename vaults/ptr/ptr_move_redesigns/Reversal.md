# Reversal

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
For each Injury the user has, Reversal's Damage Base is increased by +1.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Damage increases as user takes more Injuries. Contest Type: Cool.
