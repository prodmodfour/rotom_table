# Belch

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Cone 2
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Belch cannot be used if the user has not traded in a Digestion Buff during this Scene.

## Unlock Conditions
— Large Belly

## Notes
Very high power AoE but requires Berry/Digestion Buff consumption as a prerequisite.
