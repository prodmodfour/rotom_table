# Natural Gift

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Berry
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Refer to the Move Keywords Berry list. Natural Gift deals damage according to the Berry list and Natural Gift's Type is also defined there. The Berry's Digestion Buff is nullified and is not used.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Berry-dependent type and damage. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Desperation.
