# Acid Spray

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Acid Spray lowers the target's Special Defense 2 Combat Stages.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Guaranteed SpDef drop on hit makes this a strong setup move despite low damage. Contest Type: Smart.
