# Calm Mind

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Raise the user's Special Attack 1 Combat Stage and raise the user's Special Defense 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Dual special buff. The special counterpart to Bulk Up. Contest Type: Smart.
