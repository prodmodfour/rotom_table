# Power Swap

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user and the target trade Combat Stage values for the Attack stat, and then for the Special Attack stat.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Beauty.
