# Aqua Ring

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self, Coat
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Aqua Ring covers the user in a Coat that heals the user at the beginning of their turn. The user is healed a Tick of Hit Points each turn.

## Unlock Conditions
— Water Creation 3
- Water Manipulation 3
- Medicine 4

## Notes
Sustained healing via Coat mechanic.
