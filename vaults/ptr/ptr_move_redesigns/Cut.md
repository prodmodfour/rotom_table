# Cut

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, Pass
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Cut ignores up to 5 Damage Reduction. Defenses are not considered Damage Reduction for this purpose.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Pass keyword allows hitting multiple targets in a line. Useful for bypassing Damage Reduction effects. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
