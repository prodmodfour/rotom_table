# Draco Meteor

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 13
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 8, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 7

## Effect
After dealing damage, the user's Special Attack is lowered by 2 Combat Stages.

## Unlock Conditions
- Individual Special Attack 45
- Dragon Type
- Focus 4

## Notes
Extremely powerful Dragon-type AoE with a harsh Special Attack penalty. Smite keyword.
