# Wide Guard

## Properties
	- Type: Rock
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Burst 1, Interrupt, Shield, Trigger
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If an ally adjacent to Wide Guard's user is hit by a Move, you may use Wide Guard as an Interrupt. All targets adjacent to Wide Guard's user, including the user, are instead not hit by the triggering Move and do not suffer any of its effects.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal. Keywords: Interrupt, Shield, Trigger. Team-wide protective move.
