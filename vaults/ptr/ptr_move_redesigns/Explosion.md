# Explosion

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 25
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 2
	- Energy Cost: 8

## Effect
The user's Hit Points are set to -50% of their full Hit Point value. This Hit Point loss cannot be prevented or reduced in any way. The user's loyalty toward its trainer may be lowered.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 30

## Notes
Extremely powerful self-sacrificing move. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Big Show.
