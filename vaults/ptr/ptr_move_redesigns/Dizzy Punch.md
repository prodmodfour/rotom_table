# Dizzy Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Dizzy Punch Confuses the target on a roll of 17+.

## Unlock Conditions

## Notes
