# Astonish

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 3
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Astonish Flinches the target on 15+. Once per scene, if the target is unaware of the user's presence, Astonish automatically Flinches the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Low-power Ghost physical with surprise flinch utility. Contest Type: Smart.
