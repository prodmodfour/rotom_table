# Giga Drain

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
After the target takes damage, the user gains Hit Points equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Draining move, upgraded version of Mega Drain. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Good Show!
