# Psyshock

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
When calculating damage, the target subtracts their Defense from Psyshock's damage instead of their Special Defense. Psyshock is still otherwise Special (Special Evasion is used to avoid it, Mirror Coat can reflect it, etc.).

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 3
- Individual Special Attack 20
- Combat 4
- Insight 3

## Notes
Targets physical Defense despite being Special class.
