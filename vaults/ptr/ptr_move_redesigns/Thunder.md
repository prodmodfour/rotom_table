# Thunder

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 11
	- AC: 7
	- Range: 12, 1 Target, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Thunder Paralyzes its target on 15+. If the target is in Sunny Weather, Thunder's Accuracy Check is 11. If the target is in Rainy Weather, Thunder cannot miss. If the target is airborne as a result of Fly or Sky Drop, Thunder cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act. Keywords: Smite. Weather-dependent accuracy; extremely powerful under Rain.
