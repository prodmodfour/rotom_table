# Wild Charge

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Recoil 1/3
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
No additional effect. The user takes recoil damage equal to 1/3 of the damage dealt.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Steady Performance. Keywords: Dash, Recoil 1/3. High-power physical Electric attack with recoil cost.
