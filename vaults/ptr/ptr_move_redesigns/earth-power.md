# Earth Power

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Groundsource
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Earth Power lowers the Special Defense of all legal targets by 1 Combat Stage on a roll of 16+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Earth Manipulation 4
- Individual Special Attack 25
- Focus 4

## Notes
Strong special Ground attack with SpDef-lowering secondary effect. Groundsource keyword.
