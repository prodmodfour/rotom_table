# Worry Seed

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
You choose one of the target's Abilities. Worry Seed changes that Ability to Insomnia for the remainder of the encounter.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Excitement. Ability replacement move — shuts down key Abilities by overwriting them with Insomnia.
