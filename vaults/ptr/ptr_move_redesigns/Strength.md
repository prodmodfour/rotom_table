# Strength

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Push
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
You may immediately initiate a Push Maneuver as a Free Action. The Maneuver automatically hits, but you must still make the Opposed Roll.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Push keyword. Grants +1 Power. Contest Type: Tough. Steady Performance.
