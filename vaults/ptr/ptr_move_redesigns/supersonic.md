# Supersonic

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 6
	- Range: 4, 1 Target, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target becomes Confused. On miss, the target suffers a -2 penalty to Accuracy Rolls for one full round.

## Unlock Conditions
- Resonant Body OR Vocal 
- Focus 2

## Notes
Sonic keyword. Even a miss applies a debuff.
