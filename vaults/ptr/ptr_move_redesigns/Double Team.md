# Double Team

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self, Illusion, Coat
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user gains 3 activations of Double Team. The user may either activate Double Team when being targeted by an attack to increase their Evasion by +2 against that attack, or when making an attack to increase their Accuracy by +2 for that attack.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Versatile Coat move providing flexible offensive or defensive accuracy bonuses. Illusion keyword. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Reliable.
