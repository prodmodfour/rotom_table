# Cross Poison

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, Pass
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Cross Poison scores a Critical Hit on a roll of 18+ and Poisons the target on a roll of 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Pass keyword allows hitting multiple targets in a line. Combines crit chance with poison chance. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
