# Hammer Arm

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The user lowers their Speed 1 Combat Stage after using this move.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Self-debuff on use balances high power. Contest Type: Cool.
