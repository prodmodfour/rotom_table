# Tail Slap

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 3
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Five Strike
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Hits up to five times (Five Strike keyword). No additional effect.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Tail]
- Combat 2

## Notes
Multi-hit move with Five Strike keyword — rolls separately for each hit.
