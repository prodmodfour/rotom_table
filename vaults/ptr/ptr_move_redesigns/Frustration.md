# Frustration

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Frustration's Damage Base is equal to 9 minus the user's Loyalty Value. Using Frustration may make your Pokemon dislike you.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5 or TM

## Notes
Damage scales inversely with loyalty. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Desperation.
