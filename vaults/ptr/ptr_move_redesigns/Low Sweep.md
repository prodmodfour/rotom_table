# Low Sweep

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Lowers the target's Speed 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Reliable Fighting move with guaranteed Speed debuff. Contest Type: Tough.
