# Pollen Puff

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
When used on a foe, Pollen Puff deals damage as normal. When used on an ally, Pollen Puff instead restores Hit Points equal to half of the ally's full Hit Point value.

## Unlock Conditions
- Gas Production [Spore Release]
- Medicine 3


## Notes
Dual-purpose move: damages enemies or heals allies. Ribombee's signature move in the games.
