# Echoed Voice

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 3, 1 Target, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If Echoed Voice was used by any combatant in the Encounter on the previous round, its Damage Base increases by +4. If Echoed Voice was used during both of the previous two rounds, its Damage Base increases by +8 instead.

## Unlock Conditions
- Vocal OR Resonant Body
- Individual Special Attack 10
- Interacted with another pokemon with Vocal or Resonant Body

## Notes
Scaling damage move that rewards consecutive use. Sonic keyword.
