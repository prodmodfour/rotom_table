# Captivate

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Cone 2, Friendly, Social
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Captivate lowers the target's Special Attack 2 Combat Stages. Captivate may not affect something that is the same gender as the user or something that is genderless.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Gender-dependent AoE SpAtk debuff. Friendly and Social keywords. Contest Type: Beauty.
