# Wake-Up Slap

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the target is Asleep, Wake-Up Slap has a Damage Base of 10 instead, and cures the target of Sleep.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal. Conditional power boost against sleeping targets with built-in wake-up.
