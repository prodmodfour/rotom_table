# Foul Play

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The target reveals its Attack stat. When calculating damage, add the target's Attack stat instead of the user's Attack stat.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Uses the target's Attack for damage calculation. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
