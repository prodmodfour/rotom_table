# Bubble Beam

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Bubble Beam lowers the target's Speed on 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Upgraded Bubble — higher damage, single target, slightly lower Speed drop chance. Contest Type: Beauty.
