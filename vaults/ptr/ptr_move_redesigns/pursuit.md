# Pursuit

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the foe is fleeing or being switched out, Pursuit may be used as an Interrupt, targeting the triggering foe. When used as an Interrupt, Pursuit grants the user a +5 bonus to all Movement Speeds, and has a Damage Base of 8.

## Unlock Conditions
- Opportunist 2

## Notes
Interrupt capability on fleeing/switching targets.
