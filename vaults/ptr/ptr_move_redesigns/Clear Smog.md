# Clear Smog

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Clear Smog resets the target's Combat Stages to their defaults and destroys all Coats on the target. Clear Smog cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Auto-hit move. Useful for removing enemy buffs. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Sabotage.
