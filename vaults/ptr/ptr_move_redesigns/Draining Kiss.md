# Draining Kiss

## Properties
	- Type: Fairy
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
After the target takes damage, the user gains Hit Points equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
HP-draining special Fairy attack. Melee range despite being Special class. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Good Show!
