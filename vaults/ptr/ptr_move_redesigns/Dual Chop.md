# Dual Chop

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
No additional effect beyond damage. Double Strike causes this move to hit twice.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Dragon-type multi-hit attack. Each strike is rolled separately. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Reliable.
