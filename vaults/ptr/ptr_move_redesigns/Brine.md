# Brine

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the target's Hit Points are under 50%, Brine's Damage Base is increased to 13.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Finisher move — nearly doubles in power against low-HP targets. Contest Type: Smart.
