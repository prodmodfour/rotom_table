# Captivate

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Cone 2, Friendly, Social
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Captivate lowers the target's Special Attack 2 Combat Stages. The target becomes infatuated if they fail a DC 10 + {User's Charm} Focus Check

- Performance 5
