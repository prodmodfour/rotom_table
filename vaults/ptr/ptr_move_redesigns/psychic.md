# Psychic

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 5, 1 Target, Push
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The target is Pushed 1 meter in any direction. Psychic lowers the target's Special Defense 1 Combat Stage on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 4
- Telekinetic
- Individual Special Attack 25
- Focus 5
- Psychic Type

## Notes
Grants Telekinetic.
