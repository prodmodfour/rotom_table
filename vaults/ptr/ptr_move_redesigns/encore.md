# Encore

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Roll 1d6. On a result of 1 or 2, the target becomes Confused. On a result of 3 or 4, the target becomes Suppressed. On a result of 5 or 6, the target becomes Enraged.

## Unlock Conditions
- Performance 4 OR Command 4
- Charm 3
- Deception 2

## Notes
Random disruptive status effect with Social keyword.
