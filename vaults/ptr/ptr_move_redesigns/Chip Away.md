# Chip Away

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Ignore any Armor, Damage Reduction, or changes in the target's Defense or Special Defense (such as from Combat Stages) when calculating damage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Pierces all defensive buffs and damage reduction. Excellent counter to setup-heavy defenders. Contest Type: Tough.
