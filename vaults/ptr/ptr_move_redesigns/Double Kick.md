# Double Kick

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 3
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
No additional effect beyond damage. Double Strike causes this move to hit twice.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Basic Fighting-type multi-hit attack. Each hit is rolled separately. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Reliable.
