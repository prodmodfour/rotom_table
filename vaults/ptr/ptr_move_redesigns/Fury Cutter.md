# Fury Cutter

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If Fury Cutter is used successfully and consecutively on the same target, the Damage Base is increased by +4 to a maximum of 16. For example, the first hit would have a DB of 4; the second hit a DB of 8; the third hit a DB of 12; the fourth and further hits a DB of 16. If Fury Cutter misses or fails to damage its target, its Damage Base resets.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Escalating damage with consecutive hits. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Reliable.
