# Tickle

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Lower the target's Attack by 1 Combat Stage and lower the target's Defense by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Excitement. Dual debuff that weakens both physical stats.
