# Dragon Rage

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If it hits, Dragon Rage causes the target to lose 15 Hit Points. Dragon Rage is Special and interacts with other moves and effects as such (Special Evasion may be applied to avoid it, Mirror Coat can reflect it, etc.).

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Fixed-damage move dealing exactly 15 HP. Useful at low levels, scales poorly. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
