# Dream Eater

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Dream Eater can only target Sleeping targets. After the target takes damage, the user gains Hit Points equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target. Dream Eater does not wake up sleeping targets.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20

## Notes
Powerful HP-draining attack restricted to sleeping targets. Does not break Sleep. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Good Show!
