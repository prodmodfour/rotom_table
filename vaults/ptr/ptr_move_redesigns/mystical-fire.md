# Mystical Fire

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Mystical Fire lowers the target's Special Attack by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Sorcery

## Notes
Guaranteed Special Attack debuff.
