# Thunder Fang

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Thunder Fang Paralyzes or Flinches on 18-19 during Accuracy Check; flip a coin to determine whether the foe gets Paralyzed or Flinches. On 20 during Accuracy Check, the foe is both Paralyzed and Flinched.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Steady Performance. Part of the elemental Fang trio with Ice Fang and Fire Fang.
