# Brave Bird

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, Dash, Push, Recoil 1/3
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The target is pushed back 2 meters.

## Unlock Conditions
— Flier
- Focus 6
- Acrobatics 4 OR Athletics 4

## Notes
High-power recoil move. User takes 1/3 of damage dealt as recoil. Dash and Push keywords.
