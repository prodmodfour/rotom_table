# Throat Chop

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
For 2 rounds after being hit, the target cannot use sound-based moves.

## Unlock Conditions
- Combat 4
- Deception 3
- Individual Attack 20
- Dark Type

## Notes
Gen 7 move. Not in PTU 1.05. Adapted to PTU conventions (EOT, DB 8). Specifically counters Sonic-keyword moves like Hyper Voice, Uproar, etc.
