# Curse

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
The user loses 1/3rd of their Max Hit Points (this loss cannot be prevented in any way) and a target within 8 meters becomes Cursed.

## Unlock Conditions
- Ghost Type
- Occult Education 4
- Focus 4
