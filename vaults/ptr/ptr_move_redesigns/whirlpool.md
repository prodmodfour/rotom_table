# Whirlpool

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 3, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Swift Action: The target is put in a Vortex.

## Unlock Conditions
- Water Creation 2
- Water Manipulation 3
- Focus 4

## Notes
Trapping move that deals residual damage over time via Vortex status.
