# Night Shade

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target loses Hit Points equal to the level of Night Shade's user. Do not apply weakness or resistance. Do not apply stats.

## Unlock Conditions
- Ghost Type
- Occult Education 1

## Notes
Level-based fixed damage. Ignores type effectiveness and stat modifiers.
