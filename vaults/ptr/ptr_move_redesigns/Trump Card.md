# Trump Card

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Whenever Trump Card is used, the user gains a Trump Count after the attack is resolved. Trump Card's Damage Base is increased by +2 for each Trump Count.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act. Scaling damage move that grows stronger with repeated use.
