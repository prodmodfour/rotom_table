# Ice Beam

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Ice Beam Freezes on 19+ during Accuracy Check.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Premier special Ice attack. Reliable with Freeze chance. Contest Type: Beauty.
