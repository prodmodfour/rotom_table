# Power Trip

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 2
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Power Trip's Damage Base is increased by +2 for each positive Combat Stage the user has, across all stats.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Gen 7 move. Similar to Stored Power but Physical/Dark. Contest Type: Tough.
