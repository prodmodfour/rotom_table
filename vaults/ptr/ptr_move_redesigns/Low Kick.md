# Low Kick

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Weight Class
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Low Kick's Damage Base is equal to twice the target's Weight Class.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Damage scales with target's weight. Weight Class keyword. Stronger against heavier targets. Contest Type: Tough.
