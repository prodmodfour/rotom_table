# Psych Up

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user's Combat Stages are changed to match the target's Combat Stages. Psych Up cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
