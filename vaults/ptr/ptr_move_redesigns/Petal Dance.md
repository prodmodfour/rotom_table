# Petal Dance

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, all adjacent foes, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
After damage is dealt, the user becomes Enraged and Confused.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25

## Notes
Powerful multi-target attack with self-inflicted drawback. Smite keyword. Grass-type equivalent of Outrage. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Safe Option.
