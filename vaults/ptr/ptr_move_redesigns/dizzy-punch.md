# Dizzy Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Dizzy Punch Confuses the target on a roll of 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Hands]
- Individual Attack 15
- Combat 4
- Individual Speed 25

## Notes
Solid Normal-type physical attack with Confusion chance.
