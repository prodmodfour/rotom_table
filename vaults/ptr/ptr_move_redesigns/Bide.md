# Bide

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Burst 1, Friendly
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user may use Bide as a Reaction Move upon being Hit by a Damaging Move. During their next available turn, the user may Shift and then use Bide, causing all Adjacent foes to lose X HP, where X is the amount of Damage taken since declaring use of Bide. Loss of life through effects such as Poison is not counted as Damage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Counter-style revenge move. Damage reflects what was received. Friendly keyword means allies are not affected. Contest Type: Tough.
