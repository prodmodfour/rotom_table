# Hex

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Once a Scene, if Hex's target has a Status Affliction, you may have Hex's Damage Base be 13 instead.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Punishes statused targets with massive damage boost. Contest Type: Smart.
