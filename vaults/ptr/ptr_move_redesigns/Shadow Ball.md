# Shadow Ball

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Shadow Ball lowers the target's Special Defense by 1 Combat Stage on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Exhausting Act.
