# Fire Fang

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Fire Fang Burns or Flinches on 18-19 during Accuracy Check; flip a coin to determine whether the foe gets Burned or Flinches. On 20 during Accuracy Check, the foe is Burned and Flinches.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Dual secondary effect (Burn/Flinch). Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
