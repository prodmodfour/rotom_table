# Steel Beam

## Properties
	- Type: Steel
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 14
	- AC: 3
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 7

## Effect
The user loses half of their maximum Hit Points after using Steel Beam. This HP loss cannot be prevented in any way.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 30

## Notes
Gen 8 move. Extremely powerful but heavy self-damage cost. Recoil is HP loss, not recoil damage.
