# Wood Hammer

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Recoil 1/3
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
No additional effect. The user takes recoil damage equal to 1/3 of the damage dealt.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Desperation. Keywords: Dash, Recoil 1/3. Strongest physical Grass-type attack with recoil.
