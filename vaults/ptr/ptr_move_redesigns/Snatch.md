# Snatch

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Interrupt
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If the target uses a Self-Targeting Move, you may use Snatch. You gain the benefits of the Self-Targeting Move instead of the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Interrupt move. Contest Type: Smart. Attention Grabber.
