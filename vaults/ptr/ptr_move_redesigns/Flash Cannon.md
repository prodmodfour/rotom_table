# Flash Cannon

## Properties
	- Type: Steel
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Flash Cannon lowers the target's Special Defense by 1 Combat Stage on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act.
