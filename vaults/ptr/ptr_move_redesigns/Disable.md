# Disable

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 1 Target, Trigger
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Disable may be used as a Free Action that does not take up a Command whenever the user is hit by a Move. That Move becomes Disabled for the attacker.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Reactive move-locking effect. Triggered when hit by an attack. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Excitement.
