# Nightmare

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Nightmare can only hit legal targets that are Asleep. The target gains Bad Sleep.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Requires target to be Asleep. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Excitement.
