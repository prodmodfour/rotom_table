# Entrainment

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target gains one of the user's Abilities for 3 turns.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Ability-sharing utility move. Can be used offensively to impose a detrimental ability or supportively to share a beneficial one. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Catching Up.
