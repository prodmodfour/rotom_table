# Toxic

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target is Badly Poisoned. If the user is Poison-Type, Toxic cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Excitement. Premier poison-inflicting move. Auto-hit for Poison-type users.
