# Tera Blast

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Tera Blast's type changes to match the user's Tera Type when Terastallized. When Terastallized, if the user's Attack is higher than its Special Attack, Tera Blast becomes a Physical move instead.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Gen 9 move. Not in PTU 1.05. Damage Base and frequency adapted to fit PTU balance conventions (EOT, DB 8). Versatile move that adapts to the user's Tera Type and offensive stats.
