# Ice Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Ice Punch Freezes the target on 19+ during Accuracy Check.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Reliable physical Ice move with Freeze chance. Part of the elemental Punch trio. Contest Type: Beauty.
