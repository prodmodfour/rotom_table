# Icy Wind

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Cone 2
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
All Legal Targets have their Speed lowered 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Ice Creation 2
- Wind Manipulation 2


## Notes
AoE Speed debuff with damage.
