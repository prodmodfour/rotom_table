# Earthquake

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 3, Groundsource
	- Energy Cost: 6

## Effect
Earthquake can hit targets that are underground, including those using the Move Dig.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25 or TM

## Notes
Powerful AoE Ground attack with a large burst radius. Grants Groundshaper capability. Groundsource keyword. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Desperation.
