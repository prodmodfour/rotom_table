# Gastro Acid

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target's Ability is disabled until the end of the encounter. If the target has more than one Ability, you choose one of them to disable.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Ability suppression move. Contest Type: Beauty. Contest Effect: Sabotage.
