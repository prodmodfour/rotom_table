# Dragon Dance

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
The user's Attack is raised by 1 Combat Stage and the user's Speed is raised by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Focus 6
- Individual Attack 15
- Individual Speed 15
- Dragon Type
- Performance 3

## Notes
Premier offensive setup move combining Attack and Speed boosts.
