# Cross Chop

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Cross Chop scores a Critical Hit on a roll of 16+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Hands] OR Natural Weapon [Arms]
- Combat 6
- Individual Attack 25


## Notes
High-crit Fighting attack with an expanded critical range.
