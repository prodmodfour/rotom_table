# Roost

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user regains Hit Points equal to half of its full Hit Points. If the user is a Flying Type, it loses the Flying Type until the start of their next turn.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Temporarily removes Flying type. Contest Type: Cool.
