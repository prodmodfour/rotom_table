# Superpower

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Superpower lowers the user's Attack and Defense by 1 Combat Stage each.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Powerful but self-debuffing. Dash keyword. Contest Type: Tough. Desperation.
