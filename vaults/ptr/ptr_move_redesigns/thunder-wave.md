# Thunder Wave

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Thunder Wave cannot miss. Thunder Wave Paralyzes the target. Targets immune to Electric Attacks are immune to Thunder Wave's effects.

## Unlock Conditions
- Electricity Generation 1
- Electricity Manipulation 3

## Notes
Auto-hit paralysis, one of the most reliable status moves in the game.
