# Volt Switch

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 5, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If Volt Switch successfully hits its target, the user deals damage and then immediately is returned to its Poke Ball in the same turn. A new Pokemon may immediately be sent out. Using Volt Switch lets a Trapped user be recalled.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal. Electric-type pivot move, counterpart to U-Turn.
