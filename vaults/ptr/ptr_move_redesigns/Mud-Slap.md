# Mud-Slap

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 2
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 3, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
The target's Accuracy is lowered by -1.

## Unlock Conditions
None
