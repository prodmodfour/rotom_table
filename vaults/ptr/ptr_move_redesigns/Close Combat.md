# Close Combat

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
After dealing damage, the user's Defense and Special Defense are each lowered by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25

## Notes
High-power melee attack with defensive drawback. Dash keyword allows closing distance. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Seen Nothing Yet.
