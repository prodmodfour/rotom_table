# Hail

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field, Weather
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The weather changes to Hail for 5 rounds. While it is Hailing, all non-Ice Type Pokemon lose a Tick of Hit Points at the beginning of their turn.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Weather move. Replaces any existing weather. Contest Type: Beauty.
