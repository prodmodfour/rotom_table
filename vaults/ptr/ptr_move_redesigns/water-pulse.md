# Water Pulse

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Water Pulse Confuses the target on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Water Creation 2
- Water Manipulation 3
