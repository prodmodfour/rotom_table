# Acupressure

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target or Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Roll 1d6. On a result of 1, raise the target's Attack 2 Combat Stages. On a result of 2, raise the target's Defense 2 Combat Stages. On a result of 3, raise the target's Special Attack 2 Combat Stages. On a result of 4, raise the target's Special Defense 2 Combat Stages. On a result of 5, raise the target's Speed 2 Combat Stages. On a result of 6, raise the target's Accuracy by +2.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Random but powerful +2 boost. Can target an ally. Contest Type: Cool.
