# Final Gambit

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 6

## Effect
Final Gambit lowers the user to 0 Hit Points and causes them to Faint. Final Gambit then deals 1 point of damage to the target for every Hit Point lost by the user. Final Gambit does not cause items to activate.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20

## Notes
Self-sacrificing move, damage scales with user's current HP. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Big Show.
