# Focus Blast

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 7
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Smite, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Focus Blast lowers the target's Special Defense 1 Combat Stage on 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Focus 7
- Individual Special Attack 35
- Combat 5


## Notes
Powerful but inaccurate. Smite and Aura keywords.
