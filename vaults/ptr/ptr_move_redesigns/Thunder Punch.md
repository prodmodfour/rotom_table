# Thunder Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Thunder Punch Paralyzes the target on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act. Part of the elemental Punch trio with Ice Punch and Fire Punch.
