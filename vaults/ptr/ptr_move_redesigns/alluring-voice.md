# Alluring Voice

## Properties
	- Type: Fairy
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the target has had any of its stats raised during this turn, the target becomes Confused.

## Unlock Conditions
— Sound [Singer]
- Performance 6

## Notes
Gen 9 move. Punishes stat-boosting strategies. Sonic keyword means it is sound-based. Not in PTU 1.05.
