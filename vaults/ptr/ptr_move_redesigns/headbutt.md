# Headbutt

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Headbutt Flinches the target on 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Head]
- Individual Attack 10
- Athletics 3

## Notes
Basic Normal-type headbutt with Flinch chance. Dash keyword.
