# Wild Charge

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Recoil 1/3
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
No additional effect. The user takes recoil damage equal to 1/3 of the damage dealt.

## Unlock Conditions
- Electricity Generation 3
- Athletics 4
- Individual Attack 20
- Individual Speed 20

## Notes
Keywords: Dash, Recoil 1/3. High-power physical Electric attack with recoil cost.
