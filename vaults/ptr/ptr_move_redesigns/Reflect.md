# Reflect

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Blessing
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Blessing — Any user affected by Reflect may activate it when receiving Physical Damage to resist the Damage one step. Reflect may be activated 2 times, and then disappears.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Blessing keyword. Reduces Physical damage. Contest Type: Smart.
