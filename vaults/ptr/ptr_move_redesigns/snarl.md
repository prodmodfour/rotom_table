# Snarl

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Cone 2, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
All legal targets have their Special Attack lowered by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Vocal 
- Intimidate 3

## Notes
AoE debuff. Sonic keyword.
