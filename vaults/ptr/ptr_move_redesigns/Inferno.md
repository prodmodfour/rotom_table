# Inferno

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 9
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Inferno Burns the target. Inferno ignores the target's Evasion if there are no other combatants or Rough or Blocking Terrain within 2 meters of the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Guaranteed Burn on hit but very low accuracy. Gains accuracy bonus against isolated targets. Contest Type: Beauty.
