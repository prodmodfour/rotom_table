# Dragon Breath

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Dragon Breath Paralyzes the target on a roll of 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Mid-range Dragon special attack with Paralysis chance. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act.
