# Grass Knot

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 5, 1 Target, Weight Class
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Grass Knot's Damage Base is equal to twice the target's Weight Class.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Damage scales with target's weight. Weight Class keyword. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Incentives.
