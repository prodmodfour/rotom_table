# Gust

## Properties
	- Type: Flying
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
If the target is airborne as a result of Fly or Sky Drop, Gust can hit them, ignoring Range and has a Damage Base of 8 instead.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Basic Flying special attack with anti-air bonus. Grants Guster. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Steady Performance.
