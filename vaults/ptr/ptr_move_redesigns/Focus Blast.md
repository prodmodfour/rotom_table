# Focus Blast

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 7
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Smite, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Focus Blast lowers the target's Special Defense 1 Combat Stage on 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or TM

## Notes
Powerful but inaccurate. Smite and Aura keywords. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act.
