# Dark Pulse

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Dark Pulse Flinches the target on a roll of 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Ranged special Dark attack with Flinch chance. Aura keyword. Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act.
