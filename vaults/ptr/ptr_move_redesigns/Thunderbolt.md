# Thunderbolt

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Thunderbolt Paralyzes the target on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Cool. Contest Effect: Exhausting Act. Staple Electric-type special attack with reliable accuracy.
