# Rain Dance

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field, Weather
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The weather becomes Rainy for 5 rounds. While Rainy, Water-Type Attacks gain a +5 bonus to Damage Rolls, and Fire-Type Attacks suffer a -5 Damage penalty.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Weather move. Contest Type: Tough.
