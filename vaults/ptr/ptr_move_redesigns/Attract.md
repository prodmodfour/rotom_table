# Attract

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 3, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Attract Infatuates the target if its gender is the opposite of the user's. Attract fails when used by or against Genderless targets.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Gender-dependent control move. Social keyword. Contest Type: Cute.
