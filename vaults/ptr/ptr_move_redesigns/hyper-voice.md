# Hyper Voice

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Close Blast 3, Sonic, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
All Legal Targets are pushed back to the squares immediately outside the blast, away from the user.

## Unlock Conditions
- Vocal OR Singer OR Resonant Body
- Individual Special Attack 25
- Focus 5

## Notes
AoE with pushback. Sonic and Smite keywords.
