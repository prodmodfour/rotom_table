# Uproar

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1, Spirit Surge, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
All Pokemon and Trainers within 5 meters of the user are cured of Sleep.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Unsettling. Keywords: Spirit Surge, Sonic. AoE damage with Sleep-curing utility.
