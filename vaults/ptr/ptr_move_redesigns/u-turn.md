# U-Turn

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If U-Turn successfully hits its target, the user deals damage and then immediately is returned to its Poke Ball in the same turn. A new Pokemon may immediately be sent out. Using U-Turn lets a Trapped user be recalled.

## Unlock Conditions
- Acrobatics 3
- Bug Type
- Individual Speed 15
- Individual Attack 15

## Notes
Keywords: Dash. Premier pivot move — deal damage and switch out safely.
