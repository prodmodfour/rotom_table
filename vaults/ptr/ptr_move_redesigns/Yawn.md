# Yawn

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 2, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target falls Asleep at the end of their next turn. Yawn cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Excitement. Social keyword. Delayed but guaranteed Sleep — forces switches or sacrificed turns.
