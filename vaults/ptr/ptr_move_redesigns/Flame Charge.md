# Flame Charge

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Raise the user's Speed 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or TM

## Notes
Speed-boosting attack with Dash keyword. Contest Type: Tough. Contest Effect: Excitement.
