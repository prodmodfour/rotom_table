# Guard Swap

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user and the target trade Combat Stage values for the Defense Stat, and then for the Special Defense Stat.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Combat Stage swap for defensive stats. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Inversed Appeal.
