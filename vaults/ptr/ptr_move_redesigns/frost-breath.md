# Frost Breath

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If Frost Breath hits, it is a Critical Hit.

## Unlock Conditions
- Ice Creation 2 
- Ice Manipulation 4
- Individual Special Attack 15
- Focus 3

## Notes
Guaranteed critical hit. Grants Freezer.
