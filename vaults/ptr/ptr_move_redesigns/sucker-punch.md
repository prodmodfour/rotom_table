# Sucker Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Interrupt, Trigger
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
If an adjacent foe targets the user with a Damaging Attack, Sucker Punch may be used as an Interrupt Move against the triggering foe.

## Unlock Conditions
- Deception 4
- Individual Attack 20
- Combat 3
- Insight 4

## Notes
Interrupt/Trigger move. Reactive attack. Saving Grace.
