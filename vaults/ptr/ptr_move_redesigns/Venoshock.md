# Venoshock

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the target is Poisoned, Venoshock has a Damage Base of 13 instead.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Incentives. Synergizes with poison-inflicting moves for massive damage.
