# Terrain Pulse

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Terrain Pulse's type changes based on the active terrain. It becomes Electric-type during Electric Terrain, Grass-type during Grassy Terrain, Fairy-type during Misty Terrain, and Psychic-type during Psychic Terrain. When terrain is active, Terrain Pulse's Damage Base is doubled to 10.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Gen 8 move. Not in PTU 1.05. Adapted to PTU conventions (At-Will base, DB 5 / DB 10 with terrain). Terrain-dependent move similar to Weather Ball.
