# Horn Leech

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
After the target takes damage, the user gains Hit Points equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Draining physical Grass move with Dash. Contest Type: Smart.
