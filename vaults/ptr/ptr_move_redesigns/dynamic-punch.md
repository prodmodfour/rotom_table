# Dynamic Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 9
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Dynamic Punch Confuses the target. Dynamic Punch ignores the target's Evasion if they are Flanked.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Hands]
- Individual Attack 30
- Combat 6
- Athletics 4
- Power 4

## Notes
Very powerful but extremely inaccurate (AC 9). Guaranteed Confusion on hit. Ignores Evasion against Flanked targets.
