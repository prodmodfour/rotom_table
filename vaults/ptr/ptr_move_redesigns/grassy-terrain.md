# Grassy Terrain

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The area becomes Grassy for 5 rounds. While Grassy, all Pokemon and Trainers standing on the ground recover a Tick of Hit Points at the start of every turn, and Grass-Type attacks performed by grounded Pokemon and Trainers gain a +10 bonus to Damage Rolls.

## Unlock Conditions
- Plant Manipulation 5
- Focus 5

Terrain-setting move.
