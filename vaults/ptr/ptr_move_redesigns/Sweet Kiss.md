# Sweet Kiss

## Properties
	- Type: Fairy
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 6
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target becomes Confused. On miss, the target suffers a -2 penalty to Accuracy Rolls for one full round.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Social keyword. Even a miss applies a debuff. Contest Type: Cute. Excitement.
