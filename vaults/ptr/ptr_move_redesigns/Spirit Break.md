# Spirit Break

## Properties
	- Type: Fairy
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Spirit Break lowers the target's Special Attack by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Gen 8 move. Guaranteed SpAtk debuff on hit. Physical Fairy move.
