# Synthesis

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user regains Hit Points equal to half of its full Hit Point value. If it is Sunny, the user gains 2/3 of its full Hit Point value instead. If it is Rainy, Sand Storming, or Hailing, the user gains 1/4 of its full Hit Point value instead.

## Unlock Conditions
- Photovore
- Focus 3

## Notes
Weather-dependent healing move, counterpart to Moonlight and Morning Sun.
