# Stored Power

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 2
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 10, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
For every Combat Stage the user has above 0, add +2 to Stored Power's Damage Base, up to a maximum of Damage Base 20.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 10

## Notes
Scales with positive Combat Stages. Contest Type: Tough. Incentives.
