# Flatter

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Social
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect
The target's Special Attack is raised by +1 Combat Stage. Flatter Confuses the target.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 5

## Notes
Trades a stat boost for Confusion. Contest Type: Smart. Contest Effect: Excitement.
