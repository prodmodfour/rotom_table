# Magnet Rise

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Self, Swift Action
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user gains the Levitate Ability for 5 turns. Magnet Rise may be activated as a Swift Action if the user is otherwise given an action that consumes a Command.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Grants Magnetic capability. Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Sabotage.
