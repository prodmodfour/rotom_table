# Hydro Pump

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 11
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Push
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The target is pushed away from the user 3 meters.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 20 or TM

## Notes
High power with knockback. Push keyword. Contest Type: Beauty.
