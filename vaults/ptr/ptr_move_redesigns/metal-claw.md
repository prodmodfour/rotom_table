# Metal Claw

## Properties
	- Type: Steel
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Spirit Surge
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Raise the user's Attack 1 Combat Stage on 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Claws] or Natural Weapon [Pincer]
- Steel Type

## Notes
Spirit Surge keyword.
