# Stomping Tantrum

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
If the user's last move on the previous turn failed or had no effect, Stomping Tantrum's Damage Base is doubled to 16.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
Gen 7 move. Rewards failed moves with doubled power on follow-up.
