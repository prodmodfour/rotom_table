# Wish

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 15, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
At the end of the user's next turn, the target regains Hit Points equal to half of its full Hit Point value. If the user targets themselves and are replaced in battle, the replacement is healed.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or Move Tutor

## Notes
Contest Type: Cute. Contest Effect: Reflective Appeal. Delayed healing that can be passed to a switch-in.
