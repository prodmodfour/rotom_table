# Work Up

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Raise the user's Attack by 1 Combat Stage and raise the user's Special Attack by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Focus 3
- Normal Type OR Fighting Type
## Notes
Mixed offensive setup move that boosts both Attack and Special Attack.
