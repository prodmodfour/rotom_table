# Ingrain

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self, Coat
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Ingrain applies a Coat to the user, which has the following effect: the user cannot be pushed or pulled, and cannot be switched out. At the beginning of each of the user's turns, the user gains a Tick of Hit Points.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Coat effect. Trades mobility for sustain healing. Contest Type: Smart.
