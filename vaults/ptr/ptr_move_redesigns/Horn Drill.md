# Horn Drill

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Execute
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Roll 1d100. This roll may not be modified in any way. If you roll X or lower, the target Faints. X is equal to 30 + The User's Level - The Target's Level.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 25

## Notes
One-hit KO move. Execute keyword. Accuracy scales with level difference. Contest Type: Cool.
