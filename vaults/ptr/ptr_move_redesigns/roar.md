# Roar

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1, Sonic, Social
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
When declaring Roar, the user does nothing and may not Shift. At the end of the round, the user Shifts and uses Roar. Targets hit by Roar immediately Shift away from the user using their highest usable movement capability, and towards their Trainer if possible. If the target is an owned Pokemon and ends this shift within 6 meters of their Poke Ball, they are immediately recalled to their Poke Ball. If that Trainer sends out a replacement, they do not lose their Pokemon turn.

## Unlock Conditions
- Vocal
- Intimidate 4

## Notes
Sonic keyword. Social keyword. Forced switch-out mechanic.
