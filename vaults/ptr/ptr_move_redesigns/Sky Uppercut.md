# Sky Uppercut

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Sky Uppercut may be used as an Interrupt when against a target initiating Bounce, Fly, or Sky Drop to allow the user to Shift and use Sky Uppercut. If Sky Uppercut successfully hits its target, the triggering Move fails (though the target may take their next turn normally).

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15

## Notes
Anti-air interrupt move. Contest Type: Cool. Exhausting Act.
