# Surf

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Line 6
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
As a Shift Action, the user may Move to any open square in Surf's area of effect without provoking any Attacks of Opportunity.

## Unlock Conditions
Level 15 or TM

## Notes
AoE with built-in repositioning. Contest Type: Beauty. Exhausting Act.
