Hail is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Field, Weather.

The weather changes to Hail for 5 rounds. While it is Hailing, all non-Ice Type Pokemon lose a Tick of Hit Points at the beginning of their turn.

As a Daily x2 move, Hail can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
