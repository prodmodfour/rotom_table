Resonance Beam is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Line 4.

All targets have their Special Defense lowered by 1 Combat Stage on 20+. This Effect Range is extended by +1 for each foe targeted by this Move.
