[[trait-definition|Traits]] and moves are unlocked throughout play. Each Learned Trait or move has its own set of conditions for unlocking. [[moves-are-universally-available|Moves are universally available]] — unlock conditions are the only gate, with no species-specific move lists.

Roleplay conditions are preferred, but simpler conditions are sometimes used (e.g., unlocked at level 4). [[roleplay-unlock-conditions-are-session-scoped|Roleplay conditions should describe something that happens during the training session]], not past events. [[training-unlocks-traits-and-moves|Training]] is also an unlock condition type — using a [[training-dual-check-system|dual-check system]] where both Pokemon and Trainer must pass. Conditions typically compose with [[move-unlock-and-or-logic|AND/OR logic]].

Sometimes a condition may be unattainable for a specific instance of a Pokemon -- for example, requiring a specific gender, or requiring something the Pokemon has already failed to do.

Evolution uses a parallel system of [[evolution-trigger-conditions|species-specific trigger conditions]].
