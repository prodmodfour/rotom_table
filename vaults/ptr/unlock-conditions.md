[[trait-definition|Traits]] and moves are unlocked throughout play. Each Learned Trait or move has its own set of conditions for unlocking.

Roleplay conditions are preferred, but simpler conditions are sometimes used (e.g., unlocked at level 4). [[tms-as-unlock-conditions|TMs]], [[move-tutors-as-unlock-conditions|Move Tutors]], and [[training-unlocks-traits-and-moves|training successes]] are also unlock condition types. Conditions typically compose with [[move-unlock-and-or-logic|AND/OR logic]].

Sometimes a condition may be unattainable for a specific instance of a Pokemon -- for example, requiring a specific gender, or requiring something the Pokemon has already failed to do.

Evolution uses a parallel system of [[evolution-trigger-conditions|species-specific trigger conditions]].
