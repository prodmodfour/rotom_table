In PTR, [[trait-definition|Traits]] replace PTU's Abilities, Capabilities, and Natures. This is part of a broader simplification that also [[ptr-removes-features-edges-classes|removes Features, Edges, and Classes]].

Where PTU used multiple overlapping systems for character customization, PTR consolidates them into a single Trait mechanic with two categories: [[innate-traits]] and [[learned-traits]]. PTR also [[base-stat-relations-removed|removes Base Stat Relations]].
