"Shiny Pokemon" is a catch-all term for Pokemon with unusual characteristics. The most common kind simply have an alternate color palette. Rarer "shinies" result from environmental adaptation or extreme genetic mutation — these may have different [[trait-definition|Traits]], Movesets, or even Types from standard specimens.

This concept encompasses both cosmetic variants and mechanical ones. The mechanically distinct variants connect to [[type-shifts-defy-expectations]] and [[niche-competition-drives-adaptation]] — type shifts and ability changes are the mechanisms by which variants emerge. [[giant-pokemon-variants]] are another form of variant that falls under this umbrella.
