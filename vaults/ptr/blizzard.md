Blizzard is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 11, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 7, and a range of 4, Ranged Blast 2, Smite.

Blizzard Freezes all legal target on 15+. If the target is in Hailing Weather, Blizzard cannot miss. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
