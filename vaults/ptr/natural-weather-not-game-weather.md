Despite their names, Weather Conditions are not usually found as natural occurrences. A bright sunny day does not count as Sunny Weather, nor does ordinary rain count as Rainy Weather. Only particularly severe examples — a tropical rainstorm, a blinding blizzard — qualify as their corresponding [[weather-as-game-keyword]].

This distinction separates the fictional environment from the mechanical game state. The GM can describe rain without activating Rain Weather's damage modifiers. Weather as a game keyword is activated by moves and [[trait-definition|traits]], not by narrative description alone.
