[[Tympole]] vibrate their cheeks to produce rhythmic ultrasonic patterns that other Tympole can hear but humans and most other Pokemon cannot. These signals warn of danger and coordinate group movement — Tympole swim and attack in swarms, relying on this private communication channel to stay synchronized. Their chorus also creates visible ripples on the water's surface, making a Tympole colony's presence detectable even if the sounds themselves are inaudible. This swarm behavior and acoustic coordination explains why Tympole carries Resonant Body and why the line has consistently high Perception scores.
