Using a Jump capability consumes distance from the main capability used to Shift (e.g., Overland). A combatant can also use Jump as a whole Shift Action by itself.

This means jump is not a free bonus on top of movement — it trades horizontal ground for vertical clearance within the same [[movement-is-atomic-per-shift|atomic shift action]]. The app must subtract jump distance from the remaining shift budget when jumps occur mid-movement.
