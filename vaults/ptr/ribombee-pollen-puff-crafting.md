[[Ribombee]]'s specialized fur allows it to roll collected pollen into compact puffs that serve as both food and medicine. The effects of each puff depend on the type and ratio of pollen ingredients used — different blends produce nutritional supplements, healing remedies, or combat projectiles. In Alola, these pollen puffs are sold as valuable nutritional supplements. Ribombee also crafts puffs specifically to cheer up distressed Pokemon it senses through its inherited [[cutiefly-aura-sensing|aura-reading ability]], demonstrating an empathetic application of its crafting skill. This is the biological basis for the [[pollen-puff]] move's dual offensive/healing function.
