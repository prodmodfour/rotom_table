Training for a [[learned-traits|Learned Trait]] requires both the Pokemon and the Trainer to pass their respective checks in the same [[training-session-one-hour|training session]].

Each session gives one attempt. If both pass, the Trait is gained immediately — there is no accumulation of successes.

The [[training-pokemon-check|Pokemon check]] is specified by the Learned Trait itself. The [[training-trainer-social-skill-choice|Trainer check]] uses a social skill of the Trainer's choice, modified by the [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|Pokemon's social skill hierarchy]].
