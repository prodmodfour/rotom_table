Shell Trap is a Fire-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 15, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Interrupt, Trigger.

If the user is hit by a Melee attack, they may use Shell Trap as an Interrupt.
