Electrify is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 6, 1 Target..

Until the end of the user's next turn, the target's damaging Water-Type attacks and Melee attacks of any Type deal Electric-Type Damage instead of their usual Type.
