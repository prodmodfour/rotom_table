Environmental hazards modify encounter difficulty independently of enemy levels and composition. Dark caves impose visibility penalties (see [[raw-darkness-penalties-with-presets]]). Arctic battles involve ice that breaks under heavy weight classes, slow terrain, and acrobatics checks. Factories offer interactive machinery as hazards.

Simple environment rules can turn a mundane encounter into a genuine trial. The [[multi-tag-terrain-system]] supports layering these effects onto the grid. [[collateral-damage-constrains-tactics]] is another environment tool — forcing restraint rather than imposing penalties.
