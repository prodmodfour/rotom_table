Rarity is inversely correlated with food chain position. Producers and herbivores (Sewaddle eating leaves) are the most numerous. Mid-chain predators (Pidgey eating Sewaddle) are less common. Apex predators (Seviper) are rarest.

This ecological pattern naturally aligns with [[fun-game-progression]] because powerful Pokemon tend to be predators. It is a consequence of [[sensible-ecosystems]] — believable worlds follow energy pyramid distribution.
