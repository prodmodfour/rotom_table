The qualitative meaning of [[skill-modifiers-from-traits-or-circumstance|skill modifier]] values:

| Modifier | Label |
|----------|-------|
| 0 | Untrained |
| 1 | Okay |
| 2 | Decent |
| 3 | Good |
| 4 | Professional |
| 5 | Talented |
| 6 | Expert |
| 7 | Gifted |
| 8 | Genius |
| 9 | Best in a nation |
| 10 | Perfect |

Combined with [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier|1d20 + Modifier]] and the [[skill-check-dc-table|DC table]], this gives a sense of how capable a character is at a given skill.
