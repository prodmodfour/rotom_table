Skill ranks (Pathetic, Untrained, Novice, Adept, Expert, Master) no longer exist in PTR. The [[skill-rank-dice-progression|rank-to-dice progression]] is replaced by [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier|1d20 + Modifier]].

[[skill-modifiers-from-traits-or-circumstance|Modifiers]] come from Traits or Circumstance Bonuses instead of rank tiers.
