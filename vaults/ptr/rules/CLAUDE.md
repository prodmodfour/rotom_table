# Rules

~376 atomic notes on PTR game mechanics: combat, stats, conditions, movement, terrain, energy, capture, training, breeding, skills, and design principles.

- Trait definitions live in `../ptr_traits/`, but trait *design rationale* notes (e.g. `effect-spore-design.md`, `technician-design.md`) are here.
- Move keyword mechanics (e.g. `spirit-surge.md`) are here, not in `../moves/`.
