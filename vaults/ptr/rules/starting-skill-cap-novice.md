PTR removes skill ranks and edges — see [[skill-ranks-removed]] and [[ptr-removes-features-edges-classes]].

In PTU, at Level 1, skills could not be raised above Novice rank. The only Adept-rank skill available at creation came from the trainer's background — see [[background-shapes-starting-skills]]. Edges spent on skill ranks during creation were limited to Untrained→Novice (or Pathetic→Untrained, though [[pathetic-permanent-at-creation]] blocked this for background Pathetic skills).

The Adept Skill Edge becomes available at Level 2 via [[skill-rank-level-gates]].
