The Suppressed volatile status downgrades move frequencies: At-Will becomes EOT, and EOT/Scene×2 become Scene. PP Up benefits are also disabled while Suppressed.

This is a powerful debuff that limits offensive output by restricting how often moves can be used. The app's move frequency tracking must apply this downgrade while the Suppressed condition is active and restore original frequencies on cure.
