Evolution conditions must be achievable by wild Pokemon living in their natural habitat, not just by trained Pokemon. A condition that requires a trainer (e.g., "be wielded in battle") excludes every wild specimen of that species from ever evolving, which breaks the ecology. Wild Pokemon of every evolutionary stage exist, so every evolution must be reachable without human intervention.

Conditions should describe something the species naturally does or experiences — feeding, fighting, traveling, surviving. Training can also satisfy these conditions, but it should never be the only path.
