The Poison type has two identity traits: [[poison-coated-natural-weapon|Poison Coated]] and [[poison-expulsion|Poison Expulsion]].

**Poison Coated** is the more universal of the two -- every Poison type has it on at least one natural weapon. It represents the fundamental Poison identity: your body is toxic, and contact with you is dangerous. The specific weapons that are coated vary by species (fangs, claws, stingers, pincers, hands, body).

**Poison Expulsion** grants Poison-type Struggle Attacks and represents the ability to expel toxic fluid at range. Not every Poison type has this -- some are purely contact-venomous. A Skorupi stings; it doesn't spit acid. Poison Expulsion appears on Poison types whose biology includes ranged toxic projection (e.g. [[Alolan Grimer]] oozing toxin, [[Shroomish]] releasing spores).

Unlike the Manipulation family, Poison has no ambient environmental control -- there is no "poison the ground in a radius" effect. Poison's ambient expression is through body contact and weapon coating rather than area manipulation.
