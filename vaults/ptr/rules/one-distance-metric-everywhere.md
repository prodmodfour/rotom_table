All grid distance measurements in the app use the same metric. Movement, range, burst containment, line length, and area effects all use [[ptu-alternating-diagonal-everywhere]]. No exceptions.

Asymmetry between metrics (where attacks reach further diagonally than cardinally, or bursts use a different rule than movement) creates confusion and exploitable inconsistencies. A single metric eliminates these problems.
