Body-slot armor (per Sept 2015 errata):

- **Light Armor:** 5 Damage Reduction against Physical Damage. $8,000.
- **Special Armor:** 5 Damage Reduction against Special Damage. $8,000.
- **Heavy Armor:** 5 Damage Reduction against all Damage. Speed default CS becomes −1. $12,000.

Off-Hand shields:

- **Shield:** +1 Evasion. Can be readied (Standard Action) for +4 Evasion and 10 DR until end of next turn, but become Slowed. Can function as Small Melee Weapon if used two-handed. ~$3,000.
