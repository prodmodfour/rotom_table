Capture is a two-step process:

1. Throw a Poke Ball as an AC 6 status attack with [[throwing-range-scales-with-athletics]]. This uses the full accuracy system per [[full-accuracy-for-pokeball-throws]].
2. On hit, roll 1d100 and subtract the trainer's level ([[trainer-level-aids-capture]]) and any [[poke-ball-type-capture-modifiers]]. If the result is equal to or under the pokemon's capture rate, it is captured.

[[natural-roll-extremes-in-capture]] can override both steps. [[capture-costs-standard-action]].
