After 1 minute (6 rounds) without air, a Pokemon or Trainer starts suffocating. They take 1 Injury per round while suffocating. These injuries cannot be healed by anything except breathing — once the target can breathe again, the suffocation injuries are removed.

Pokemon with [[water-breathing|Water Breathing]] do not suffocate from being underwater.
