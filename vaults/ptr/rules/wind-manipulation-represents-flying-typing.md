[[wind-manipulation|Wind Manipulation]] is a default capability of Flying-type Pokemon, representing their innate connection to air currents. All Flying types are assumed to have some degree of wind control unless there is an explicit reason to omit it (e.g. a Flying type whose flight is purely mechanical or wing-based with no aerokinetic component).

This parallels [[light-manipulation-represents-fairy-typing]], where the typing itself justifies the trait without requiring explicit lore about the element. No ecology note about "this bird controls wind" is needed -- the Flying typing implies it.

Potency typically scales with the evolutionary stage: a base-stage bird might have Wind Manipulation [1], while a fully evolved apex flier could have Wind Manipulation [3].
