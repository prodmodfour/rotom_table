Water terrain defaults to movement cost 1 (basic terrain). Swimmers already pay via their lower Swim speed — double-dipping with cost 2 is an unintended double penalty.

For rough currents, rapids, or whirlpools, the GM uses the terrain painter to overlay slow terrain on water cells. Default water is calm.
