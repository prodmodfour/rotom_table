Weaker, simpler Pokemon appear on earlier routes; stronger, more advanced species appear later. This is the system's first principle for populating a game world with wild encounters.

The principle constrains encounter design: [[pseudo-legendary-placement]] reserves rare species for difficult-to-reach locations. It works in concert with [[sensible-ecosystems]] — progression should feel natural, not arbitrary. The [[energy-pyramid-rarity]] pattern naturally supports this since powerful predators are rarer.
