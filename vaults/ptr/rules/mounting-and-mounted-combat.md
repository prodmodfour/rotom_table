Mounting a Pokemon is a Standard Action requiring a DC 10 Acrobatics or Athletics check. Trainers with Expert+ in either skill can mount as a Free Action during their Shift, provided they move at least 2 meters first.

While mounted, the Trainer uses the mount's movement [[trait-definition|traits]] during their Trainer turn. During the Pokemon turn, the mount can use any remaining unused movement plus a Standard Action. Both rider and mount can easily [[intercept-as-bodyguard-positioning|intercept]] attacks for each other due to no distance requirement.

If either rider or mount is hit for 25%+ of their max HP or by a Push effect, the rider must pass a DC 10 Acrobatics/Athletics check to remain mounted. The Mounted Prowess Edge grants auto-success on mounting and +3 to stay-mounted checks.
