Effects should be classified by their intent and purpose, not just their surface mechanics. Two effects that look similar mechanically may behave differently because they serve different purposes.

Life Orb recoil and Belly Drum's HP cost are both "lose HP" effects, but recoil is an incidental cost of attacking (the Pokemon didn't choose to take damage) while Belly Drum is a deliberate sacrifice (the cost IS the point). This drives [[hp-loss-recoil-vs-self-cost]]: recoil absorbs temp HP, self-cost bypasses it.
