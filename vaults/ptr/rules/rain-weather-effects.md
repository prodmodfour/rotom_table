While Rainy, Water-Type attacks gain +5 damage and Fire-Type attacks suffer -5 damage. Thunder and Hurricane cannot miss in Rain. Ability interactions: Hydration cures one status per turn, Rain Dish recovers a Tick per turn, Swift Swim grants +4 Speed CS, Desert Weather grants 1/16th max HP per turn, Dry Skin gains a Tick per turn.

Rain is one of four [[weather-as-game-keyword]] types that fundamentally shifts type matchup dynamics by amplifying Water and suppressing Fire.
