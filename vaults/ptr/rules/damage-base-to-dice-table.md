Damage Base (DB) translates to a dice roll for actual damage. The attacker's relevant stat is added after rolling.

| DB | Rolled Damage | DB | Rolled Damage |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1d6+1 | 15 | 4d10+20 |
| 2 | 1d6+3 | 16 | 5d10+20 |
| 3 | 1d6+5 | 17 | 5d12+25 |
| 4 | 1d8+6 | 18 | 6d12+25 |
| 5 | 1d8+8 | 19 | 6d12+30 |
| 6 | 2d6+8 | 20 | 6d12+35 |
| 7 | 2d6+10 | 21 | 6d12+40 |
| 8 | 2d8+10 | 22 | 6d12+45 |
| 9 | 2d10+10 | 23 | 6d12+50 |
| 10 | 3d8+10 | 24 | 6d12+55 |
| 11 | 3d10+10 | 25 | 6d12+60 |
| 12 | 3d12+10 | 26 | 7d12+65 |
| 13 | 4d10+10 | 27 | 8d12+70 |
| 14 | 4d10+15 | 28 | 8d12+80 |

On a [[crit-doubles-dice-not-stats|Critical Hit]], the dice roll is added a second time but the stat bonus is not. Set damage (average roll) can be used instead of rolling to speed up play.
