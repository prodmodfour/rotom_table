Pokemon accumulate Experience from encounters. When total XP reaches the next level's threshold, the Pokemon levels up per [[level-up-ordered-steps]]. The chart tops out at [[pokemon-level-range-1-to-20|level 20]].

The full table of thresholds is in [[ptr-xp-table]].
