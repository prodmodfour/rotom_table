The Intercept maneuvers let a combatant sacrifice their own safety to protect an ally by physically moving into the attack's path. Both are Full Action Interrupts triggered by attacks on allies within movement range.

Intercept Melee: push the ally 1 meter away, occupy their space, take the hit. Requires an Acrobatics/Athletics check with DC = 3x the distance in meters. On failure, still shifts a third of the check result.

Intercept Ranged: pick a square between attacker and target within movement range, shift toward it (half check result in meters). If you reach it, you take the attack.

Pokemon need Loyalty 4+ to intercept for their Trainer or any ally. This ties the bodyguard mechanic to the bond between Pokemon and Trainer, rewarding relationship investment with tactical options.
