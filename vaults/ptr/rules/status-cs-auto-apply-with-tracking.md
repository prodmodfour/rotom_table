When [[burned-renamed-to-burning|Burning]], Paralysis, or Poison is applied, the app automatically applies their inherent combat stage effects (Burn: -2 Def CS, Paralysis: -4 Speed CS, Poison: -2 SpDef CS), tagged by source. On cure, only the source-tagged stages are reversed. After Take a Breather (which resets all stages), condition-sourced stages are re-applied.

This is [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]] applied to a core PTU mechanic. Manual CS adjustments remain independent of source-tagged ones. The [[condition-source-tracking]] system makes this possible.
