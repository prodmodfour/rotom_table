The encounter budget formula must have a verifiable PTU source. The previous formula `avgPokemonLevel * 2 * playerCount` cited "Core p.473" — a page that does not exist in PTU 1.05. This false citation was removed per [[no-false-citations]].

The verified PTU Chapter 11 formula: baseline XP per player = average Pokemon level × 2. Total level budget = baseline XP × number of trainers. This budget is distributed across enemy Pokemon — see [[level-distribution-shapes-difficulty]] and [[action-economy-constrains-encounter-size]] for how distribution affects difficulty. Note: PTU's "average Trainer level × 4" shortcut no longer applies since [[only-pokemon-have-levels|trainers have no levels in PTR]].

The budget feeds the [[encounter-xp-formula]] in reverse: design the encounter to yield the desired XP reward. This is part of [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven]] — a GM-driven process, not a random table roll.
