Spirit Surge is a move keyword. A move with Spirit Surge will have its Effect activate even if the move does not hit a target. If the move requires a certain Accuracy Roll threshold (e.g. "on 19+"), the roll must still meet that threshold.

This means Spirit Surge moves trigger their secondary effects — stat boosts, terrain creation, hazard removal, etc. — regardless of whether a valid target is in range, as long as any accuracy-gated condition is met.
