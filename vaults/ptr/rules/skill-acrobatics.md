Acrobatics covers agility, balance, jumping, and physical tasks requiring precision and finesse. Gymnasts, parkour practitioners, and dancers excel here.

Common checks: keeping balance on narrow ledges, jumping across gaps, dodging falling debris, accurate throwing, juggling, mounting a Pokemon mid-combat. Acrobatics mitigates [[falling-damage-by-weight-and-distance|falling damage]] and is tested during Intercept maneuvers.

Opposed checks: obstacle course races, recovering footing on unstable ground, reaction speed alongside Perception.
