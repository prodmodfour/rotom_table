[[scary|Scary]] and [[very-scary|Very Scary]] describe how a Pokemon looks and feels to encounter — not what it does ecologically. A Pokemon can be an [[apex-predator|apex predator]] without being Scary if its appearance is docile or non-threatening.

[[Alolan Muk]] hunts Trubbish and Garbodor as an apex garbage-ecosystem predator, but its demeanor with humans is quiet and friendly. It is not visually intimidating, so it does not get Scary despite its predatory role.

The trait answers: "Would a person recoil upon seeing this Pokemon?" — not "Is this Pokemon dangerous?"
