Anything that previously required AP in PTU becomes a [[trait-definition|Trait]] in PTR. These converted Traits are rebalanced in consideration of no longer having a cost — removing the AP gate changes their power budget, so they cannot be ported over as-is.

This is part of [[ap-removed-in-ptr|AP's removal]]. Where AP spending acted as a cost-gate on certain mechanics (e.g. [[ap-spend-for-roll-bonus|roll bonuses]], [[trainer-ap-drain-heals-injury|injury healing]]), those mechanics now exist as Traits with adjusted balance.
