PTR has six [[movement-traits]]: [[landwalker|Landwalker]], [[flier|Flier]], [[swimmer|Swimmer]], [[phaser|Phaser]], Burrower, and Teleporter. Each is a [[trait-definition|trait]] that grants X meters of movement in that mode per turn.
