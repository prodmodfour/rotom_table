The massive damage injury threshold (50%+ of max HP) is evaluated against real HP lost after temp HP absorption, not total incoming damage. If temp HP absorbs most of the hit, the Pokemon didn't suffer a massive blow to its actual health.

This rewards [[temp-hp-as-meaningful-shield]] strategies. The threshold uses [[real-max-hp-for-all-percentages]] — the real maximum, not injury-reduced effective max. Massive damage is one of two injury sources alongside [[hp-marker-injury-thresholds]]. A single attack can trigger both — see [[stacking-injuries-from-single-attack]].

Moves that "set" or "lose" HP are exempt — see [[set-lose-hp-immune-to-massive-damage]].
