A swarm acts multiple times per turn, spending Swarm Points equal to its [[swarm-multiplier-scale]]. The first Standard Action each turn is free. Additional actions cost Swarm Points: At-Will = 1, EOT = 2, Scene = 3, Daily = 4. After each action, the swarm subtracts 5 from its Initiative and acts again at the new value.

Accuracy rolls against swarms gain a bonus equal to the swarm multiplier. Single-target damage is resisted one step further. AoE attacks are treated as one step more super-effective. Status effects cost the swarm 1 Swarm Point instead of fully disabling it.

This is a specialized extension of [[action-economy-per-turn]] for mass encounters.
