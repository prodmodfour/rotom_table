"Rest" means any period without rigorous physical or mental activity. What qualifies as rest is GM-discretionary — sleep and sitting count, meals usually count, extended travel almost never counts.

This matters because [[natural-healing-rate]] requires continuous half-hour rest periods. The app should not assume or enforce what counts as rest; it accepts the GM's declaration that rest is happening.
