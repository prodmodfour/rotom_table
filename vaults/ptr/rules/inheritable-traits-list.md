Each species has a curated inheritable traits list — [[innate-traits]] that can only be obtained through breeding. This is analogous to how PTU had species-specific Egg Move Lists for moves. When two Pokemon breed, the child may receive traits from its parents that appear on the child species' inheritable traits list.

Inheritable traits are distinct from the species' normal innate traits. A Pokemon's full set of potential innate traits comes from both sources: [[hatched-pokemon-trait-pool]].
