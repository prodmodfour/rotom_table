Command covers leadership, authority, presence, and directing others. Characters with high Command can become the center of attention with a few well-chosen words.

Common checks: giving orders to Pokemon (especially [[loyalty-varies-by-origin|low-loyalty]] ones), directing panicking crowds, keeping subordinates in line, making a show of authority, delivering rallying speeches, helping allies recover from mental status conditions in combat.

Command also affects [[training-dual-check-system|training]] effectiveness — the Trainer's ability to communicate what they want from their Pokemon.

Opposed checks: Command vs Intimidate to rally allies against fear. Command vs Focus or Intimidate to snap allies out of supernatural mental influence (legendary Pokemon, Ghost/Psychic-types).
