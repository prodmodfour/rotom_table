Cone attack shapes use fixed 3m-wide rows per literal text. The first cell is at distance 1 (directly in front), then all subsequent rows are 3 cells wide. No expansion with distance.

For diagonal cones, the 3-wide row wraps around the diagonal axis, including the diagonal corner cell to produce a 7-cell diamond pattern. Both move targeting and measurement preview must produce identical cell sets.
