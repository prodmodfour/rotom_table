Environment presets use RAW values for darkness penalties: Blindness at -6 accuracy ("Dim Cave") and Total Blindness at -10 accuracy ("Dark Cave"). Total Blindness also removes map awareness and prevents Priority/Interrupt moves per RAW.

The app should not ship with a simplified -2 flat penalty that under-penalizes darkness and could mislead GMs. When RAW defines multiple severity tiers, provide separate presets for each tier rather than inventing a single simplified value.

Per-combatant Darkvision/Blindsense tracking is the correct long-term approach for automatically negating darkness penalties.
