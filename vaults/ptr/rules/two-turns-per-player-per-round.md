Each combatant in an encounter — trainer or Pokemon — has its own turn in the initiative order based on its Speed stat. A trainer and each of their active Pokemon are separate combatants with separate turns.

The system does not limit how many Pokemon a trainer can have active or command per round. That constraint is handled by the GM based on the encounter context (e.g. league rules, narrative logic), not by the combat system itself.

Even if a trainer is KO'd, their Pokemon still take their turns, and vice versa.
