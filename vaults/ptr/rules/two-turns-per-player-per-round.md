Each player gets two turns per combat round: one for their Trainer and one for a Pokemon. Even if the Trainer is knocked out, the player still takes their Pokemon turn, and vice versa.

This is the fundamental combat structure in PTU. The app's turn tracker must manage paired turns per player, not a single turn. Initiative order determines when each individual turn occurs within the round.
