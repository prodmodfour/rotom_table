The Psychic type's identity traits are two complementary traits: [[mind-manipulation|Mind Manipulation]] for mental influence and [[telekinetic|Telekinetic]] for physical object manipulation via psychic force.

Mind Manipulation is the primary type marker -- it modifies Struggles to be Psychic-type and grants ambient psychic awareness (telepathy, emotion reading, mind detection). Telekinetic is secondary, representing the physical side of psychic power -- moving objects without touching them. Not every Psychic type needs both; a psychic that operates purely through mental influence might have Mind Manipulation without Telekinetic.

Non-Psychic types can also have these traits when their species has psychic capabilities. [[Mismagius]] (Ghost) and [[Noctowl]] (Normal/Flying) both have Mind Manipulation because their species have psychic abilities regardless of typing. The trait follows capability, not just type -- but Psychic typing guarantees it.
