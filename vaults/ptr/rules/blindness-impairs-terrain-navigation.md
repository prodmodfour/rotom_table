Blinded combatants must make a DC 10 Acrobatics check when moving over Rough or Slow Terrain or become [[tripped-costs-shift-to-stand|Tripped]]. This makes [[slow-terrain-doubles-movement|Slow]] and Rough terrain significantly more dangerous when vision is impaired.

Totally Blinded combatants lose all map awareness and must declare shifts as distance relative to their position. They choose to move Slowly (movement halved, like [[slowed-halves-movement]]) or Quickly (full speed but Tripped if they hit Blocking, Rough, or Slow Terrain). The app should hide the grid from Totally Blinded combatants on the player view, consistent with [[information-asymmetry-by-role]].
