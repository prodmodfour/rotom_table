Injuries are gained when a combatant's HP crosses specific marker thresholds: 50% of max HP, 0%, -50%, -100%, and every -50% thereafter. Each threshold crossed awards 1 Injury.

These thresholds use [[real-max-hp-for-all-percentages]] — the real maximum, not injury-reduced effective max. Combined with [[massive-damage-after-temp-hp]] (50%+ damage in one hit), these are the two sources of combat injuries.
