Weapons are Main Hand [[equipment-slots|equipment]] that modify [[struggle-is-not-a-move|Struggle Attacks]] and may grant Moves based on [[skill-combat|Combat]] modifier.

## Weapon Categories

- **Large Melee:** +1 AC, +2 DB to Struggle. Two-handed.
- **Small Melee:** +1 DB to Struggle. One-handed.
- **Foot Weapons:** Small Melee stats but +1 AC. Uses Feet slot instead.
- **Short Range:** Range 4 meters. One-handed.
- **Long Range:** +1 AC, +1 DB, range 12 meters (minimum 4 meters). Two-handed.

## Quality Tiers

- **Crude:** Only modifies Struggle Attacks. No Moves granted. ~$500 or less.
- **Simple:** Grants one Move at Combat +4 or higher. ~$3000.
- **Fine:** Grants one Move at Combat +4 and another at Combat +8. ~$6000.

One-handed weapons wielded in the Off-Hand slot lose the Master Move and take −2 to Accuracy. Weapon modifications (AC, DB, range) also apply to Moves granted by the weapon. Weapon Moves never benefit from STAB.
