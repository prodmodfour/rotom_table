Charm Check DCs by disposition: Very Friendly has no DC (automatic), Friendly is DC 15, Neutral is DC 12, Fearful is DC 8, Hostile is DC 15, and Very Hostile is DC 30.

Fearful Pokemon are easier to charm than Friendly ones — fear makes them more receptive to kindness than confidence does.
