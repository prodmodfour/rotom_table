Moves that can be used multiple times per Scene (Scene 2, Scene 3, etc.) can still only be used Every Other Turn within that Scene. They cannot be used on consecutive turns. This prevents a Pokemon from dumping all uses of a powerful move in quick succession.

This is a constraint layered on top of [[scene-frequency-definition]] — the total uses are scene-scoped, but the pacing is turn-scoped.
