When the rules state a maneuver's action cost, follow the literal text. Do not infer implicit additional costs based on the maneuver's thematic purpose.

Only Full Actions (explicitly listed on p.227: Take a Breather, Coup de Grâce, Intercept) consume both Standard and Movement Actions.

Note: Sprint was a Standard Action in the predecessor system but is [[sprint-removed|removed in PTR]], replaced by the [[energy-for-extra-movement]] system.
