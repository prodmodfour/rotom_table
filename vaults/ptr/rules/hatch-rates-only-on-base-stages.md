Only base-stage Pokemon should have a value in the `Average Hatch Rate` field. Evolved forms hatch as their base stage and evolve later, so their Hatch Rate field should be left blank (present but empty).

This prevents confusion about whether an evolved Pokemon can appear directly from an egg. [[Lopunny]] doesn't hatch -- [[Buneary]] does.
