Any trainer may spend 1 Action Point as a free action before making an Accuracy Roll or Skill Check to add +1 to the result. This can only be done once per roll. Trainers can also spend AP to boost their Pokémon's rolls.

This is a universal option available whenever AP exists (per [[ap-pool-scales-with-level]]) and creates a meaningful resource-management decision in every check.
