Roleplay [[unlock-conditions]] for [[learned-traits|Learned Traits]] should describe something that happens during the [[training-session-one-hour|training session]] itself — not things that happened in the Pokemon's past. Past events are hard to track and verify, making them unreliable as gates. A session-scoped roleplay condition gives the GM something concrete to observe and adjudicate in the moment.
