Larger-than-normal variants of typically small Pokemon add unpredictability to encounters. Giant Trapinch, [[Magikarp]], or Ditto are good candidates. Giant Pokemon receive boosted stats and changes to their Move and Ability lists to account for their size — Body Slam, Earthquake, and Bounce become natural fits.

This is a variant tool alongside [[type-shifts-defy-expectations]] and [[shiny-pokemon-as-variants]], used within [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven]] to break routine.
