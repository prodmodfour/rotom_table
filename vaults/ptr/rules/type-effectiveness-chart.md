The standard Pokemon type effectiveness chart applies. PTR uses 17 types — Flying has been removed. Key interactions by attacking type:

**Normal:** No super-effective. Resisted by Rock, Steel. Immune by Ghost.
**Fire:** Super-effective vs Grass, Ice, Bug, Steel. Resisted by Fire, Water, Rock, Dragon.
**Water:** Super-effective vs Fire, Ground, Rock. Resisted by Water, Grass, Dragon.
**Electric:** Super-effective vs Water. Resisted by Electric, Grass, Dragon. Immune by Ground.
**Grass:** Super-effective vs Water, Ground, Rock. Resisted by Fire, Grass, Poison, Bug, Dragon, Steel.
**Ice:** Super-effective vs Grass, Ground, Dragon. Resisted by Fire, Water, Ice, Steel.
**Fighting:** Super-effective vs Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, Steel. Resisted by Poison, Psychic, Bug, Fairy. Immune by Ghost.
**Poison:** Super-effective vs Grass, Fairy. Resisted by Poison, Ground, Rock, Ghost. Immune by Steel.
**Ground:** Super-effective vs Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, Steel. Resisted by Grass, Bug.
**Psychic:** Super-effective vs Fighting, Poison. Resisted by Psychic, Steel. Immune by Dark.
**Bug:** Super-effective vs Grass, Psychic, Dark. Resisted by Fire, Fighting, Poison, Ghost, Steel, Fairy.
**Rock:** Super-effective vs Fire, Ice, Bug. Resisted by Fighting, Ground, Steel.
**Ghost:** Super-effective vs Psychic, Ghost. Resisted by Dark. Immune by Normal.
**Dragon:** Super-effective vs Dragon. Resisted by Steel. Immune by Fairy.
**Dark:** Super-effective vs Psychic, Ghost. Resisted by Fighting, Dark, Fairy.
**Steel:** Super-effective vs Ice, Rock, Fairy. Resisted by Fire, Water, Electric, Steel.
**Fairy:** Super-effective vs Fighting, Dragon, Dark. Resisted by Fire, Poison, Steel.

## Dual-type interactions

- Both types neutral → neutral (x1)
- Both types weak → doubly super-effective (x2)
- Both types resistant → doubly resisted (x0.25)
- One weak, one resistant → neutral (x1)
- Either type immune → immune (x0)
- Three types (rare) → triply super-effective (x3) or triply resisted (x0.125)
