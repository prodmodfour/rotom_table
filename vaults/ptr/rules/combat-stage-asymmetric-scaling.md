Combat stages range from -6 to +6. Buffs have a wider effect range than debuffs — going from 0 to +6 doubles the stat (x2.0), while going from 0 to -6 only reduces to x0.4. The intervals are not uniform.

The full multiplier table: -6=x0.4, -5=x0.5, -4=x0.57, -3=x0.67, -2=x0.8, -1=x0.9, 0=x1.0, +1=x1.1, +2=x1.2, +3=x1.33, +4=x1.5, +5=x1.67, +6=x2.0.

This applies to Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed only — never HP. The app multiplies the [[individual-stats-vs-base-stats|individual stat]] by the stage multiplier and rounds down per [[always-round-down]].
