Movement Traits are [[trait-definition|traits]] that grant X movement in a specific mode per turn. Without a movement trait for a mode, that mode is unavailable.

The movement traits are:
- [[landwalker|Landwalker]] — ground movement (replaces PTU Overland)
- [[flier|Flier]] — aerial movement (replaces PTU Sky)
- [[swimmer|Swimmer]] — water movement (replaces PTU Swim)
- Phase
- Burrow
- Teleporter

Additional movement-adjacent traits:
- [[jump|Jump]] — vertical and horizontal jumping distance
- [[naturewalk|Naturewalk]] — ignore difficult terrain in a specific terrain type
