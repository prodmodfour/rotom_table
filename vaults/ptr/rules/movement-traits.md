Movement Traits are [[trait-definition|traits]] that grant X movement in a specific mode per turn. Without a movement trait for a mode, that mode is unavailable.

The six movement modes are:
- Land
- Sky
- Water
- Phase
- Burrow
- Teleporter

These replace PTU's movement capabilities (Overland, Swim, Sky, Burrow, Teleporter).
