Stats exist in three tiers:

- **[[pokemon-base-stats-from-species|Base stat]]** — the species-level stat from the Pokedex. Fixed for all members of a species. Cannot be modified by [[movement-traits|traits]], but [[vitamins-raise-base-stats|vitamins]] can raise them by 1.
- **[[individual-stats-vs-base-stats|Individual stat]]** — the stat a specific character actually has. Built from the base stat plus [[five-stat-points-per-level|stat point allocation]]. This is the persistent, out-of-combat value on the character sheet.
- **[[effective-stat-formula|Effective stat]]** — the stat as it exists in combat, after applying [[combat-stage-asymmetric-scaling|combat stages]], multipliers, and modifiers. Temporary — only meaningful during an encounter.

The progression is: base stat → individual stat → effective stat.
