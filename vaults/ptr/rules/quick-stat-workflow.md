For fast encounter setup: pick 2–3 species with clear stat profiles (one attacking stat and HP as highest base stats), distribute stat points across each Pokemon's top 3–4 stats, and add moves and [[trait-definition|traits]]. Since [[base-stat-relations-removed|Base Stat Relations are removed]], stat allocation is unconstrained — put points wherever makes sense for the encounter role. Emergency fallback: distribute stats evenly across all 6 stats (weaker but functional).

Each Pokemon gets [[five-stat-points-per-level|5 stat points per level]] on top of base stats. For a level 10 wild Pokemon, that's 50 points to distribute freely.

This workflow supports [[encounter-creation-is-gm-driven]] by reducing prep time. It is a candidate for [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]] — the app could generate quick-statted wild Pokemon automatically. A parallel workflow exists for trainers via [[quick-npc-building]].
