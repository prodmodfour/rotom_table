The base capture rate starts at 100 and subtracts the target's level × 2. With [[pokemon-level-range-1-to-20|PTR's level range of 1–20]], this gives base rates from 98 (level 1) to 60 (level 20). All other modifiers — [[hp-tiered-capture-modifiers]], [[evolution-stage-capture-modifiers]], [[status-capture-bonus-hierarchy]], [[rarity-capture-penalty]] — adjust from this base.
