Initiative immediately recalculates when Speed combat stages change mid-encounter (Paralysis, Agility, stat stage moves). Combatants who have already acted this round retain their "acted" flag — reordering cannot grant double turns.

This keeps the turn order responsive to in-combat events, supporting [[tactical-mechanics-must-matter]]. Static initiative that ignores speed changes would make speed-altering moves feel disconnected from their mechanical impact.
