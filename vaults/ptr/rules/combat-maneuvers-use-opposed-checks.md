Combat maneuvers (Push, Trip, Grapple, Disarm, Dirty Trick) resolve through opposed skill checks rather than damage rolls. Each maneuver specifies which skills can be used by attacker and defender (Combat, Athletics, Acrobatics, Stealth).

The app needs an opposed check resolution system: both parties roll their skill check, and the higher result wins. This is distinct from the standard accuracy-roll-vs-evasion attack system.

Manipulate maneuvers (Bon Mot, Flirt, Terrorize) are trainer-exclusive and use social skills (Deception, Charm, Intimidate) in opposed checks.
