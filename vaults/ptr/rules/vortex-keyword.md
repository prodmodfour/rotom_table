Vortex is a move keyword. While in a Vortex, the target is [[slowed-halves-movement|Slowed]], [[trapped-is-only-recall-blocker|Trapped]], and loses 1 [[tick-value-one-tenth-max-hp|tick]] of HP at the end of each turn (adapted from PTU's beginning-of-turn timing per [[persistent-tick-timing-end-of-turn]]).

At the end of each turn, the target may roll 1d20 to dispel the Vortex. The DC starts at 20 and drops by 6 each turn, automatically wearing off on the fifth turn:

| Turn | Escape DC |
|---|---|
| 1 | 20 |
| 2 | 14 |
| 3 | 8 |
| 4 | 2 |
| 5 | Auto-dispel |

Dispelling the Vortex removes the Slowed and Trapped conditions it applied. The Vortex is also destroyed if the caster switches out or faints.

The tick damage is flat HP loss (not typed damage) — it does not use the source move's type, damage base, or damage class. No type effectiveness applies.
