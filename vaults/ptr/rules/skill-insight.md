Insight covers reading people, gut instinct, empathy, and detecting deception. Replaces PTU's "Intuition" skill. Characters with high Insight make the right call on a hunch without necessarily understanding why.

Common checks: discerning someone's emotional state, detecting lies or half-truths, reading body language and unspoken social cues, connecting scattered clues into a coherent picture, sensing when something is off about a situation.

Also covers artistic interpretation and practical intuition-driven crafts like cooking — tasks where feel and instinct matter more than formal knowledge.

Opposed checks: most commonly Insight vs [[skill-deception|Deception]] to see through lies and manipulation.
