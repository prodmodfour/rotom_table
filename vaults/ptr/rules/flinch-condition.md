Flinch is a Volatile status condition. Upon becoming Flinched:

- The target's Initiative is lowered by 5 for the rest of the scene (stacks with multiple Flinch applications)
- The target becomes [[vulnerable-condition|Vulnerable]] for 1 full round

Unlike the core book version, Flinch does **not** skip the target's turn — they still act, but at reduced priority and with no evasion. Switching out removes the Initiative penalty. The stacking Initiative reduction makes repeated Flinching progressively more punishing.
