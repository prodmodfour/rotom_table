Poisoned and Badly Poisoned can stack — a target can be afflicted with both simultaneously. Each ticks independently at end of turn (see [[persistent-tick-timing-end-of-turn]]) and each has its own save check ([[poisoned-save-dc-10|DC 10]] and [[badly-poisoned-save-dc-15|DC 15]] respectively).

This is part of the [[poison-identity-through-trigger-frequency|poison identity design]]: lower individual DCs are offset by the ability to layer multiple poison effects.
