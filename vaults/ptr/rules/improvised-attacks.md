Combatants can attempt attacks not defined by Moves or Struggle — throwing rocks, pushing trees onto enemies, kicking sand. The GM adjudicates these case by case.

**General guidelines:**
- Easily replicable improvised attacks should be weaker than their Move equivalents (higher AC, lower DB).
- Common implements with type associations (rocks, snowballs) are treated as Normal-type to prevent easy type coverage.
- Elaborate environmental attacks (triggering a landslide, collapsing a structure) may emulate a Move's effect. The GM may require skill checks and base the attack stat on the relevant skill modifier × 3.
- One-time environmental tricks should be rewarded generously — they're hard to set up and can't be repeated.
