In PTR, [[humans-and-pokemon-share-skill-list|humans and Pokemon share the same skill list]].

In PTU, the Pokedex assigned each species a roll value in six skills: Athletics, Acrobatics, Combat, Stealth, Perception, and Focus. These were fixed by species and updated on evolution per [[evolution-updates-skills-capabilities]].
