The Ghost type's identity traits are two traits: [[phaser|Phaser]] for phase movement and [[intangibility|Intangibility]] for becoming untargetable. Together these capture the core Ghost identity -- existing partially outside the physical world.

Unlike every other type's identity trait, neither Phaser nor Intangibility modifies Struggle type. Ghost types have no type-specific Struggle modifier. Their typing is expressed through movement and defensive capability rather than ambient elemental control. This means a pure Ghost type's Struggles remain Normal-type unless another trait (e.g. [[mind-manipulation|Mind Manipulation]] on [[Mismagius]]) overrides them.

Not every Ghost type has both traits. The [[Honedge]] line has neither Phaser nor Intangibility -- as spirit-inhabited objects, they are physically anchored rather than ethereal. Their Ghost typing is expressed through [[soulstealer|Soulstealer]] instead. This is a valid exception: the identity trait is omitted because the species' specific identity contradicts it.
