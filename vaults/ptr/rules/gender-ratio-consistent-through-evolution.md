A Pokemon's gender ratio does not change through evolution. If [[Eevee]] is 87.5% M / 12.5% F, then all eight of its evolutions must also be 87.5% M / 12.5% F.

Gender ratio is a species-level biological trait determined at birth (or hatching). Evolution restructures the body but does not alter the population's sex ratio. Any deviation from the base stage's ratio in an evolved form is almost certainly a data entry error.
