Persuasion covers reasoned argument, logical appeals, and convincing others through evidence and rhetoric. Borrowed from D&D's skill design. Where [[skill-charm|Charm]] wins people over through warmth and likability, Persuasion wins them over through sound reasoning.

Common checks: making a logical case to an authority figure, negotiating terms of an agreement, convincing someone to change course based on evidence, presenting arguments in a formal setting, talking someone down from a rash decision with clear reasoning.

Persuasion is one of the five social skills used in [[training-dual-check-system|training]] and [[pokemon-social-skill-hierarchy|Pokemon social interactions]]. It represents the trainer's ability to make a Pokemon understand *why* something is being asked of it.
