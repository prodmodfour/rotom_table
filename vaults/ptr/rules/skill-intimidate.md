Intimidate covers scare tactics, coercion, and projecting physical menace. Characters with high Intimidate are imposing even when silent.

Common checks: scaring off wild Pokemon, bullying a guard into compliance, warding off muggers, avoiding violence through implied threat. Despite its unsavory connotations, Intimidate is often used defensively to prevent conflict.

Opposed checks: rarely opposed by another Intimidate check. More commonly opposed by Command (to rally allies against fear) or Focus (to resist being cowed). Intimidate vs Focus tests whether disrupting someone's concentration through raw menace succeeds.
