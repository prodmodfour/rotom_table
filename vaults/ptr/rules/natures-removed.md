Natures do not exist in PTR. The PTU mechanic where each Pokemon had a Nature that raised one stat and lowered another has been removed entirely. Stat modification is now handled through [[trait-definition|Traits]].

This is part of the same consolidation where [[traits-replace-abilities-capabilities-natures|Traits replace Abilities, Capabilities, and Natures]].
