Accuracy modifiers (from [[accuracy-cs-is-direct-modifier]], AP bonuses, etc.) do not affect which die results trigger move effects or critical hits. A Flamethrower with +4 accuracy that rolls a natural 16 does not trigger the burn effect (which requires a natural 18+) even though the modified total is 20.

Effect triggers and crit ranges always reference the natural die result, not the modified total. The app must track both the natural roll and the modified total separately.
