Confusion uses a three-outcome save check at the start of the confused target's turn:

- **1-8:** Target hits itself with a typeless physical Struggle Attack (auto-hit, resisted 1 step). No other actions this turn.
- **9-15:** Target acts normally.
- **16+:** Target is cured of Confusion.

The self-hit is a [[struggle-is-not-a-move]] attack, meaning move-modifying effects don't apply to it. The app should automate the save roll and apply the self-damage when the confused target's turn begins.
