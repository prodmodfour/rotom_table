In PTR, tick damage from Burning, Poisoned, Badly Poisoned, and [[vortex-keyword|Vortex]] occurs at the end of the afflicted target's turn. This replaces the PTU timing where burn, poison, and Vortex ticked at the beginning of turn or when the target "takes or is prevented from taking a Standard Action."

The end-of-turn timing is simpler and more predictable — it fires exactly once per turn regardless of what actions the target took or was prevented from taking.

Note: Cursed retains its original trigger of "takes a Standard Action" (see [[condition-text-differences-are-mechanical]]).
