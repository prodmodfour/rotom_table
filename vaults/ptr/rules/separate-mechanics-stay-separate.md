Game mechanics that the rules describe as separate concepts remain separate in the app. The GM explicitly invokes each one. No implicit bundling of distinct actions, even when they commonly co-occur in typical play.

[[new-day-is-counter-reset-only]] because New Day and extended rest are separate concepts — a new day can occur without rest (forced march, captured). [[extended-rest-drained-ap-only]] because Drained AP and Bound AP are separate mechanics with different lifecycles. [[bound-ap-persists-until-unbound]] because binding effects are independent of daily resets.

This keeps the game mechanics modular and preserves edge case scenarios that bundling would eliminate.
