Combat covers physical fighting — unarmed martial arts, weapons training, brawling, and battle techniques.

Common checks: performing combat maneuvers (pushing, tripping, grappling, disarming), using weapons effectively, breaking obstacles with brute force, martial arts demonstrations.

Outside battle, Combat can resolve situations like two characters grabbing for the same item or shoving each other without intent to injure.
