For the first 8 hours of rest each day, each continuous half hour of [[rest-definition]] heals 1/16th of max HP, using [[real-max-hp-for-all-percentages]]. After 8 hours, further rest provides no HP recovery.

This is blocked entirely by [[heavily-injured-action-tax]] (5+ injuries). The rate can be doubled by [[bandages-double-natural-healing]].
