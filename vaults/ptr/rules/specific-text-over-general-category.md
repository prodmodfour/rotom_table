When specific wording in PTU conflicts with what a general category would imply, the specific text wins. This is the core principle behind [[decouple-behaviors-from-categories]].

Cursed's tick trigger text omits "or is prevented from taking a Standard Action" while Burn/Poison include it — that textual difference is mechanical, not editorial. See [[condition-text-differences-are-mechanical]].

Sleep is classified as volatile but doesn't behave like other volatiles — its specific text and video game behavior override the category's implied behavior package. See [[sleep-volatile-but-persists]].
