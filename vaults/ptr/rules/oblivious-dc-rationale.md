[[oblivious|Oblivious]] has a low Pokemon DC (10 Focus) because emotional detachment is natural — many Pokemon are already inclined to tune out provocations and romantic signals without any training. The high Trainer DC (20) reflects that deliberately cultivating obliviousness in a Pokemon is awkward and unintuitive. A trainer is essentially trying to teach a Pokemon to *not* react, which goes against most training instincts of building responsiveness and engagement.
