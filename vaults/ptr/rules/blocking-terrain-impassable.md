Blocking Terrain cannot be shifted through or targeted through. Walls, large obstructions, and similar barriers are Blocking Terrain. This is the hardest terrain constraint — unlike [[slow-terrain-doubles-movement]] or Rough Terrain, there is no increased cost; passage is simply impossible.

Blocking Terrain is a flag in the [[multi-tag-terrain-system]] and is central to map design, creating chokepoints and forcing tactical positioning.
