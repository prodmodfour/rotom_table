A "scene" encompasses one encounter or combat plus surrounding narrative. When a scene ends, all [[scene-frequency-definition|scene-frequency]] moves reset. This boundary also determines when [[ap-refresh-per-scene]] occurs and constrains [[daily-moves-once-per-scene]].

The definition of when a scene starts and ends is implicit in PTU — the GM determines scene boundaries based on narrative flow. This makes scene boundaries a GM judgment call, not a mechanical formula.
