Stealth covers hiding, sneaking, moving undetected, sleight of hand, and larceny. Field researchers studying reclusive Pokemon and law enforcement operatives both benefit from Stealth.

Common checks: moving unseen and unheard, pickpocketing, picking locks, escaping bindings, covert reconnaissance, ambushing foes, sneaking past enemies entirely.

Opposed checks: almost always Stealth vs Perception. Also Stealth vs a crafting skill (Technology Education, Occult Education) when escaping constructed restraints or defeating engineered locks.
