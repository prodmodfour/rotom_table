The [[shift-renamed-to-movement-action|Movement Action]] can be split between other actions within a turn. The atomic unit of movement is 1 square.

A combatant with Land 4 can move 2 squares, take another action, then move 2 more squares — splitting their [[movement-traits|movement trait]] budget freely between actions.

This supersedes the previous rule that movement was a single continuous path (see [[movement-is-atomic-per-shift]]).
