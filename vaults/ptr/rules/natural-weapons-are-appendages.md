[[natural-weapons|Natural Weapons]] describe physical body structures that function as weapons — fangs, claws, blades, stingers, horns. They represent the Pokemon's biological "hardware."

A Pokemon does not need a Natural Weapon trait to hunt or attack. Pokemon have [[moves-are-universally-available|moves]] for that. [[Barboach]] hunts by ambushing prey in mud, but it doesn't need Natural Weapon [Body] because its moves handle the actual attack. Natural Weapons are for when the appendage itself is a notable feature of the species' combat identity.
