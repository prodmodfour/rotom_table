Trainers can remove one Injury as an Extended Action by Draining 2 AP. This is subject to the [[daily-injury-healing-cap]].

This gives trainers an active way to manage injuries outside of Pokemon Centers, at the cost of [[extended-rest-drained-ap-only]] being needed to restore the AP later.
