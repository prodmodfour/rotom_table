Each Poke Ball type applies a flat modifier to the capture roll. Lower modifiers make capture easier (the roll must come in under the capture rate):

- Basic Ball: +0
- Great Ball: −10
- Ultra Ball: −15
- Master Ball: −100 (guaranteed capture)
- Friend Ball: −5
- Luxury Ball: −5
- Heal Ball: −5
- Cherish Ball: −5
- Park Ball: −15

Many additional ball types have [[conditional-poke-ball-bonuses]] that activate only in specific situations.
