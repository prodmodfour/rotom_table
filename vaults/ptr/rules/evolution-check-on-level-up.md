Level threshold is one type of [[evolution-trigger-conditions|evolution trigger condition]]. On level up, check the Pokemon's Pokedex entry to see if it evolves at this level. [[evolution-is-optional]] — the trainer may refuse. If evolution occurs, the [[level-up-ordered-steps]] continues with the evolved form.
