Stuck and Slow occupy their own condition category, separate from both Persistent and Volatile. PTU p.238 explicitly separates them: Take a Breather cures "all Volatile Status effects AND the Slow and Stuck conditions." The "AND" makes them mechanically distinct categories.

Their capture bonuses are independent: Stuck = +10, Slow = +5. These do not stack with the volatile condition +5 bonus because Stuck and Slow are not volatile conditions. The word "Additionally" in PTU p.214 introduces independent bonuses, not bonuses on top of a category they don't belong to.
