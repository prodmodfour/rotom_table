Stuck and Slow occupy their own condition category, separate from both Persistent and Volatile. In PTR, Take a Breather does not cure Slow or Stuck — only Volatile conditions.

Their capture bonuses are independent: Stuck = +10, Slow = +5. These do not stack with the volatile condition +5 bonus because Stuck and Slow are not volatile conditions. The word "Additionally" in the rules introduces independent bonuses, not bonuses on top of a category they don't belong to.
