A hatched Pokemon's innate trait pool is composed of two sources:

1. **Species innate traits** — the normal [[innate-traits]] for its species (all of them, or at least the potential to unlock them).
2. **Inherited traits** — a random selection of non-species-specific innate traits from its parents, drawn from the child species' [[inheritable-traits-list]].

Both categories follow the same unlock system — [[inherited-traits-require-unlock]]. Breeding grants the potential to unlock a trait, not immediate access.
