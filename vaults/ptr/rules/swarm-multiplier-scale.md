Swarms are scaled by size: less than a dozen Pokemon = multiplier 1, 15–25 = multiplier 2, 25–40 = multiplier 3, 40–60 = multiplier 4, 60+ = multiplier 5. The multiplier determines [[swarm-hp-bars]] and [[swarm-action-economy]].

Swarms are a formal answer to [[action-economy-constrains-encounter-size]] — they collapse many foes into a single entity to keep combat manageable.
