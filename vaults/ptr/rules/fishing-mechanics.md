Fishing requires a fishing rod and bait or a lure. Rod quality determines what can be caught:

- **Old Rod:** small, unevolved Pokemon at level 2 or under
- **Good Rod:** unevolved Pokemon at GM-determined levels
- **Super Rod:** Pokemon of any size and evolutionary stage, at GM's discretion

To fish, cast a line and roll 1d20 every 5 minutes. On a 15+, a wild Pokemon is on the line. After 3 failed rolls, bait is consumed (lures persist). Then make a DC 8 [[skill-athletics|Athletics]] check to reel it in. On success, the Pokemon can be caught with a hand net or Poke Ball — it may attack, so keep a Pokemon ready.

On a failed Athletics check, roll 1d20: on 10 or lower, the Pokemon escapes with the lure. Bait is always lost on a failed reel.
