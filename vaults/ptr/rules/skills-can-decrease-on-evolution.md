Skills can decrease when a Pokemon evolves. This is expected and intentional.

Evolution often involves significant physical changes — increased size, new body structures, shifted proportions — that can reduce a Pokemon's aptitude in certain areas even as it gains power overall. A small, agile predator may become a larger, more imposing one that is harder to conceal and less nimble.

Examples:
- [[Houndour]] → [[Houndoom]]: Stealth +3 → +2, Acrobatics +3 → +2 (larger body, horns, and imposing presence make it harder to hide and less agile)

When reviewing species, do not flag skill decreases as errors. Evaluate whether the decrease makes physical or behavioral sense for the evolutionary change.
