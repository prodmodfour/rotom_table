All HP percentage calculations use real max HP, not injury-reduced effective max HP. The rules explicitly state that effects based on Max HP use the real maximum.

This applies universally: capture rate thresholds (75%, 50%, 25%), [[massive-damage-after-temp-hp]] checks, and any future HP-percentage-based mechanics. A Pokemon with injuries at full effective health still counts as below 100% for capture purposes.
