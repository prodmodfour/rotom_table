Roar's forced recall does NOT override the Trapped condition. A Trapped Pokemon hit by Roar still shifts away (Trapped restricts recall, not movement) but is not recalled to its Poke Ball.

PTU is deliberate about which moves bypass Trapped — it explicitly states this for U-Turn, Baton Pass, Volt Switch, Parting Shot, and Round Trip. Roar is not among them. If PTU intended Roar to bypass Trapped, it would say so.

This follows [[silence-means-no-effect]]: absence of explicit bypass text = Trapped applies normally.
