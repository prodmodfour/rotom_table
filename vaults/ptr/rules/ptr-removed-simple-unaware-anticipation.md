The following PTU Abilities were removed in PTR and do not exist as Traits:

- **Simple** — doubled stat stages
- **Unaware** — ignored opponent stat stages
- **Anticipation** — detected super-effective moves

Their functions are either absorbed into other PTR mechanics or no longer relevant.

Pokemon whose lore references behaviors resembling these abilities should use other PTR traits instead. For example, [[Bidoof]]'s obliviousness is expressed through the [[oblivious]] trait, not Simple or Unaware.
