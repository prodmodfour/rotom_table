The Slowed condition is a stacking debuff that reduces a Pokemon's [[movement-traits|movement]] by 2 per stack (floor of 1). It can be removed by switching out or at the end of a Scene as an Extended Action. Switching out clears all stacks per [[recall-clears-then-source-reapplies]].

Each source that inflicts Slowed adds one stack. Multiple sources (e.g. Sticky Web + Mean Look) accumulate independently — a Pokemon hit by both has Slowed 2 (−4 movement).

Slowed is distinct from [[slow-terrain-doubles-movement]] — Slow Terrain doubles movement cost per cell, while Slowed reduces total movement budget. Both can apply simultaneously, creating severe movement restriction. The app should apply Slowed stacks as a modifier to the combatant's [[movement-traits]], separate from terrain cost calculations.
