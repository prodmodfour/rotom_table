Any period of [[rest-definition|rest]] fully cures all [[fatigue-levels|Fatigue]] levels. No minimum duration — if you can rest, fatigue clears. This is much easier than recovering fatigue in combat via [[take-a-breather-recovers-fatigue|Taking a Breather]], which costs a Full Action and only removes 1 level.
