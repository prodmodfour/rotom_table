Moves that "set" or "lose" Hit Points (Pain Split, Endeavor) never trigger massive damage injuries. PTU explicitly excludes them.

This follows [[separate-mechanics-stay-separate]]: "dealing damage" and "setting/losing HP" are distinct operations with different consequences. See [[hp-loss-recoil-vs-self-cost]] for the broader taxonomy of HP reduction types. The massive damage threshold is defined in [[massive-damage-after-temp-hp]].
