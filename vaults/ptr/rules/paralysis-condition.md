Paralysis is a Persistent status condition. The paralyzed target's Initiative is halved. Electric-type Pokemon are immune per [[type-grants-status-immunity]].

At the start of each turn, the paralyzed target makes a DC 11 save check. On success, they act normally. On failure:

- They may take a Standard Action OR a Movement Action, but not both
- They become [[vulnerable-condition|Vulnerable]] for 1 full round
- They cannot take [[attack-of-opportunity-trigger-list|Attacks of Opportunity]] for 1 full round

Cured by Paralyze Heal, Full Heal, or [[extended-rest-clears-persistent-status|Extended Rest]].
