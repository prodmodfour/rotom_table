Index of mechanical differences between PTR and its PTU 1.05 base.

## Traits replace multiple systems

- [[ptr-removes-features-edges-classes]] — Features, Edges, and Classes removed entirely
- [[traits-replace-abilities-capabilities-natures]] — Abilities, Capabilities, and Natures unified into Traits
- [[trait-definition]] — the Trait as PTR's unified progression mechanic
- [[innate-traits]] — species-determined traits (replacing Abilities/Capabilities)
- [[learned-traits]] — earned through play via unlock conditions
- [[unlock-conditions]] — traits and moves unlocked through play, not leveling
- [[unlock-level-up-split]] — unlocking traits/moves is separate from leveling/stats

## Skill system

- [[ptr-skill-list]] — 18 skills in a flat list, no categories
- [[skill-categories-removed]] — Body, Mind, Spirit groupings removed
- [[skill-ranks-removed]] — rank progression (Pathetic→Master) removed
- [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier]] — 1d20 + Modifier replaces Xd6 dice pools
- [[skill-check-dc-table]] — DC thresholds (5/10/15/20/25)
- [[skill-modifiers-from-traits-or-circumstance]] — modifiers from Traits or Circumstance Bonuses
- [[humans-and-pokemon-share-skill-list]] — same skill list for humans and Pokemon

## Levels and stats

- [[pokemon-level-range-1-to-20]] — compressed from PTU's 1–100
- [[only-pokemon-have-levels]] — trainers no longer level up
- [[trainers-are-human-species]] — trainers defined as "Human species," typeless, no levels
- [[five-stat-points-per-level]] — replaces PTU's +1 per level
- [[base-stat-relations-removed]] — stat allocation no longer constrained by base stat ordering
- [[total-xp-unchanged]] — same total XP despite compressed level range

## Death

- [[death-at-ten-injuries-or-negative-hp]] — no friendly/non-friendly distinction; death conditions apply universally

## AP removed

- [[ap-removed-in-ptr]] — Action Points removed entirely
- [[ap-gated-mechanics-become-traits]] — AP-costing mechanics converted to Traits, rebalanced for cost removal

## Energy and fatigue

- [[frequency-replaced-by-energy-costs]] — At-Will/EOT/Scene/Daily system removed
- [[energy-resource]] — new combat resource derived from Stamina
- [[stamina-stat]] — new stat that derives Energy
- [[move-energy-cost]] — each move has an Energy Cost
- [[energy-stamina-scaling]] — Energy = max(3, floor(2 × sqrt(Stamina)))
- [[energy-regain-rate]] — 1 Energy regenerated per turn start
- [[zero-energy-causes-fatigue]] — hitting 0 Energy causes 1 Fatigue level
- [[energy-overdraft]] — spending more than you have causes 3 Fatigue levels
- [[fatigue-levels]] — stacking debuff (−2 attack, evasion, movement per level)
- [[fatigue-causes-unconsciousness]] — unconscious at 5 Fatigue
- [[take-a-breather-recovers-fatigue]] — extended to also recover Fatigue
- [[rest-cures-fatigue]] — 1 hour of rest fully cures all Fatigue
- [[low-distribution-energy-discount]] — rare/signature moves may get favorable costs
- [[downside-moves-energy-discount]] — drawback moves may get low costs

## Movement

- [[shift-renamed-to-movement-action]] — Shift Action renamed to Movement Action
- [[movement-traits]] — traits grant X movement in a mode per turn (replaces MP + rates)
- [[movement-splitting]] — movement can be split between other actions (1 square = atomic unit)
- [[movement-path-freedom]] — each movement segment has free pathing between start and end
- [[movement-capability-types]] — PTU capabilities replaced by six movement traits
- [[typical-movement-profile]] — most Pokemon have Land 4 and Water 2
- [[energy-for-extra-movement]] — spend 5 Energy for extra movement in one mode, once per turn
- [[sprint-removed]] — Sprint action removed; extra movement via energy

## Moves

- [[moves-are-universally-available]] — no species-specific move lists; any character can unlock any move
- [[no-moves-known-limit]] — no cap on moves known (replaces PTU's 6-slot limit)
- [[trainer-move-list]] — trainers unlock moves from the same universal pool as Pokemon
- [[move-unlock-and-or-logic]] — move unlock conditions compose with AND/OR logic

## Health

- [[pokemon-hp-formula]] — (Level × 5) + (HP stat × 3) + 10
- [[trainer-hp-formula]] — (HP stat × 3) + 10 (no level component)

## Items

- [[items-unchanged-from-ptu]] — item system retained as-is from PTU 1.05
- [[item-proficiency-traits]] — traits can grant proficiency with specific items
