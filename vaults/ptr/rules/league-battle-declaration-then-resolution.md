League Battles use a two-phase trainer system In the declaration phase, trainers declare actions from lowest to highest speed (no execution). In the resolution phase, declared actions resolve from highest to lowest speed.

This allows the strategic depth the rules intend — faster trainers see what slower trainers declared before their own actions resolve, gaining an information advantage. League Battles are a specific, named mode with explicit rules and should not be approximated by the generic combat flow.
