Each individual Pokemon has a personal ranking of the five social skills: Command, Charm, Intimidate, Persuasion, and Deception. This ranking determines DC modifiers whenever those skills are used on that Pokemon — during [[training-dual-check-system|training]], suppressing compulsive behaviors like [[violent-instincts]] or [[kleptomaniac]], or any other interaction.

The top-ranked skill has +0 DC, with each subsequent rank adding +2:

| Rank | DC Modifier |
|------|-------------|
| 1st  | +0          |
| 2nd  | +2          |
| 3rd  | +4          |
| 4th  | +6          |
| 5th  | +8          |

This ordering is per individual, not per species. It is usually randomly determined at creation, but the GM may choose a specific ordering.

**Example:** A Pokemon with the hierarchy Command > Deception > Persuasion > Intimidate > Charm would add +0 to Command-based Trainer DCs, +2 to Deception, +4 to Persuasion, +6 to Intimidate, and +8 to Charm.
