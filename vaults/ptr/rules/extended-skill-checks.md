Extended skill checks model complex, multi-step tasks. The DC is set normally, then multiplied by a factor of 2–5 based on task complexity. The character rolls [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier]] at each time interval and accumulates the total. Attempts are limited to half their [[skill-modifiers-from-traits-or-circumstance|skill modifier]] (rounded down per [[always-round-down]]) — hitting this limit represents reaching the edge of one's expertise.

The GM decides the time interval between rolls (minutes, hours, days) and whether the task will eventually complete with enough time or whether hitting the attempt limit means failure.
