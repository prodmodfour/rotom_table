[[light-manipulation|Light Manipulation]] is a trait associated with the Fairy type, representing a Pokemon's innate connection to light, glamour, and supernatural radiance. In dual-typed lines where Fairy is secondary, Light Manipulation may appear only at later evolutionary stages as the Pokemon comes more into its fairy nature.

The [[Impidimp]] >> [[Morgrem]] >> [[Grimmsnarl]] line demonstrates this: Impidimp (Dark/Fairy) has no light traits, but Morgrem gains Light Manipulation 1 as its fairy aspect develops, and Grimmsnarl inherits it. No explicit lore about light is required — the fairy typing itself justifies the trait.
