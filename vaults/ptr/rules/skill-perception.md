Perception covers awareness of surroundings, attention to detail, and active investigation. Characters with high Perception have both sharp senses and a trained eye for what others miss.

Common checks: spotting traps, noticing a hidden Pokemon, finding evidence at a crime scene, searching rooms for hidden objects, detecting ambushes. Often a reactive skill — the GM calls for a check when there's something to notice.

Perception alone identifies details but may not explain them. Spotting Unown inscriptions requires Perception; reading them requires [[skill-occult-education|Occult Education]]. Finding a trap requires Perception; disarming it requires [[skill-stealth|Stealth]] or [[skill-technology-education|Technology Education]].

Opposed checks: most commonly Perception vs [[skill-stealth|Stealth]]. Also Perception vs [[skill-deception|Deception]] to see through disguises.
