Burning has a DC 18 save check at the end of the afflicted target's turn, rolled after taking the tick of damage. On success, Burning is cured.

The save happens after the tick so that even a successful save still costs one round of damage. All other Burning mechanics (1 [[tick-value-one-tenth-max-hp|tick]] of damage, -2 Def CS) are unchanged from PTU's Burned.
