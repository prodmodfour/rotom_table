Pokemon Center healing time is calculated from the pre-healing injury count (at arrival). For under 5 injuries, each adds 30 minutes to the base 1 hour. At 5 or more injuries, each adds 1 hour instead.

Pokemon Centers heal HP, cure all status conditions, and restore daily-frequency moves. They can also remove injuries, subject to the [[daily-injury-healing-cap]]. The healing process takes longer because it must address each injury — the time reflects the work being done, not the result. See [[arrival-state-for-time-calculations]].
