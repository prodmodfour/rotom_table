Occult Education covers supernatural lore and phenomena — legendary Pokemon mythology, ancient places of power, aura theory, psychic phenomena, and Ghost-type peculiarities.

Common checks: studying magical ruins, researching lore about legendary Pokemon, analyzing strange psychic or aura disturbances, understanding Ghost-type capabilities (invisibility, phasing), applying Cleanse Tags, operating Dowsing Rods.

Whether Occult Education sees heavy use depends on the campaign. GMs and players should discuss its relevance before character creation. Characters developed around this skill tend to have narrow specializations (e.g. ancient ruins vs legendary Pokemon vs psychic research).
