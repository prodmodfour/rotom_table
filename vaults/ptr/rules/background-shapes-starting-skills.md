PTR removes skill ranks — see [[skill-ranks-removed]]. The rank-based background system below no longer applies.

In PTU, during character creation, a trainer's background modified their skill ranks: one skill was raised to Adept, one to Novice, and three were lowered to Pathetic. The three Pathetic skills could not be raised during character creation — see [[pathetic-permanent-at-creation]].

This is the only mechanism that creates Adept-rank skills at Level 1, since the [[starting-skill-cap-novice]] otherwise limits edges to Novice raises only.
