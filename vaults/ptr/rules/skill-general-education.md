General Education covers broad academic knowledge — history, politics, current events, literature, philosophy, mathematics, and natural sciences. Characters with high General Education are masters of trivia and regional knowledge.

Common checks: recalling political stances of local leaders, knowing historical context for a location or event, conducting research in a library or database. Acts as a catch-all for setting-specific common knowledge not covered by other Education skills.

Also represents research ability — when the information sought isn't specialized enough for another Education skill, General Education governs the process of finding it.
