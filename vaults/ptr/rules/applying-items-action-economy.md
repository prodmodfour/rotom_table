Applying a Restorative or X Item to another target is a Standard Action. The target forfeits their next Standard and Shift Actions. The target may refuse, in which case the item is not used and no actions are forfeited.

Using a Restorative Item on yourself is a Full-Round Action but does not forfeit further actions.

The Medic Training Edge removes the target's action forfeit, making item support much more efficient in combat.
