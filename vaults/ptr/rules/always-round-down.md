When working with decimals, always round down to the nearest whole number, even if the decimal is .5 or higher. 3.9999 rounds down to 3.

This global math rule applies to all derived stats ([[evasion-from-defensive-stats]], [[traits-derived-from-skills]]), damage calculations, and healing formulas. Inherited from PTU 1.05 via [[raw-fidelity-as-default]].
