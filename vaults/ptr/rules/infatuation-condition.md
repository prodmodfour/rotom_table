Infatuation is a Volatile status condition. While Infatuated, the target has a "crush" (the source of the Infatuation):

- **Attacks not targeting the crush:** −5 penalty to all Damage Rolls
- **Attacks targeting the crush:** Attack and Special Attack are halved for determining damage

At the end of each turn, the target may make a DC 16 save check to cure the Infatuation.

Unlike the core book version, Infatuation does **not** block actions — the target always acts, but deals significantly reduced damage whether they attack their crush or anyone else.
