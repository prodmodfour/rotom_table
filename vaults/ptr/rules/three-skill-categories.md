PTR no longer uses skill categories — see [[skill-categories-removed]] and [[ptr-skill-list]].

In PTU, skills were organized into three categories: Body (Acrobatics, Athletics, Combat, Intimidate, Stealth, Survival), Mind (General Education, Medicine Education, Occult Education, Pokémon Education, Technology Education, Guile, Perception), and Spirit (Charm, Command, Focus, Intuition).

This categorization matters mechanically because [[categoric-inclination-edge]] grants +1 to all skills in a chosen category, and the [[structured-edge-objects]] data model must store the chosen category.
