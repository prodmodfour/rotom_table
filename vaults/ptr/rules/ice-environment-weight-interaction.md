A battle over a frozen lake creates a unique challenge: any Pokemon with Weight Class 5 or higher breaks the ice and falls into the water. Ground-source attacks and certain other moves also create holes in the ice. The ice can additionally be treated as [[slow-terrain-doubles-movement|slow terrain]].

This is a specific example of [[environment-modifies-encounter-difficulty]] where the environment interacts with Pokemon attributes (weight class) rather than just movement or accuracy. It forces team composition decisions before the fight even starts.
