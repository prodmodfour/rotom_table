In full contact encounters (wild pokemon, criminal fights, any non-League battle), all participants — trainers and pokemon alike — act in simple order from highest to lowest Speed. No declaration phase, no separation of trainer turns from pokemon turns.

This contrasts with [[league-battle-declaration-then-resolution]], which uses a two-phase system. The app must support both initiative modes, selectable per encounter based on combat type.
