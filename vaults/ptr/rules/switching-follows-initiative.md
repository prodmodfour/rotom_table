Fainted Pokemon switching happens on the trainer's next available turn in initiative order, not as an immediate reaction interrupting normal turn flow. No "interrupt" or "reaction" switching exists in PTU.

If a Pokemon faints on its own turn, the trainer can switch immediately (it's already their initiative slot). Otherwise, the fainted Pokemon remains on the field until the trainer's initiative count arrives. The recall is a Shift Action, the replacement send-out is a Free Action, and the replacement can act that turn if its initiative count hasn't passed.
