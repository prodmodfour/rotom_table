All tokens are passable on the VTT grid. Movement can pass through any occupied square (ally or enemy). Cannot end movement on an occupied square (no stacking).

Enemy-occupied squares count as rough terrain imposing a -2 accuracy penalty when targeting through them. This is the literal reading and provides the most tactical flexibility.
