Trainers have six equipment slots: Head, Main Hand, Off-Hand, Body, Feet, and Accessory. Only one piece of equipment may occupy each slot.

Equipping an item or switching one for another costs a Standard Action. Handing equipment to another trainer is a Standard Action but does not equip it for them. Equipping a Held Item onto a Pokemon is a Standard Action that causes the Pokemon to forfeit their next turn.

Most Held Items are Accessory-slot items when used by Trainers, but some specify a different slot.
