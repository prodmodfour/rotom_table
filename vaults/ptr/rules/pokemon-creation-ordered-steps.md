Creating a Pokemon follows an ordered workflow: start with [[pokemon-base-stats-from-species]] from the Pokedex, apply [[natures-raise-one-lower-one]], distribute [[stat-points-equal-level-plus-ten]], calculate [[pokemon-hp-formula]], assign initial [[innate-traits]] per [[trait-unlock-schedule]], and fill move slots per [[six-move-slot-limit]].

The GM assigns Nature and [[trait-definition|Traits]]; the Trainer distributes stat points. The app should automate this sequence per [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]].
