Creating a Pokemon follows an ordered workflow: start with [[pokemon-base-stats-from-species]] from the Pokedex, distribute stat points ([[five-stat-points-per-level|5 per level]]), calculate [[pokemon-hp-formula]], assign initial [[innate-traits]], and assign moves per [[no-moves-known-limit]].

The GM assigns [[trait-definition|Traits]]; the Trainer distributes stat points. [[base-stat-relations-removed|Base Stat Relations do not apply]]. The app should automate this sequence per [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]].
