Repels suppress wild Pokemon encounters based on level:

| Type | Duration | Level Threshold | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Repel | 1 hour | Level 3 and under | $200 |
| Super Repel | 2 hours | Level 6 and under | $300 |
| Max Repel | 5 hours | Level 8 and under | $400 |

Spraying a wild Pokemon directly is an AC 6 Status Attack. If it hits (and the Pokemon is within the level threshold), the Pokemon immediately shifts away as far as possible as an Interrupt, forfeiting its next Shift Action.
