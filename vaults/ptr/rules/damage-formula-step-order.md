PTR's damage calculation follows a strict nine-step order:

1. Find initial Damage Base from the move
2. Apply Five/Double-Strike modifications
3. Add DB modifiers (e.g. [[stab-adds-to-damage-base]]) for final DB
4. Modify for [[crit-doubles-dice-not-stats]] if applicable
5. Roll damage or use set damage
6. Add attacker's relevant stat and other bonuses
7. Subtract defender's relevant stat and damage reduction
8. Apply type effectiveness multipliers
9. Subtract from target's HP and check for injuries

Step order matters: STAB is pre-roll (step 3), stats are post-roll (step 6), type effectiveness is last (step 8). Per [[non-immune-attacks-deal-damage]], a minimum of 1 damage applies at step 7 and step 8.
