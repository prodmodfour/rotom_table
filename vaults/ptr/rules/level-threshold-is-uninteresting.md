Level threshold is the least interesting [[evolution-trigger-conditions|evolution trigger condition]]. It adds no narrative weight — the Pokemon simply exists long enough and evolves. Preferred conditions are behavioral, environmental, or item-based, because they create a moment the player and Pokemon earn together. Level threshold should be avoided unless no better condition fits the species.
