[[naturewalk|Naturewalk]] (Ocean) is the catch-all terrain type for all aquatic environments — freshwater rivers, lakes, marshes, and open ocean alike. A Pokemon with Naturewalk (Ocean) ignores water-based terrain penalties regardless of whether the water is salt or fresh. This means a freshwater species like [[Goldeen]] uses Naturewalk (Ocean) even though its [[habitat-types|habitat]] is Freshwater.
