Upon evolving, a Pokemon's stats are completely rebuilt: take the new form's [[pokemon-base-stats-from-species]], reapply any [[vitamins-raise-base-stats]], then redistribute all stat points from scratch per [[five-stat-points-per-level]]. [[base-stat-relations-removed|Base Stat Relations do not apply]].

This is a [[clear-then-reapply-pattern]] — the entire stat block is reconstructed, not patched.
