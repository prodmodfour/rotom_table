Learned Traits are [[trait-definition|traits]] that a character has unlocked due to its own personal story. Both Pokemon and trainers can have Learned Traits. They are pre-designed and come from the Learned Traits catalog.

Learned Traits can:

- Give a character new abilities.
- Improve a character's current Traits and Skills.

This makes [[training-unlocks-traits-and-moves|training]] the main way to unlock new skills.

Learned Traits cannot increase [[base-stats-unmodifiable-by-traits|Base Stats]] — that is what levelling is for.

Learned Traits are gained through [[unlock-conditions]], separate from stat growth via the [[unlock-level-up-split]]. Training uses a [[training-dual-check-system|dual-check system]] where both Pokemon and Trainer must pass.
