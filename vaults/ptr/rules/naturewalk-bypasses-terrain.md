The Naturewalk [[trait-definition|trait]] lets a Pokemon ignore terrain penalties (Slow, Rough) in specific habitat types. An Oddish with Naturewalk (Forest, Grassland) is not hindered by grassy terrain that would slow or impede others.

Naturewalk is a [[innate-traits|species trait]] from the Pokedex, making habitat-native Pokemon more effective fighters in their home terrain. This rewards [[sensible-ecosystems]] in encounter design — placing Pokemon in their native habitats gives them a mechanical edge.
