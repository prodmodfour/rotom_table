Teleportation is the most restricted movement type. Only one teleport action per round. The Pokemon must have line of sight to the destination. They must end each teleport touching a surface — chaining teleports to simulate flight is not allowed.

These constraints prevent Teleporter from being a universal bypass for all spatial challenges. Despite being instant repositioning, it has meaningful limitations that preserve [[tactical-mechanics-must-matter|tactical positioning decisions]].
