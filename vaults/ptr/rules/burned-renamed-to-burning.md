PTR renames the "Burned" condition to "Burning." The mechanical identity is the same — a Persistent condition that deals tick damage and applies -2 Def CS — but the name changes.

This is part of a broader set of PTR revisions to Persistent conditions (see [[persistent-effects-give-saves]]).
