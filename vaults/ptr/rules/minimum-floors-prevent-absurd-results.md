When a formula can produce zero through rounding, a minimum of 1 is applied. Damage and healing actions should always provide at least token benefit.

[[non-immune-attacks-deal-damage]] applies a minimum 1 floor at both the post-defense and post-type-effectiveness steps, ensuring every non-immune hit deals at least 1 damage. [[rest-heals-minimum-one]] applies a minimum 1 HP per rest period, preventing rest from being completely useless for low-HP Pokemon.

The post-defense floor matches the rules. The post-type-effectiveness floor and the rest healing floor are Rotom Table design decisions where the rules are silent.
