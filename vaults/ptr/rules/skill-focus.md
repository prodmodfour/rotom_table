Focus covers concentration, willpower, mental resilience, and performing under stress. Characters with high Focus are intensely driven and unshakable once committed to a task.

Common checks: maintaining concentration while injured or distressed, performing delicate tasks under pressure (disarming a bomb, picking a lock while poisoned), resisting supernatural mental influence (possession, psychic domination), keeping self-control in the face of strong fear or anger.

Focus is often tested alongside another skill — the other skill determines *what* you're doing, Focus determines whether you can keep doing it under adverse conditions.

Also governs psychic and supernatural abilities — Telepathy, Telekinesis, and similar powers use Focus for their checks.

Opposed checks: Focus vs Intimidate to resist being cowed. Focus vs Focus when two psychics contest each other's mental influence.
