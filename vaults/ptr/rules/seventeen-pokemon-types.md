Each Pokemon has one or two elemental Types from 17 options: Bug, Dark, Dragon, Electric, Fairy, Fighting, Fire, Ghost, Grass, Ground, Ice, Normal, Poison, Psychic, Rock, Steel, and Water. Types determine damage relationships per [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves]] and can grant immunities per [[type-grants-status-immunity]].
