A training session lasts one hour and focuses on one Pokemon. During a session, a Trainer works toward [[training-unlocks-traits-and-moves|unlocking a Learned Trait]] using the [[training-dual-check-system|dual-check system]] — one attempt per session.

[[training-overwork-injury-risk|Multiple sessions per day are possible]] but risk injury beyond the first session for a given Pokemon.
