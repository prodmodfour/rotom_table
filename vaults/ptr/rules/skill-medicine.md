Medicine covers healing, biology, diagnosis, and medical treatment for both humans and Pokemon. Renamed from PTU's "Medicine Education" to reflect its broader practical scope.

Common checks: performing first aid in the field, constructing splints, cleaning and dressing wounds, diagnosing diseases (Pokerus, radiation sickness from malformed stones), inventing new treatments, operating medical devices, understanding Pokemon and human biology.

Extends beyond treatment into biological research — gene therapy, biological augmentation, and understanding the organic sciences that Technology Education explicitly excludes.
