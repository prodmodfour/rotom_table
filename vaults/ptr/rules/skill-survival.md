Survival covers wilderness activities — starting fires, building shelter, navigating, foraging, tracking, geology, and spelunking. Rangers and conservation-minded trainers excel here.

Common checks: navigating storms, foraging for food, setting up camp, scouting an area to learn its common Pokemon and resources, identifying mushrooms and berries, searching for elemental stones and fossils. Difficulty scales with ecosystem complexity — open savanna is easier than dense rainforest or arctic tundra.

Opposed checks: tracking (Survival vs target's Stealth), with environmental modifiers.

Survival also counts as an Education skill for purposes of breadth — it bridges practical outdoor knowledge with academic understanding of geography and ecology.
