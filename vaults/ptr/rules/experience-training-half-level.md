> [!note] Superseded
> Training no longer gives XP in PTR. See [[training-no-longer-gives-xp]].

In PTU, a Pokemon receiving Experience Training gained XP equal to half its own Level plus a bonus from its Trainer's Command Rank: Pathetic/Untrained +0, Novice/Adept +5, Expert/Master +10, Virtuoso +15. Each Pokemon could receive this only once per day.
