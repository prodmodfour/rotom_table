Level 1 trainers start with 10 HP and 5 in each other stat, then distribute 10 points freely with a maximum of 5 points in any single stat. This constraint prevents extreme specialization at character creation while allowing meaningful differentiation.

After creation, stat points are gained one per level with no per-stat cap — trainers don't follow Base Relations. See [[per-level-gains-exactly-as-listed]].
