Temporary HP represents protective buffs — fairy shields, chef's cooking, cheerleader morale, root energy. These buffs narratively absorb part of an impact. Temp HP buffing strategies should be mechanically rewarded.

This drives [[massive-damage-after-temp-hp]] (only real HP lost counts toward injury threshold, so temp HP shields from massive damage checks) and [[hp-loss-recoil-vs-self-cost]] (recoil from Life Orb absorbs temp HP because it's an incidental cost, while deliberate sacrifices like Belly Drum bypass it).

The distinction is [[intent-based-classification]]: incoming effects interact with temp HP, deliberate self-costs don't. Multiple temp HP sources don't stack — see [[temp-hp-highest-only]].
