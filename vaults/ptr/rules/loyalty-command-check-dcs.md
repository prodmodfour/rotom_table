Owned Pokemon with low loyalty require a Command, Charm, or Persuade check to obey in battle. The DC depends on loyalty rank:

- Rank 0: DC 20
- Rank 1: DC 10
- Rank 2: DC 5
- Rank 3+: no check required

Starting loyalty depends on origin per [[loyalty-varies-by-origin]].
