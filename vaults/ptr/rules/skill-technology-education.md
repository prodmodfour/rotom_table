Technology Education covers machines, engineering, computers, chemistry (inorganic), and electronics. Characters with high Technology Education are comfortable in factories, workshops, and labs.

Common checks: repairing vehicles or machinery, hacking databases, cracking electronic locks, building custom equipment, crafting Poke Balls, understanding and disabling superweapons or security systems (laser tripwires, proximity sensors, mines).

Important boundary: Technology Education deals with the inorganic. Biology, organic chemistry, and medical applications fall under [[skill-medicine|Medicine]] even when advanced technology is involved.

What Technology Education covers varies by campaign time period — driving a car is common knowledge in modern settings but may require this skill in historical ones.
