Performance covers entertaining an audience — singing, dancing, playing instruments, acting on stage, storytelling, and any other form of public display meant to captivate. This is a new PTR skill with no PTU equivalent.

Common checks: putting on a show for a crowd, performing in a competition, busking for money, creating a distraction through spectacle, inspiring morale through song or story, ceremonial displays.

Performance differs from other social skills in that it targets an audience rather than an individual. [[Skill-charm|Charm]] wins someone over personally; [[skill-deception|Deception]] fools them; [[skill-persuasion|Persuasion]] convinces them with logic. Performance captivates a group through art and spectacle.

Pokemon with high Performance excel at displays, dances, or vocalizations — behaviors that in PTU might have been handled by Contests.
