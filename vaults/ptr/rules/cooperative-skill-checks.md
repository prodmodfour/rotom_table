Two cooperative skill check variants:

- **Team Check:** The GM sets a normal DC and multiplies it by the expected number of participants. Each participant rolls [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier]], and the sum of all rolls is compared to the Team DC.
- **Assisted Check:** The primary actor rolls normally, adding half the helper's [[skill-modifiers-from-traits-or-circumstance|skill modifier]] (rounded down per [[always-round-down]]) as a bonus.
