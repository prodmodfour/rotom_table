PTU defines two cooperative skill check variants:

- **Team Check:** The GM sets a normal DC and multiplies it by the expected number of participants. Each participant rolls their [[skill-check-xd6-vs-dc]], and the sum of all rolls is compared to the Team DC.
- **Assisted Check:** The primary actor rolls normally, adding half the helper's skill rank as a bonus. The helper must have at least Novice rank to assist.

Both variants extend the base [[skill-check-xd6-vs-dc]] mechanic. The app should support both modes when resolving group actions.
