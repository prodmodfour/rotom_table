In combat accuracy rolls, a natural 1 always misses and a natural 20 always hits, regardless of any modifiers. No amount of accuracy bonuses can save a natural 1, and no amount of evasion can dodge a natural 20.

This parallels [[natural-roll-extremes-in-capture]] in the capture system but operates on the d20 accuracy roll rather than the d100 capture roll. Both express the same design philosophy: no situation is truly hopeless or truly guaranteed.

A natural 20 is also a [[crit-doubles-dice-not-stats]] trigger by default.
