[[opportunist|Opportunist]] is the identity trait for Dark-type Pokemon. Unlike the Manipulation family of traits, Opportunist is not elemental control -- it is a combat behavior. Dark types fight dirty: they exploit openings, strike when foes are vulnerable, and take every advantage offered.

Mechanically, Opportunist grants Dark-type Struggle Attacks and additional Attacks of Opportunity per round. This dual function captures both the type identity (Dark Struggles) and the behavioral archetype (predatory opportunism). Every Dark type has Opportunist unless there is a specific reason to omit it.

This is the least intuitive type→trait mapping because Opportunist doesn't share a name with its type and its primary identity reads as a combat trait rather than a type marker. But the Struggle modification makes it functionally equivalent to the Manipulation family -- it's how Dark types express their typing in ambient, non-move interactions.
