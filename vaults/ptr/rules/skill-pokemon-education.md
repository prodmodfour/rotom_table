Pokemon Education covers knowledge about Pokemon species — biology, behavior patterns, diets, evolution, type effectiveness, move capabilities, and ecological roles.

Common checks: understanding wild Pokemon behavior, identifying species from indirect evidence (tracks, claw marks, territorial signs), recalling detailed move and stat information beyond common knowledge, identifying Pokemon eggs, diagnosing unusual Pokemon conditions.

Type effectiveness, a species' types, and basic stat tendencies are common knowledge for anyone. More detailed information — specific move timings, rare species behaviors, evolutionary triggers — requires investment in Pokemon Education.
