Falling damage is a typeless Physical attack with Damage Base scaling by both distance fallen and weight class. Weight Class 1-2: +1 DB per meter, max DB 20. Weight Class 3-6: +2 DB per meter, max DB 28. Heavier creatures hit harder.

Falls of 4+ meters also cause injuries: 1 injury per 2 meters fallen (or 1 per 3 meters for Pokemon with natural Sky speeds, since they can arrest falls partially).

This interacts with [[environment-modifies-encounter-difficulty]] — elevated terrain, bridges, cliffs, and the [[ice-environment-weight-interaction]] all create falling hazards. The app should calculate falling damage automatically when a combatant drops from elevation.
