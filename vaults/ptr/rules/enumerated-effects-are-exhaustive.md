When the rules enumerates specific effects of a mechanic, only those effects apply. No implicit additional benefits beyond what is listed.

Extended rest explicitly restores "Drained AP" — so it restores Drained AP and nothing else not listed. Per-level trainer gains are exactly Stat Points, Features, and Edges — see [[per-level-gains-exactly-as-listed]]. Faint explicitly clears Persistent and Volatile conditions — see [[other-conditions-source-dependent-faint]].

This prevents scope creep where mechanics quietly accumulate unintended side effects.
