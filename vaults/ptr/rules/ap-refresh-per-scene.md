Action Points are completely regained at the end of each Scene. This makes AP a scene-scoped tactical resource, not a daily one.

The distinction between scene refresh (restores all AP) and daily mechanics is important: Drained AP is only restored by extended rest ([[extended-rest-drained-ap-only]]), and Bound AP persists until the binding effect ends ([[bound-ap-persists-until-unbound]]). Scene refresh applies to the AP pool's maximum, while Drain/Bind reduce the available pool.
