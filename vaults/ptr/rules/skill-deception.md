Deception covers lying, manipulation, disguise, acting, and social subterfuge. Replaces PTU's "Guile" skill. Characters with high Deception think quickly on their feet and are convincing liars.

Common checks: lying convincingly, spinning believable cover stories, impersonating someone, blending into a crowd to tail a target, fast-talking out of suspicion, manipulating someone with half-truths, tricking a wild Pokemon into a trap.

Also covers streetwise instincts — reading shady situations, gathering underworld information, haggling on the black market, telling an undercover cop from a real dealer.

Opposed checks: Deception vs [[skill-insight|Insight]] to see through lies. Deception vs [[skill-perception|Perception]] to see through disguises. Used in Manipulate [[combat-maneuvers-use-opposed-checks|combat maneuvers]].
