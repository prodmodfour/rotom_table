Loyalty ranks have named tiers that describe the Pokemon's attitude toward its trainer:

| Rank | Name |
|---|---|
| 0 | Defiant |
| 1 | Distrustful |
| 2 | Wary |
| 3 | Fond |

At low loyalty, [[loyalty-command-check-dcs]] apply in battle. Loyalty is [[loyalty-is-visible|visible to both player and GM]].
