Not every trait must increase in potency when a Pokemon evolves. Evolution represents a shift in ecological niche, body plan, and capability -- but some traits plateau because the species' relationship to that ability doesn't deepen. A [[Hoothoot]] and [[Noctowl]] both have [[mind-manipulation|Mind Manipulation]] [1] because the line's psychic sensitivity is ambient and secondary to its role as a hunter; evolution sharpens its predatory traits ([[apex-predator|Apex Predator]], higher Power, faster movement) without amplifying its psychic reach.

Forcing every ranked trait to increase on evolution leads to power inflation and obscures what actually changes about a species when it evolves. The interesting design question is: what does this evolution *specialize in*?
