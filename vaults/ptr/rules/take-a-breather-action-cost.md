Taking a Breather is a Full Action. The user must also use their Shift Action to move as far away from enemies as possible using their highest [[movement-traits|movement trait]]. They then become Tripped and Vulnerable until the end of their next turn.

This makes [[take-a-breather-resets-combat-state]] a deliberate tactical trade-off: escaping status effects costs an entire turn of actions and leaves you exposed.
