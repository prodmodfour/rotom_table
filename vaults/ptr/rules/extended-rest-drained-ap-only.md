Extended rest clears only Drained AP, not Bound AP. PTU p.252 explicitly states extended rest restores "Drained AP." Bound AP is a separate mechanic tied to specific feature/ability effects and remains until the binding effect ends.

This follows [[enumerated-effects-are-exhaustive]] and [[separate-mechanics-stay-separate]].
