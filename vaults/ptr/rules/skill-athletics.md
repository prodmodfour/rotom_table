Athletics covers physical endurance, strength, climbing, swimming, and strenuous exertion. Active, physically trained characters excel here.

Common checks: climbing trees or cliffs, staying afloat in rough water, long-distance travel over harsh terrain, carrying heavy loads, fishing. Athletics also factors into [[throwing-range-scales-with-athletics|throwing range]].

Opposed checks: extended chases, holding a door against someone pushing it open, endurance contests.

When in doubt, if the task involves strenuous physical activity but not precision or finesse, Athletics is the go-to skill.
