A combatant's grid footprint is determined by Size: Small and Medium occupy 1x1 meter, Large occupies 2x2, Huge occupies 3x3, Gigantic occupies 4x4.

The app's VTT must render tokens at these dimensions and use the full footprint for adjacency calculations, movement cost, and [[flanking-scales-with-target-size]].
