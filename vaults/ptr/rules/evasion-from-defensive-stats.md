Evasion values are derived by dividing the related combat stat by 5 and rounding down, capped at +6. Physical Evasion comes from Defense, Special Evasion from Special Defense, and Speed Evasion from Speed.

The cap at +6 (at 30 stat points) means investment beyond 30 in a defensive stat yields no additional evasion. The app auto-calculates these per [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]], and [[flanking-penalty-post-cap]] applies penalties after this cap.
