Snacks (including Berries) can be consumed as an Extended Action to store a Digestion Buff. A Pokemon or Trainer may hold only one Digestion Buff at a time. The buff can be traded in during battle to activate the snack's effect.

**Berry activation conditions:** Digestion Buffs from Berries can only be traded in when the user is at 50% HP or lower, or when suffering a status the Berry cures. Some Berries specify alternate triggers (e.g. being hit by a specific type).

**Common snacks:**

| Item | Effect | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Candy Bar | Heals 5 HP | $75 |
| Honey | Heals 5 HP, can be used as Bait | $100 |
| Leftovers | Recover 1/16 max HP per turn for rest of encounter | $350 |
| Black Sludge | Poison-type only: recover 1/8 max HP per turn | $500 |

**Refreshments** are consumed outside combat as Extended Actions. They heal HP directly. A Pokemon or Trainer can consume one per half hour.

| Item | Effect | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Enriched Water | 20 HP | $75 |
| Super Soda Pop | 30 HP | $125 |
| Sparkling Lemonade | 50 HP | $250 |
| MooMoo Milk | 80 HP | $500 |
