Every non-immune attack deals at least 1 damage. Two minimum-1 floors are applied: after defense subtraction and after type effectiveness multiplication. Only full type immunity (x0 multiplier) produces 0 damage.

The post-defense floor matches PTU 1.05 RAW. The post-type-effectiveness floor is a Rotom Table [[minimum-floors-prevent-absurd-results]] decision — PTU is silent on whether resistance can reduce post-defense minimum damage to zero, and Rotom Table says it cannot. These floors apply at steps 7 and 8 of [[damage-formula-step-order]].
