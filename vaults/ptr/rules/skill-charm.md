Charm covers likability, warmth, and making others favorably disposed toward you. Characters with high Charm are charismatic and know what to say to draw people closer.

Common checks: gaining entry to restricted areas through friendliness, haggling prices down, cutting favorable deals, improving the disposition of wild Pokemon, preventing conflict through diplomacy, making a good first impression.

Charm can employ many different approaches — seduction, flattery, emotional appeals, earnest sincerity — all toward the end of building positive relationships.

Opposed checks: rare. Usually tested against a static DC set by the GM. Occasionally used to compare persuasiveness when a third party is evaluating competing arguments.
