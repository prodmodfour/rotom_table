Delicate skill tasks performed during combat (picking a lock, mixing volatile chemicals, harvesting fragile specimens) require a DC 16 [[skill-focus|Focus]] check in addition to the normal skill check. Each increment of 4 by which the Focus check fails imposes an additional −1 penalty to the skill check.

If the character was hit by a damaging attack in the current or previous round, they take an automatic −2 penalty on top of Focus penalties. Each Injury gained in that time adds another −2.

This does not apply to most combat actions — Moves, maneuvers, traits, and standard combat skill checks are exempt. It only triggers for tasks requiring careful, sustained concentration in a hostile environment.
