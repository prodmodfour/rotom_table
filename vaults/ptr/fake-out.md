Fake Out is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Priority.

You may only use Fake Out with Priority upon joining an encounter; if you do, Fake Out Flinches the target. Switching out resets the requirement of joining an encounter.
