Decorate is a Fairy-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target gains +2 CS in both Attack and Special Attack.
