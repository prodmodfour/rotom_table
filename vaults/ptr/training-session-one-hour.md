A training session lasts one hour and focuses on one Pokemon. During a session, a Trainer works toward [[training-unlocks-traits-and-moves|unlocking Traits or moves]] by attempting to earn a training success.

[[training-overwork-injury-risk|Multiple sessions per day are possible]] but risk injury beyond the first session for a given Pokemon.
