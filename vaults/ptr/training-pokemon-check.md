During a [[training-dual-check-system|training session]], the Pokemon rolls a [[skill-check-1d20-plus-modifier|skill check]] relevant to the [[learned-traits|Learned Trait]] being trained. The Learned Trait specifies which skill(s) and DC(s) the Pokemon must pass.

Some traits require a single check (e.g., [[improved-athletics|Improved Athletics]]: DC 10+X Athletics), while others require multiple checks in different skills (e.g., [[teamwork|Teamwork]]: DC 10 Command, DC 10 Persuasion, DC 10 Charm).
