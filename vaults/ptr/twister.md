Twister is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 4, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, Ranged Blast 3.

Twister Flinches the target on 18+. Small or Medium targets in the central square of the blast are not hit. Any Pokémon Airborne as a result of Bounce, Fly, or Sky Drop above the Blast are hit, ignoring range, and Twister has a Damage Base of 8 against those targets instead. The 18+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
