Retaliate is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Retaliate's DB is doubled to DB 14 (4d10+15 / 40) if an ally has been Fainted by a Damaging Move used by the Target in the last 2 rounds of Combat.
