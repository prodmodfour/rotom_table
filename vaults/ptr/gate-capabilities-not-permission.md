Skill rank requirements in PTU gate what you can DO with equipment, not whether you can HOLD it. Any trainer can equip any weapon; the skill rank determines which weapon-granted moves become available.

This applies to normal weapons (Crude/Simple/Fine) and to [[living-weapon-gates-moves]]. No PTU weapon requires a minimum rank just to hold or equip it. Separating "equip/engage" from "move availability" is the correct model.
