[[Corviknight]] is the strongest bird Pokemon in the Galar region and serves as the backbone of the Galar Taxi company, transporting people between locations thanks to its intelligence, flying skill, and sheer carrying capacity. However, in Paldea, Corviknight is constantly attacked by Tinkatuff and Tinkaton -- who pelt it with rocks during flight -- so the region uses Squawkabilly for taxi service instead. This regional variation shows how predator-prey dynamics shape the practical roles Pokemon fill in different societies. [[Noctowl]] serves a similar taxi role in Kitakami.
