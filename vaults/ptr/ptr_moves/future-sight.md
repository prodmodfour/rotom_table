# Future Sight

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: —
	- Range: 10, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Future Sight does nothing on the turn it is used. At the end of the user's next turn, Future Sight hits, even if the user is no longer on the field. Future Sight cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
- Focus 6
- Occult Education 4

## Notes
Delayed auto-hit attack.
