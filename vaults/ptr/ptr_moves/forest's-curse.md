# Forest's Curse

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Swift Action. The target gains the Grass Type in addition to its other Types for 5 turns.

## Unlock Conditions
- Plant Manipulation 4
- Sorcery OR Occult Education 5
