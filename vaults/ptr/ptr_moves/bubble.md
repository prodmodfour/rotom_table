# Bubble

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Bubble lowers the target's Speed.

## Unlock Conditions
— Water Creation 1
- Water Manipulation 3
## Notes
AoE Water move with Speed debuff chance.
