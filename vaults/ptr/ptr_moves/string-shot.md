# String Shot

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Cone 2
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect
Targets have their Speed CS lowered by -1. If this lowers their Speed CS to -6, or if their Speed CS was already at -6, the target is instead Stuck.

## Unlock Conditions
- Threaded

## Notes
AoE Speed debuff. Grants Threaded.
