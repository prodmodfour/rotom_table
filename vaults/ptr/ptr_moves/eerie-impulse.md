# Eerie Impulse

## Properties
	- Type: Electric
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
The target's Special Attack is lowered by 2 Combat Stages.

## Unlock Conditions
- Electricity Manipulation 2
- Intimidate 4 


## Notes
Strong Special Attack debuff. Grants Glow capability.
