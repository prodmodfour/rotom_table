# Twister

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, Ranged Blast 3
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Small or Medium targets in the central square of the blast are not hit. Twister Flinches the target on 18-20 during Accuracy Check. Any Pokemon Airborne as a result of Fly or Sky Drop above the Blast are hit, ignoring range, and Twister has a Damage Base of 8 against those targets instead.

## Unlock Conditions
- Dragon Type
- Wind Manipulation 1

## Notes
AoE Dragon move with anti-air properties and Flinch chance.
