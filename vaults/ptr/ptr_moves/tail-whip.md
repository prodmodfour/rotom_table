# Tail Whip

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1, Friendly
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect
All legal targets have their Defense lowered by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Tail]

## Notes
Basic debuffing move that hits all adjacent foes.
