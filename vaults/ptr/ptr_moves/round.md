# Round

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: X
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1, Sonic
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Round's Damage Base is equal to 6, plus +2 more for each previous use of Round by any Trainer or Pokemon this round, up to a maximum of DB 12.

## Unlock Conditions
- Vocal OR Singer OR Resonant Body
- Performance 3

## Notes
Sonic keyword. Stacks with allies using Round in the same round.
