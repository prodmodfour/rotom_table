# Aurora Beam

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Aurora Beam lowers the target's Attack 1 Combat Stage on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
— Ice Creation 2
- Ice Manipulation 2
- Light Creation 2
- Light Manipulation 2
- Focus 4
