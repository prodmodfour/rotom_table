# Heal Pulse

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 6, 1 Target, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Restores 50% of the target's max Hit Points. Heal Pulse's user may not target itself with Heal Pulse.

## Unlock Conditions
- Focus 5
- Medicine 5

## Notes
Ally-only ranged heal. Aura keyword.
