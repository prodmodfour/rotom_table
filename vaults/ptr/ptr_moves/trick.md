# Trick

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 5, 2 Targets
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Both targets must be hit for Trick to succeed. The user may target itself or willing allies with Trick; you do not need to roll for Accuracy Check in these cases. Both targets lose their Held Item or Accessory Slot Item, and gain the other target's Held Item or Accessory Slot Item. If a target has no Item, it still can gain the other target's Item.

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 3
- Deception 3
- Stealth 3

## Notes
More versatile than Switcheroo — can swap items between any two targets.
