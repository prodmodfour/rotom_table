# Circle Throw

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Push
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target is Pushed 6 meters minus their Weight Class. On 15+, the target is also Tripped.

## Unlock Conditions
— Combat 5
- Power 4

## Notes
Repositioning attack with weight-dependent push distance and trip chance. Push keyword.
