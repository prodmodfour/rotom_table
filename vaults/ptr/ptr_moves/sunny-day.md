# Sunny Day

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field, Weather
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The weather becomes Sunny for 5 rounds. While Sunny, Fire-Type Attacks gain a +5 bonus to Damage Rolls, and Water-Type Attacks suffer a -5 Damage penalty.

## Unlock Conditions
- Fire Generation 3
- Fire Manipulation 3
- Focus 4

## Notes
Weather move.
