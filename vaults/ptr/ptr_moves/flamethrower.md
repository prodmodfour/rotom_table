# Flamethrower

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Flamethrower Burns the target on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Fire Generation 3
- Fire Manipulation 1
- Individual Special Attack 10

## Notes
Reliable high-power Fire move.
