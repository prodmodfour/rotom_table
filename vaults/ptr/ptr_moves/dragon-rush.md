# Dragon Rush

## Properties
	- Type: Dragon
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Push, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The target is Pushed 3 meters. Dragon Rush Flinches the target on a roll of 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Individual Attack 30
- Athletics 5
- Combat 5
- Dragon Type

## Notes
Powerful physical Dragon attack with Push, Flinch, Dash, and Smite keywords.
