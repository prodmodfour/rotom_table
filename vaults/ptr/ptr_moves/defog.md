# Defog

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field, Weather
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The Weather becomes Clear, and all Blessings, Coats, and Hazards are destroyed. Clear Weather is the default weather, conferring no innate bonuses or penalties.

## Unlock Conditions
- Wind Manipulation 3 

## Notes
Powerful field-clearing utility move. Removes weather, blessings, coats, and hazards all at once.
