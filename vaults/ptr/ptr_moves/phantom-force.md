# Phantom Force

## Properties
	- Type: Ghost
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Set-Up
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Set-Up Effect: The user is removed from the field, and their turn ends. Resolution Effect: Phantom Force's user appears adjacent to any legal target on the field, ignoring [[movement-traits|movement traits]], and then uses Phantom Force's attack. Phantom Force cannot be avoided by Moves with the Shield Keyword, the Dodge trait, or similar effects, and Intercepts may not be attempted in response.

## Unlock Conditions
- Ghost Type
- Intangibility
- Combat 4

## Notes
Two-turn attack. Bypasses shields and dodge. Ghost-type equivalent of Shadow Force.
