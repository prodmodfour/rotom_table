# Barrier

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Hazard
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The user creates a Barrier of psychic energy. The user places up to 4 segments of Barrier; each segment must be continuous with another segment, and at least one must be adjacent to the user. These barriers count as blocking terrain and last until the end of the encounter or until they are destroyed. Each Barrier segment is 2 meters tall, 1 meter wide, and 2 centimeters thick. Each segment's Hit Points are the user's Barrier Trait X value * 15 and Damage Reduction is 15, and each segment takes damage as if it was Psychic Typed.

## Unlock Conditions
- Barrier 1

## Notes
Terrain-creating move with Hazard keyword. Segments function as destructible walls. Powered by the [[barrier|Barrier X]] trait.
