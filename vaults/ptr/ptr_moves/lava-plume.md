# Lava Plume

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Lava Plume Burns all targets on 16+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Fire Generation 4
- Earth Manipulation 3
- Fire Manipulation 4


## Notes
AoE Fire with high Burn chance. Hits all adjacent targets.
