# Zen Headbutt

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Zen Headbutt Flinches the target on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Head]
- Focus 4
- Athletics 4
- Individual Attack 20

## Notes
Keywords: Dash. Strong physical Psychic attack with good Flinch chance.
