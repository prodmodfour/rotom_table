# Night Daze

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 3
	- Range: 4, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Night Daze lowers the target's Accuracy by -1 on 13+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Species: Zoroark

## Notes
Zoroark's signature move. Accuracy debuff chance.
