# Bulldoze

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 1
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
All legal targets have their Speed lowered 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
— Earth Manipulation 3

## Notes
AoE Ground attack with guaranteed Speed debuff on hit.
