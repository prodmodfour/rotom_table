# Fake Out

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Priority, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
You may only use Fake Out with Priority upon joining an encounter; if you do, Fake Out Flinches the target. Switching out resets the requirement of joining an encounter.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Hands] 
- Deception 3

## Notes
Priority move with conditional Flinch.
