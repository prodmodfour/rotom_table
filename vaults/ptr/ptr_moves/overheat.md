# Overheat

## Properties
	- Type: Fire
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 13
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 8, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 7

## Effect
Lower the user's Special Attack 2 Combat Stages after damage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Fire Generation 5
- Fire Manipulation 3
- Individual Special Attack 40


## Notes
Extremely powerful but self-debuffing. Smite keyword. AoE blast.
