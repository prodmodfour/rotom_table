# Withdraw

## Properties
	- Type: Water
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 0

## Effect
The user becomes Withdrawn. While Withdrawn, the user becomes immune to Critical Hits and gains 15 Damage Reduction. However, while Withdrawn, the user cannot Shift, and may only use self-targeting Moves. The user may stop being Withdrawn as a Shift Action.

## Unlock Conditions
- Shell 1

## Notes
Powerful defensive stance with significant mobility tradeoff.
