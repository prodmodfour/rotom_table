# Energy Ball

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Energy Ball lowers the target's Special Defense by 1 Combat Stage on a roll of 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Grass Type
- Focus 4
- Individual Special Attack 20

## Notes
Strong special Grass attack with SpDef-lowering chance. Good range.
