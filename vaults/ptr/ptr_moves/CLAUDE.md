# PTR Moves

~382 PTR move stat blocks with energy costs and effects.

[[moves-are-universally-available|Any Pokemon can learn any move]] if it meets the move's [[unlock-conditions]]. There are no per-species move lists in PTR.

## What you can't know without exploring here

- A move's PTR stats (energy cost, damage base, effects, unlock conditions)
- The energy cost balance across move types and power levels

- Move *keyword mechanics* (e.g. `spirit-surge.md`) live in `../rules/`, not here. This folder has stat blocks only.

When creating or modifying moves: read move_philosophy.md

PTR does not have move frequency (Scene, EOT, At-Will, etc.). Never add frequency to moves or traits.
