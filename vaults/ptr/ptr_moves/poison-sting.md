# Poison Sting

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 2
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Poison Sting Poisons the target on 17+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Poison Coated [Stinger]
- Poison Expulsion

Basic Poison-type attack with poison chance.
