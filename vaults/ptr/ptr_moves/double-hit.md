# Double Hit

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 4
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
No additional effect beyond damage. Double Strike causes this move to hit twice.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Tail] OR Natural Weapon [Arms]
- Individual Attack 10
- Combat 3

## Notes
Hits twice via Double Strike keyword. Each hit is rolled separately.
