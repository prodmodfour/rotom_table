# Imprison

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: 10, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target is Locked for the rest of the Scene. A Locked target may not use any Moves the user knows. Imprison cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 4
- Focus 6


## Notes
Disables shared moves on a target. Cannot miss.
