# Low Kick

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Weight Class
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Low Kick's Damage Base is equal to twice the target's Weight Class.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon: Legs 
- Combat 4
- Athletics 3

## Notes
Damage scales with target's weight. Weight Class keyword. Stronger against heavier targets.
