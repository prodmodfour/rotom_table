# Bug Buzz

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Cone 2 or Close Blast 2, Sonic, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Bug Buzz lowers the Special Defense of all targets on 19+.

## Unlock Conditions
— Sound [Wing Buzz]

## Notes
Powerful AoE Bug special with flexible targeting (Cone or Blast). Sonic and Smite keywords.
