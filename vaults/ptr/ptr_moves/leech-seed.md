# Leech Seed

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
At the beginning of each of the target's turns, Leech Seed's target loses a Tick of Hit Points. Leech Seed's user then gains Hit Points equal to the amount the target lost. Leech Seed lasts until the target faints or is returned to a Poke Ball. Grass Types and targets immune to Grass Attacks are immune to Leech Seed.

## Unlock Conditions
- Plant Matter Production: Seeds
- Medicine 3

## Notes
Draining status over time. Grass-types are immune.
