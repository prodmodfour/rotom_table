# Play Rough

## Properties
	- Type: Fairy
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 9
	- AC: 4
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Play Rough lowers the target's Attack 1 Combat Stage on 17-20 during Accuracy Check.

## Unlock Conditions
- Athletics 4
- Individual Attack 20
- Combat 3
- Fairy Type

## Notes
Strong Fairy-type physical attack with Attack debuff chance.
