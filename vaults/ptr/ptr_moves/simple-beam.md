# Simple Beam

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
You choose one of the target's Abilities. Simple Beam changes that Ability to Simple for the remainder of the encounter.

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 4
- Focus 4
- Normal Type
