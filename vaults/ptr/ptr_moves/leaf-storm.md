# Leaf Storm

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 13
	- AC: 4
	- Range: 8, Ranged Blast 3, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 7

## Effect
Lower the user's Special Attack 2 Combat Stages after damage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Plant Matter Production: Leaves
- Plant Manipulation 5
- Individual Special Attack 30
- Focus 5

## Notes
Devastating AoE with self-debuff. Smite keyword.
