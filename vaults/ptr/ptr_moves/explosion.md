# Explosion

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 25
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Burst 2
	- Energy Cost: 8

## Effect
The user's Hit Points are set to -50% of their full Hit Point value. This Hit Point loss cannot be prevented or reduced in any way. The user's loyalty toward its trainer may be lowered. The user's Explosive Trait is not triggered.

## Unlock Conditions
- Explosive

## Notes
Extremely powerful self-sacrificing move.
