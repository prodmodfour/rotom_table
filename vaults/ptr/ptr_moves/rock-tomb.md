# Rock Tomb

## Properties
	- Type: Rock
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 5
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Rock Tomb lowers the target's Speed by -1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Rock Creation 2
- Earth Manipulation 2
