# Counter

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Reaction, Trigger
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
Counter may be used as a Reaction when the user is hit by a damaging Physical Attack. The triggering attack is resolved with the user resisting it one step further. After the attack is resolved, if Counter's user was not Fainted, the triggering foe loses Hit Points equal to twice the amount of Hit Points lost by the user from the triggering attack. Counter is Physical, and while it cannot miss, it cannot hit targets immune to Fighting-Type Moves.

## Unlock Conditions
- Combat 5
- Insight 5
- Perception 5

## Notes
Powerful reactive move that doubles physical damage back.
