# Poison Jab

## Properties
	- Type: Poison
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Poison Jab Poisons the target on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Poison Coated [Hands] OR Poison Coated [Stinger]
- Individual Attack 10


## Notes
Strong Poison-type physical attack with poison chance.
