# Lucky Chant

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Blessing
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Blessing -- Any user affected by Lucky Chant may activate it when receiving a Critical Hit to cause the attack to instead deal damage as if it was not a Critical Hit. Lucky Chant may be activated 3 times, and then disappears.

## Unlock Conditions
- Singer
- Focus 5


## Notes
Anti-crit Blessing. 3 activations.
