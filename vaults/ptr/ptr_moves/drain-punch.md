# Drain Punch

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 8
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Aura
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage they dealt to the target.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Hands]
- Individual Attack 20
- Combat 5
- Focus 4
- Medicine 3

## Notes
HP-draining physical Fighting attack. Aura keyword.
