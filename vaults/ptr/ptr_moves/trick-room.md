# Trick Room

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Field
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Starting at the beginning of the next round, for 5 rounds, the area is considered Rewinding. While Rewinding, Initiative is reversed, and participants instead go from lowest Initiative to highest.

## Unlock Conditions
- Mind Manipulation 5
- Focus 5
- Occult Education 4

## Notes
Field-wide Initiative reversal. Extremely powerful for slow teams.
