# Twineedle

## Properties
	- Type: Bug
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 3
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Double Strike
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
Twineedle Poisons the target on an Accuracy Check of 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Species: Beedrill

## Notes
Keywords: Double Strike. Each hit rolls separately for accuracy and poison chance.
