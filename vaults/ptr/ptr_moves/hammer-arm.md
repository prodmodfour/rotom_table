# Hammer Arm

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
The user lowers their Speed 1 Combat Stage after using this move.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Arms] OR Natural Weapon [Hands]
- Individual Attack 25
- Combat 5

## Notes
Self-debuff on use balances high power.
