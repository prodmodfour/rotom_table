# Mirror Coat

## Properties
	- Type: Psychic
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: See Effect
	- AC: —
	- Range: Any, 1 Target, Reaction
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Mirror Coat may be used as a Reaction when the user is hit by a damaging Special Attack. Resolve the triggering attack, with Mirror Coat's user resisting the attack one step further. After the attack is resolved, if Mirror Coat's user was not Fainted, the triggering foe then loses Hit Points equal to twice the amount of Hit Points lost by the user from the triggering attack. Mirror Coat is Special, and while it cannot miss, it cannot hit targets immune to Psychic-Type Moves.

## Unlock Conditions
- Insight 5
- Individual Special Defense 20
- Focus 5

## Notes
Special counterattack. Reaction move.
