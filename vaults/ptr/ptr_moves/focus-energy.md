# Focus Energy

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 5

## Effect
Swift Action. The user becomes Pumped. While Pumped, the user's Critical Range is extended by 2, or 18+ if the Critical Range is not otherwise extended. Being switched will cause this effect to end.

## Unlock Conditions
- Focus 4
