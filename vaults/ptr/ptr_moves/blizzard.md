# Blizzard

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 11
	- AC: 7
	- Range: 4, Ranged Blast 2, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Blizzard Freezes all legal targets on 15+. If the target is in Hailing Weather, Blizzard cannot miss.

## Unlock Conditions
— Ice Creation 5
- Ice Manipulation 5
- Individual Special Attack 40

## Notes
Powerful AoE Ice nuke. High AC but perfect accuracy in Hail. Smite keyword.
