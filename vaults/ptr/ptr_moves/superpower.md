# Superpower

## Properties
	- Type: Fighting
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target, Dash
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Superpower lowers the user's Attack and Defense by 1 Combat Stage each.

## Unlock Conditions
- Athletics 6
- Combat 5
- Individual Attack 30
- Power 5

## Notes
Powerful but self-debuffing. Dash keyword.
