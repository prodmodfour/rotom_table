# Moonblast

## Properties
	- Type: Fairy
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 10
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 6, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
Moonblast lowers the target's Special Attack by 1 Combat Stage on 15+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Light Creation 2 
- Light Manipulation 2
- Individual Special Attack 30
- Focus 5

## Notes
Strong Fairy-type special attack with debuff chance.
