# Thrash

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 12
	- AC: 3
	- Range: Melee, All Adjacent Foes, Smite
	- Energy Cost: 4

## Effect
After damage is dealt, the user becomes Enraged and Confused.

## Unlock Conditions
- Individual Attack 30
- Athletics 5
- Combat 5
- Violent Instincts

## Notes
High-power AoE with severe self-inflicted drawbacks. Keywords: Smite.
