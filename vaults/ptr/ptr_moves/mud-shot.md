# Mud Shot

## Properties
	- Type: Ground
	- Damage Class: Special
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 3
	- Range: 3, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 2

## Effect
The target's Speed is lowered by -1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Earth Manipulation 3
- Individual Special Attack 10

## Notes
Guaranteed Speed debuff.
