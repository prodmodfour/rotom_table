# Trailblaze

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 5
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 1

## Effect
After dealing damage, the user's Speed is raised by 1 Combat Stage.

## Unlock Conditions
- Plant Manipulation 1
- Athletics 3
- Individual Speed 10

## Notes
Gen 9 move. Not in PTU 1.05. Adapted to PTU conventions (At-Will, DB 5). Low-power physical Grass attack with built-in Speed boost, similar to Flame Charge.
