# Worry Seed

## Properties
	- Type: Grass
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: 2
	- Range: 8, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
You choose one of the target's Abilities. Worry Seed changes that Ability to Insomnia for the remainder of the encounter.

## Unlock Conditions
- Plant Matter Production: Seeds
- Focus 3
- Medicine 3

## Notes
Ability replacement move — shuts down key Abilities by overwriting them with Insomnia.
