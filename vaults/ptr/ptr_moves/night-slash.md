# Night Slash

## Properties
	- Type: Dark
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 7
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, Pass
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
Night Slash is a Critical Hit on 18+.

## Unlock Conditions
- Natural Weapon [Claws] OR Natural Weapon [Pincer] OR Natural Weapon [Blade]
- Combat 3
- Dark Type
- Individual Attack 15

## Notes
High crit rate move. Pass keyword allows moving through target.
