# Avalanche

## Properties
	- Type: Ice
	- Damage Class: Physical
	- Damage Base: 6
	- AC: 2
	- Range: Melee, 1 Target
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
When declaring Avalanche, the user does nothing and may not Shift. At the end of the round, the user Shifts and uses Avalanche on any legal target. If the target damaged the user this round, Avalanche has a Damage Base of 12 instead.

## Unlock Conditions
— Ice Creation 4
- Ice Manipulation 2
- Power 4
- Individual HP 30

## Notes
Delayed attack that rewards taking a hit — DB doubles if the user was damaged.
