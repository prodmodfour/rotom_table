# Belly Drum

## Properties
	- Type: Normal
	- Damage Class: Status
	- Damage Base: —
	- AC: —
	- Range: Self
	- Energy Cost: 3

## Effect
The user gains +6 Attack Combat Stages, and loses Hit Points equal to half of their Max Hit Points.

## Unlock Conditions
— Large Belly
- Individual HP 40

## Notes
Extreme risk/reward — maximizes Attack at the cost of half HP.
