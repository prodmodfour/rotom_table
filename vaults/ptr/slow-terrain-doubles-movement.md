Slow Terrain causes every square meter to count as two when shifting through it. Examples include uneven earth, mud, deep snow or water (not deep enough for Underwater), and ice. This applies to both Pokemon and Trainers.

Slow Terrain often overlaps with Rough Terrain but they are distinct flags in the [[multi-tag-terrain-system]]. Certain Pokemon bypass Slow Terrain via [[naturewalk-bypasses-terrain]].
