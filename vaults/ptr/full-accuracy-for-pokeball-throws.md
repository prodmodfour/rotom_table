Poke Ball throws are "Status Attack Rolls" and follow the full accuracy system: thrower's accuracy stages, target's Speed Evasion, flanking penalties, and rough terrain modifiers all apply.

PTU ch9 p.271 says "Resolve the attack like you would any other." The Snag Machine's -2 penalty to "Poke Ball attack rolls" confirms PTU's rules engine treats them as modifiable attack rolls. Speed Evasion is the appropriate evasion type since Poke Balls don't target Defense or Special Defense stats.

The evasion text's use of "moves" is treated as shorthand for "attacks with accuracy checks," not a deliberate exclusion of item-based attacks.
