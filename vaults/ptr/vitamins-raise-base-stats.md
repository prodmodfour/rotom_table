Each Vitamin type raises a specific base stat by 1: HP Up (HP), Protein (Attack), Iron (Defense), Calcium (Special Attack), Zinc (Special Defense), Carbos (Speed). Vitamin effects persist through evolution and are reapplied during [[evolution-rebuilds-all-stats]].

A maximum of [[five-vitamin-limit-per-pokemon]] applies.
