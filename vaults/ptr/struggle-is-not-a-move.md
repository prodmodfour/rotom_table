Struggle Attacks are explicitly not Moves. Effects that alter or modify Moves do not apply to Struggle Attacks. STAB never applies. Struggle has AC 4, DB 4, is Melee-range, Physical, and Normal-type.

At Expert Combat skill rank or higher, Struggle upgrades to AC 3 and DB 5. Trainer Struggle Attacks can be modified by equipped Weapons.

This distinction matters because many [[trait-definition|traits]], features, and items key off "when you use a Move" — Struggle deliberately bypasses all of those.
