[[Breloom]] uses a two-phase combat approach: first it releases incapacitating spores from the holes in its mushroom cap, then it closes distance with light, springy footwork to deliver rapid punches with its elastic, stretching arms. Its technique is compared to professional boxing. The seed clusters on its tail are made of hardened toxic spores that are horrible to eat, serving as a deterrent against rear attacks. This spore-then-strike pattern explains why Breloom is Grass/Fighting rather than pure Grass -- the spores are setup, not the main event. The combination of Effect Spore and Technician in the redesign captures both phases: passive spore contact punishment and precise, efficient striking.
