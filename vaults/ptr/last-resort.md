Last Resort is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 14, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

Last Resort can only be used after the user has performed 5 other different Moves in its Move List during a single fight, without being switched out.
