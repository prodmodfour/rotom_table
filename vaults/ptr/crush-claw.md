Crush Claw is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 3, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash.

Crush Claw lowers the target’s Defense by -1 CS on Even-Numbered Rolls.
