[[Ninetales]] can understand human speech and possesses intelligence far beyond typical Pokemon. Its gleaming red eyes are said to grant it the power to control minds, and its flames can hypnotize opponents. According to legend, it is the reincarnation of nine united noble saints.

This supernatural intelligence is the mature form of the cunning already present in [[vulpix-feigning-behavior|Vulpix's feigning behavior]] — what begins as clever survival instinct becomes genuine otherworldly awareness after evolution. It justifies [[Ninetales]]'s Telepath trait and its very high Focus and Perception skills. A Ninetales is not simply a powerful animal; it is a thinking creature that acts with deliberation and memory.
