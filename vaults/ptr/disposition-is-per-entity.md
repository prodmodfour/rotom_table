A Pokemon's disposition is not a single global attitude — it is tracked separately per entity. A wild Pokemon may be Friendly toward one trainer and Hostile toward another.

This means [[disposition-determines-starting-loyalty]] uses the disposition toward the specific trainer who captures it, not a blanket value. Similarly, [[disposition-charm-check-dcs]] apply per trainer attempting the check.
