The horn on [[Goldeen]] and [[Seaking]] serves both as a primary weapon and a social status marker. Goldeen compete to determine whose horn is strongest and thickest — horn quality signals fitness within the species. The horn is powerful enough to shatter thick aquarium glass in a single ram. In [[Seaking]], the horn becomes a tool for nest-building: males bore holes into riverbed boulders to create sheltered spawning cavities. The horn's dual role as weapon and construction tool explains why both stages carry the Horn trait, and why the line's combat identity centers on physical charge attacks like Horn Attack and Megahorn.
