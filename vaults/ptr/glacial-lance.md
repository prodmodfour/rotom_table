Glacial Lance is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 13, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Line 8, Smite.

Legal targets hit by Glacial Lance are Stuck and Trapped until the end of their next turn.

As a Daily move, Glacial Lance can be used once per day, refreshing on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]]. It is still limited to [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|one use per scene]].
