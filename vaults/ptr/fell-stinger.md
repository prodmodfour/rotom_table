Fell Stinger is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 3, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

If the user successfully knocks out the target with Fell Stinger, raise the user's Attack by +2 CS.
