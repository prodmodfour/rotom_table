Helping Hand is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 4, 1 Target, Priority.

Helping Hand grants the target +2 on its next Accuracy Roll this round, and +10 to its next Damage Roll this round.
