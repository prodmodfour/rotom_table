Coup de Grace is a Full Action against a Fainted or completely helpless target. If the attack hits, it is automatically a Critical Hit with +5 bonus damage that is multiplied as part of the crit (normally +10, or +15 with [[sniper|Sniper]]). This auto-crit ignores any immunities to critical hits.

This is a deliberate execution mechanic for finishing off helpless targets. The app should enforce the Full Action cost and helpless-target requirement, then apply the enhanced crit automatically.
