Thief is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 6, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Thief takes the target's held item and attaches it to Thief's user if the user is not holding anything.
