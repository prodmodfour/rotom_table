How the level budget is distributed across enemies matters independently of the total budget. Six level-20 enemies (even split) plays very differently from two level-40 plus four level-10 (concentrated). Even splits create casual encounters; concentrated distributions create more challenging ones.

This interacts with [[action-economy-constrains-encounter-size]] — more enemies means more actions per round regardless of individual power. The distribution is a core decision within [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]].
