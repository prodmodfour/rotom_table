The Vulpix line originally came to Alola with human settlers. To escape [[niche-competition-drives-adaptation|niche competition]] in the lowlands, they migrated to the snowy peaks of Mount Lanakila. Over generations, they adapted to the cold — their Fire typing gave way entirely to Ice, and their fur evolved to produce ice crystals (see [[vulpix-line-ice-crystal-fur]]).

This is a canonical example of regional adaptation: a permanent evolutionary divergence driven by habitat pressure, not a temporary [[type-shifts-defy-expectations|type shift]] applied by a GM. The Alolan form is a distinct population, not a variant of the original.
