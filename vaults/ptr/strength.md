Strength is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Push.

You may immediately initiate a Push Maneuver as a Free Action. The Maneuver automatically hits, but you must still make the Opposed Roll.

Strength grants the +1 Power [[move-granted-capabilities|capability]] while known.
