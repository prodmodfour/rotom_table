Nightmare is a Ghost-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

Nightmare can only hit Legal Targets that are Asleep. The target gains Bad Sleep.
