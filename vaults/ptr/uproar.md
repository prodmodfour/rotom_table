Uproar is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 5, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Burst 1, Spirit Surge, Sonic.

All Pokémon and Trainers within 5 meters of the user are cured of Sleep.
