Strength Sap is a Grass-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Daily x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The user gains Hit Points equal to the higher of the target's Attack or Special Attack; the target then loses -1 CS in that Stat.

As a Daily x2 move, Strength Sap can be used in 2 different scenes during the day but [[daily-moves-once-per-scene|never twice in the same scene]]. Its daily uses refresh on [[extended-rest-refreshes-daily-moves|extended rest]].
