[[Combee]] and [[Vespiquen]] form a rigid eusocial colony structure. Combee gather in groups of roughly a hundred, foraging nectar from sunrise to sunset and clustering together at night to form a living beehive for sleep. They inhabit caves and hollow trees in secluded forest areas. The colony's absolute loyalty to its Vespiquen queen means Combee will never defy her orders and will risk their lives — even killing threats — to protect her. Only female Combee (12.5% of the population) can evolve into Vespiquen, making queen succession a rare and significant event. This extreme gender-ratio-gated evolution explains the Pack Hunt and Pack Mon traits that define the line's combat identity.
