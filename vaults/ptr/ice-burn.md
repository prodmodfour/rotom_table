Ice Burn is an Ice-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 14, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of 10, 1 Target, Set-Up, Full Action.

Set-Up Effect: The user may shift, then ends their turn. Resolution Effect: The user attacks with Ice Burn. Ice Burn Burns on 15+. The 15+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
