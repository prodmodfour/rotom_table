[[Alolan Vulpix]] live in small packs called skulks, led and protected by an [[Alolan Ninetales]]. Members of the skulk cooperate to survive in the harsh mountain environment, helping one another find food and shelter. Weavile is a natural predator of the skulk, which further incentivizes group cohesion.

This social structure distinguishes the Alolan line from Kantonian [[Vulpix]], which are primarily solitary. It also shapes [[alolan-ninetales-territorial-guardian|Alolan Ninetales's territorial behavior]] — it defends not just its own domain, but the skulk under its care.
