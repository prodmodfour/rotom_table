Foul Play is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target.

The target reveals its Attack stat. When calculating damage, add the target's Attack stat instead of the user's Attack stat.
