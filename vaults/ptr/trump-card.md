Trump Card is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 6, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Whenever the user uses Trump Card, the user gains a Trump Count after the attack is resolved. Trump Card's DB is increased by +2 for each Trump Count.
