Pokemon Health = (Level x 5) + (HP stat x 3) + 10. This is a core [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]] target -- the app derives pokemon HP automatically from level and HP stat, recalculating whenever either changes.

The formula differs from [[trainer-hp-formula]] in that trainers have no level component because [[only-pokemon-have-levels]].
