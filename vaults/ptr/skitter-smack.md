Skitter Smack is a Bug-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, Dash, Full Action.

The user Shifts up to its Overland Capability, ignoring Attacks of Opportunity, before attacking with Skitter Smack. On a hit, the target’s Special Attack is lowered 1 CS.
