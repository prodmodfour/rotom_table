Copycat is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, no AC, and a range of 4, 1 Target.

Use the Move the target has used on their last turn. You may choose new targets for the Move. Copycat cannot miss.
