Intercept maneuvers (Melee and Ranged) require a minimum Loyalty of 4. At that rank, a Pokemon can intercept attacks aimed at its Trainer or any ally.

This creates a cross-system dependency between the combat maneuver system and the loyalty/bond system. The app must check loyalty thresholds when validating Intercept actions.

Additional constraints: cannot intercept cannot-miss moves (Aura Sphere, Swift), cannot intercept Priority/Interrupt moves unless faster than the user, cannot intercept while asleep/confused/enraged/frozen/stuck/paralyzed.
