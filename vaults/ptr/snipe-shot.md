Snipe Shot is a Water-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 8, 1 Target.

Snipe Shot is a Critical Hit on 18+. This Move may not be Intercepted, nor may any [[trait-definition|Traits]], Moves, or Features be activated to change this Move’s target. Any of those existing effects (such as Follow Me) fail.
