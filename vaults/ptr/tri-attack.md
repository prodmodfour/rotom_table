Tri Attack is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 8, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

Tri Attack gives the target a Status ailment on 17+. If this effect is triggered, roll 1d3; on 1 the target is Paralyzed; on 2 the target is Burned; on 3 the target is Frozen. The 17+ threshold is a natural die result; [[modifiers-dont-shift-effect-triggers|accuracy modifiers do not shift it]].
