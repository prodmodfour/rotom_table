[[Ninetales]] is legendarily vengeful. Anyone who grabs one of its tails or otherwise mistreats it risks a thousand-year curse — and once laid, the curse cannot be lifted, not even by Ninetales itself. This vindictive streak is not mere aggression; it is a supernatural enforcement of boundaries, proportional to the offense.

Ninetales lives for a thousand years, sustained by the energy stored in its [[ninetales-nine-tails-mystical-energy|nine tails]]. Its lifespan and its curse share the same timescale — a Ninetales that curses you will outlive the punishment.

This lore grounds the Cursed Body trait on [[Ninetales]]: contact with it carries real supernatural risk. It also reinforces why wild [[Ninetales]] encounters should be treated with extreme caution in [[wild-encounter-motivations|encounter design]].
