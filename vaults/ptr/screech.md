Screech is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 4, and a range of Burst 2, Friendly, Sonic.

Lower the Defense of all legal targets by -2 CS.
