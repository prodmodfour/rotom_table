In PTR, Sleep is now cured when the sleeping target takes any damage. However, HP Loss (such as from [[tick-value-one-tenth-max-hp|tick]] effects like Burning or Poison) does not wake the target.

The damage vs HP Loss distinction matters: damage is dealt by attacks and goes through defenses, while HP Loss is flat and bypasses defenses entirely. A sleeping target ticking from Poison stays asleep; a sleeping target hit by an attack wakes up.
