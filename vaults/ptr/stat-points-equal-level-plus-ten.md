In PTR, Pokemon gain [[five-stat-points-per-level|5 Stat Points per level]], replacing PTU's formula of Level + 10 total stat points. Stat allocation is no longer constrained by Base Stat Relations, which [[traits-replace-abilities-capabilities-natures|were removed in PTR]].
