Fiery Wrath is a Dark-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 9, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, 1 Target.

: Fiery Wrath Flinches the target on a 17+. Once per Scene, Fiery Wrath may instead be used as a Fire-Type Move.
