When a Pokemon evolves, it can immediately learn any Moves that its new form learns at a level lower than the minimum evolution level but that the previous form could not learn. This gives the evolved form access to its early signature moves without waiting.

For stone/trade evolutions without a minimum level, [[stone-evolution-moves-at-current-level]] uses the Pokemon's current level as the upper bound.
