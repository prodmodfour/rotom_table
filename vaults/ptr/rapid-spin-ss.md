Rapid Spin [SS] is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 5, At-Will frequency, AC 2, and a range of Melee, 1 Target, [[spirit-surge|Spirit Surge]].

Rapid Spin destroys all Hazards within 5 meters, removes Leech Seeds, and removes the user’s Trapped or Stuck status. If Rapid Spin hits, the user’s speed raises 1 CS.
