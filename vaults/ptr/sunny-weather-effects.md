While Sunny, Fire-Type attacks gain +5 damage and Water-Type attacks suffer -5 damage. Thunder and Hurricane are AC 11 in Sun. Ability interactions: Dry Skin loses a Tick per turn, Thermosensitive grants +2 Atk/SpAtk CS, Desert Weather adds Fire resistance, Sun Blanket heals 1/16th max HP per turn, Leaf Guard cures one status per turn, Harvest retains Digestion Buffs, Chlorophyll grants +4 Speed CS, Flower Gift creates a stat-boosting Burst 4.

Sunny is one of four [[weather-as-game-keyword]] types and the mirror of [[rain-weather-effects]]. It also interacts with Frozen status via [[frozen-save-modified-by-weather]] (granting a +4 bonus to cure saves).
