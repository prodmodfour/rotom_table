Captivate is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-eot-restriction|EOT]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of Cone 2, Friendly, Social.

Captivate lowers the target's Special Attack by -2 CS. Captivate may not affect something that is the same gender as the user or something that is genderless.
