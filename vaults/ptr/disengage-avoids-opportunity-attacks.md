The Disengage maneuver uses a Shift Action to move 1 meter without provoking an [[attack-of-opportunity-trigger-list|Attack of Opportunity]]. Normal shifting away from an adjacent foe triggers AoO; Disengage is the safe escape.

The trade-off is distance — only 1 meter instead of full movement. A combatant that needs to fully retreat must either Disengage 1m first (using their Shift Action) then rely on other movement options, or accept the AoO and shift normally.
