Three natural roll outcomes override normal [[capture-workflow]] mechanics:

- **Natural 1 on accuracy:** always misses, even if modifiers would cause the total to hit
- **Natural 20 on accuracy:** always hits, and subtracts an additional −10 from the capture roll
- **Natural 100 on capture roll:** always captures the target without fail, regardless of capture rate

These extremes ensure that no situation is truly hopeless or truly guaranteed (except Master Ball). The same natural-1-miss and natural-20-hit principle applies to combat accuracy rolls — see [[natural-one-misses-natural-twenty-hits]].
