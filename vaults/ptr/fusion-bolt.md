Fusion Bolt is an Electric-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Physical]] move with a Damage Base of 10, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 8, 1 Target, Smite.

Fusion Bolt has its Damage Base increased by +3 if Fusion Flare was used this round or last round by any participant of the encounter.
