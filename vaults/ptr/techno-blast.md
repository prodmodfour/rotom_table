Techno Blast is a Normal-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 12, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 6, Ranged Blast 2.

Techno Blast’s Type can be any Type while holding the appropriate Drive item or Plate item.
