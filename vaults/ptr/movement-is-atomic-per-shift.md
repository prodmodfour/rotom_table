**Superseded by [[movement-splitting]].**

Previously, movement could not be split around other actions within a turn — it had to be completed as a single continuous path. This is no longer the case; the [[shift-renamed-to-movement-action|Movement Action]] can now be split between other actions, with each square as the atomic unit.
