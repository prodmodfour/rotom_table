Mist Ball is a Psychic-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Special]] move with a Damage Base of 7, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene x2]] frequency, AC 2, and a range of 12, 1 Target.

Mist Ball lowers the target’s Special Attack by 1 Combat Stage on an Even-Numbered Roll.
