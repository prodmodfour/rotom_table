Clangorous Soul is a Dragon-type [[type-effectiveness-excludes-status-moves|Status]] move with no Damage Base, [[scene-frequency-definition|Scene]] frequency, no AC, and a range of Self.

The user loses 1/3rd of their Max Hit Points and has each of its stats raised by +1 Combat Stage.
