Pokemon levels range from 1 to 20. This replaces PTU's 1-100 range, with each [[ptr-level-absorbs-five-ptu-levels|PTR level absorbing 5 PTU levels]]. Each level up grants [[five-stat-points-per-level|5 Stat Points]].

Despite the compressed range, the [[total-xp-unchanged|total XP required from level 1 to max is unchanged]].
