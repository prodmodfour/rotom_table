# Constructing UML Package Diagrams

Step-by-step guide for building [[uml-package-diagram|package diagrams]] in the [[uml-internal-view|internal view]].

## Construction steps

1. **Develop initial package diagram** — the entire business system starts as one organization unit. Use job descriptions and organization charts to identify [[uml-worker|worker]] roles and [[uml-business-object|business objects]].
2. **Find additional organization units** — subdivide into divisions, teams, or groups. Only create subdivisions important for illustrating business processes.
3. **Assign workers and business objects** — make structure and assignments clearly recognizable
4. **Find additional elements** — check for missing workers, units, or business objects
5. **Verify** — all relevant workers and business objects included; no unrelated elements present

## Key distinction

Package diagrams representing organization units should not be confused with organization charts. Package diagrams include business objects alongside employees, providing more comprehensive modeling.
