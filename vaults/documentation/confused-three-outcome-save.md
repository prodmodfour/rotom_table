When a Confused Pokemon attempts to act, it makes a save with three possible outcomes: act normally, hit itself, or do nothing. The app resolves this automatically — rolling the save and applying the result without GM intervention.

This is [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]] for a condition that triggers every turn. Because the save outcomes are fully deterministic given the roll, there is no reason to require manual resolution.
