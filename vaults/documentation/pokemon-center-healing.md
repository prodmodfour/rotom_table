Pokemon Center healing fully restores HP to the [[effective-max-hp-formula]] ceiling and clears ALL [[status-condition-categories]] (persistent, volatile, and other).

Injuries are healed up to a daily cap of 3 (shared with [[natural-injury-healing]]). When all injuries are healed, the injury timestamp is cleared.

Energy is fully restored.

The healing time is calculated by [[pokemon-center-time-formula]].
