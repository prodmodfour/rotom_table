# Hold, Priority, and Interrupt System

Out-of-turn actions: holding turns, priority declarations, and interrupts.

## Hold

PTR p.227. A combatant who has not yet acted this round may hold their turn. The held combatant is removed from the current turn order and may be released later, inserting at the current position with full action economy.

## Priority

Three tiers of priority declaration:

- **Standard**: inserts a full turn, removes the original turn.
- **Limited**: consumes the Standard Action on the priority turn; remaining actions happen at normal initiative.
- **Advanced**: consumes the Standard Action; if the combatant has already acted, it forfeits next round's turn.

## Interrupt

Direct-resolve or GM-mediated out-of-turn reactions. In [[battle-modes|League Battles]], uncommandable Pokemon forfeit next round's turn on accept.
