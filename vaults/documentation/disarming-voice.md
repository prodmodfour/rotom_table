Disarming Voice is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Fairy"`, `damageBase: 4`, `frequency: "At-Will"`, `ac: null`, `range: "Burst 1"`.

## Frequency

At-Will frequency requires no usage tracking. `checkMoveFrequency` always permits use.

## Resolution

Disarming Voice flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 4 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Fairy-type users and type effectiveness.
