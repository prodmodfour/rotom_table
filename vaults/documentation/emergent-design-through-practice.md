The philosophy vault does not begin with a blueprint. There is no master document that prescribes what Rotom Table's design philosophy should be. Instead, the philosophy emerges from practice — from the accumulated effect of many small, individual acts of design.

Each time raw material is digested into atomic notes, a piece of the philosophy takes shape. Each time existing notes are matured — refined, questioned, connected — the relationships between ideas become clearer. Over time, a coherent design philosophy crystallizes from these individual acts without anyone having planned the whole.

This is the same principle that operates at the rule level through [[per-conflict-decree-required]]: thoughtful case-by-case decisions, not sweeping generalizations. At the vault level, it means the philosophy is discovered through the work, not prescribed before it.
