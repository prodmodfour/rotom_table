The app must never claim PTR authority for mechanics that are not in the rules. Bogus citations (referencing nonexistent pages) are always removed immediately.

When a mechanic claims PTR basis but has none, it must either be replaced with an actual PTR-sourced implementation or clearly labeled as an app-specific tool with no RAW claim. This is why the encounter budget formula citing a nonexistent "Core p.473" had to be replaced — see [[encounter-budget-needs-ptu-basis]].
