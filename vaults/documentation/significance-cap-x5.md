Significance multiplier presets cap at x5, matching PTU's stated range of "x1 to about x5." The five preset tiers — Insignificant through Legendary — stay within this boundary.

GMs who want to exceed x5 can always enter a custom value via manual input. The presets will not suggest it because presets carry implicit authority: if the app offers x7 as a preset, players and GMs may treat it as a standard PTU value when it is not.

This is an application of [[presets-stay-within-raw]]: the app's suggestions match the rules, and the GM's manual input is unconstrained.
