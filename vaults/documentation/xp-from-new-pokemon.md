Trainers earn XP when they encounter a Pokemon species for the first time (the "Pokedex bonus").

The app tracks [[trainer-owned-species-tracking|which species a trainer has seen]] and awards this bonus automatically on first encounter. This is part of [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]] — the GM does not manually calculate or distribute Pokedex XP.

The awarded XP feeds into the broader [[xp-distribution-flow]], which determines when a trainer levels up according to the [[pokemon-experience-chart]].
