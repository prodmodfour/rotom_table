# UML Business Rules

Domain-derived rules depicted in IT system models. They originate from business strategies, requirements, technical guidelines, and restrictions — not from technology itself.

## Two categories

- [[uml-static-business-rule]] — can be verified at any time; documented in [[uml-class-diagram|class diagrams]]
- [[uml-dynamic-business-rule]] — can only be verified when specific events occur; documented in [[uml-statechart-diagram|statechart diagrams]]

Many requirements cannot be modeled as business rules. Requirement catalogs serve as complementary documentation beyond the [[uml-modeling-it-systems|IT system model]].
