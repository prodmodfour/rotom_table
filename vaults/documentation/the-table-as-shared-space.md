The app serves a physical gathering, not isolated players. Each player's phone is their private character sheet and combat interface, but the group shares a communal display — a TV or projector that functions as the center of the table.

Players can request changes to the shared display, but the GM controls it. This mirrors the physical tabletop dynamic: anyone can ask "can we look at the map?", but the GM decides what's shown. The shared display is the digital equivalent of the battle mat everyone leans over.

Joining the game should be as frictionless as sitting down at the table. A QR code on the GM's screen gets players connected over LAN without URLs, passwords, or account creation.
