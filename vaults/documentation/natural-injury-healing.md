An injury can be healed naturally once 24 hours have passed since the entity's last injury. Natural healing is subject to the daily 3-injury cap (shared with [[pokemon-center-healing]]).

Trainers also have access to ap drain injury healing as an alternative method. Pokemon can only heal injuries naturally or via [[pokemon-center-healing]].
