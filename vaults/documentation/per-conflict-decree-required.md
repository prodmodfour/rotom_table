Each ambiguity or rule conflict in PTU is resolved by an individual decree, not by a blanket policy. A decree addresses the specific text, the specific conflict, and the specific resolution — nothing more.

This prevents overgeneralization. A decree about how Burn interacts with damage does not automatically govern how Poison interacts with damage, even if the situations look similar. Each case is examined on its own terms, grounded in the PTU text that produced the ambiguity.

Decrees accumulate into the system's body of law, but they accumulate one at a time. The GM authors each decree through [[gm-delegates-authority-into-system]], and the system enforces it going forward.
