An extended rest applies eight consecutive [[thirty-minute-rest]] periods (4 hours of in-game time).

Beyond HP restoration, extended rest:

- Clears all persistent [[status-condition-categories]] (Burning, Frozen, Paralyzed, Poisoned, Badly Poisoned) per [[extended-rest-clears-persistent-status]]
- Fully restores [[energy-resource|Energy]]
- Cures all [[fatigue-levels|Fatigue]] per [[rest-cures-fatigue]]
