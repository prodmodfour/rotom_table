Every applied condition should track what caused it. Source information determines how the condition interacts with other mechanics — whether it clears on faint, whether it's reversed by Take a Breather, whether it persists through switching.

This enables [[status-cs-auto-apply-with-tracking]] (Burn-sourced CS changes reversed on cure, re-applied after Take a Breather), [[other-conditions-source-dependent-faint]] (move-inflicted Stuck clears on faint, terrain-based Stuck doesn't), and [[recall-clears-then-source-reapplies]] (conditions clear on recall per RAW, re-apply on send-out if terrain/weather source persists).
