Hail is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Ice"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Field, Weather"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Hail skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The weather changes to Hail for 5 rounds. While Hailing, all non-Ice Type Pokemon lose a Tick of Hit Points at the beginning of their turn.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
