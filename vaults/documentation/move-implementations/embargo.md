Embargo is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Dark"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 0`, `ac: 2`, `range: "6, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 0 — no Energy is deducted per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Embargo skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

The target cannot use or benefit from held items for the remainder of the encounter. Embargo may only affect one target at a time; using Embargo on a new target frees the previous target.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
