Night Shade is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Ghost"`, `damageBase: "See Effect"`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "8, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Night Shade flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with a variable Damage Base. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

The target loses Hit Points equal to the level of Night Shade's user. Do not apply weakness or resistance. Do not apply stats.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
