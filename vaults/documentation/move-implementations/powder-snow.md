Powder Snow is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Ice"`, `damageBase: 4`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Line 4"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Powder Snow flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 4 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Ice-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Secondary Effect

Powder Snow Freezes all legal targets on 19+. The [[type-status-immunity-utility]] prevents Freeze application on Ice-types.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Sheer Force, Technician in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
