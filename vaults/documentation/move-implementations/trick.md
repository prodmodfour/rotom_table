Trick is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "5, 2 Targets"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Trick skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Both targets must be hit for Trick to succeed. The user may target itself or willing allies with Trick; you do not need to roll for Accuracy Check in these cases. Both targets lose their Held Item or Accessory Slot Item, and gain the other target's Held Item or Accessory Slot Item. If a target has no Item, it still can gain the other target's Item.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
