Ingrain is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Grass"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self, Coat"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Ingrain skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

Ingrain applies a Coat to the user. While the Coat is active, the user cannot be pushed or pulled and cannot be switched out. At the beginning of each of the user's turns, the user gains a Tick of Hit Points.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
