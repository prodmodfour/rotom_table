Heal Block is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: 2`, `range: "6, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Heal Block skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Until the end of the encounter, the target may not gain Hit Points or Temporary Hit Points from any source. This effect ends if the target is switched out or Takes a Breather.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
