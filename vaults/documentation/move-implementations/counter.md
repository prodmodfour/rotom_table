Counter is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Physical"`, `type: "Fighting"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "Melee, 1 Target, Reaction, Trigger"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Counter may be used as a Reaction when the user is hit by a damaging Physical Attack. The triggering attack is resolved with the user resisting it one step further. After resolution, if Counter's user was not Fainted, the triggering foe loses Hit Points equal to twice the HP lost by the user from the triggering attack. Counter is Physical and cannot miss, but cannot hit targets immune to Fighting-Type Moves.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Tough Claws in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
