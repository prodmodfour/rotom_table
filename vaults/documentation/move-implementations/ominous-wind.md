Ominous Wind is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Ghost"`, `damageBase: 6`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "6, 1 Target, Spirit Surge"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Ominous Wind flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 6 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Ghost-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Secondary Effect

On 19+, the user has each of its stats raised by +1 Combat Stage per the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Sheer Force, Technician in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
