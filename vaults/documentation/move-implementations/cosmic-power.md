Cosmic Power is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Cosmic Power skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The user's Defense is raised by 1 Combat Stage and Special Defense is raised by 1 Combat Stage, applied through the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
