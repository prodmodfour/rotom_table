Copycat is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "4, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Copycat skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null). Copycat cannot miss.

## Effect

The user copies the Move the target used on their last turn. The user may choose new targets for the copied Move.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
