Bestow is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 0`, `ac: null`, `range: "Melee, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 0 — no Energy is deducted per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Bestow skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The user gives its held item to the target, unless the target is already holding an item. Using Bestow is a Swift Action.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Tough Claws in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
