Clear Smog is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Poison"`, `damageBase: 5`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "6, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Clear Smog flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 5 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Poison-type users and type effectiveness. Clear Smog cannot miss.

## Effect

Clear Smog resets the target's Combat Stages to their defaults and destroys all Coats on the target.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Technician in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
