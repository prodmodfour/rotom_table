Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Air Cutter flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 6 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Normal-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Air Cutter is a Critical Hit on 18+.
