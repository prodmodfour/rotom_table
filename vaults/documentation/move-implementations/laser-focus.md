Laser Focus is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 10`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 10 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Laser Focus skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null). Laser Focus is a Swift Action.

## Effect

The user's next damaging move is guaranteed to be a Critical Hit.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
