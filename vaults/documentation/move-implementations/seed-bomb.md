## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Seed Bomb flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 8 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Grass-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].
