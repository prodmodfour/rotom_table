Dream Eater is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: 10`, `energyCost: 4`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Melee, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 4 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Dream Eater flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 10 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Psychic-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Dream Eater can only target Sleeping targets. After the target takes damage, the user gains HP equal to half of the damage dealt. Dream Eater does not wake up sleeping targets.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Tough Claws in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
