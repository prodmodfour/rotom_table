Rototiller is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Ground"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Burst 2"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Rototiller skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

All Grass-type Pokemon in the area raise their Attack and Special Attack 1 Combat Stage per the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
