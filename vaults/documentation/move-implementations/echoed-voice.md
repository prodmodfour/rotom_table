Echoed Voice is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: 4`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: 2`, `range: "3, 1 Target, Sonic"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Echoed Voice flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 4 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Normal-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

If Echoed Voice was used by any combatant in the Encounter on the previous round, its Damage Base increases by +4. If used during both of the previous two rounds, its Damage Base increases by +8 instead.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Technician, Punk Rock in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
