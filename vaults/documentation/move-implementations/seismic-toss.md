Seismic Toss is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Physical"`, `type: "Fighting"`, `damageBase: "See Effect"`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Melee, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Seismic Toss flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with a variable Damage Base. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

The target loses Hit Points equal to the level of Seismic Toss's user.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Tough Claws in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
