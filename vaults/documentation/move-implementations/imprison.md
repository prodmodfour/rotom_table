Imprison is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "10, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Imprison skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. Imprison cannot miss.

## Effect

The target is Locked for the rest of the Scene. A Locked target may not use any Moves the user knows.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
