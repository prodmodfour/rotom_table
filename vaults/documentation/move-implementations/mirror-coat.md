Mirror Coat is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: "See Effect"`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Any, 1 Target, Reaction"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Mirror Coat cannot miss, but it cannot hit targets immune to Psychic-Type Moves.

## Effect

Mirror Coat may be used as a Reaction when the user is hit by a damaging Special Attack. Resolve the triggering attack, with Mirror Coat's user resisting the attack one step further. After the attack is resolved, if Mirror Coat's user was not Fainted, the triggering foe then loses Hit Points equal to twice the amount of Hit Points lost by the user from the triggering attack.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
