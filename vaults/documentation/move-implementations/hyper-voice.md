Hyper Voice is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: 9`, `energyCost: 4`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Close Blast 3, Sonic, Smite"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 4 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Hyper Voice flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 9 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Normal-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

All legal targets are pushed back to the squares immediately outside the blast, away from the user.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Punk Rock in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
