Aromatherapy is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Grass"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Burst 1"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Aromatherapy skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

All allies in the burst are cured of one status condition of their choice.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
