Twister is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Dragon"`, `damageBase: 4`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: 2`, `range: "6, Ranged Blast 3"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Twister flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 4 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Dragon-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Secondary Effect

Small or Medium targets in the central square of the blast are not hit. Twister Flinches the target on 18-20 during Accuracy Check. Any Pokemon Airborne as a result of Fly or Sky Drop above the Blast are hit, ignoring range, and Twister has a Damage Base of 8 against those targets instead.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Sheer Force, Technician in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
