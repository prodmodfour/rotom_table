Night Slash is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Physical"`, `type: "Dark"`, `damageBase: 7`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Melee, Pass"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Night Slash flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 7 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Dark-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Night Slash is a Critical Hit on 18+.
