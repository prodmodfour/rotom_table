Confuse Ray is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Ghost"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: 2`, `range: "6, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Confuse Ray skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

The target becomes Confused. Confusion follows the [[confused-three-outcome-save]] resolution.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
