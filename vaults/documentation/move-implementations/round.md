Round is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: "See Effect"`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Burst 1, Sonic"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Round flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with variable DB as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Normal-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Round's Damage Base is equal to 6, plus +2 more for each previous use of Round by any Trainer or Pokemon this round, up to a maximum of DB 12.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Punk Rock in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
