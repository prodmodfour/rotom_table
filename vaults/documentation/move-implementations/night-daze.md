Night Daze is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Dark"`, `damageBase: 9`, `energyCost: 4`, `ac: 3`, `range: "4, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 4 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Night Daze flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 9 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Dark-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 3 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Secondary Effect

Night Daze lowers the target's Accuracy by -1 on 13+.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Sheer Force in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
