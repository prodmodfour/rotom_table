Double Team is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self, Illusion, Coat"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Double Team skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The user gains 3 activations of Double Team. The user may activate Double Team when being targeted by an attack to increase Evasion by +2 against that attack, or when making an attack to increase Accuracy by +2 for that attack.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
