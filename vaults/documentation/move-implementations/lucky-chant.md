Lucky Chant is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Blessing"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Lucky Chant skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

Blessing — Any user affected by Lucky Chant may activate it when receiving a Critical Hit to cause the attack to instead deal damage as if it was not a Critical Hit. Lucky Chant may be activated 3 times, and then disappears.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
