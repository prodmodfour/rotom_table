Acupressure is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Melee, 1 Target or Self"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Acupressure skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Roll 1d6 to determine which stat is raised by 2 Combat Stages: 1=Attack, 2=Defense, 3=Special Attack, 4=Special Defense, 5=Speed, 6=Accuracy (+2). Applied through the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Tough Claws in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
