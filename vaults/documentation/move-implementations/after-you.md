After You is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 5`, `ac: null`, `range: "6, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 5 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, After You skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

After You is a Swift Action. The target takes their turn immediately after the user finishes, ignoring Initiative. May only affect a target that has not yet acted that round and can only affect willing targets.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
