Focus Energy is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 5`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 5 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Focus Energy skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]].
No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

Swift Action. The user becomes Pumped. While Pumped, the user's Critical Range is extended by 2, or 18+ if not otherwise extended. Being switched will end this effect.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
