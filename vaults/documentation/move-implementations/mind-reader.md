Mind Reader is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 6`, `ac: null`, `range: "6, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 6 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Mind Reader skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null). Mind Reader is a Swift Action.

## Effect

The target becomes Read to the user until the end of the user's turn. The user may end this effect when making an Attack on the target, causing that attack to automatically hit.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
