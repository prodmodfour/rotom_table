Magic Room is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Field"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Magic Room skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The area becomes Useless for 5 rounds. While Useless, Pokemon may not benefit from the effects of any Held Items, and Trainers cannot benefit from any Accessory-Slot equipment. This does not affect consumable or activated items, only Items with Static effects or Triggers.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
