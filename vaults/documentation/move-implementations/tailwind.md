Tailwind is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Flying"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Field (allies)"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Tailwind skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

For the remainder of the encounter, all allied trainers and Pokemon gain +5 to their Initiative. Multiple instances of Tailwind cannot stack.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
