Odor Sleuth is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self, Swift Action"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Odor Sleuth skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null). Odor Sleuth is a Swift Action.

## Effect

For the rest of the turn, the user's Normal-Type and Fighting-Type Moves can hit and affect Ghost-Type targets, and the user can see through Illusion traits, Moves with the Illusion keyword, and effects created by the Illusionist trait, ignoring all effects from those.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
