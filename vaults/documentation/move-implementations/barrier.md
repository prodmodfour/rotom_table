Barrier is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "Hazard"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Barrier skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The user creates a Barrier of psychic energy. The user places up to 4 segments of Barrier; each segment must be continuous with another segment, and at least one must be adjacent to the user. These barriers count as blocking terrain and last until end of encounter or destruction. Each segment is 2m tall, 1m wide, 2cm thick. Each segment's HP equals the user's Barrier Trait X value * 15 with DR 15, taking damage as Psychic-typed.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
