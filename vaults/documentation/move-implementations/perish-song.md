Perish Song is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 4`, `ac: null`, `range: "Burst 15, Sonic"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 4 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Perish Song skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. Perish Song cannot miss.

## Effect

All targets, including the user, receive a Perish Count of 3. At the beginning of each of the target's turns, their Perish count is lowered by 1. Once a Perish Count reaches 0, set the Pokemon's Hit Points to 0. A Perish Count disappears if a target returns to their Poke Ball, Takes a Breather, or is knocked out. Perish Song never causes Massive Damage.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Punk Rock in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
