Earth Power is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Ground"`, `damageBase: 9`, `energyCost: 4`, `ac: 2`, `range: "6, 1 Target, Groundsource"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 4 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Earth Power flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 9 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Ground-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Earth Power lowers the target's Special Defense by 1 Combat Stage on 16+, applied through the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Range and Targeting

The Groundsource keyword means the move originates from the ground — it can hit underground targets but not those with Levitate or Sky movement.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Sheer Force in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
