Refresh is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "Self"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Refresh skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The user is cured of all Poison, Burning, and Paralysis.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
