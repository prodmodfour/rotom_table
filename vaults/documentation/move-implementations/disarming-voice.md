Disarming Voice is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Fairy"`, `damageBase: 4`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: null`, `range: "Burst 1"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Disarming Voice flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 4 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Fairy-type users and type effectiveness. Disarming Voice cannot miss.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Technician in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
