Grassy Terrain is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Grass"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Field"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Grassy Terrain skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The area becomes Grassy for 5 rounds. While Grassy, all grounded Pokemon and Trainers recover a Tick of Hit Points at the start of every turn, and Grass-Type attacks by grounded Pokemon and Trainers gain a +10 bonus to Damage Rolls.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
