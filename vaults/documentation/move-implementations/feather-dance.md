Feather Dance is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Flying"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Burst 1, Friendly"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Feather Dance skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

All legal targets have their Attack lowered 2 Combat Stages, applied through the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
