Misty Terrain is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Fairy"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: null`, `range: "Field"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Misty Terrain skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The area becomes Misty for 5 turns. While Misty, all Pokemon and Trainers standing on the ground ignore the first turn of all Status Afflictions, and Dragon-type attacks targeting or originating from a grounded Pokemon or Trainer take a -10 penalty to Damage Rolls.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
