Mist is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Ice"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "Blessing"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Mist skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

Blessing — Any user affected by Mist may activate it when having Combat Stages lowered by any effect; if they do, those Combat Stages are instead not lowered. Mist may be activated 3 times, and then disappears.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
