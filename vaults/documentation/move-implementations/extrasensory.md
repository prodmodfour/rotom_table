Extrasensory is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Special"`, `type: "Psychic"`, `damageBase: 8`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "5, 1 Target"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

Extrasensory flows through the standard [[damage-flow-pipeline]] with DB 8 as the base. The [[nine-step-damage-formula]] applies STAB for Psychic-type users and type effectiveness. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Secondary Effect

Extrasensory Flinches the target on 12+. Flinch prevents the target from taking actions for the remainder of the turn, tracked as a volatile condition.

## Trait Interactions

Flagged for Sheer Force in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
