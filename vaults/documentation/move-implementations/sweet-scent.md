Sweet Scent is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 3`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Burst 2, Friendly"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 3 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Sweet Scent skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Targets hit by Sweet Scent gain a -2 Penalty to Evasion. Total Evasion may not be lowered to a negative value.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
