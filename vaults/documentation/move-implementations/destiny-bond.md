Destiny Bond is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Ghost"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: null`, `range: "Burst 10, Friendly"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Destiny Bond skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

All enemy targets in the burst become Bound to the user until the end of the user's next turn. If a Bound target causes the user to Faint through a Damaging Attack, the Bound target immediately faints after their attack is resolved.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
