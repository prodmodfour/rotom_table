Haze is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Ice"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 2`, `ac: null`, `range: "Field"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 2 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Haze skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).

## Effect

The Combat Stages of the user and all Pokemon and Trainers in the encounter are set to their default state (usually 0), applied through the [[combat-stage-system]].

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
