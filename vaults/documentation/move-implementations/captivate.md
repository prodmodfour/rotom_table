Captivate is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `energyCost: 1`, `ac: 2`, `range: "Cone 2, Friendly, Social"`.

## Energy

Energy cost 1 is deducted from the user's Energy pool per [[move-energy-system]].

## Resolution

As a Status move, Captivate skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. An accuracy roll against AC 2 is required via the [[evasion-and-accuracy-system]].

## Effect

Captivate lowers the target's Special Attack 2 Combat Stages (applied through the [[combat-stage-system]]). The target becomes Infatuated if they fail a DC 10 + {User's Charm} Focus Check.

## Range and Targeting

The area of effect is visualized by the [[measurement-aoe-modes]] system.

## Trait Interactions

No trait-interaction flags are set in the [[moves-csv-source-file]].
