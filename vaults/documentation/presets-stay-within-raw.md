The app's presets and suggested values must stay within PTU's stated ranges. When PTU says "x1 to about x5" for significance multipliers, the presets cap at x5 — see [[significance-cap-x5]].

GMs who want to exceed RAW ranges can always enter custom/manual values. The presets should not suggest values outside the rules as written, because presets carry implicit authority ("the app says this is valid").
