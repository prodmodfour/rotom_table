Trainers have six combat stats: HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

These mirror the six Pokemon stats but follow different allocation and scaling rules. Trainers allocate stat points from a [[trainer-stat-budget]] at level-up, and their stats feed into [[trainer-derived-stats]] like evasion and HP totals.

The auto parse stat feature tags system detects `[+Stat]` patterns in feature text using these six stat names as its vocabulary, enabling automatic stat modification from equipped features.
