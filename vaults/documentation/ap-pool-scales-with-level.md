A trainer's Action Point pool increases with level.

The app recalculates AP automatically on level-up, treating it as a derived stat rather than a manually tracked value. This is part of [[automate-routine-bookkeeping]] — the trainer sees their updated AP pool without manual entry.

AP is spent during scenes on trainer features and actions, then restored at scene end via [[scene-end-ap-restoration]].
