# Weather Tick Automation

Weather damage and ability effects at turn boundaries.

**Weather damage:** Hail and Sandstorm deal tick damage at turn start to non-immune combatants. Type-based and trait-based immunities apply.

**Weather ability effects:**
- *Turn start:* Ice Body, Rain Dish, Sun Blanket (heal in matching weather); Solar Power (damage in Sun).
- *Turn end:* Dry Skin (heal in Rain, damage in Sun); Desert Weather (heal in Sandstorm).
