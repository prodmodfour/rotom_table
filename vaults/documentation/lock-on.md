Lock-On is stored in [[movedata-reference-table]] with `damageClass: "Status"`, `type: "Normal"`, `damageBase: null`, `frequency: "At-Will"`, `ac: null`, `range: "10, 1 Target"`.

## Frequency

At-Will frequency requires no usage tracking. `checkMoveFrequency` always permits use.

## Resolution

As a Status move, Lock-On skips the [[damage-flow-pipeline]]. No accuracy roll is needed (AC is null).
