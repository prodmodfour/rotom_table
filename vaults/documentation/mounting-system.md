# Mounting System

Trainer-Pokemon mounting for Mountable Pokemon.

## Mount

Standard Action, DC 10 Acrobatics/Athletics check. Expert Mounting trait makes it a Free Action during Shift. Mounted Prowess trait grants auto-success. GM can override with `skipCheck`.

## Dismount

Places rider in nearest unoccupied adjacent cell. Auto-dismount triggers on faint.

## Linked Movement

Both partners move together, decrementing shared movement budget. Movement budget recalculated from mount's Overland speed each round.

## Ride as One

Evasion swap mechanic wired into damage calculation. Cavalier's Reprisal detects when a mounted pair is attacked.
