# UML Internal View

A [[uml-view|view]] that describes the internal processes, activities, relationships, and structures of a [[uml-business-system|business system]]. Unlike the [[uml-external-view|external view]], this considers:

- Whether processing happens manually or through IT support
- Internal documentation requirements
- Supplier dependencies
- How IT systems and personnel deliver goods and services

Uses [[uml-package-diagram|package diagrams]], [[uml-class-diagram|class diagrams]], and [[uml-activity-diagram|activity diagrams]]. Activity diagrams of the internal view shift focus from actor interactions to worker responsibilities and are suitable as a basis for instructions.
