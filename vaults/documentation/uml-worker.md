# UML Worker

A [[uml]] stereotype used to describe the roles of people who execute [[uml-business-process|business processes]] within a [[uml-business-system|business system]].

## Key characteristics

- Workers are people located **inside** the business system
- Can communicate with other workers and external [[uml-actor|actors]]
- Responsible for providing goods and services
- Represented with a distinctive symbol (actor inside circle) or class notation with «Worker» stereotype

A single worker symbol represents a role, not an individual — one symbol may correspond to many real employees.
