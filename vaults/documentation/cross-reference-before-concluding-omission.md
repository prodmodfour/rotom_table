Before concluding that PTR omits a rule, check across multiple chapters and the errata. PTR's rules are distributed — a mechanic might be defined in one chapter, modified in another, and clarified in an erratum.

A rule that appears missing from the expected chapter may be stated elsewhere: damage formulas in the combat chapter, exceptions in the Pokemon chapter, clarifications in the GM chapter, corrections in the errata. Concluding omission after checking only one location leads to false [[silence-means-no-effect]] judgments or, worse, inventing a rule and falsely citing PTR for it.

The cross-reference check is: search the relevant chapter, then the errata, then adjacent chapters (abilities, items, GM tools). Only after that search comes up empty does [[silence-means-no-effect]] apply.
