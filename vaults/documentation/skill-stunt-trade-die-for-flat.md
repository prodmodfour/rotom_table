Skill stunts allow a trainer to trade a die from their skill roll for a flat bonus. This trade-off is encoded in the [[structured-edge-objects]] metadata so the app can present the option and apply it without manual calculation.

The choice to trade is made at roll time — the app shows the stunt as an available option and handles the arithmetic of removing a die and adding the flat value.
