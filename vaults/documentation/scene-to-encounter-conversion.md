# Scene-to-Encounter Conversion

Flow for converting a narrative scene into a tactical encounter:

1. GM initiates encounter creation from a scene.
2. A setup modal shows entity counts and encounter budget difficulty, letting the GM choose [[battle-modes|battle type]] (Full Contact or Trainer League) and significance tier (scales XP).
3. The system creates the encounter: scene Pokemon become wild enemy combatants, scene characters become player combatants, auto-placed on grid. Inherits scene weather.
